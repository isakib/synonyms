4 AADMI (INSAAN) [MAN,HUMAN]

[ side note:putting summary first because discourse is a bit more elaborate and drill down deep in to subtleties and nuancies which will be better understood once summary is in mind]

Summary:
1 INS: when the discussion includes the social aspect of human then INS and its derived forms are used
2 ADAM: when historical aspect is under discussion then ADAM and BANI ADAM children of ADAM is used
3 BASHAR: when natural,physical aspects are discussed than BASHAR is used

Details are as follows

Synonyms: INS,ANSIYAAN,ADAM,BASHAR

INS(انس) and its derivatives like INSAAN(انسان) INSIYYAN(انسيّا) etc and also ADAM(ادم) and BASHAR(بشر) are used for human and man in the Quran,

4.0.1 In ghareeb ul Quran many a meaning of INS are mentioned.Ibne Qatiba wrote that human are called INS because he is visible and can be perceived with eyes and the proof he gave is from this ayah
Surat Al-Qaşaş (The Stories) - سورة القصص
28:29
فَلَمَّا قَضَىٰ مُوسَى الْأَجَلَ وَسَارَ بِأَهْلِهِ آنَسَ مِن جَانِبِ الطُّورِ نَارًا قَالَ لِأَهْلِهِ امْكُثُوا إِنِّي آنَسْتُ نَارًا لَّعَلِّي آتِيكُم مِّنْهَا بِخَبَرٍ أَوْ جَذْوَةٍ مِّنَ النَّارِ لَعَلَّكُمْ تَصْطَلُونَ
And when Moses had completed the term and was traveling with his family, he perceived from the direction of the mount a fire. He said to his family, "Stay here; indeed, I have perceived a fire. Perhaps I will bring you from there [some] information or burning wood from the fire that you may warm yourselves."

and from this aspect opp of INS is JIN(جّن) which means invisible or concealed and cannot be perceive by eyes.and this seems to be the case as these two creation are said as SAQALAN (ثقلان) by ALLAH and they are used in contrast with each other

Surat Adh-Dhāriyāt (The Winnowing Winds) - سورة الذاريات
51:56

And I did not create the jinn and mankind except to worship Me.


4.0.2 ibne abbas reported that human is called INSAAN or INSIYYA as LI INNA HU UHDE ELAYHE FA NASIYA (لانّه عهد إليه فنسي) meaning a covenant was taken from him which he forgets. so from this point of view INSAAN or INSIYYA is made of root NaSiYa(نسي)

4.0.3 imam raghib describe it to be derived from UNS(اُنس).meaning a creation which is created to live together in social life and harmony. To cherish and in connection/relation with each other. from this aspect the opp is WAHUSH( وحش)

4.0.4 and Ibn ul Faris has put together both definition
1) being visible 2) anything which is different from wild and beastly ways and behaviour

INS is ISM UL JINS( إسم إلجنس) this means that INS can refer to a single person as well as the whole of mankind.same is the case with word INSAAN but generally it means mankind.its singular is INSIYYUN(اِنسيُُ)

So eat and drink and be contented. And if you see from among humanity anyone, say, 'Indeed, I have vowed to the Most Merciful abstention, so I will not speak today to [any] man.' "

and its plural is NAAS(ناس),UNAS(اناس),UNASI(اناسي)

O mankind, worship your Lord, who created you

4.1 UNAS( اُناس):used for such a group of humans which is different from others owing to tribe,classification or anyother reason

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة
2:60
وَإِذِ اسْتَسْقَىٰ مُوسَىٰ لِقَوْمِهِ فَقُلْنَا اضْرِب بِّعَصَاكَ الْحَجَرَ ۖ فَانفَجَرَتْ مِنْهُ اثْنَتَا عَشْرَةَ عَيْنًا ۖ قَدْ عَلِمَ كُلُّ أُنَاسٍ مَّشْرَبَهُمْ ۖ كُلُوا وَاشْرَبُوا مِن رِّزْقِ اللَّهِ وَلَا تَعْثَوْا فِي الْأَرْضِ مُفْسِدِينَ
And [recall] when Moses prayed for water for his people, so We said, "Strike with your staff the stone." And there gushed forth from it twelve springs, and every people knew its watering place. "Eat and drink from the provision of Allah , and do not commit abuse on the earth, spreading corruption."

and ANASIYYA(اناسيُُ) : a big group of people

4.2 ADAM:is from root Aa-Da-M basic meaning is favourable and tenderness. and ADAAM() meaning every favourable and tender thing.this meaning is from Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) saying which he told to mughira bin sheba (may ALLAH be please with him)

لو نَظَرْتَ اِلَيهافانّه احري ان يّؤدم بينَكما
(Tirmidhi,Nisai)
"If you see you fiancee once then it there will be more chance of love and happiness between you"
there are other reference but this is strong as it confirms with hadith.

ADAM( ادم) : name of father of all man.yet it also refers to human as individual or BANI ADAM children of adam refering to the all of mankind

and ADAM or BANI ADAM(بَني ادم) in Quran refers to History of humans e.g Adam's birth from clay,taking covenant from all mankind,prostration of Angels,to be superior among creation,saytan's leading Adam and his children astray and his dialogue with ALLAH

4.3 BASHAR(بشر):basic meaning is exhibiting something with beauty and elegance.whereas UNS(انس) mean only exhibit ALBASHARAH(البشرة) means the topmost layer of human skin (side note: which contains pain receptors)

Surat Al-Muddaththir (The Cloaked One) - سورة المدثر
74:29
لَوَّاحَةٌ لِّلْبَشَرِ
Blackening the skins.
it's plural is BASHARUN( بَشَرُُ) and ABSHARUN(ابشارُُ) and human is also said BASHR because his skin is clean and pure of hairs n wool as compared to other animals and is visible BASHAR is used for singular,plural,masculin,feminine but it's dual is BASHARAIN(بَشَرَين)

Surat Al-Mu'minūn (The Believers) - سورة المؤمنون

They said, "Should we believe two men like ourselves while their people are for us in servitude?"

and BASHAR is used when human's physical and material aspect,his built,natural traits and weakness are discussed as evident from the aforementioned ayah. and BASHAR in contrast with MALK( مَلك) meaning angel who is devoid of all those traits mentioned above

So what is his superiority over us that we should believe him, well if there is to be an angel who would be devoid of these traits then it can be conidered
ALLAH has answered to this objection is this ayah
