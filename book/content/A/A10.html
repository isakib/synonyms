10 AZMAISH KARNA[ To Put to Trial]

Synonyms: IMTAHANA(MA-HA-NA),BALA,IBTALA(BA-LA-WU) and FATANA

10.1 IMTAHANA(إِمتَحَنَ):it is such a trial in which there is ease instead of hardship and it includes the aspect of generousity and some times before this trial the one undergoing it is also trained and educated. ALLAH said

Surat Al-Mumtaĥanah (She that is to be examined) - سورة الممتحنة
60:10
يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا إِذَا جَاءَكُمُ الْمُؤْمِنَاتُ مُهَاجِرَاتٍ فَامْتَحِنُوهُنَّ ۖ اللَّهُ أَعْلَمُ بِإِيمَانِهِنَّ ۖ فَإِنْ عَلِمْتُمُوهُنَّ مُؤْمِنَاتٍ فَلَا تَرْجِعُوهُنَّ إِلَى الْكُفَّارِ ۖ لَا هُنَّ حِلٌّ لَّهُمْ وَلَا هُمْ يَحِلُّونَ لَهُنَّ ۖ وَآتُوهُم مَّا أَنفَقُوا ۚ وَلَا جُنَاحَ عَلَيْكُمْ أَن تَنكِحُوهُنَّ إِذَا آتَيْتُمُوهُنَّ أُجُورَهُنَّ ۚ وَلَا تُمْسِكُوا بِعِصَمِ الْكَوَافِرِ وَاسْأَلُوا مَا أَنفَقْتُمْ وَلْيَسْأَلُوا مَا أَنفَقُوا ۚ ذَٰلِكُمْ حُكْمُ اللَّهِ ۖ يَحْكُمُ بَيْنَكُمْ ۚ وَاللَّهُ عَلِيمٌ حَكِيمٌ
O you who have believed, when the believing women come to you as emigrants, examine them. Allah is most knowing as to their faith. And if you know them to be believers, then do not return them to the disbelievers; they are not lawful [wives] for them, nor are they lawful [husbands] for them. But give the disbelievers what they have spent. And there is no blame upon you if you marry them when you have given them their due compensation. And hold not to marriage bonds with disbelieving women, but ask for what you have spent and let them ask for what they have spent. That is the judgement of Allah ; He judges between you. And Allah is Knowing and Wise.

10.2 BALA(بَلَى):(YABLOW يَبلُو BALAAAN بَلاَء) BALA is such a trial in which there is hardship and severity ,this trial can be in both the form of good and evil. ALLAH said

Surat Al-'A`rāf (The Heights) - سورة الأعراف
7:168
وَقَطَّعْنَاهُمْ فِي الْأَرْضِ أُمَمًا ۖ مِّنْهُمُ الصَّالِحُونَ وَمِنْهُمْ دُونَ ذَٰلِكَ ۖ وَبَلَوْنَاهُم بِالْحَسَنَاتِ وَالسَّيِّئَاتِ لَعَلَّهُمْ يَرْجِعُونَ
And We divided them throughout the earth into nations. Of them some were righteous, and of them some were otherwise. And We tested them with good [times] and bad that perhaps they would return [to obedience].

because trial is generally painful so the usage is more for evil for e.g.

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة
2:155
وَلَنَبْلُوَنَّكُم بِشَيْءٍ مِّنَ الْخَوْفِ وَالْجُوعِ وَنَقْصٍ مِّنَ الْأَمْوَالِ وَالْأَنفُسِ وَالثَّمَرَاتِ ۗ وَبَشِّرِ الصَّابِرِينَ
And We will surely test you with something of fear and hunger and a loss of wealth and lives and fruits, but give good tidings to the patient,

and IBTALA (إِبتَلَى) means to make something upside down or to inspect by varying conditions. ALLAH said

Surat Al-'Insān (The Man) - سورة الانسان
76:2
إِنَّا خَلَقْنَا الْإِنسَانَ مِن نُّطْفَةٍ أَمْشَاجٍ نَّبْتَلِيهِ فَجَعَلْنَاهُ سَمِيعًا بَصِيرًا
Indeed, We created man from a sperm-drop mixture that We may try him; and We made him hearing and seeing.

then IBTALA-(إِبتَلاَ) is generally from a sudden accident. such an incident which other people can see .see the ayah below:

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة
2:124
وَإِذِ ابْتَلَىٰ إِبْرَاهِيمَ رَبُّهُ بِكَلِمَاتٍ فَأَتَمَّهُنَّ ۖ قَالَ إِنِّي جَاعِلُكَ لِلنَّاسِ إِمَامًا ۖ قَالَ وَمِن ذُرِّيَّتِي ۖ قَالَ لَا يَنَالُ عَهْدِي الظَّالِمِينَ
And [mention, O Muhammad], when Abraham was tried by his Lord with commands and he fulfilled them. [ Allah ] said, "Indeed, I will make you a leader for the people." [Abraham] said, "And of my descendants?" [ Allah ] said, "My covenant does not include the wrongdoers."

it is clear that these matters were ,exile,leaving offspring in barren field helpless,to prepare for sacrificing son,to enter into fire etc etc. which all other people could have seen.

10.3 FATANA(فَتَنَ): like IBTALA- it includes the hardship in trial . FATANA means to put gold or silver in a furnace ,heat it ,melt it and find out its impurities. ALLAH said

Surat Adh-Dhāriyāt (The Winnowing Winds) - سورة الذاريات
51:13
يَوْمَ هُمْ عَلَى النَّارِ يُفْتَنُونَ
[It is] the Day they will be tormented over the Fire

and word FITNAH(فِتنَة) is mostly used for bad meaning. FITNAH means trial,sadness,grief,embarrasment,delirious,warning ,punishment and disease. likewise FATTAN(فَتَّان) means evil person,thief,devil .although in IBTALA- trial on a human is not because of personal sins or wickedness. Period of Ibtala- and Period of FATAN difference is obvious. In Ibtala- the bad aspect is related to natural disasters wheras in FITNAH the bad aspect is mostly self created by human (individually). ALLAH said

Surat Al-'Anfāl (The Spoils of War) - سورة الأنفال
8:73
وَالَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا بَعْضُهُمْ أَوْلِيَاءُ بَعْضٍ ۚ إِلَّا تَفْعَلُوهُ تَكُن فِتْنَةٌ فِي الْأَرْضِ وَفَسَادٌ كَبِيرٌ
And those who disbelieved are allies of one another. If you do not do so, there will be fitnah on earth and great corruption.

the most prominent difference between IBATALA- and FITNAH is that in FITNAH trial is normally of those things which a person love and are near to one's heart. Thus this Ayah of Holy Quran

Surat Al-'Anfāl (The Spoils of War) - سورة الأنفال
8:28
وَاعْلَمُوا أَنَّمَا أَمْوَالُكُمْ وَأَوْلَادُكُمْ فِتْنَةٌ وَأَنَّ اللَّهَ عِندَهُ أَجْرٌ عَظِيمٌ
And know that your properties and your children are but a trial and that Allah has with Him a great reward.

is a powerful proof. in Surat Al-Baqarah ,the narrative of haroot and maroot, ALLAH said

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة
2:102
وَاتَّبَعُوا مَا تَتْلُو الشَّيَاطِينُ عَلَىٰ مُلْكِ سُلَيْمَانَ ۖ وَمَا كَفَرَ سُلَيْمَانُ وَلَٰكِنَّ الشَّيَاطِينَ كَفَرُوا يُعَلِّمُونَ النَّاسَ السِّحْرَ وَمَا أُنزِلَ عَلَى الْمَلَكَيْنِ بِبَابِلَ هَارُوتَ وَمَارُوتَ ۚ وَمَا يُعَلِّمَانِ مِنْ أَحَدٍ حَتَّىٰ يَقُولَا إِنَّمَا نَحْنُ فِتْنَةٌ فَلَا تَكْفُرْ ۖ فَيَتَعَلَّمُونَ مِنْهُمَا مَا يُفَرِّقُونَ بِهِ بَيْنَ الْمَرْءِ وَزَوْجِهِ ۚ وَمَا هُم بِضَارِّينَ بِهِ مِنْ أَحَدٍ إِلَّا بِإِذْنِ اللَّهِ ۚ وَيَتَعَلَّمُونَ مَا يَضُرُّهُمْ وَلَا يَنفَعُهُمْ ۚ وَلَقَدْ عَلِمُوا لَمَنِ اشْتَرَاهُ مَا لَهُ فِي الْآخِرَةِ مِنْ خَلَاقٍ ۚ وَلَبِئْسَ مَا شَرَوْا بِهِ أَنفُسَهُمْ ۚ لَوْ كَانُوا يَعْلَمُونَ
And they followed [instead] what the devils had recited during the reign of Solomon. It was not Solomon who disbelieved, but the devils disbelieved, teaching people magic and that which was revealed to the two angels at Babylon, Harut and Marut. But the two angels do not teach anyone unless they say, "We are a trial, so do not disbelieve [by practicing magic]." And [yet] they learn from them that by which they cause separation between a man and his wife. But they do not harm anyone through it except by permission of Allah . And the people learn what harms them and does not benefit them. But the Children of Israel certainly knew that whoever purchased the magic would not have in the Hereafter any share. And wretched is that for which they sold themselves, if they only knew.

and this disbelief was study of magic, in craving of which those people were arrested/involved. This craving is refered to as FITNAH

Summary:
10.1 IMTAHAN: In this trial there is ease instead of hardship and in that trial ,previous training and education is tested
10.2 BALA- & IBTALA:This a severe trial and generally of those occurences which others can also see meaning accidents
10.3 FITNAH: in it of itself severe ,but alluring. meaning generally with those things which are near to a person's heart. Forget others ,some times even the one undergoing trial is neither aware of the trial nor that he/she is going through it
