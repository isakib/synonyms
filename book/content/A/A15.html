15 AAG KA ANGARA [FIREBALL]
Synonyms: SHIHABUN ,JIZWATUN, QABASUN

15.1 SHIHABUN(شهَابٌ): it such a fireball in which there is sparkle and flame whether it's of fire or in the sky and its plural is SHUHUB(شُهُب). in the Quran

Surat Al-Jinn (The Jinn) - سورة الجن
72:8
وَأَنَّا لَمَسْنَا السَّمَاءَ فَوَجَدْنَاهَا مُلِئَتْ حَرَسًا شَدِيدًا وَشُهُبًا
And we have sought [to reach] the heaven but found it filled with powerful guards and burning flames.

15.2 JIWATUN(جِذوَةٌ): such a fireball in which brightness is gone and ashes comes over . in the Quran:

Surat Al-Qaşaş (The Stories) - سورة القصص
28:29
فَلَمَّا قَضَىٰ مُوسَى الْأَجَلَ وَسَارَ بِأَهْلِهِ آنَسَ مِن جَانِبِ الطُّورِ نَارًا قَالَ لِأَهْلِهِ امْكُثُوا إِنِّي آنَسْتُ نَارًا لَّعَلِّي آتِيكُم مِّنْهَا بِخَبَرٍ أَوْ جَذْوَةٍ مِّنَ النَّارِ لَعَلَّكُمْ تَصْطَلُونَ
And when Moses had completed the term and was traveling with his family, he perceived from the direction of the mount a fire. He said to his family, "Stay here; indeed, I have perceived a fire. Perhaps I will bring you from there [some] information or burning wood from the fire that you may warm yourselves."


15.3 QABASUN(قَبَسٌ): borrowed fireball or flame (see detail in AAG JALANA)


Summary:
15.1 SHIHAB: sparkling flaming fireball
15.2 JIZWAH: such a fireball on which ashes are coming and brightness ended
15.3 QABAS: borrowed fireball or flame
