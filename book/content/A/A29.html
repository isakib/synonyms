29 AAHISTA AAHISTA [SLOWLY SLOWLY]

Synonyms: RUWYD(R-W-D),RUKHA-(R-K-W),3URF,YUSR,ISTADRAJ,DALLA

29.1 RUWYD( رُوَيدَا): derived from ALRUWD(الرّود) and gerund of ARWADU(اَرْوَدُ) is MASAGGR(مصغّر). It is said IMSH 3LA ALRUWD(إِمش علي الرود) meaning walk slowly . SARU SAYRAN RUWAYDAN(سَارُوْا سَيْراً رُوَيْداً). they walked softly and slowly slowly. and ARWADU meaning to be slow in one's work. also it is said ALDAHR AUWRADU ZU GHAYRIN (الدَهْرُ اَوْرَدُ ذُوُ غِيرٍ) meaning Time keeps on doing it's work quietly quietly. it doesn't let it be known. also it is said RUWAYDA ZAYDAN(رُوَيْداً زَيْداً) give Zayd extension (more time for deadline ). so RUWAYD(رُوَيدَ) means slowly slowly and quietly quietly delaying and give respite.
ALLAH said:

Surat Aţ-Ţāriq (The Nightcommer) - سورة الطارق
86:17
فَمَهِّلِ الْكَافِرِينَ أَمْهِلْهُمْ رُوَيْدًا
So allow time for the disbelievers. Leave them awhile


29.2 RUKHAAN(رُخَاءً): such a soft and slowly blowing wind that doesn't move anything (Breeze).Gentle and soft flowing of wind. ALLAH said

Surat Şād (The Letter "Saad") - سورة ص
38:36
فَسَخَّرْنَا لَهُ الرِّيحَ تَجْرِي بِأَمْرِهِ رُخَاءً حَيْثُ أَصَابَ
So We subjected to him the wind blowing by his command, gently, wherever he directed,


29.3 3RFAN(عُرفاً): gently flowing pleasant breeze. AL3RF العرف meaning (smell). it is mostly used for fragrance. and normally such winds paced slowly. like early morning wind. In the Quran

Surat Al-Mursalāt (The Emissaries) - سورة المرسلات
77:1
وَالْمُرْسَلَاتِ عُرْفًا
By those [winds] sent forth in gusts

29.4 YUSR(يُسْر) : meaning ease and comfort and it's antonym is 3SR(عسر) meaning difficulty. and YUSRAN يُسْراً refers to such a condition when some thing can be done easily and comfortably without any formality.and it doesn't contain any shortcomings. ALLAH said

Surat Adh-Dhāriyāt (The Winnowing Winds) - سورة الذاريات
51:3
فَالْجَارِيَاتِ يُسْرًا
And those [ships] sailing with ease

here YUSR doesn't mean that speed is slow but it means that there is no rocking or shortcoming or inclination or noise even though it's pacing swiftly and owing to this it seems that its a slow and smooth ride


29.5 ISTADRAJ(إستدراج):DARJAH(درجة) is step of a ladder when ascending upstairs and ISTADRJ(إستدرج) meaning to slowly slowly and gradually bring one thing close to another thing so that it doesn't become aware (imperceptible) . so ISTADRAJ has to include two elements of being gradual and slowlness
ALLAH Said

Surat Al-'A`rāf (The Heights) - سورة الأعراف
7:182
وَالَّذِينَ كَذَّبُوا بِآيَاتِنَا سَنَسْتَدْرِجُهُم مِّنْ حَيْثُ لَا يَعْلَمُونَ
But those who deny Our signs - We will progressively lead them [to destruction] from where they do not know.


29.6 DALLA(دلَّي): DALWUN(دَلْوٌ) is such a bucket which is empty of water and ADLA(ادْلَي) means to place an empty bucket in the wall for it to be filled up ,which slowly slowly reaches to the water and DALLA meaning to slowly slowly and gradually accomplish a task or achieve the objective. ALLAH said

Surat Al-'A`rāf (The Heights) - سورة الأعراف
7:22
فَدَلَّاهُمَا بِغُرُورٍ ۚ فَلَمَّا ذَاقَا الشَّجَرَةَ بَدَتْ لَهُمَا سَوْآتُهُمَا وَطَفِقَا يَخْصِفَانِ عَلَيْهِمَا مِن وَرَقِ الْجَنَّةِ ۖ وَنَادَاهُمَا رَبُّهُمَا أَلَمْ أَنْهَكُمَا عَن تِلْكُمَا الشَّجَرَةِ وَأَقُل لَّكُمَا إِنَّ الشَّيْطَانَ لَكُمَا عَدُوٌّ مُّبِينٌ
So he made them fall, through deception. And when they tasted of the tree, their private parts became apparent to them, and they began to fasten together over themselves from the leaves of Paradise. And their Lord called to them, "Did I not forbid you from that tree and tell you that Satan is to you a clear enemy?"


Summary:
29.1 RUWYD to let loose someone's rope slowly slowly so that someone can reach its end
29.2 RUKHA- Wind breeezing slowly and softly without any resistance
29.3 3URFAN Wind gently blowing and also include being pleasant
29.4 YUSRAN To finish a task informally and comfortably without any resistance
29.5 ISTADRAJ gradually and slowly approach some other thing
29.6 DALLA gradually and slowly achieve one's objective
