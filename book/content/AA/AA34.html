34 ALAG HONA (JUDA HONA-3LAYHDA HONA)
Synonyms: TAFARRAQA from FARAQA,I3TAZALA from 3AZALA,TAJANNABA from JANABA,AMTAZ from MAZA , TAZAYYAL from ZAYYALA. they have been discussed linguistically(N.B. Topic 33) now let's see their exampes

34.1 TAFARRAQA(تَفَرَّقَ): plural of TAJAMMU3(تَجَمُّع) meaning to be separate from each other and to be parted. explode in to separation. ALLAH said

Surat 'Āli `Imrān (Family of Imran) - سورة آل عمران
3:103
وَاعْتَصِمُوا بِحَبْلِ اللَّهِ جَمِيعًا وَلَا تَفَرَّقُوا ۚ وَاذْكُرُوا نِعْمَتَ اللَّهِ عَلَيْكُمْ إِذْ كُنتُمْ أَعْدَاءً فَأَلَّفَ بَيْنَ قُلُوبِكُمْ فَأَصْبَحْتُم بِنِعْمَتِهِ إِخْوَانًا وَكُنتُمْ عَلَىٰ شَفَا حُفْرَةٍ مِّنَ النَّارِ فَأَنقَذَكُم مِّنْهَا ۗ كَذَٰلِكَ يُبَيِّنُ اللَّهُ لَكُمْ آيَاتِهِ لَعَلَّكُمْ تَهْتَدُونَ
And hold firmly to the rope of Allah all together and do not become divided. And remember the favor of Allah upon you - when you were enemies and He brought your hearts together and you became, by His favor, brothers. And you were on the edge of a pit of the Fire, and He saved you from it. Thus does Allah make clear to you His verses that you may be guided.


34.2 IETAZALA(اِعتَزَلَ): to withdraw from something whether its a deed or a matter. whether this withdrawal is apparent or abstract it will be used in all cases. let see their examples

a) withdrawal from matters meaning abstract withdrawal

Surat Ad-Dukhān (The Smoke) - سورة الدخان
44:21
وَإِن لَّمْ تُؤْمِنُوا لِي فَاعْتَزِلُونِ
But if you do not believe me, then leave me alone."

b) withdrawal from deeds meaning apparent withdrawal

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة
2:222
وَيَسْأَلُونَكَ عَنِ الْمَحِيضِ ۖ قُلْ هُوَ أَذًى فَاعْتَزِلُوا النِّسَاءَ فِي الْمَحِيضِ ۖ وَلَا تَقْرَبُوهُنَّ حَتَّىٰ يَطْهُرْنَ ۖ فَإِذَا تَطَهَّرْنَ فَأْتُوهُنَّ مِنْ حَيْثُ أَمَرَكُمُ اللَّهُ ۚ إِنَّ اللَّهَ يُحِبُّ التَّوَّابِينَ وَيُحِبُّ الْمُتَطَهِّرِينَ
And they ask you about menstruation. Say, "It is harm, so keep away from wives during menstruation. And do not approach them until they are pure. And when they have purified themselves, then come to them from where Allah has ordained for you. Indeed, Allah loves those who are constantly repentant and loves those who purify themselves."

34.3 TAJANNABA(تَجَنَّبَ): to move aside and keep distance. to avoid

Surat Al-'A`lá (The Most High) - سورة الأعلى
87:10
سَيَذَّكَّرُ مَن يَخْشَىٰ
He who fears [ Allah ] will be reminded.

Surat Al-'A`lá (The Most High) - سورة الأعلى
87:11
وَيَتَجَنَّبُهَا الْأَشْقَى
But the wretched one will avoid it -

34.4 IMTAZA(إِمتَازَ): separation between good thing and bad thing from one another based on preference,blessing

Surat Yā-Sīn (Ya Sin) - سورة يس
36:59
وَامْتَازُوا الْيَوْمَ أَيُّهَا الْمُجْرِمُونَ
[Then He will say], "But stand apart today, you criminals.

apart from aforementioned words, following word are also used to mean ' to separate'
KHALA (KH-LA-W),KHALASA,FASALA,INTABAZA(NA-BA-ZA),TAZAYYALA(ZA-YA-LA),TAJAFA(JA-FA-W)

34.5 KHALA(خَلاَ),YUKHLU(يَخلُو) : KHLA-(خلاء) is empty place where there are no building or houses etc ,nothing exist. This word can be used for both time and place. like KHLA ALZAMAN(خَلا الزَمَان) meaning era goes by and as adverb of place KHLA ALRAJL(خَلا الرَجل) meaning to meet someone in seclusion.

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة
2:14
وَإِذَا لَقُوا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا قَالُوا آمَنَّا وَإِذَا خَلَوْا إِلَىٰ شَيَاطِينِهِمْ قَالُوا إِنَّا مَعَكُمْ إِنَّمَا نَحْنُ مُسْتَهْزِئُونَ
And when they meet those who believe, they say, "We believe"; but when they are alone with their evil ones, they say, "Indeed, we are with you; we were only mockers."

34.6 KHALASA(خَلَصَ):to purify some thing from mixture and to separate impurity from it. and its antonym is KHALATA(خَلَطَ).

Surat Yūsuf (Joseph) - سورة يوسف
12:80
فَلَمَّا اسْتَيْأَسُوا مِنْهُ خَلَصُوا نَجِيًّا ۖ قَالَ كَبِيرُهُمْ أَلَمْ تَعْلَمُوا أَنَّ أَبَاكُمْ قَدْ أَخَذَ عَلَيْكُم مَّوْثِقًا مِّنَ اللَّهِ وَمِن قَبْلُ مَا فَرَّطتُمْ فِي يُوسُفَ ۖ فَلَنْ أَبْرَحَ الْأَرْضَ حَتَّىٰ يَأْذَنَ لِي أَبِي أَوْ يَحْكُمَ اللَّهُ لِي ۖ وَهُوَ خَيْرُ الْحَاكِمِينَ
So when they had despaired of him, they secluded themselves in private consultation. The eldest of them said, "Do you not know that your father has taken upon you an oath by Allah and [that] before you failed in [your duty to] Joseph? So I will never leave [this] land until my father permits me or Allah decides for me, and He is the best of judges.

meaning to consult they secluded from other people to somewhere isolated.

34.7 FASALA(فَصَلَ):two things to separate in such a way that there exist distance between them. to depart from some place

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة
2:249
فَلَمَّا فَصَلَ طَالُوتُ بِالْجُنُودِ قَالَ إِنَّ اللَّهَ مُبْتَلِيكُم بِنَهَرٍ فَمَن شَرِبَ مِنْهُ فَلَيْسَ مِنِّي وَمَن لَّمْ يَطْعَمْهُ فَإِنَّهُ مِنِّي إِلَّا مَنِ اغْتَرَفَ غُرْفَةً بِيَدِهِ ۚ فَشَرِبُوا مِنْهُ إِلَّا قَلِيلًا مِّنْهُمْ ۚ فَلَمَّا جَاوَزَهُ هُوَ وَالَّذِينَ آمَنُوا مَعَهُ قَالُوا لَا طَاقَةَ لَنَا الْيَوْمَ بِجَالُوتَ وَجُنُودِهِ ۚ قَالَ الَّذِينَ يَظُنُّونَ أَنَّهُم مُّلَاقُو اللَّهِ كَم مِّن فِئَةٍ قَلِيلَةٍ غَلَبَتْ فِئَةً كَثِيرَةً بِإِذْنِ اللَّهِ ۗ وَاللَّهُ مَعَ الصَّابِرِينَ
And when Saul went forth with the soldiers, he said, "Indeed, Allah will be testing you with a river. So whoever drinks from it is not of me, and whoever does not taste it is indeed of me, excepting one who takes [from it] in the hollow of his hand." But they drank from it, except a [very] few of them. Then when he had crossed it along with those who believed with him, they said, "There is no power for us today against Goliath and his soldiers." But those who were certain that they would meet Allah said, "How many a small company has overcome a large company by permission of Allah . And Allah is with the patient."


34.8 INTABAZA(إِنتَبَذَ):NABAZA(نَبَذَ) meaning to throw something considering it worthless (not worth a glance.turn away) . and INTABAZA means to separate from others owing to self assumption of one being inferior to others. ALLAH said
Surat Maryam (Mary) - سورة مريم
19:16
وَاذْكُرْ فِي الْكِتَابِ مَرْيَمَ إِذِ انتَبَذَتْ مِنْ أَهْلِهَا مَكَانًا شَرْقِيًّا
And mention, [O Muhammad], in the Book [the story of] Mary, when she withdrew from her family to a place toward the east.

at this place ,INTABAZA is used for Mary because to avoid people's allegation and objections,criticism owing to pregnancy with Jesus(AS), she isolated her from people to the western house reclusively.

34.9 TAZAYYAL(تَزَيَّل):ZAL(زَال) meaning to displace a thing from its place. from this relation TAZAYYAL means something left its place to move here or there. to displace aside. ALLAH said

Surat Al-Fatĥ (The Victory) - سورة الفتح
48:25
هُمُ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا وَصَدُّوكُمْ عَنِ الْمَسْجِدِ الْحَرَامِ وَالْهَدْيَ مَعْكُوفًا أَن يَبْلُغَ مَحِلَّهُ ۚ وَلَوْلَا رِجَالٌ مُّؤْمِنُونَ وَنِسَاءٌ مُّؤْمِنَاتٌ لَّمْ تَعْلَمُوهُمْ أَن تَطَئُوهُمْ فَتُصِيبَكُم مِّنْهُم مَّعَرَّةٌ بِغَيْرِ عِلْمٍ ۖ لِّيُدْخِلَ اللَّهُ فِي رَحْمَتِهِ مَن يَشَاءُ ۚ لَوْ تَزَيَّلُوا لَعَذَّبْنَا الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا مِنْهُمْ عَذَابًا أَلِيمًا
They are the ones who disbelieved and obstructed you from al-Masjid al-Haram while the offering was prevented from reaching its place of sacrifice. And if not for believing men and believing women whom you did not know - that you might trample them and there would befall you because of them dishonor without [your] knowledge - [you would have been permitted to enter Makkah]. [This was so] that Allah might admit to His mercy whom He willed. If they had been apart [from them], We would have punished those who disbelieved among them with painful punishment


34.10 TAJAFA(تَجَافَي):JAFWATUN(جَفوَةٌ) and JAFAUN(جَفَاءٌ) meaning cruelty and JAFA(جَفَا) YAJFU(يَجفُوا) meaning to not stay at a place. and from that TAJAFA means to change places owing to impatience or to stay away from that place. ALLAH said

Surat As-Sajdah (The Prostration) - سورة السجدة
32:16
تَتَجَافَىٰ جُنُوبُهُمْ عَنِ الْمَضَاجِعِ يَدْعُونَ رَبَّهُمْ خَوْفًا وَطَمَعًا وَمِمَّا رَزَقْنَاهُمْ يُنفِقُونَ
They arise from [their] beds; they supplicate their Lord in fear and aspiration, and from what We have provided them, they spend.

Summary:
34.1 TAFARRAQA: To separate from gathering and to be different
34.2 I3TAZAL: To withdraw from some deed or matter or humans
34.3 TAJANNABA To separate from something and go away afar to be saved from trouble
34.4 IMTAZA to be separated from others on the basis of some speciality
34.5 KHALA To separate for meeting in seclusion
34.6 KHALASA To separate mixture from the real thing and real remains
34.8 INTABAZA To separate from others owing to inferiority complex
34.9 TAZAYYALA To leave one's place and turn aside for separation
34.10 TAJAFA To separate from something owing to impatience.
