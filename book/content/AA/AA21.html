21 ISHARA KARNA [To Point]

Synonyms: AASHARA(SH-WA-RA/SH-YA-RA),RAMZ,TAGHAMAZA,3ARRADA

21.1 AASHARA( اَشَارَ):To make some one understand something with closed mouth using hand gestures. In the Quran
Surat Maryam (Mary) - سورة مريم
19:29
فَأَشَارَتْ إِلَيْهِ ۖ قَالُوا كَيْفَ نُكَلِّمُ مَن كَانَ فِي الْمَهْدِ صَبِيًّا
So she pointed to him. They said, "How can we speak to one who is in the cradle a child?"

21.2 RAMAZA( رَمَزَ): to point with lips or low whistling voice. any speech that is like AASHARA is RAMZA. in the Holy Quran

Surat 'Āli `Imrān (Family of Imran) - سورة آل عمران
3:41
قَالَ رَبِّ اجْعَل لِّي آيَةً ۖ قَالَ آيَتُكَ أَلَّا تُكَلِّمَ النَّاسَ ثَلَاثَةَ أَيَّامٍ إِلَّا رَمْزًا ۗ وَاذْكُر رَّبَّكَ كَثِيرًا وَسَبِّحْ بِالْعَشِيِّ وَالْإِبْكَارِ
He said, "My Lord, make for me a sign." He Said, "Your sign is that you will not [be able to] speak to the people for three days except by gesture. And remember your Lord much and exalt [Him with praise] in the evening and the morning."


21.3 TAGHAMAZ(تَغََامز): GH-MA-ZA(غمز) meaning to communicate using eye brow and eye lash. and as per Ibn ul Faris to poke an animal so that it starts walking. and TAGHAMAZ meaning to make fun of someone using gestures of eye brow. ALLAH said

Surat Al-Muţaffifīn (The Defrauding) - سورة المطففين
83:30
وَإِذَا مَرُّوا بِهِمْ يَتَغَامَزُونَ
And when they passed by them, they would exchange derisive glances.

21.4 3ARRAD( عَرَّض):word 3ARADA(عَرَضَ) meaning to present. and 3ARRADA meaning to talk in signs and gestures and as per Imam Raghib T3RYD تعريض meaning to talk multifacet discourse (hinting )which can be applied to truth and false ,and apparent and abstract . ALLAH said

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة
2:235
وَلَا جُنَاحَ عَلَيْكُمْ فِيمَا عَرَّضْتُم بِهِ مِنْ خِطْبَةِ النِّسَاءِ أَوْ أَكْنَنتُمْ فِي أَنفُسِكُمْ ۚ عَلِمَ اللَّهُ أَنَّكُمْ سَتَذْكُرُونَهُنَّ وَلَٰكِن لَّا تُوَاعِدُوهُنَّ سِرًّا إِلَّا أَن تَقُولُوا قَوْلًا مَّعْرُوفًا ۚ وَلَا تَعْزِمُوا عُقْدَةَ النِّكَاحِ حَتَّىٰ يَبْلُغَ الْكِتَابُ أَجَلَهُ ۚ وَاعْلَمُوا أَنَّ اللَّهَ يَعْلَمُ مَا فِي أَنفُسِكُمْ فَاحْذَرُوهُ ۚ وَاعْلَمُوا أَنَّ اللَّهَ غَفُورٌ حَلِيمٌ
There is no blame upon you for that to which you [indirectly] allude concerning a proposal to women or for what you conceal within yourselves. Allah knows that you will have them in mind. But do not promise them secretly except for saying a proper saying. And do not determine to undertake a marriage contract until the decreed period reaches its end. And know that Allah knows what is within yourselves, so beware of Him. And know that Allah is Forgiving and Forbearing.


Summary:
hand gestures are AASHARA, lips gestures RAMZ, eyes and eye lashes and eye brow GHAMAZ, and 3ARRADA means to talk in hints and clues
