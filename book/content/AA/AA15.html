15 IKHTIAR (RAKHNA) [(To Have)Authority ]

Synonyms: KHAYARAH,MALK,WALAYAH,AMKANA

15.1 KHAYARAH(خِيَرَة): KHAYR(خير) meaning good, and best and KHAYARAH and IKHTAAR(اختار) meaning to choose or employ or like some good thing from two or more than two things. ALLAH said

Surat Al-'Aĥzāb (The Combined Forces) - سورة الأحزاب
33:36
وَمَا كَانَ لِمُؤْمِنٍ وَلَا مُؤْمِنَةٍ إِذَا قَضَى اللَّهُ وَرَسُولُهُ أَمْرًا أَن يَكُونَ لَهُمُ الْخِيَرَةُ مِنْ أَمْرِهِمْ ۗ وَمَن يَعْصِ اللَّهَ وَرَسُولَهُ فَقَدْ ضَلَّ ضَلَالًا مُّبِينًا
It is not for a believing man or a believing woman, when Allah and His Messenger have decided a matter, that they should [thereafter] have any choice about their affair. And whoever disobeys Allah and His Messenger has certainly strayed into clear error.


15.2 MALK(مَلْك): MILK(مِلك) is all that is in someone's hold and no other have any hold or authority over it .MALK and MILK both are similar in meaning relative to that in MALK authority in such a thing which is one's ownership and hold. and MALIK مَلِك is a king as public is in his hold and control and he is their manager. In the Quran.

Surat Ţāhā (Ta-Ha) - سورة طه
20:87
قَالُوا مَا أَخْلَفْنَا مَوْعِدَكَ بِمَلْكِنَا وَلَٰكِنَّا حُمِّلْنَا أَوْزَارًا مِّن زِينَةِ الْقَوْمِ فَقَذَفْنَاهَا فَكَذَٰلِكَ أَلْقَى السَّامِرِيُّ
They said, "We did not break our promise to you by our will, but we were made to carry burdens from the ornaments of the people [of Pharaoh], so we threw them [into the fire], and thus did the Samiri throw."

meaning samiri overpowered us by taking jewellery from us with deception and made a calf out of it.such that we lost authority and control over our own will and wish.

15.3 WALAYAH(وَلَاية): If WAW is Maftooh . and if WAW is maksoor that is WILAYAH(وِلايَة) then it means country ,kingdom, or kingship. and WALAYAH meaning to be trustee of some thing
and ALWALA-(الولاء) meaning that inheritance which is obtained of one's free slave. and WALAYAH is used in the Quran in the meaning of Inheritance. When First of the immigrants and Ansar establsihed brotherhood then they were also inheritors to each other but later on these commandments were revoked. and only real inheritors were delcared actual inheritors. (See Surah ANFAAL 72-75). Also Quran used MAWLA مولي (plural MAWALY موالي) in meaning of inheritor. ALLAH said

Surat An-Nisā' (The Women) - سورة النساء
4:33
وَلِكُلٍّ جَعَلْنَا مَوَالِيَ مِمَّا تَرَكَ الْوَالِدَانِ وَالْأَقْرَبُونَ ۚ وَالَّذِينَ عَقَدَتْ أَيْمَانُكُمْ فَآتُوهُمْ نَصِيبَهُمْ ۚ إِنَّ اللَّهَ كَانَ عَلَىٰ كُلِّ شَيْءٍ شَهِيدًا
And for all, We have made heirs to what is left by parents and relatives. And to those whom your oaths have bound [to you] - give them their share. Indeed Allah is ever, over all things, a Witness.

so WALAYAH is more wide in meaning than MALK. ALLAH said

Surat Al-Kahf (The Cave) - سورة الكهف
18:44
هُنَالِكَ الْوَلَايَةُ لِلَّهِ الْحَقِّ ۚ هُوَ خَيْرٌ ثَوَابًا وَخَيْرٌ عُقْبًا
There the authority is [completely] for Allah , the Truth. He is best in reward and best in outcome.


15.4 AMKANA(اَمْكَنَ): MAKAN(مكان) meaning place,situation,level and AMKANA and MAKKANA both means to give someone authority over some place. To give authority,and power. and AMKAN ALAMR(اَمْكَنَ الامر) meaning to make some work possible and easy. so AMKANA includes the meaning of authority along with place or position. ALLAH said

Surat Al-'Anfāl (The Spoils of War) - سورة الأنفال
8:71
وَإِن يُرِيدُوا خِيَانَتَكَ فَقَدْ خَانُوا اللَّهَ مِن قَبْلُ فَأَمْكَنَ مِنْهُمْ ۗ وَاللَّهُ عَلِيمٌ حَكِيمٌ
But if they intend to betray you - then they have already betrayed Allah before, and He empowered [you] over them. And Allah is Knowing and Wise.

Summary:

15.1 KHAYARAH authority to select from two or more than two things
15.2 MALK authority to control something (at one's disposal)
15.3 WALAYAH to have all the authority of something in hold of some one
15.4 AMKANA when authority includes the concept of place or position
