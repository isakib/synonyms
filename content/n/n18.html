18 NI’MAT

[Blessing]

Synonyms: NI’MAH(نعمة), NA’MAH (نعمة), AALAA(ألاء)( ألي)

18.1 ni’mah(نعمة): meaning graciousness, bestowal, kindliness, favor; such a favor which you bestow on someone (pl: ni’am(نعم) and ana’m(أنعم)). Ni’mah(نعمة) is unisex and it is used for all kinds of blessings whether small or big, less or more. Allah states that:    

________________

Surat Al-E-Imran (Family of Imran) - سُوۡرَةُ آل عِمرَان

3:171

يَسۡتَبۡشِرُونَ بِنِعۡمَةٍ۬ مِّنَ ٱللَّهِ وَفَضۡلٍ۬

They rejoice because of favour from Allah and kindness, and that Allah wasteth not the wage of the believers.

________________

18.2 na’mah (نعمة): meaning all those things which are a basis for rectification and betterment in economy. Goods of luxury and its accessories. Allah states that:

________________

Surat Ad-Dukhan (The Smoke) - سُوۡرَةُ الدّخان

44:27

وَنَعۡمَةٍ۬ كَانُواْ فِيہَا فَـٰكِهِينَ

And pleasant things wherein they took delight!

________________

18.3 aalaa(ألاء): (plural of ila(ألي)) meaning blessing. Ra’la yaaloo(رالى يالو) meaning to lessen, to leave margin, to make mistake as is stated:

________________

Surat Al-E-Imran (Family of Imran) - سُوۡرَةُ آل عِمرَان

3:118

لَا يَأۡلُونَكُمۡ خَبَالاً۬

O ye who believe! Take not for intimates others than your own folk, who would spare no pains to ruin you; they love to hamper you. Hatred is revealed by (the utterance of) their mouths, but that which their breasts hide is greater. We have made plain for you the revelations if ye will understand.

________________

And ala(ألاء) means all those blessings which cater for the needs of people and come continuously and he does not feel the lack of something to spend his life and usually it is used in the plural sense because there is not just one such blessing. Hence aalaai(ألاءي) is used. Allah states that:  

________________

Surat Ar-Rahman (The Merciful) - سُوۡرَةُ الرَّحمٰن

55:10-13

فَبِأَىِّ ءَالَآءِ رَبِّكُمَا تُكَذِّبَانِ وَٱلۡحَبُّ ذُو ٱلۡعَصۡفِ وَٱلرَّيۡحَانُ فِيہَا فَـٰكِهَةٌ۬ وَٱلنَّخۡلُ ذَاتُ ٱلۡأَكۡمَامِ وَٱلۡأَرۡضَ وَضَعَهَا لِلۡأَنَامِ

And the earth hath He appointed for (His) creatures, Wherein are fruit and sheathed palm-trees, Husked grain and scented herb. Which is it, of the favours of your Lord, that ye deny?

________________

Summary:

1. NAI’MAH(نعمة): bestowal, favor that is done on someone.

2.NA’MAH(نعمة): goods and accessories of luxury

3. AALA(ألاء): basic needs of life.
