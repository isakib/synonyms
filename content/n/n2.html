2 NA-PAAK [Profane]

Synonyms: RIJS, KHABEETH, NAJAS

2.1 RIJS (رِجْس): Refers to filth or grime (see GANDAGI [filth]) and also to profanity. And it refers to filth or those things that are rendered unlawful or impure by Shariah regardless of bacterial nature or by meaning. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al Tawbah (The repentance): سورۃ التوبھ 9:95

فَأَعْرِضُوا عَنْهُمْ ۖ إِنَّهُمْ رِجْسٌ ۖ وَمَأْوَاهُمْ جَهَنَّمُ

So turn away from them. Surely, they are Rijsun [i.e. Najasun (impure) because of their evil deeds], and Hell is their dwelling place,

2.2 KHABEETH (خبیث): Means impure or filthy (Opposite TAYYAB (طیّب)). This word is used both materially and figuratively. Materially it refers to a waste and value less thing. KHABEES-AL-HADEED (خبیث الحدید) means the iron rust, or grime of iron. This word also refers to rotting, smelly and filthy things. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Baqarah (The cow): سورۃ البَقَرَۃ  2:267

وَلَا تَيَمَّمُوا الْخَبِيثَ مِنْهُ تُنفِقُونَ وَلَسْتُم بِآخِذِيهِ إِلَّا أَن تُغْمِضُوا فِيهِ

 and do not even aim at getting anything which is bad, in order that out of it ye may give away something, when ye yourselves would not receive it except with closed eyes

When used figuratively, it refers to a person indulged in obscenity or has a mischievous nature. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Noor (The light): سورۃ النور 24:26

الْخَبِيثَاتُ لِلْخَبِيثِينَ وَالْخَبِيثُونَ لِلْخَبِيثَاتِ

Women impure are for men impure, and men impure for women impure

2.3 NAJAS (نَجَسٌ): This word is also used for exoteric and esoteric sort of filthiness. DAAUN-NAJISUN-WA-NAAJISUN (دَاءٌ نَجَسٌ وَّ نَاجِسٌ) means an incurable disease. NAJJASAS-SABI (نَجَّسَ الصَّبِی) means in order to save a child from evil put a charm around his neck. NUJUS (نُجُس) are those who make these charms and spells of protection (dictionary al Manjad). This word is never used in material meaning in Quran (Opposite TAHIR (طاھر), TUHOOR (طھور)). When used figuratively then it refers to the filth of polytheism and infidelity that turns into an incurable disease. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al Tawbah (The repentance): سورۃ التوبھ 9:28

يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا إِنَّمَا الْمُشْرِكُونَ نَجَسٌ فَلَا يَقْرَبُوا الْمَسْجِدَ الْحَرَامَ بَعْدَ عَامِهِمْ هَٰذَا

O you who believe (in Allah's Oneness and in His Messenger (Muhammad SAW)! Verily, the Mushrikun (polytheists, pagans, idolaters, disbelievers in the Oneness of Allah, and in the Message of Muhammad SAW) are Najasun (impure). So let them not come near Al-Masjid-al-Haram (at Makkah) after this year,

Summary:

2.1 RIJS: Things rendered impure by Sharia Laws.

2.2 KHABEETH: Waste and filthy things. People who are indulged in obscenity.

2.3 NAJAS: People who are indulged in the ideas of infidelity and polytheism to the level of being incurable. Things that are completely rendered filthy by Shariah.
