Near.( کرنا-ہونا نزدیک)

The words which are used for ‘Near’ in the glorious Quran are Ka’ada(Kaod) (دکا,کود), Qaruba(قرب), Da’ani(نید), Azifa(فزا), Zalafa(زلف), qa’ab(

قاب),Iktarn(اقترن).

1-Ka’ada-(دکا)Which means to be closer to do some job or If anyone willing to work but didn’t do it.

(ق-م): It is a proved function and wants another action with it. It is only used for past tense. The Quran says:

The lightning almost snatches away their sight.-يَكَادُ الْبَرْقُ يَخْطَفُ أَبْصَارَهُمْ ۖ[2:20].

2- (قرب)Qaruba- Near: Genarally this word is used i.e; to be near ‘distance’(فاصل), ‘period’(مدّت), ‘origin’(نسب) and ‘attaches’(منزلت). For example:

It(calamity) will descend near their home. -دَارِهِمْ مِّن قَرِيبًا تَحُلُّ أَوْ [13:31].-

For ‘distance’:

And do not approach them until they are pure(from menstruation). - ۖيَطْهُرْنَ حَتَّىٰ تَقْرَبُوهُنَّ وَلَا [2:222].

For ‘origin’:

We will not exchange our oath for a price, even if he should be a near relative.-ۙقُرْبَىٰ ذَا كَانَ وَلَوْثَمَنًا بِهِ نَشْتَرِي لَا[5:106].

For ‘attaches’:

And to forego it is nearer to righteousness. - ۚلِلتَّقْوَىٰأَقْرَبُ تَعْفُوا وَأَن [2:237].

Word used for ‘attaches’ and ‘time’ is ‘Iqtaraba’(اقترب). For example:

But prostrate and draw near [to Allah ] -ب رِوَاقْتَوَاسْجُدْ [96:19].

The Hour has come near, and the moon has split [in two]. -الْقَمَرُ وَانشَقَّ السَّاعَةُ اقْتَرَبَتِ [54:1].
