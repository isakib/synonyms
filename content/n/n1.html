1 NA UMEED HONA [become hopeless, despair]

Synonyms: YAA’SA, ISTAA’ASA, QANATA, ABLASA

1.1 YAA’SA (یَأَسَ): Means withdrawing of hope and trust, to lose hope regarding some matter. To despair. This can be before a hope or after it.  Allah says in the Quran

Surah Yusuf (Joseph): سورۃ یُوسُف 12:87

إِنَّهُ لَا يَيْأَسُ مِن رَّوْحِ اللَّهِ إِلَّا الْقَوْمُ الْكَافِرُونَ

 Indeed, no one despairs of relief from Allah except the disbelieving people."

 And ISTAA’ASA (اِسْتَیْئَسَ) means gradually lose expectations in a matter. To give up after a lot of experimentation. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Yusuf (Joseph): سورۃ یُوسُف 12:110

حَتَّىٰ إِذَا اسْتَيْأَسَ الرُّسُلُ وَظَنُّوا أَنَّهُمْ قَدْ كُذِبُوا جَاءَهُمْ نَصْرُنَا

[They continued] until, when the messengers despaired and were certain that they had been denied, there came to them Our victory,

1.2 QANATA (قَنَطَ): Means to lose hope in goodness, to lose hope. QANOOT (قنوط) means extreme hopelessness. QANOOT (قنوط) in terms of quality or QANOOTI (قنوطی) is a person who only sees the dark sides of a matter or looks only at the risks and problems of a matter and is habitual of these things and neglects the positive aspects of the matter. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Fussilat (Explained in Detail): سورۃ فُصّلَت 41:49

لَّا يَسْأَمُ الْإِنسَانُ مِن دُعَاءِ الْخَيْرِ وَإِن مَّسَّهُ الشَّرُّ فَيَئُوسٌ قَنُوطٌ

 The human being never tires of praying for good things; but when adversity afflicts him, he despairs and loses hope.

1.3 ABLASA (اَبْلَسَ): Means to be in grief due to extreme hopelessness. To be in ignorance. To be in grief and broken hearted (dictionary al Manjad). When lose of hope causes grief then this word is used. The Satan is also called IBLEESS (ابلیس) because he has lost hope in Allah’s compassion. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Ar-Rom (The Romans) - سورة الروم 30:49

وَإِن كَانُوا مِن قَبْلِ أَن يُنَزَّلَ عَلَيْهِم مِّن قَبْلِهِ لَمُبْلِسِينَ

 Although they were before this—before it was sent down upon them—in despair.

Summary:

1.1 YAA’SA: To despair, to lose hope ISTAA’ASA: Means gradually lose hope.

1.2 QANATA: To lose hope in goodness, extreme hopelessness.

1.3 ABLASA: To lose hope and become gloomy.
