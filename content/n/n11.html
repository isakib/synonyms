11 NIJAAT PANA [salvation, to be free]

Synonyms: NAJAA, FAAZA

Page 843

11.1 NAJAA (نَجٰی): Means to be free from a trouble, to get freedom of any sort. A very common word. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Yusuf (Joseph): سورۃ یُوسُف 12:45

وَقَالَ الَّذِي نَجَا مِنْهُمَا وَادَّكَرَ بَعْدَ أُمَّةٍ

But the one who was freed and remembered after a time

11.2 FAAZA (فاز): Means to be free from trouble and reach a beloved thing. To get goodluck with saftey. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Ale- Imran (The family of Imran): سورۃ آل عِمْرن 3:185

ۖ فَمَن زُحْزِحَ عَنِ النَّارِ وَأُدْخِلَ الْجَنَّةَ فَقَدْ فَازَ

 Whoso is removed from the Fire and is made to enter paradise, he indeed is triumphant.

FAAZA (فاز) is from dictionary of antonyms. FAWZ (فوز) means to get salvation and also get perished. FAWWAZ-AR-RUJL (فَوَّزَ الرَّجُل) means the man died. And MAFAZA (مفازة) means the reason for salvation or place of salvation and also the reason and place of damnation. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Ale- Imran (The family of Imran): سورۃ آل عِمْرن 3:188

فَلَا تَحْسَبَنَّهُم بِمَفَازَةٍ مِّنَ الْعَذَابِ ۖ وَلَهُمْ عَذَابٌ أَلِيمٌ

think escape the penalty. For them is a penalty Grievous indeed.

In this ayah the word MAFAZAH (مفازة) means the place or reason of damnation.

Summary:

11.1 NAJAA:  To get free from trouble.

11.2 FAAZA: To get free from trouble and reach and objective.
