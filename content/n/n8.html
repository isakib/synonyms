8 NAAK [Nose]

Synonyms: ANF, KHURTOOM

8.1 ANF (اَنْف): Means a nose, a famous body part. Nose is protruding body part so any part of something that is protruding is also called ANF (اَنْف), for instance ANF-AL-JABAL (انف الجبل) means the protruding part of mountain. In the Quran

Surah Al-Maeda (The table spread): سورۃ المَائدہ 5:45

وَالْعَيْنَ بِالْعَيْنِ وَالْأَنفَ بِالْأَنفِ

 an eye for an eye, a nose for a nose,

8.2 KHURTOOM (خُرْطُوْم): Means the snout of an elephant or a long nose which extends till feet. And IN-KHARTAMA (اِنْخَرْطَمَ) means to be arrogant, wrathful and full of pride. KHARATEEM-AL-QAUM (خراطیم القوم) means the leader of a nation and arrogant people (dictionary al Manjad). Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Qalam (The Pen):سورۃ القلم  68:15-16

إِذَا تُتْلَىٰ عَلَيْهِ آيَاتُنَا قَالَ أَسَاطِيرُ الْأَوَّلِينَ سَنَسِمُهُ عَلَى الْخُرْطُومِ  

When Our verses are recited to him, he says, "Legends of the former peoples." We will brand him upon the snout.

Summary:

8.1 ANF: Nose, a popular body part.

8.2 KHURTOOM: A long nose extending to ground. A nose of an arrogant person.
