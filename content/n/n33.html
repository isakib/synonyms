33 NAIKI-NAIK KAM [piety, Good deeds]

Synonyms: URF, MAAROOF, HASANAH, KHAIR, BIRR

33.1 URF (عُرف) and MAAROOF (مَعْرُوْف): ARAFA (عَرَفَ) (Opposite NAKARA (نَکَرََ) something being surprising) means to recognize something. MAAROOF (معروف) and URF (عرف) are those things that are considered good in a society. And is a good custom of a society. Ways of good people. National custom that is liked. For instance to sit politely and respectfully in front of the elders and address them politely. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Araf (The heights): سورۃ الأعرَاف 7:199

خُذِ الْعَفْوَ وَأْمُرْ بِالْعُرْفِ وَأَعْرِضْ عَنِ الْجَاهِلِينَ

In another place in Holy Quran

Surah Ale- Imran (The family of Imran): سورۃ آل عِمْرن 3:104

وَلْتَكُن مِّنكُمْ أُمَّةٌ يَدْعُونَ إِلَى الْخَيْرِ وَيَأْمُرُونَ بِالْمَعْرُوفِ وَيَنْهَوْنَ عَنِ الْمُنكَرِ

 And let there be among you a community calling to virtue, and advocating righteousness, and deterring from evil.

33.3 HASANAH (حَسَنَةٌ): (Plural HASNAAT (حَسَنَات)) Any pleasing and liked thing that is in accordance with the mind and Shariah (Opposite SAYYIAH (سَیِّئَةً) (Plural SAYYIAT (سیّئات))).  Allah says in the Quran

Surah Hud (Hud): سورۃ ھود 11:114

إِنَّ الْحَسَنَاتِ يُذْهِبْنَ السَّيِّئَاتِ

The good deeds take away the bad deeds.

33.4 KHAIR (خَیْر): (Plural KHAIRAAT (خیرات)) (Opposite SHARR (شرّ)) Means a virtue at its best. A thing from which everyone can benefit from. Greater virtues. Good deeds done at a larger scale. KHAIR (خَیْر) also refers to those things with which everyone is pleased. For instance arranging drinking water for travelers. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Ale- Imran (The family of Imran): سورۃ آل عِمْرن 3:114

وَيَأْمُرُونَ بِالْمَعْرُوفِ وَيَنْهَوْنَ عَنِ الْمُنكَرِ وَيُسَارِعُونَ فِي الْخَيْرَاتِ

 and advocate righteousness and forbid evil, and are quick to do good deeds.

33.5 BIRR (بِرّ): Always having a temperament to do piety and when given a chance do it (Opposite ITHM (اثم)). For Detail see NAIK BAKHT [fortunate] above. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Maeda (The table spread): سورۃ المَائدہ 5:2

وَتَعَاوَنُوا عَلَى الْبِرِّ وَالتَّقْوَىٰ ۖ وَلَا تَعَاوَنُوا عَلَى الْإِثْمِ وَالْعُدْوَانِ

 And cooperate in righteousness and piety, but do not cooperate in sin and aggression.

Summary:

33.1 URF and MAAROOF: Good customs of society. Good deeds.

33.2 HASANAH: An action in accordance  with mind and Shariah rules.

33.3 KHAIR: Any work that is liked by all. Everyone benefits from it.

33.4 BIRR: Always having a temperament for a virtue.
