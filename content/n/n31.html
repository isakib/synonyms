31. NEECHAY KERNA – RAKHNA, PAST KERNA [to lower]

Synonyms: KHAFADA, GHADDA, QASARA, KHASHAA’

31.1 KHAFADA (خَفَضَ): Means to lower, to kneel (Opposite RAFAA’ (رفع)). KHAFID (خافِض) and RAFIAA’ (رافِع) both are the names of Allah. KHAFID (خافِض) means the lowerer and RAFIAA (رافِع) means the exalter. KHAFADA-AS-SAUT (خَفَضَ الصَّوت) means he lowered the voice.  KHAFAD-AL-JANAH (خَفَضَ الجناج) means to lower the arm. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Isra (The Night Journey): سُوۡرَةُ الإسرَاء 17:24

وَاخْفِضْ لَهُمَا جَنَاحَ الذُّلِّ مِنَ الرَّحْمَةِ

And lower to them the wing of humility out of mercy

 31.2 GHADDA (غَضَّ): Means to lower gaze or voice, GHADDA-SAUTA (غَضَ الصَوْتُہُ) means he lowered the voice. GHADDA-BASARA (غَضَّ بَصَرُہُ) means he lowered his gaze or removed his gaze from an illicit thing. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Noor (The light): سورۃ النور 24:31

وَقُل لِّلْمُؤْمِنَاتِ يَغْضُضْنَ مِنْ أَبْصَارِهِنَّ

And tell the believing women to lower their gaze (from looking at forbidden things),

In another place in Quran

Surah Luqman (Luqman): سُوۡرَةُ لقمَان 31:19

وَاقْصِدْ فِي مَشْيِكَ وَاغْضُضْ مِن صَوْتِكَ

And be moderate in your pace and lower your voice

31.3 QASARA (قَصَر): To be unable to reach the length and end of something, to lessen, to shorten, to perform lesser than the required task. Like SALATUL-QASAR (الصَلٰوۃُ القَصْر), QASUR-RUT-TARF (قَصَرُ الطَّرْف) means to restrain the gaze and not use it to the total extent of sight. In this meaning it is specific for sight. In the Quran

Surah As-Saaffat (The Aligners): سُوۡرَةُ الصَّافات37:48

وَعِندَهُمْ قَاصِرَاتُ الطَّرْفِ عِينٌ

And with them will be women limiting [their] glances, with large, [beautiful] eyes,

31.4  KHASHAA’ (خَشَعَ): KHASHAA’ (خَشَعَ) is a fear which affects emotions. Such effects can also be visible in eyes, voice and expressions. Thus for lowering them in fear this word is used. For lowering voice and eyes the word GHADDA (غَضَّ) is also used but the difference is that when the reason is due to fear then KHASHAA’ (خَشَعَ). Allah says in the Quran

For sight:

Surah Al-Maarij (The Ascending Stairs): سُوۡرَةُ المعَارج 70:44

خَاشِعَةً أَبْصَارُهُمْ تَرْهَقُهُمْ ذِلَّةٌ

Their eyes cast down; overwhelmed by humiliation.

For voice:

Surah Ta-Ha (Ta-ha): سورۃ طٰھٰ  20:108

وَخَشَعَتِ الْأَصْوَاتُ لِلرَّحْمَٰنِ

Voices will be hushed before the Merciful,

For face:

Surah Al-Ghashiya (The overwhelming): سُوۡرَةُ الغَاشِیَة 88:2

وُجُوهٌ يَوْمَئِذٍ خَاشِعَةٌ

(Some) faces on that day shall be downcast,

Summary:

31.1 KHAFADA: To get pressed or press. To lower gaze.

31.2 GHADDA: Lowering of sight and voice.

31.3 QASARA: To limit ones’ gaze. To not use full sight.

31.4 KHASHAA’:  To lower eyes, voice or face due to fear.
