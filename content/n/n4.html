4 NAADAN [foolish, unintelligent]

Synonyms: JAHIL, SUFAHAA’

Page 838

4.1JAHIL (جَاھِل): (Opposite AALIM (عالم)) lacking knowledge, unknown. JAHALA (جَاھِل) means a mind of a person being empty of knowledge regardless of the reason. A very common word. In the Quran

Surah Al-Baqarah (The cow): سورۃ البَقَرَۃ  2:273

يَحْسَبُهُمُ الْجَاهِلُ أَغْنِيَاءَ مِنَ التَّعَفُّفِ

An ignorant [person] would think them self-sufficient because of their restraint,

4.2 SUFAHAA’ (سُفَھَاء): (Singular SAFEEHA (سَفِیْھَه)) (Opposite of SAFAH (سفھ) is HILM (حِلم)) means 1. Half witted and 2. A person with low morals. And SAFEEHA (سَفِیْھَه) refers to an unintelligent person who cannot attain any skill or knowledge due to his dumbness and also has low moral values. And argues without knowing or understanding anything. Foolish. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Baqarah (The cow): سورۃ البَقَرَۃ  2:142

سَيَقُولُ السُّفَهَاءُ مِنَ النَّاسِ مَا وَلَّاهُمْ عَن قِبْلَتِهِمُ الَّتِي كَانُوا عَلَيْهَا

The foolish among the people will say, "What has turned them away from their qiblah, which they used to face
