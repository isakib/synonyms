30 NEECHAY [Below, lower]

Synonyms: THAT, ASFAL

30.1 THAT (تَحْت): THAT (تَحْت) is a noun of place and its opposite is FAUQ (فوق) which means up. Both used materially and figuratively. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Maeda (The table spread): سورۃ المَائدہ 5:66

وَلَوْ أَنَّهُمْ أَقَامُوا التَّوْرَاةَ وَالْإِنجِيلَ وَمَا أُنزِلَ إِلَيْهِم مِّن رَّبِّهِمْ لَأَكَلُوا مِن فَوْقِهِمْ وَمِن تَحْتِ أَرْجُلِهِم

And if only they upheld [the law of] the Torah, the Gospel, and what has been revealed to them from their Lord, they would have consumed [provision] from above them and from beneath their feet.

30.2 ASFAL (اَسْفَل): Means the lower one (Opposite A’LAA (اعلٰی)). This word is used in two ways 1. The upper part of a thing is called A’LAA (اعلٰی) and its lower part is called ASFAL (اَسْفَل). For instance ASFALAHU-AGHLAZU-MIN-AA’LAHU (اَسْفَلہُ اَغْلَظُ مِنْ اَعْلَاُہ) means the lower part is harder than the upper part.

For highness in status and honor the word A’LAA (اَعْلٰی) is used and for dishonor and inferiority the word ASFALA (اَسْفَل)  (Feminine SUFLA (سُفُلٰی)). Prophet Muhammad (S.A.A.W) said “الْيَدُ الْعُلْيَا خَيْرٌ مِنَ الْيَدِ السُّفْلَى” means ‘The upper hand is better than the lower hand’. In this Hadeeth the word ULIA (عُلْيَا) and SUFLA (سُفْلَى) are used figuratively which means the one who gives charity and the ones who receive it. In the Quran

Surah Al Tawbah (The repentance): سورۃ التوبھ 9:40

وَجَعَلَ كَلِمَةَ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا السُّفْلَىٰ ۗ وَكَلِمَةُ اللَّهِ هِيَ الْعُلْيَا

and made the word of those who disbelieved the lowest, while the Word of God is the Highest.

SAFIL (سَافِل) means to be inferior in honor and status. ASSIFLAH (اَسِفْلَۃ) means scurvy people. Allah says in Quran

Surah At-Teen (The Fig) - سورة التين 95:4-5

لَقَدْ خَلَقْنَا الْإِنسَانَ فِي أَحْسَنِ تَقْوِيمٍ  ثُمَّ رَدَدْنَاهُ أَسْفَلَ سَافِلِينَ

We created man in the best design. Then reduced him to the lowest of the low.

Summary:

30.1 THAT: Below. Noun of place.

30.2 ASFAL: The lower part of something. To be low in honor. Lower; Inferior.
