20 NUKSAN, NUKSAN HONA

[Damage, loss]

Synonyms: DARRA(ضر), DAIR(ضير), KHASARA(خسارا), KASAAD(كساد), BAKHS(بخس), BAWAAR(بوار), MAGHRAM(مغرم)

20.1 darra(ضر): meaning difficulty, loss (ant: nafa(نفع)). It is a common word. Allah states that:

________________

Surat Al-Araf (The Heights) - سُوۡرَةُ الاٴعرَاف

7:188

قُل لَّآ أَمۡلِكُ لِنَفۡسِى نَفۡعً۬ا وَلَا ضَرًّا إِلَّا مَا شَآءَ ٱللَّهُ‌ۚ

Say: For myself I have no power to benefit, nor power to hurt, save that which Allah willeth. Had I knowledge of the Unseen, I should have abundance of wealth, and adversity would not touch me. I am but a warner, and a bearer of good tidings unto folk who believe.

________________

And dair(ضير) also means damage, disadvantage, loss. According to Imam Raghib, daarah(ضاره) and darra(ضره) have the same meaning. It is stated in the Quran:

________________

Surat Al-Shuara (The Poets) - سُوۡرَةُ الشُّعَرَاء

26:50

قَالُواْ لَا ضَيۡرَ‌ۖ إِنَّآ إِلَىٰ رَبِّنَا مُنقَلِبُونَ

They said: It is no hurt, for lo! unto our Lord we shall return.

________________

20.2 khasara (خسارا): meaning the decrease in the selling of goods, to have loss in something instead of profit, failure. Allah states that:

________________

Surat Fatir (The Angels)  - سُوۡرَةُ فَاطِر

35: 39

وَلَا يَزِيدُ ٱلۡكَـٰفِرِينَ كُفۡرُهُمۡ إِلَّا خَسَارً۬ا

He it is Who hath made you regents in the earth; so he who disbelieveth, his disbelief be on his own head. Their disbelief increaseth for the disbelievers, in their Lord's sight, naught save abhorrence. Their disbelief increaseth for the disbelievers naught save loss.

________________

20.3 kasaad(كساد): meaning not wanting to buy anything, to bear loss, trade merchandise not being sold.

كساد بازارى is famous which means bearish behavior in the market. It is stated in the Quran:

________________

Surat Al-Tawba (Repentence) - سُوۡرَةُ التّوبَة

9:24

وَتِجَـٰرَةٌ۬ تَخۡشَوۡنَ كَسَادَهَا

Say: If your fathers, and your sons, and your brethren, and your wives, and your tribe, and the wealth ye have acquired, and merchandise for which ye fear that there will be no sale, and dwellings ye desire are dearer to you than Allah and His messenger and striving in His way: then wait till Allah bringeth His command to pass. Allah guideth not wrongdoing folk.

________________

20.4 bakhs(بخس): meaning worthless and useless thing. Yabkhas(يبخس) means to decrease something through cruelty. Allah states that:  

________________

Surat Al-Jinn (The Jinn) - سُوۡرَةُ الجنّ

72:13

فَمَن يُؤۡمِنۢ بِرَبِّهِۦ فَلَا يَخَافُ بَخۡسً۬ا وَلَا رَهَقً۬ا

And when we heard the guidance, we believed therein, and whoso believeth in his Lord, he feareth neither loss nor oppression.

________________

20.5 bawaar(بوار): boor(بور) and bawaar(بوار) meaning extreme devaluation of something and reaching near the limit of death, to keep bearing loss gradually finally resulting in the end of trade merchandise and destruction. Allah states that:

________________

Surat Fatir (The Angels) - سُوۡرَةُ فَاطِر

35:29

وَأَنفَقُواْ مِمَّا رَزَقۡنَـٰهُمۡ سِرًّ۬ا وَعَلَانِيَةً۬ يَرۡجُونَ تِجَـٰرَةً۬ لَّن تَبُورَ

Lo! those who read the Scripture of Allah, and establish worship, and spend of that which We have bestowed on them secretly and openly, they look forward to imperishable gain

________________

20.6 maghram(مغرم): alghurm(الغرم): that type of financial loss which a person has to bear without anyone’s breach of trust or fraud, ransom, and gharim (غرم) means to pay off one’s loan.  Ghareem(غريم) means the one who gives loan as well as the one who takes loan. Allah states that:  

________________

Surat Al-Tawba (Repentance) - سُوۡرَةُ التّوبَة

9:60

وَفِى ٱلرِّقَابِ وَٱلۡغَـٰرِمِينَ وَفِى سَبِيلِ ٱللَّهِ وَٱبۡنِ ٱلسَّبِيلِ‌ۖ

The alms are only for the poor and the needy, and those who collect them, and those whose hearts are to be reconciled, and to free the captives and the debtors, and for the cause of Allah, and (for) the wayfarer; a duty imposed by Allah. Allah is Knower, Wise.

________________

And maghram(مغرم) and ghurm(غرم) both have the same meaning. Mughram(مغرم) is that person who has to pay ransom. Allah states that:

________________

Surat Hud - سُوۡرَةُ هُود

56:65-66

إِنَّا لَمُغۡرَمُونَ لَوۡ نَشَآءُ لَجَعَلۡنَـٰهُ حُطَـٰمً۬ا فَظَلۡتُمۡ تَفَكَّهُونَ

If We willed, We verily could make it chaff, then would ye cease not to exclaim: Lo! we are laden with debt!

________________

Summary:

1. DARRA(ضر): loss, it is common.

2. KHASARA(خسارا): loss in trade, decrease in goods of merchandise.

3. KASAAD(كساد): decrease or end in demand of sales goods.

4. BAKHS(بخس): to get worthless or useless thing instead of good quality thing.

5. BAWAAR(بوار): to get ruined after continuous losses.  

6. MAGHRAM(مغرم): ransom, the amount which one has to pay without any reason.
