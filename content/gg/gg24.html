24 GHUSNA

[To enter]

Synonyms: WALAJA(ولج), JASA(جاس)(JAUS جوس)

24.1 walaja(ولج): to enter a narrow place, go inside. It is stated in the Quran:  

________________

Surat Al-Araf (The Heights) - سُوۡرَةُ الاٴعرَاف

7:40

وَلَا يَدۡخُلُونَ ٱلۡجَنَّةَ حَتَّىٰ يَلِجَ ٱلۡجَمَلُ فِى سَمِّ ٱلۡخِيَاطِ‌ۚ

Lo! they who deny Our revelations and scorn them, for them the gates of heaven will nor be opened not will they enter the Garden until the camel goeth through the needle's eye. Thus do We requite the guilty.

________________

24.2 jasa (جاس): meaning to reach the middle of something and meaning to reach the limits in desire of something. And jasa fi-lbait(جاس في البيت) meaning that he entered the house for burglary and crime. And jawwas(جواس) meaning to create chaos in a nation. It is stated in the Quran:  

________________

Surat Al-Isra (Children of Israel) - سُوۡرَةُ بنیٓ اسرآئیل / الإسرَاء

17:5

بَعَثۡنَا عَلَيۡڪُمۡ عِبَادً۬ا لَّنَآ أُوْلِى بَأۡسٍ۬ شَدِيدٍ۬ فَجَاسُواْ خِلَـٰلَ ٱلدِّيَارِ‌ۚ

So when the time for the first of the two came, We roused against you slaves of Ours of great might who ravaged (your) country, and it was a threat performed.

________________

Summary:

1.WALAJA(ولج): used to show entering in a narrow space.

2. JASA(جاس): to enter to create chaos and destruction.
