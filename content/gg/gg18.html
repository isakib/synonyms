18 GAUD [cradle]

Synonyms: MAHD, HUJOOR

18.1 MAHD (مَھْد): Rocker or provenience or the mother’s lap for an infant. Until an infant is lactating his mother lap becomes his cradle. This word is also for brought up or the ways of brought up. (Also see ‘BICHONA’ [Bed]). Allah says in the Quran

Surah Ale- Imran (The family of Imran): سورۃ آل عِمْرن 3:46

 وَيُڪَلِّمُ ٱلنَّاسَ فِى ٱلۡمَهۡدِ وَڪَهۡلاً۬

He will speak to the people in the cradle and in maturity and will be of the righteous."

18.2 HUJOOR (حُجُور): (singular HIJR (حِجْر)) HUJR (حُجْر) means a cradle. The phrase “فُلاَنٌ فِیْ حِجْرِ فُلاَنٌ” means so and so  brought in so and so’s cradle or guardianship (dictionary al Manjad) or under the charge of so and so or he is under limitation of financial and control by so and so. In the Quran

Surah An-Nisa (The women): سورۃ النِّسا 4:23

 وَرَبَـٰٓٮِٕبُڪُمُ ٱلَّـٰتِى فِى حُجُورِڪُم

And your stepdaughters in your guardianship

Summary:

18.1 MAHD: Mother’s lap for an infant.

18.2: HUJOOR: In case of older children their cradle refers to their guardian who can put limitations to their control, or who nurture them
