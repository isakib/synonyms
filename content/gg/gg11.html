11. GALA [throat, neck]

Synonyms: HULQOOM, HANAJIR

11.1 HULQOOM (حُلْقُوْم): Means throat or neck, the place of a body of animal from where he is slaughtered. A very famous body part. HULQOOM (حُلْقُوْم) and HALAQ (حلق) means the same. In the Quran

Surah Al-Waqiah (The Inevitable): سُوۡرَةُ الواقِعَة 56:83

 فَلَوۡلَآ إِذَا بَلَغَتِ ٱلۡحُلۡقُومَ

So when it has reached the throat.

 

11.2 HANAJIR (حَنَاجِر): (Singular HANJRAH (حنجرہ)) larynx, wind pipe (dictionary al Manjad). In the Quran

Suran Al-Ahzaab (The clans): سورۃ الأحزَاب 33:10

 وَبَلَغَتِ ٱلۡقُلُوبُ ٱلۡحَنَاجِرَ وَتَظُنُّونَ بِٱللَّهِ ٱلظُّنُونَا۟

and the hearts reached the throats, and you harbored doubts about God.

 

Summary

11.1 HULQOOM : HALAQ means neck. Including the outer skin and everything.

11.2 HANAJIR: Means the internal windpipe, or larynx.
