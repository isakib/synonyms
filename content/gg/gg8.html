8 GIRWI RAKHNA [pawn, pledge]

Synonyms:  RAHANA, ABSALA

8.1 RAHANA (رَھَنَ): Means to leave something as a guarantee in return for a loan to the creditor. To pledge. RAHN (رَھْن) is the thing that is pawned. A common word. The object can be a living or a non-living thing.  RIHANUN (رِھَانٌ) is the thing that is set aside as a bet during a competition. The object left as a security for loans. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Baqarah (The cow): سورۃ البَقَرَۃ  2:283

 وَإِن كُنتُمۡ عَلَىٰ سَفَرٍ۬ وَلَمۡ تَجِدُواْ كَاتِبً۬ا فَرِهَـٰنٌ۬ مَّقۡبُوضَةٌ۬‌ۖ

This word is used materially and also semantically. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Muddaththir (The Cloaked One): سورۃ المدَّثّر 74:38

كُلُّ نَفۡسِۭ بِمَا كَسَبَتۡ رَهِينَةٌ

Every soul is hostage to what it has earned

 

8.2 ABSALA (اَبْسَلَ): To give someone to pangs of death, to pledge (dictionary al Manjad). To keep as a hostage. When the object pawned is a living thing then this word is used, otherwise RAHN (رہن) is more common. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Ana’am (The cattle): سورۃ الانعام 6:70

 وَذَڪِّرۡ بِهِۦۤ أَن تُبۡسَلَ نَفۡسُۢ بِمَا كَسَبَتۡ

 But remind with it, lest a soul becomes damned on account of what it has earned.

Summary:

8.1 RAHANA: Leave something as a guarantee in return for a loan.

8.2 ABSALA: To give oneself or any living thing for arrest or for death (hostage).
