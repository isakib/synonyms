20 GHAAT [Drinking place]

Synonyms: MASHRAB, WIRD

20.1 MASHRAB (مَشْرَبْ): A place to drink or fetch water from. Drinking place. A common word

Surah Al-Baqarah (The cow): سورۃ البَقَرَۃ 2:60

 قَدۡ عَلِمَ ڪُلُّ أُنَاسٍ۬ مَّشۡرَبَهُمۡ‌ۖ

And each tribe recognized its drinking-place.

20.2 WIRD (وِرْد): WARADA (وَرَدَ) means to reach a place to drink water (Opposite SADAR (صَدَر) means to return from a place after drinking water). And WIRD (وِرْد) is place made for drinking water, like tanks are made for animals to drink water from. A thirsty is also known as WIRD   وِرۡدً۬ا (19:89). Allah says in the Quran

Surah Hud (Hud): سورۃ ھود 11:98

 يَقۡدُمُ قَوۡمَهُ ۥ يَوۡمَ ٱلۡقِيَـٰمَةِ فَأَوۡرَدَهُمُ ٱلنَّارَ‌ۖ وَبِئۡسَ ٱلۡوِرۡدُ ٱلۡمَوۡرُودُ

He will go before his people on the Day of Resurrection and will lead them to the Fire for watering-place. Ah, hapless is the watering-place (whither they are) led.
