4- RAAH DALNA RAAIJ KERNA [Put to Way, Put Into Practice]

 

Qur’an has referred them by the words SANAH (سن ) SHARAA (شرع  ) AND IBTADAAA (ابتدع ).

 

4.1   SANAH  (سنة ): San: means if something is issued or put in practice and if it runs properly. It is used as both essential and plural such as the prophet (P.B.U.H) says MUN SANAH SANATUN (من سن سنة ) anybody who puts a way into practice. it is used for both good and bad meanings and when it is related to Allah then it is called Divine Rule or Allah’s Practice. Allah says

           Surat Fāţir (Originator) - سورة فاطر 35:43

فَهَلْ يَنْظُرُوْنَ اِلَّا سُنَّتَ الْاَوَّلِيْنَ ۚ فَلَنْ تَجِدَ لِسُنَّتِ اللّٰهِ تَبْدِيْلًا

          Then do they await except the way of the former peoples? But you will never find in the way of Allah any change.

 

4.2   SHARAA  (شرع ): means clear and set path but it is only applied on Allah’s Commands. And SHARAALILQOME (شرع للقوم) means to make law for the nation and in Shariat, it means Islamic law, Divine commands, code. SHARAA (شرع  ) are in fact those laws that are changed by Allah according to the requirement of that period. For more information, refer to TAREEQA AUR DEEN (طريقة اور دين ) . Allah says:

(Surat-Shuraa) سورة الشورى 42:13

  شَرَعَ لَكُمْ مِّنَ الدِّيْنِ مَا وَصّٰى بِهٖ نُوْحًا

 He has ordained for you of religion what He enjoined upon Noah.

 

 However according to dictionary, this word can be related to other than Allah or false gods: such as Allah says

Surat Ash-Shūraá (The Consultation) - سورة الشورى 42:21

   اَمْ لَهُمْ شُرَكٰۗؤُا شَرَعُوْا لَهُمْ مِّنَ الدِّيْنِ مَا لَمْ يَاْذَنْۢ بِهِ اللّٰهُ

Or have they other deities who have ordained for them a religion to which Allah has not consented?

4.3   IBTADAA  (ابتدع ): ILABADAA (الابداع) means invent something without following anyone. And BIDAAT (بدعت) is every that new tradition which is included in religion after considering it as of religion and there is no evidence of it in Shariat. And IBTADAAA (ابتدع ) means to include a new thing in Shariat and call it as a part of it. And prophet (P.B.U.H) said every BIDAAT (بدعت) no matter how good it may seemed is a sheer misguidance. Allah Says

Surat Al-Ĥadīd (The Iron) - سورة الحديد 57:27

  وَرَهْبَانِيَّةَۨ ابْتَدَعُوْهَا مَا كَتَبْنٰهَا عَلَيْهِمْ

We did not prescribe it for them except [that they did so] seeking the approval of Allah.

Summary:

4.1          SANNA: is used to put every good and bad thing in practice. however, according to Sharia, Sanna: means only the actions and talks of messenger (P.B.U.H) of Allah.

4.2          SHARAA: The establishment of a clear and set path for the people by Allah via messenger. It was being changed from Adam (A.S) till Hazrat Muhammad (P.B.U.H) but now, this part of religion is also non-changeable because He was the last prophet of Allah and deen was completed on him.

4.3          IBTADAA: To include a tradition or method in religion after considering it a part of Shariat and there is no evidence about it in Shariat.
