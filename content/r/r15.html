15 : LIGHT _ LUSTRE _ LIGHTEN,ILLUMINATE

for light following words are used :

انار (light) , اضاء (lighting), نار (flame , light), جلى ,تجلى (glorification) , وهج (flame, blaze , heat , glare ) اشرق (sunrise, rdiate ,shine) اسفر (brighten, uncover) ,ابصر(sight, vision, eyes)

1انار : نور in sense of light which irradiate the things ,diffusing light which help in seeing things .

نور light is used in both corporeal and incorporeal meanings

as Allah says in quran (sorah az-zummar # 22)

what is he whose heart Allah has open for islam so that he is in light from his lord.

(in this verse noor means both righteous path and enlightened heart)

there are three types of نور light :

1 light of bright irradiated things: like sun , moon, stars, lamp, which is essential to see substantial (material) things and without this light human beings cannot see the things around

2 eyesight : in absence of eyesight the light of bright irradiated things is of no use

3 light of revelation (inspiration) وحى or faith : in absence of this light man cannot get the benifits of guidence

as man cant see material things without eyes similarly teachings of Allah does'nt work on blind hearted person
