6- RUTBA- RUTBA PANA [Status-To Achieve Status]

Qur’an has referred them by the words DARAJA  (درجة ) ZULFA  (  زلفى ) QARABA  (قرب ) AND MAKANA (  مکن ).

 

6.1   DARAJA  (درجة ) : means edge, superiority, status, rank. The antonym of DARAJ (درج) is DARAK (درك ) And both of these words are the two dimensions of one thing. If someone ascends to the ceiling above via stairs then the same stairs or sticks are called DARAJAAT (درجات ) and in terms of descending, the same stairs are called DARAKAAT (دركات ) for the same reason, the idiom of DARAJAAT-UL-JANNAH (درجات الجنة ) and DARAKAAT-UN-NAAR (دركات النار ) is used. And status means such an excellence or dominance which is from one terminal to the second. Allah says:

           Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة  2:228

وَلَهُنَّ مِثْلُ الَّذِيْ عَلَيْهِنَّ بِالْمَعْرُوْفِ ۠ وَلِلرِّجَالِ عَلَيْهِنَّ دَرَجَةٌ

         And due to the wives is similar to what is expected of them, according to what is reasonable. But the men have a degree over them [in responsibility and authority].

 

6.2   ZULFA  (  زلفى ): means value and attachment, and the proximity in status. Allah says:

Surat Şād (The Letter "Saad") - سورة ص 38:40

 وَاِنَّ لَهٗ عِنْدَنَا لَزُلْفٰى وَحُسْنَ مَاٰبٍ  

And indeed, for him is nearness to Us and a good place of return.

But when this matter goes in Ifaal-e-baab, it gives the meaning of proximity in the distance. Allah says:

Surat Ash-Shu`arā' (The Poets) - سورة الشعراء 26:64

  وَاَزْلَفْنَا ثَمَّ الْاٰخَرِيْنَ  

 And We advanced thereto the pursuers.

6.3    QARABA  (قرب ): QAROBA  (قرب ) means to get closer and the action which becomes source to get closer in status. And QURBAAN (قربان ) is that scarification by which one needs to achieve the status and rank.  Allah says:

Surat Al-Mā'idah (The Table Spread) - سورة المائدة 5:27

  وَاتْلُ عَلَيْهِمْ نَبَاَ ابْنَيْ اٰدَمَ بِالْحَقِّ  ۘاِذْ قَرَّبَا قُرْبَانًا

And recite to them the story of Adam's two sons, in truth, when they both offered a sacrifice [to Allah ].

At another occasion:

Surat Al-Wāqi`ah (The Inevitable) - سورة الواقعة 56:10,11

   ڌاُولٰۗىِٕكَ الْمُقَرَّبُوْنَوَالسّٰبِقُوْنَ السّٰبِقُوْنَ

And the forerunners, the forerunners. Those are the ones brought near [to Allah ].

6.4    MAKANA (  مکن ): means having a high-rise status. And MAKAAN (مكان ) means place, location, residence. A place that has a body.
