17 LAIN DAIN KARNA [to do a dealing, to do a transaction amongst]

Synonyms: TADAYAN, ADARA

17.1 TADAYAN (تَدَایَنَ): DAIN (دَیْن) means a debt, loan (See UDHAAR [loan] for more details). TADAYAN (تَدَایَنَ) means to ask loan amongst each other. To deal with each other on loan (dictionary al Manjad). Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Baqarah (The cow): سورۃ البَقَرَۃ  2:282

يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا إِذَا تَدَايَنتُم بِدَيْنٍ إِلَىٰ أَجَلٍ مُّسَمًّى فَاكْتُبُوهُ ۚ

O you who believe! When you contract a debt for a fixed period, write it down.

 

17.2 ADARA (اَدَار):  DARA (دَارَ) means to circulate, go around. ADAARAH (اَدَارہ) means to circularize or revolve something. ADAAR-ASH-SHAI (اَدَارَ الشَّئْ) means to deal in something, to give and take on something or to do a transaction.

Surah Al-Baqarah (The cow): سورۃ البَقَرَۃ  2:282

ا ۖ إِلَّا أَن تَكُونَ تِجَارَةً حَاضِرَةً تُدِيرُونَهَا بَيْنَكُمْ

Except when it is an immediate transaction which you conduct among yourselves.

Summary:

 17.1 TADAYAN: To deal by loans.

17.2 ADARA: Immediate transactions.
