Fawj

Means a fastly moving group(of people), cavalry or infantry or Military troops. (muff)(Plural:Afwaaj).

A group larger that Tai'fah(Full 205). However in Qura'n, this word has mostly been used in the meaning of a group rather than the cavalry. We do not find in Qura'n , the word fawj being used in the meaning of military force a concept being associated with this word in the modern times.

Allah, Most Exhalted is He, says:

وَيَوْمَ نَحْشُرُ مِنْ كُلِّ أُمَّةٍ فَوْجًا مِمَّنْ يُكَذِّبُ بِآيَاتِنَا

"And (remember) the Day when We shall gather out of every nation a troop of those who denied Our Ayât (proofs, evidences, verses, lessons, signs, revelations, etc.)."[27:83]
