Tawwwaba, “lawtna, lawtaana, rujoo` kurna”, P.768

{

synonyms: RAJAA,AABA(AUB),TABA(TUB),AANABA(NOB),BAAA(BU-)

FAAA(FA-),SADARA,SARA(SER),IRTADDA(RDD),INQALIB,

EFAD,HAAD

1 RAJAA(رَجَعَ):meaning something returning to its origin.whether this thing is living or non living,action or speech,whether voluntarily or involuntary.there is a famous proverb (كُلُّ شَيْءٍ يَرجِعُ اِلي اَصْلِهِ)meaning everything returns to its origin.use of this word is common in all aspect

Surat Al-Baqarah [2:210] - The Noble Qur'an

وَإِلَى اللَّهِ تُرْجَعُ الْأُمُورُ

And to Allah [all] matters are returned

Surat Al-Baqarah [2:156] - The Noble Qur'an

الَّذِينَ إِذَا أَصَابَتْهُم مُّصِيبَةٌ قَالُوا إِنَّا لِلَّهِ وَإِنَّا إِلَيْهِ رَاجِعُونَ

Who, when disaster strikes them, say, "Indeed we belong to Allah , and indeed to Him we will return."

2 AABA(اَبَ):it is more specific than RAJAA.it is used only for living things and MAAB(ماَبَ)meaning place of return

and AYAB(اياب) meaning echo or return journey.and ZAHAB O AYAB(ذِهاب و اِياب) meaning to go and to return.

in AABA it is not necessary that return is voluntarily

Surat Al-Ghashiyah [88:25-26] - The Noble Qur'an

إِنَّ إِلَيْنَا إِيَابَهُمْ ثُمَّ إِنَّ عَلَيْنَا حِسَابَهُم 

Indeed, to Us is their return.Then indeed, upon Us is their account

when this verb goes beyond trilateral,for e.g (اَوَبَ اَوَّبَ )then it implies that return is voluntary AWAAB(اَوَّابَ) meaning the returner,the one always referring.

Surat Al-'Isra' [17:25] - The Noble Qur'an

فَإِنَّهُ كَانَ لِلْأَوَّابِينَ غَفُورًا

indeed He is ever, to the often returning [to Him], Forgiving.

3 TABA(تَابَ):meaning to give up sins,admitting sins,and vowing not to fall in sins again,to repent. If it refers to ALLAH then it means acceptance of repentence.forgive sins or to be merciful.

Surat Al-Baqarah [2:160] - The Noble Qur'an

إِلَّا الَّذِينَ تَابُوا وَأَصْلَحُوا وَبَيَّنُوا فَأُولَٰئِكَ أَتُوبُ عَلَيْهِمْ ۚ وَأَنَا التَّوَّابُ الرَّحِيمُ

Except for those who repent and correct themselves and make evident [what they concealed]. Those - I will accept their repentance, and I am the Accepting of repentance, the Merciful

4 ANABA(اَنَابَ):ANABA ILAYHE(اَنَابَ اليه)meaning to go some where repeatedly.NAUBAH(نوبة) meaning turn(as my turn),times(as many times),chance,free time and ANABA meaning to assist,to be incumbent and ANAB ILALLAH(اناب الي الّله) meaning to refer and to turn attention to ALLAH.

Surat Al-Mumtahanah [60:4]

ۖ رَّبَّنَا عَلَيْكَ تَوَكَّلْنَا وَإِلَيْكَ أَنَبْنَا

Our Lord, upon You we have relied, and to You we have returned}

5 BA-A(بَاءَ):BAU(بَوْء) basically two meaning a) to return b)to be equal meaning to return in such a state that result of their action becomes apparent to them.general usage is negative meaning returning from good state to worst.

Surat Al-Baqarah [2:61] - The Noble Qur'an

وَبَاءُوا بِغَضَبٍ مِّنَ اللَّهِ ۗ ذَٰلِكَ بِأَنَّهُمْ كَانُوا يَكْفُرُونَ بِآيَاتِ اللَّهِ وَيَقْتُلُونَ النَّبِيِّينَ بِغَيْرِ الْحَقِّ ۗ

and returned with anger from Allah [upon them]. That was because they [repeatedly] disbelieved in the signs of Allah and killed the prophets without right

meaning such was there state that they qualified for the wrath of ALLAH

6 FA-A(فَاءَ):meaning to return to good state,meaning to reform oneself. and to return to good from bad.and meaning return from near

Surat Al-Hujurat [49:9] - The Noble Qur'an

فَقَاتِلُوا الَّتِي تَبْغِي حَتَّىٰ تَفِيءَ إِلَىٰ أَمْرِ اللَّهِ ۚ

then fight against the one that oppresses until it returns to the ordinance of Allah

7 SADARA(صَدَرَ):ASDARA(اَصْدَر) meaning to return after quenching thirst. it is said (صَدَرَتِ الاِبِل مِنَ المَاءِ) meaning camels return after quenching their thirst.

Surat Al-Qasas [28:23] - The Noble Qur'an

قَالَتَا لَا نَسْقِي حَتَّىٰ يُصْدِرَ الرِّعَاءُ ۖ

They said, "We do not water until the shepherds dispatch [their flocks]

SADARA antonym is WARADA وَرَدَ meaning to go to watering hole and WARD meaning to water hole and it also means someone thirsty. and WARID (وَارِد) meaning the one who went to bring water from well or watering hole

Surat Yusuf [12:19] - The Noble Qur'an

وَجَاءَتْ سَيَّارَةٌ فَأَرْسَلُوا وَارِدَهُمْ فَأَدْلَىٰ دَلْوَهُ 

And there came a company of travelers; then they sent their water drawer, and he let down his bucket

8 SARA(صَارَ):SER(صير) consist of two meanings a)to refer b) the result meaning last echo

Surat Ash-Shuraa [42:53] - The Noble Qur'an

ۗ أَلَا إِلَى اللَّهِ تَصِيرُ الْأُمُورُ

Unquestionably, to Allah do [all] matters evolve.

9 RADDA(رَدَّ) and IRTADD(اِرْتَدَّ): RADDA meaning to return something owing to it being unacceptable.to turn it back,and RADDA meaning MURDOOD as well. 

Surat Yusuf [12:65] - The Noble Qur'an 

وَلَمَّا فَتَحُوا مَتَاعَهُمْ وَجَدُوا بِضَاعَتَهُمْ رُدَّتْ إِلَيْهِمْ

And when they opened their baggage, they found their merchandise returned to them

and IRTAD meaning to back off oneself because of something being unacceptable or to return

Surat Muhammad [47:25] - The Noble Qur'an

إِنَّ الَّذِينَ ارْتَدُّوا عَلَىٰ أَدْبَارِهِم مِّن بَعْدِ مَا تَبَيَّنَ لَهُمُ الْهُدَى ۙ

Indeed, those who reverted back [to disbelief] after guidance had become clear to them

then when RADD and IRTAD became common in usage then it was being used only for returning something or returning oneself

Surat Yusuf [12:96] - The Noble Qur'an

فَلَمَّا أَن جَاءَ الْبَشِيرُ أَلْقَاهُ عَلَىٰ وَجْهِهِ فَارْتَدَّ بَصِيرًا ۖ

And when the bearer of good tidings arrived, he cast it over his face, and he returned [once again] seeing

10 INQALAB(اِنْقَلَبَ):QALABA(قَلَبَ) meaning changing direction or state. to turn upside down and on the contrary.to bring inside out and vice versa.INQALAB meaning to change one's state or direction such that it's contrary to the inital state.to upside down,to return,to turn

Surat At-Tawbah [9:95] - The Noble Qur'an

سَيَحْلِفُونَ بِاللَّهِ لَكُمْ إِذَا انقَلَبْتُمْ إِلَيْهِمْ

They will swear by Allah to you when you return to them

11 AFADA():FADA ALMAA() meaning water to jump off a place and flow (fountain) AFADA ALANA() meaning to fill up a utensil/container so much that it starts overflow.from FAIZAN AL MAA similarity of moving along with the flow of crowd

Surat Al-Baqarah [2:199] - The Noble Qur'an

ثُمَّ أَفِيضُوا مِنْ حَيْثُ أَفَاضَ النَّاسُ

Then depart from the place from where [all] the people depart

12 HAAD(هاد):HUD(هود) meaning to refer with softness.HAVVD(هوّد) meaning to walk slowly and softness and to crawl.and HAD(هاد)meaning to refer to the truth.and HAD FIL MUNTAQ(هاد في المنطق)meaning to talk softly and slowly and HAWWADA()meaning to move voice around in the neck slowly slowly(humming),to sing,to saunter.so HADA means to softly and slowly slowly approach the truth

Surat Al-'A`raf [7:156] - The Noble Qur'an

وَاكْتُبْ لَنَا فِي هَٰذِهِ الدُّنْيَا حَسَنَةً وَفِي الْآخِرَةِ إِنَّا هُدْنَا إِلَيْكَ

And decree for us in this world [that which is] good and [also] in the Hereafter; indeed, we have turned back to You."

Summary:

1 RAJAA:something returning to its origin,general usage

2 AABA:returning of a living thing whether voluntary or involuntary.AWWAB: frequent returning of a living thing voluntarily

3 TAAB:to return from sin

4 ANAAB: to keep on continuously returning

5 BAA-A: to return to bad state/condition

6 FA-A: to return to good ,to return from near

7 SADARA: to return from well or watering hole after quenching thirst

8 SARA: last echo

9 RADD: to return something unacceptable,to reject

IRTADD:to return oneself because of something unacceptable

10 INQALAB:to turn state or direction contrary to the initial

11 AFADA:to return with the flow,to drift with the current

12 HAAD:to return softly and slowly slowly
