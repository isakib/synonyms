Surah Al-Anfal (The Spoils of War): سُوۡرَةُ الاٴنفَال 8:72

إِنَّ الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا وَهَاجَرُوا وَجَاهَدُوا بِأَمْوَالِهِمْ وَأَنفُسِهِمْ فِي سَبِيلِ اللَّهِ وَالَّذِينَ آوَوا وَّنَصَرُوا أُولَٰئِكَ بَعْضُهُمْ أَوْلِيَاءُ بَعْضٍ ۚ وَالَّذِينَ آمَنُوا وَلَمْ يُهَاجِرُوا مَا لَكُم مِّن وَلَايَتِهِم مِّن شَيْءٍ حَتَّىٰ يُهَاجِرُوا

 

Surely those who believed and fled (their homes) and struggled hard in Allah's way with their property and their souls, and those who gave shelter and helped-- these are guardians of each other; and (as for) those who believed and did not fly, not yours is their guardianship until they fly; and if they seek aid from you in the matter of religion, aid is incumbent on you except against a people between whom and you there is a treaty, and Allah sees what you do.

 

And MAULA (مَوْلٰی) (Plural MAWALI (مَوَالی)) means an heir. Allah says in the Quran

Surah An-Nisa (The women): سورۃ النِّسا 4:33

وَلِكُلٍّ جَعَلْنَا مَوَالِيَ مِمَّا تَرَكَ الْوَالِدَانِ وَالْأَقْرَبُونَ

And for all, We have made heirs to what is left by parents and relatives.

 

This law of inheritance was a part of series of fraternization. But when the living conditions of Muhajreen (Migrators i.e. the people who left Makkah and migrated to Medina) improved then this law of inheritance was abolished and only the relatives became the lawful heirs. Nonetheless it was advised to give something to friends as a gesture of good will. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Ahzaab (The clans): سورۃ الأحزَاب 33:6

فِي كِتَابِ اللَّهِ مِنَ الْمُؤْمِنِينَ وَالْمُهَاجِرِينَ إِلَّا أَن تَفْعَلُوا إِلَىٰ أَوْلِيَائِكُم مَّعْرُوفًا

 and the possessors of relationship have the better claim in the ordinance of Allah to inheritance, one with respect to another, than (other) believers, and (than) those who have fled (their homes), except that you do some good to your friends;

 

3.2 WIRATHA (وِرَاثَۃ): Those Shariah laws and divine decrees, according to which the possession of a dead goes to other people. This inheritance is not only related to property and money but also about habits and qualities. Prophet Muhammad (S.A.A.W) said “الْعُلَمَآءُ وَرَثَۃُ الأَنْبِیَاءِ” ‘The Scholars are the Inheritors of the Prophets’ and here the WARATHA (وَرَاثَۃ) mentioned is not wealth and money but knowledge and preaching. And WARITH (وَرِثَ) means to become the inheritor of a dead. AWRATHA (اَوْرَثَ) means to appoint an heir. And WARITHA (وَرَاثَۃ) and TURATH (تُرَاث) means legacy and inheritance. Allah says in the Quran

Surah An-Naml (The ant): سورۃ النَّمل 27:16

وَوَرِثَ سُلَيْمَانُ دَاوُودَ

And Solomon was David's heir.

 

Summary:

3.1 WALAYAT:  Law of inheritance as a part of series of fraternization which was later abolished.

3.2 WARATHAT: The portion of inheritance of relatives according to the Shariah Law.
