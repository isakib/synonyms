11 AASAN [EASE]

Synonyms: YASEER(YA-SA-RA) ,HAYYINUN(HA-WOW-NUN)

11.1 YASEER(يَسِير):its root YASR(يسر) includes connotation of deployment and minute. YASEER is that task which can be completed with ease and comfort (anotnym 3SEER عسِير)

Surat An-Nisā' (The Women) - سورة النساء
4:30
وَمَن يَفْعَلْ ذَٰلِكَ عُدْوَانًا وَظُلْمًا فَسَوْفَ نُصْلِيهِ نَارًا ۚ وَكَانَ ذَٰلِكَ عَلَى اللَّهِ يَسِيرًا
And whoever does that in aggression and injustice - then We will drive him into a Fire. And that, for Allah , is [always] easy.


11.2 HAYYIN(هَيِّن):its root is (هون) which includes the aspect of softness in addition to ease.conditional to that this softness doesn't include insult. but with softness includes tranquility and dignity and this is a commendable attribute . ALLAH said

Surat Al-Furqān (The Criterian) - سورة الفرقان
25:63
وَعِبَادُ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الَّذِينَ يَمْشُونَ عَلَى الْأَرْضِ هَوْنًا وَإِذَا خَاطَبَهُمُ الْجَاهِلُونَ قَالُوا سَلَامًا
And the servants of the Most Merciful are those who walk upon the earth easily, and when the ignorant address them [harshly], they say [words of] peace,

and if that softness makes someone low then its a matte of insult and disgrace and this a detestable act and HUUN(هُون) is used for this. as said

Surat Al-'Aĥqāf (The Wind-Curved Sandhills) - سورة الأحقاف
46:20
وَيَوْمَ يُعْرَضُ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا عَلَى النَّارِ أَذْهَبْتُمْ طَيِّبَاتِكُمْ فِي حَيَاتِكُمُ الدُّنْيَا وَاسْتَمْتَعْتُم بِهَا فَالْيَوْمَ تُجْزَوْنَ عَذَابَ الْهُونِ بِمَا كُنتُمْ تَسْتَكْبِرُونَ فِي الْأَرْضِ بِغَيْرِ الْحَقِّ وَبِمَا كُنتُمْ تَفْسُقُونَ
And the Day those who disbelieved are exposed to the Fire [it will be said], "You exhausted your pleasures during your worldly life and enjoyed them, so this Day you will be awarded the punishment of [extreme] humiliation because you were arrogant upon the earth without right and because you were defiantly disobedient."

therefore HAYYIN is such a task which is minuscule in comparison with the might and authority of the doer. because it's root contains the meaning of humility along with aspect of lowness and insult. absolutley ordinary and small matter. In Surat Maryam (Mary) when Angels inform Mary of Child birth without father then she exclaimed out of amazement :

Surat Maryam (Mary) - سورة مريم
19:20
قَالَتْ أَنَّىٰ يَكُونُ لِي غُلَامٌ وَلَمْ يَمْسَسْنِي بَشَرٌ وَلَمْ أَكُ بَغِيًّا
She said, "How can I have a boy while no man has touched me and I have not been unchaste?"
Surat Maryam (Mary) - سورة مريم
19:21
قَالَ كَذَٰلِكِ قَالَ رَبُّكِ هُوَ عَلَيَّ هَيِّنٌ ۖ وَلِنَجْعَلَهُ آيَةً لِّلنَّاسِ وَرَحْمَةً مِّنَّا ۚ وَكَانَ أَمْرًا مَّقْضِيًّا
He said, "Thus [it will be]; your Lord says, 'It is easy for Me, and We will make him a sign to the people and a mercy from Us. And it is a matter [already] decreed.' "

Summary:
11.1 YASEER: that matter or act which is easily/comfortable done by the doer
11.2 HAYYIN: that matter or act which is minuscule in comparison with the power and authority of the doer
