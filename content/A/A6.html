6 ARZOO ARZOO KARNA [WISH ,TO MAKE A WISH]

Synonyms: AMALA,UMNIYAH(MNY) and WADDA

6.1 AMALA(أَمَلَ): (plural AMAL أَمَال ) such a wish and expectation which though is expected and awaited yet difficult and beyond for fullfilment.thus Ibn faris had described its meaning as AS SABBAT Wa ALINTIZAR (التَثَبَّت وَ الإِنتِظَار) meaning to hope for a wish and to wait for its to come true,to be hopeful of long life.
ALLAH said:
Surat Al-Ĥijr (The Rocky Tract) - سورة الحجر
15:3
ذَرْهُمْ يَأْكُلُوا وَيَتَمَتَّعُوا وَيُلْهِهِمُ الْأَمَلُ ۖ فَسَوْفَ يَعْلَمُونَ
Let them eat and enjoy themselves and be diverted by [false] hope, for they are going to know.

6.2 UMNIYAH(أُمنِيَة): (plural AMANI أَمَانِي ) such a wish which there is no strong foundation/basis. To think of some good expectation and estimation. and then to wish for it .so this word is used for false and unreal ,desires and expectations . In the Holy Quran

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة
2:111
وَقَالُوا لَن يَدْخُلَ الْجَنَّةَ إِلَّا مَن كَانَ هُودًا أَوْ نَصَارَىٰ ۗ تِلْكَ أَمَانِيُّهُمْ ۗ قُلْ هَاتُوا بُرْهَانَكُمْ إِن كُنتُمْ صَادِقِينَ
And they say, "None will enter Paradise except one who is a Jew or a Christian." That is [merely] their wishful thinking, Say, "Produce your proof, if you should be truthful."

6.3 WADDA(وَدَّ):it contains two basic meaning a) to love something b) to wish for it. then sometime this word is used in both connotation and sometimes in one only for e.g.

Perhaps those who disbelieve will wish that they had been Muslims.

Summary:
6.1 AMALA: apparently unexpected and delayed fullfilment of wish
6.2 UMNIYAH: false and baseless wish (N.B and that wish which has a foundation RIJA please see UMEED LAGANA )
6.3 WADDA: love and wish of anything (N.B. see also KHWAHISH)
