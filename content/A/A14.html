14 AAG [FIRE ]

Synonyms: NAAR,LAZAA
NAR(نَار) :is of common usage . whether fire is blazing or intense or about to go out. whether it contains flame or not ,NAR is applicable to all of them. Whereas LAZA(لَظَى) is such an intensely hot and blazing fire in which there is no flame. in the Holy Quran

Surat Al-Ma`ārij (The Ascending Stairways) - سورة المعارج
70:15
كَلَّا ۖ إِنَّهَا لَظَىٰ
No! Indeed, it is the Flame [of Hell],
