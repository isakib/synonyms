7 AAR [Barrier]

Synonyms: BARZAKH,HAJR,HAJZ,HADD

7.1 BARZAKH(بَرزَخ): a barrier between two things where these things are quite far apart then this barrier is called BARZAKH. like between this world and hereafter or time interval between death till judgment day is called BARZAKH. But the condition of being 'quite far apart' that ibn faris imposes is not correct as the Holy Quran doesn't confirms it. ALLAH said

Surat Ar-Raĥmān (The Beneficent) - سورة الرحمن
55:19
مَرَجَ الْبَحْرَيْنِ يَلْتَقِيَانِ
He released the two seas, meeting [side by side];


Surat Ar-Raĥmān (The Beneficent) - سورة الرحمن
55:20
بَيْنَهُمَا بَرْزَخٌ لَّا يَبْغِيَانِ
Between them is a barrier [so] neither of them transgresses.

but if instead of 'quite far apart' ,just enough difference (of attribute) would be more suitable
BARZAKH is actually a third thing in between two things which also functions as barrier,but this third thing i.e BARZAKH contains attributes of both the things whether of one of them dominant or equal of both of them. World of BARZAKH is like this that for Momin(Believer) it's comfort/relief and for disbeliever it's the begining of punishment, but this relief/punishment is of lesser magnitude than that of the Hereafter
and middle BARZAKH between two rivers is like that in one ocean there is warm tidal current and on the other there is cold tidal current then water of that BARZAKH will be moderate. likewise if one side is freshwater and the other saline and bitter then BARZAKH of this water will be of intermediate state and more examples of this BARZAKH can be given which contains moderate/intermediate types of attributes for e.g between Heaven and Earth (AARAF اعرََاف
) where there will be neither Punishment nor Pleasure. Between Human and Animals is chimpanzee, between Human and Fish is mermaid, between animals and plants is coral, between solid and liquid is mercury etc etc



7.2 HIJR(حِجر): HIJARAH(حِجَارَة) commonly means stone which is hard and solid. then HIJR is also said to be every thing that is hard and can function as a barrier or hurdle which can separate one thing from another thing. as per Ibn e Faris it's meaning is ALMANA WALAHATAH ALA SHAYEE (المَنَع وَ الأَحَاطَه علَى الشَيِ).To build protective wall around the village for the protection of village, to encircle with stones,to encircle with courtyard or mansion, all of this is included in the meaning of HIJR . ALLAH said

Surat Al-Furqān (The Criterian) - سورة الفرقان
25:22
يَوْمَ يَرَوْنَ الْمَلَائِكَةَ لَا بُشْرَىٰ يَوْمَئِذٍ لِّلْمُجْرِمِينَ وَيَقُولُونَ حِجْرًا مَّحْجُورًا
The day they see the angels - no good tidings will there be that day for the criminals, and [the angels] will say, "Prevented and inaccessible."

HIJRAN MAHJORAN(حِجْرًا مَّحْجُورًا) is a word that is used as proverb by the arabs at the time of descending of an enemy or calamity. like we say "I seek refuge of ALLAH from him/it"(not used in english ,just urdu translation) then the one listening would do no harm to the speaker

then HIJR is used for such an intellect which deny the acceptance of false ,wrong theories and futile talk. so such a cautious intelligence which can think of it's own defense is HIJR.
ALLAH said

Surat Al-Fajr (The Dawn) - سورة الفجر
89:5
هَلْ فِي ذَٰلِكَ قَسَمٌ لِّذِي حِجْرٍ
Is there [not] in [all] that an oath [sufficient] for one of perception?

then HIJR is also used for forbidden,illegal and haram things

Surat Al-'An`ām (The Cattle) - سورة الأنعام
6:138
وَقَالُوا هَٰذِهِ أَنْعَامٌ وَحَرْثٌ حِجْرٌ لَّا يَطْعَمُهَا إِلَّا مَن نَّشَاءُ بِزَعْمِهِمْ وَأَنْعَامٌ حُرِّمَتْ ظُهُورُهَا وَأَنْعَامٌ لَّا يَذْكُرُونَ اسْمَ اللَّهِ عَلَيْهَا افْتِرَاءً عَلَيْهِ ۚ سَيَجْزِيهِم بِمَا كَانُوا يَفْتَرُونَ
And they say, "These animals and crops are forbidden; no one may eat from them except whom we will," by their claim. And there are those [camels] whose backs are forbidden [by them] and those upon which the name of Allah is not mentioned - [all of this] an invention of untruth about Him. He will punish them for what they were inventing.


7.3 HJZ (حجز): is something in between two things which is obstructing them AL HAWL BAYN AL SHAYEEN(الحول بين الشَيِين) and HAJIZ (حَاجِز) meaning barrier or curtain. ALLAH said
Surat An-Naml (The Ant) - سورة النمل
27:61
أَمَّن جَعَلَ الْأَرْضَ قَرَارًا وَجَعَلَ خِلَالَهَا أَنْهَارًا وَجَعَلَ لَهَا رَوَاسِيَ وَجَعَلَ بَيْنَ الْبَحْرَيْنِ حَاجِزًا ۗ أَإِلَٰهٌ مَّعَ اللَّهِ ۚ بَلْ أَكْثَرُهُمْ لَا يَعْلَمُونَ
Is He [not best] who made the earth a stable ground and placed within it rivers and made for it firmly set mountains and placed between the two seas a barrier? Is there a deity with Allah ? [No], but most of them do not know.

then HAJIZ is also used to sheild in between, stopping one thing or protecting other thing

Surat Al-Ĥāqqah (The Reality) - سورة الحاقة
69:47
فَمَا مِنكُم مِّنْ أَحَدٍ عَنْهُ حَاجِزِينَ
And there is no one of you who could prevent [Us] from him.


7.4 HADD(حدّ): The limit of any thing ,and it's last edge which separates it from other things. so it's basic meaning includes a) Limit b) Edge. so HADD is not any third thing ,it's the last edge of a thing which doesn't let it meet/mix with other things. HUDDO O ARBA3 is commonly used in urdu to explain this meaning .ALLAH said

Surat An-Nisā' (The Women) - سورة النساء
4:14
وَمَن يَعْصِ اللَّهَ وَرَسُولَهُ وَيَتَعَدَّ حُدُودَهُ يُدْخِلْهُ نَارًا خَالِدًا فِيهَا وَلَهُ عَذَابٌ مُّهِينٌ
And whoever disobeys Allah and His Messenger and transgresses His limits - He will put him into the Fire to abide eternally therein, and he will have a humiliating punishment.


Summary:
7.1 BARZAKH: between two things a third thing in which there is more or less or equal attributes of both of them and functions as a barrier
7.2 HJR: such a barrier which protect one thing from other things
7.3 HJZ: between two things such a barrier which protects and shield one thing from other thing
7.4 HADD:Last Limit or edge of one thing which prevent that thing from meeting or mixing with other things (N.B also see PARDAH and DEWAAR)
