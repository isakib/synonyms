6 CHUP HWNA -REHNA [To be Quiet-To Stay Quiet/Silence]

SYNONYMS:SAKATA(سَكَتَ) SAMATA(صَمَتَ) ANSATA(اَنْصَتَ)

6.1 SAKATA:to stop suddenly while talking continuously.to abate/ let up.to silence. opposite of speech.in this meaning this word is not used in the QURAN . yet used for the anger abation.ALLAH said

Surat Al-'A`rāf (The Heights) - سورة الأعراف

7:154

وَلَمَّا سَكَتَ عَن مُّوسَى الْغَضَبُ أَخَذَ الْأَلْوَاحَ ۖ وَفِي نُسْخَتِهَا هُدًى وَرَحْمَةٌ لِّلَّذِينَ هُمْ لِرَبِّهِمْ يَرْهَبُونَ

And when the anger subsided in Moses, he took up the tablets; and in their inscription was guidance and mercy for those who are fearful of their Lord.

6.2 SAMATA:meaning to stay quite.one who talk less,of quiet nature.As per Prophetic Narration MAN SAMATA NAJA(مَنْ صَمَتَ نَجَا) meaning that person who stays silent found salvation.antonym of SAMATA is NATAQAنَطَقَ. MAL-E-SAMAT(urdu) is cash and currency and MAL-E-NATIQ(urdu) to animals.

ALLAH said

Surat Al-'A`rāf (The Heights) - سورة الأعراف

7:193

وَإِن تَدْعُوهُمْ إِلَى الْهُدَىٰ لَا يَتَّبِعُوكُمْ ۚ سَوَاءٌ عَلَيْكُمْ أَدَعَوْتُمُوهُمْ أَمْ أَنتُمْ صَامِتُونَ

And if you [believers] invite them to guidance, they will not follow you. It is all the same for you whether you invite them or you are silent.

6.3 ANSATA:NASATA(نَصَتَ) and ANSATA meaning to listen to other's discourse quietly.In the QURAN

Surat Al-'A`rāf (The Heights) - سورة الأعراف

7:204

وَإِذَا قُرِئَ الْقُرْآنُ فَاسْتَمِعُوا لَهُ وَأَنصِتُوا لَعَلَّكُمْ تُرْحَمُونَ

So when the Qur'an is recited, then listen to it and pay attention that you may receive mercy.

SUMMARY:

6.1 SAKATA:become quiet, while talking consecutively

6.2 SAMATA:quite ,one who talk less

6.3 ANSATA:to listen to other's discourse quietly
