19 CHAWPA-AY [Four Legged]

Synonyms: WUHUSH وُحُوش BAHA-IM بَهَائِم AN3AM اَنْعَام DAWWAB دَوَّاب

19.1 WUHUSH:is plural of WAHASH(وحش). and WAHASH is antonym of INS(انس).meaning such living beings who doesn't get familiar with humans.wild animals and beast etc.In the QURAN

Surat At-Takwīr (The Overthrowing) - سورة التكوير

81:5

وَإِذَا الْوُحُوشُ حُشِرَتْ

And when the wild beasts are gathered

19.2 BAHA-IM:is plural of BAHIYMAH بَهِيْمة. and it applies to all four legged except of wild four legged.whether of land or water(aquatic).yet it mostly applies to goat,sheep and cow etc.BHM بهم is lamb or kid/billy.and one definition of BAHIYMAH is that such four legged(except beast)who don't possess hearing.and BUHM بُهم is difficult task, and ABHAM اَبْهَمْ is dumb.

19.3 AN3AM:is plural of NA3M نَعْم. so this word also applies to all animals except on beasts. yet this word is mostly used for camel.because for arabs there is no blessing greater than camel.In Quran , the word AN3AM even comes on it's own as well. as

Surat `Abasa (He Frowned) - سورة عبس

80:32

مَّتَاعًا لَّكُمْ وَلِأَنْعَامِكُمْ

[As] enjoyment for you and your grazing livestock.

20:54

كُلُوا وَارْعَوْا أَنْعَامَكُمْ ۗ إِنَّ فِي ذَٰلِكَ لَآيَاتٍ لِّأُولِي النُّهَىٰ

Eat [therefrom] and pasture your livestock. Indeed, in that are signs for those of intelligence.

yet word BAHIMAH (in singular form) mostly comes with AN3AM.as

19.4 ADDAWWAB(الدوّاب):is plural of DAABAH(دآبة).and every animal that walks on the earth's surface whether by crawling on stomach as snake etc or on two feets as human.and some types of monkeys or having four legs like wild animals or four legged whether for riding or for loading (transport).male of female for all DAABAH is a common word.but its specifically used for horses,mule and donkey. Summary:

19.1 WUHUSH:wild animals-beasts

19.2 BAHA-IM:all four legged except beasts.for grazing animals generally goat,sheep and cow etc

19.3 AN3AM:all four legged except beasts.for camels generally

19.4 DAWWAB:every 'walking' living being in the earth.
