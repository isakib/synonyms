14-shout/scream

Words used are ‘na’aqa’ (نعق), ‘sadda’( صد), ‘ja’ara’( جر), ‘istasrakha;( استصرخ( صرخ)), ‘sarra’( صر).

1)   ‘na’aqa’ (نعق) means calling someone loudly, shepherd calling his flock.

Quran says: The example of those who disbelieve is like that of one who shouts at what hears nothing but calls and cries cattle or sheep - deaf, dumb and blind   ۚ وَنِدَاءً دُعَاءً إِلَّا  يَسْمَعُ لَا بِمَا يَنْعِقُ الَّذِي كَمَثَلِ كَفَرُوا الَّذِينَ وَمَثَلُ [2:171]

2)   ‘sadda’( صد): ‘yasiddu’ means making noise without any reason,  saddad means to clap.

For example: And when the son of Mary was presented as an example, immediately your people laughed aloud.

يَصِدُّونَ مِنْهُ قَوْمُكَ إِذَا مَثَلًا مَرْيَمَ ابْنُ ضُرِبَ وَلَمَّا[43:57]

3)   ‘ja’ara’( جر): means rumbling in loud voice, high pitch noise producing or groaning by wild animals due to nervousness like deer etc. For example:

Until when We seize their affluent ones with punishment, at once they are crying [to Allah ] for help.يَجْأَرُونَ هُمْ إِذَا بِالْعَذَابِ مُتْرَفِيهِم نَا أَخَذْ إِذَا حَتَّىٰ[23:64]

4)   ‘istasrakha’( استصرخ( صرخ)): ‘sarakha’ means crying out or screaming for help. to request. ‘asrakha’         means to reach someone to help him/her who asks for help. ‘istasrakha’( استصرخ( صرخ)) means to beg for help before people.

 For example: when suddenly the one who sought his help the previous day cried out to him [once again]. يَسْتَصْرِخُهُ بِالْأَمْسِ اسْتَنصَرَهُ الَّذِي فَإِذَا [28:18]

5)   ‘sarra’( صر): ‘sarra’( صر) and صرالرجل صر means someone shouting loudly. ‘sarrah’ means a high scream. For example: And his wife approached with a cry [of alarm] and struck her face and said, "[I am] a barren old woman!"

           عَقِيمٌ عَجُوزٌ وَقَالَتْ وَجْهَهَا فَصَكَّتْ صَرَّةٍ فِي امْرَأَتُهُ فَأَقْبَلَتِ[51:29]

Conclusion:

1- ‘na’aqa’ (نعق): means calling someone loudly.

2- ‘sadda’( صد):making noise unnecessarily.

3- ‘ja’ara’( جر): groaning due to tension or nervousness.

4- ‘istasrakha’( استصرخ( صرخ)): shouting or begging for help.

5- ‘sarra’( صر): screaming or shouting.
