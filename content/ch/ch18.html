18 CHUN LAYNA [To Choose]

Synonyms: IKHTAR (KH-YA-RA)(اِخْتار (خير) ISTAFA(SA-FA-WA)(اِصْطَفَي (صفو) ,IJTABA(JA-BA-WA)(اِجْتَبَي (جبو),

ISTAKHLASA(KH-LA-SA)( اِسْتَخْلَصَ (خلص),ISTANA3A(SA-NA-3A)(اِصطَنَعَ(صنع)

18.1 IKHTAR:KHAYR خير is every such thing and discourse which is desirable to all.and IKHTAR refers to selection of the better thing from lots of things of the same kind.In the QURAN

Surat Al-'A`rāf (The Heights) - سورة الأعراف

7:155

وَاخْتَارَ مُوسَىٰ قَوْمَهُ سَبْعِينَ رَجُلًا لِّمِيقَاتِنَا ۖ فَلَمَّا أَخَذَتْهُمُ الرَّجْفَةُ قَالَ رَبِّ لَوْ شِئْتَ أَهْلَكْتَهُم مِّن قَبْلُ وَإِيَّايَ ۖ أَتُهْلِكُنَا بِمَا فَعَلَ السُّفَهَاءُ مِنَّا ۖ إِنْ هِيَ إِلَّا فِتْنَتُكَ تُضِلُّ بِهَا مَن تَشَاءُ وَتَهْدِي مَن تَشَاءُ ۖ أَنتَ وَلِيُّنَا فَاغْفِرْ لَنَا وَارْحَمْنَا ۖ وَأَنتَ خَيْرُ الْغَافِرِينَ

And Moses chose from his people seventy men for Our appointment. And when the earthquake seized them, he said, "My Lord, if You had willed, You could have destroyed them before and me [as well]. Would You destroy us for what the foolish among us have done? This is not but Your trial by which You send astray whom You will and guide whom You will. You are our Protector, so forgive us and have mercy upon us; and You are the best of forgivers.

18.2 ISTAFA:ASSAFA الصّفا and SAFW صفو means pure and clean.ASSAFIYU اَلصَّفِيُّ and ASSAFWAH اَلصَّفْوَة is a sincere friend. and ISTAFA mean to select something on the basis of purity and cleanliness of self(NAFS نفس).In the QURAN

Surat 'Āli `Imrān (Family of Imran) - سورة آل عمران

3:33

إِنَّ اللَّهَ اصْطَفَىٰ آدَمَ وَنُوحًا وَآلَ إِبْرَاهِيمَ وَآلَ عِمْرَانَ عَلَى الْعَالَمِينَ

Indeed, Allah chose Adam and Noah and the family of Abraham and the family of 'Imran over the worlds -

18.3 IJTABA:JABUW(جبو) means a) to collect something.b)then take it some where .YUJMA3U WA YUHMALU(يُجْمَعُ وَ يُحْمَلُ).in words of ibn faris JAMA3 ASSHAYEE WA ALTAJMM3(جمع الشئ والتجمّع). in addition word JABUW is used commonly for collecting tax or toll which also includes transfering it to the treasury.and MUJBA مُجبَي meaning tax. In the QURAN

Surat Al-Qaşaş (The Stories) - سورة القصص

28:57

وَقَالُوا إِن نَّتَّبِعِ الْهُدَىٰ مَعَكَ نُتَخَطَّفْ مِنْ أَرْضِنَا ۚ أَوَلَمْ نُمَكِّن لَّهُمْ حَرَمًا آمِنًا يُجْبَىٰ إِلَيْهِ ثَمَرَاتُ كُلِّ شَيْءٍ رِّزْقًا مِّن لَّدُنَّا وَلَٰكِنَّ أَكْثَرَهُمْ لَا يَعْلَمُونَ

And they say, "If we were to follow the guidance with you, we would be swept from our land." Have we not established for them a safe sanctuary to which are brought the fruits of all things as provision from Us? But most of them do not know.

and IJTABA means to bring near/closer ,by selecting some fine/excellent thing.to make closer and choosen.In the QURAN

Surat Yūsuf (Joseph) - سورة يوسف

12:6

وَكَذَٰلِكَ يَجْتَبِيكَ رَبُّكَ وَيُعَلِّمُكَ مِن تَأْوِيلِ الْأَحَادِيثِ وَيُتِمُّ نِعْمَتَهُ عَلَيْكَ وَعَلَىٰ آلِ يَعْقُوبَ كَمَا أَتَمَّهَا عَلَىٰ أَبَوَيْكَ مِن قَبْلُ إِبْرَاهِيمَ وَإِسْحَاقَ ۚ إِنَّ رَبَّكَ عَلِيمٌ حَكِيمٌ

And thus will your Lord choose you and teach you the interpretation of narratives and complete His favor upon you and upon the family of Jacob, as He completed it upon your fathers before, Abraham and Isaac. Indeed, your Lord is Knowing and Wise."

18.4 ISTAKHLASA:KHALASA(خَلَصَ) (antonym KHALATA خَلَطَ)meaning to be free from mixture(impurities) and filth of other things.tobe purified.and KHALLAS(خَلَّص) meaning to make pure.KHALAS is more eloquent than SAFA because SAFA could be clean before hand.thus ISTAKHLASA means to choose someone and to be specify for one's self. In the QURAN

Surat Yūsuf (Joseph) - سورة يوسف

12:54

وَقَالَ الْمَلِكُ ائْتُونِي بِهِ أَسْتَخْلِصْهُ لِنَفْسِي ۖ فَلَمَّا كَلَّمَهُ قَالَ إِنَّكَ الْيَوْمَ لَدَيْنَا مَكِينٌ أَمِينٌ

And the king said, "Bring him to me; I will appoint him exclusively for myself." And when he spoke to him, he said, "Indeed, you are today established [in position] and trusted."

18.5 ISTANA3A:SANA3 صنع means to make something with technical skill/mastery.and ISTANA3A means for some special objective, to select some appropriate person and then trained him specially.ALLAH said regarding Moses (PBUH).

Surat Ţāhā (Ta-Ha) - سورة طه

20:41

وَاصْطَنَعْتُكَ لِنَفْسِي

And I produced you for Myself.

Summary:

18.1 IKHTAR:To select something better from lots of things

18.2 ISTAFA:To select something on the basis of purity

18.3 IJTABA:To select something then keep it close to oneself

18.4 ISTAKHLASA:To select some person for some special objective

18.5 ISTANA3A:to select some one for some special objective and then train in an appropriate manner
