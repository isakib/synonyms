Silent ( ہونا چپ)

 

Words used are ‘sakata’ (سکت), ‘samata’(صمت), and ‘ansata’(انصت).

1-  (سکت): to be stop inbetween talking or to take pause or to be quite. Against Alkalam, Quran does have this word for these meanings. instead it is used for subsiding or reducing anger.for example:

And when the anger subsided in Moses. وَلَمَّا سَكَتَ عَن مُّوسَى الْغَضَبُ [7:154]

2-  (صمت): to keep silent. Speaking less as being silent.  Prophet Muhammad(peace be upon him said), “نجا صمت من” i.e; the one who attain silence , has succeed.The opposite of this word is oration or speech. Money or cash is termed as silent material or possessions whereas orating material means animals. For example:

And if you [believers] invite them to guidance, they will not follow you. It is all the same for you whether you invite them or you are silent. سَوَاءٌ عَلَيْكُمْ أَدَعَوْتُمُوهُمْ أَمْ أَنتُمْ صَامِتُونَ[7:193]

 

3-(انصت): means to hear someone silently. For example: So when the Qur'an is recited, then listen to it and pay attention that you may receive mercy.  وَإِذَا قُرِئَ الْقُرْآنُ فَاسْتَمِعُوا لَهُ وَأَنصِتُوا لَعَلَّكُمْ تُرْحَمُونَ[7:204]

Conclusion: (صمت) is used for speaking less or keep silent, (سکت) is used for taking pause inbetween talking and (انصت) is used for hearing other silently.
