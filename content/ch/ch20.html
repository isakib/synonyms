1.   دَكَّآءَ- dakka has basically 2 meanings: (1) To crush something and (2) lay it even with the ground.

(7:143) فَلَمَّا تَجَلَّىٰ رَبُّهُ لِلْجَبَلِ جَعَلَهُ دَكًّا                           

-but when his Lord manifested His glory to the mountain He made it crumble.

-At another place its stated:

(18:98)  فَإِذَا جَاءَ وَعْدُ رَبِّي جَعَلَهُ دَكَّاءَ 

-but when the promise of my Lord comes, He will make it level

 

Summary:

1.   haxiim- dry twig fragments

2.   HuTaam- what is broken in pieces of a thing that is dry

3.   gucaae- the rubbish of dead leaves, scum

4.   hbae- dust particles

5.   rafaat- a thing that has become old and worn out, and crumbled or broken out into small pieces

6.   bassa- breaking a hard thing or stone into pieces

7.   dakkaae- To crush something and lay it even with the ground
