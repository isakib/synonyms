25 CHATT     [ roof ]

SYNONYMS: 3ARSH, SAQAF, and BINA’A

 

 

25.1  3ARSH (عرش) : يَعْرِشُ عَرَشَ means to make a house made of wood, and make a roof on it made of wood, like (??***********************) and to put a grapevine on a fence etc. And عرش has two meanings in its root form. (1) throne. (2) the wood used to make a roof. This word is also used for the roof of a room, tent, half house, and (HARWASHA****************) which gives a shade. In the Qur’an this word is used in both meanings.

For the meaning of a roof and its wood, see 2:259.

For the example for throne, see the word “throne”.

_____________________________________

25.2  SAQAF (سَقْف) : the inside part of a roof which is seen from inside the room. Or (AASMANE KHAANA ****************************).

Refer to 16:26

____________________________________

25.3  BINA’A (بِنَآء) : means construction, or a constructed building. And it can also refer to every part of a building, whether it is the base, walls, roof, or the whole building. And when this word is used related to the floor, then it still means roof, because بِنَآء can refer to the height of a construction.

Refer to 2:23

___________________________________

SUMMARY

3ARSH : refers to the whole roof and its wood

SAQAF : the inside part of a roof

BINA’A : it means the roof when referring to the floor.
