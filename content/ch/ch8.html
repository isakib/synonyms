8 CHARANA [Grazing]

SYNONYMS:RA3A(رَعَِي) AASAAM(اَسَام)

8.1 RA3A: meaning to make some cattle graze and it's protection and to upbring. and RA3Iرَاعِي meaning shepherd,herdsman and MAR3Aمَرْعَي meaning fodder as well as pasture.to prevent something from getting wasted.and to remove the sources of external threats.antonym is AHMALاهمال meaning herd without shepherd. ALLAH said

Surat Ţāhā (Ta-Ha) - سورة طه

20:54

كُلُوا وَارْعَوْا أَنْعَامَكُمْ ۗ إِنَّ فِي ذَٰلِكَ لَآيَاتٍ لِّأُولِي النُّهَىٰ

Eat [therefrom] and pasture your livestock. Indeed, in that are signs for those of intelligence.

word RA3A basic meaning is training and upbringing therefore it can also be applied to humans. king is RA3Aراعي and public is RA3IYATرعيت

8.2 AASAAM:SAWMAسَوْم has two basic meanings a) demand b)departure- meaning to depart in demand of something. SAMAT ALIBLسامت الاِبِل meaning Camels went towards the pasture for grazing. and AASAMA meaning to take(cattle) for grazing.ALLAH said

Surat An-Naĥl (The Bee) - سورة النحل

16:10

هُوَ الَّذِي أَنزَلَ مِنَ السَّمَاءِ مَاءً ۖ لَّكُم مِّنْهُ شَرَابٌ وَمِنْهُ شَجَرٌ فِيهِ تُسِيمُونَ

It is He who sends down rain from the sky; from it is drink and from it is foliage in which you pasture [animals].

SUMMARY:

8.1 RA3A:meaning grazing and take care

8.2 AASAAM:to take out for grazing
