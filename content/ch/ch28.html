28 CHOONA

        [to touch]

 

 

SYNONYMS:  MASSA, LAMASA, and TAMASA

In the Qur’an these three words hint towards sexual intercourse between a man and woman for eg:

1)        Massa: Refer to 3:47

2)        Lamasa: Refer to 5:6

3)        Tamasa: Refer to 55:51

But according to the dictionary point of view, these three words are separate which are not mentioned often.

28.1  MASSA (مَسَّ) : when something contacts a part of the body. Massa is also used in the Qur’an with this meaning.

Refer to 21:83

Similiarly, in another place it is seen:

Refer to 2:80

_______________________________________

28.2  LAMASA (لَمَسَ) : to touch or feel something with the hand or fingers, irrespective of whether you get the thing or not.

Refer to 72:8

_______________________________________

28.3 TAMASA (طمس) : means menstrual blood. And يَطْمِثُ طَمَثَ refers to a woman’s being in the condition of menstruating, and also to a man breaking a woman’s hymen. Therefore from the meanings of intercourse, it is more specific than the first meaning of intercourse, and in the first two meanings this word is not used in the Qur’an. (see the meaning of Mujameat karna – to have intercourse).
