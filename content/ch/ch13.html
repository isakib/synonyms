13-to make move

Words used are ‘saaqa’(ساق( سوق)), ‘harraka’( حرک), ‘azjaa’(ازجی (زجو)), ‘salaka’( سلک), ‘sayyar’( سیر) and ‘asra’( اسری).

1)- ‘saaqa’(ساق( سوق)): means to drive, driving through tapping on back like shepherds drive their animals or driver who drives vehicle. Driver is termed as ‘sawwaq’. Quran says: And will drive the criminals to Hell in thirst.  وِرْدًا جَهَنَّمَ إِلَىٰ الْمُجْرِمِينَ وَنَسُوقُ[19:86]

2) ‘harraka’( حرک): ‘haraka’ means to move, to make any activity and ‘harraka’ means to move something, to make something moving forward.

For example: Move not your tongue with it, [O Muhammad], to hasten with recitation of the Qur'an. بِهِ لِتَعْجَلَ لِسَانَكَ بِهِ تُحَرِّكْ لَا [75:16]

3) ‘azjaa’(ازجی (زجو)) :means to drive slowly or softly. ‘mazajja’ means weak. For example: Do you not see that Allah drives clouds? Then He brings them together. بَيْنَهُ يُؤَلِّفُ ثُمَّ سَحَابًا يُزْجِي اللَّهَ أَنَّ تَرَ أَلَمْ [24:43]

4) ‘salaka’( سلک) means 1-)walking on path.

                                 2-)interpenetrating one thing into another or coming across, increasing.

This word is especially related to the path. Quran says: [It is He] who has made for you the earth as a bed [spread out] and inserted therein for you roadways  سُبُلًا فِيهَا لَكُمْ وَسَلَكَ مَهْدًا الْأَرْضَ لَكُمُ جَعَلَ الَّذِي [20:53]

5) ‘sayyar’( سیر): ‘saara’ means roaming, walking and travelling. ‘sayyar’( سیر) means to make something move, travel. To see off. For example: It is He who enables you to travel on land and sea. وَالْبَحْرِ الْبَرِّ فِي يُسَيِّرُكُمْ الَّذِي هُوَ[10:22]

6) ‘asra’( اسری): ‘sara’ means moving on night. ‘asra’( اسری) means to make move on night, to make travel /walk. Quran says:

"[O Lot] So set out with your family during a portion of the night. اللَّيْلِ مِّنَ بِقِطْعٍ بِأَهْلِكَ فَأَسْرِ [11:81]

At another place, Exalted is He who took His Servant by night from . لَيْلًا بِعَبْدِهِ أَسْرَىٰ الَّذِي سُبْحَانَ [17:1]

Conclusion:

1)            ‘saaqa’(ساق( سوق)): to drive(to make move from back).

2)            ‘harraka’( حرک): drive through an activity.

3)            ‘azjaa’(ازجی (زجو)): to make move slowly.

4)            ‘salaka’( سلک): to follow a path.

5)            ‘sayyar’( سیر): to make walk/roam.

6)            ‘asra’( اسری): to make move/walk at night,
