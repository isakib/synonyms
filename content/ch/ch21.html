21- TO STEAL

 

1-  saraqa: To steal- To take secretly a thing from somebody else’s  property in a secure place.

Allah SWT says:

(5:38) وَالسَّارِقَةُ فَاقْطَعُوا أَيْدِيَهُمَا وَالسَّارِقُ

-And (as for) the man who steals and the woman who steals, cut off their hands

-istaraqa means ‘to try to steal’

-  ِإستَرَقُ ٱلسَمَع  -To listen or hear by stealth

Allah SWT says:

(15:18) إِلَّا مَنِ اسْتَرَقَ السَّمْعَ فَأَتْبَعَهُ شِهَابٌ مُبِينٌ

-But he who steals a hearing, so there follows him a visible flame.

 

2-  غَلَّ:  to steal something and keep in one’s own luggage. Used for stealing and defalcation. For example to take something from booty which does not classify as stealing.

Allah SWT says:

(3:161)  وَمَنْ يَغْلُلْ يَأْتِ بِمَا غَلَّ يَوْمَ الْقِيَامَةِ

And whoever betrays, [taking unlawfully], will come with what he took on the Day of Resurrection.

 

Summary:

1-saraqa: To steal

2-galla- a combined form of stealing and  defalcation.
