27  CHURHANA

          [ to free ]

SYNONYMS: FAKKA, ANKAZA and ISTANKAZA.

 

 

27.1 FAKKA (فَكَّ): means to remove something from someone’s captivity, or to make free. الرّهن فك means to free something which is in pawn. الاسير فك means to free a captive of war.

Refer to 90:13

____________________________________

27.2 ANKAZA (اَنْقَذَ) : means to free a person from a difficulty or a punishment, to save someone.

Refer to 39:19

And اِسْتَنْفَذَ means to free something after trying a lot and desiring it.

Refer to 22:73

____________________________________

SUMMARY

FAKKA : to remove from someone’s captivity

ANKAZA: to free from a difficulty, or to save someone.
