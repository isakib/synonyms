23. CHUPNA    [ to hide yourself ]

Synonyms: GHABA, AFALA, GHARABA, 3AZABA, WAQABA, BATANA, TAWAARAA, KHAFIYA, KANNA.

23.1  GHABA (غَابَ) : this word is common. غَيْب is used for a thing that has vanished. The use of this word is common for a thing which disappears from sight because of some reason or at sometime. Its opposite is حَضَرَ . Although according to Saahib Faka Allaghta, everything that is hidden from the knowledge and awareness of humans, is GHAIB. But it can be imagined by the heart.

____________________________________

Surah An-Naml (The Ants)

27:75

وَمَا مِنْ غَائِبَةٍ فِي السَّمَاء وَالْأَرْضِ إِلَّا فِي كِتَابٍ مُّبِينٍ

And there is nothing hidden in the heaven and the earth, but is in a Clear Book.

_____________________________________

23.2  AFALA (اَفَلَ): this word is used for the disappearing or the setting of heavenly bodies (such as the sun, moon and stars).

_____________________________________

Surah Al-An'am (The Cattle)

6:77

فَلَمَّا رَأَى الْقَمَرَ بَازِغًا قَالَ هَذَا رَبِّي فَلَمَّا أَفَلَ

When he saw the moon rising up, he said: "This is my lord." But when it set.

_____________________________________

23.3  GHARABA (غَرَبَ) : this word is used for the disappearing or the setting of the sun. (like شرق for its coming out, and in طَلَعَ the sun and all planets are included).

_____________________________________

Surah Al-Kahf (The Cave)

18:16

وَتَرَى الشَّمْسَ إِذَا طَلَعَت تَّزَاوَرُ عَن كَهْفِهِمْ ذَاتَ الْيَمِينِ وَإِذَا غَرَبَت تَّقْرِضُهُمْ ذَاتَ الشِّمَالِ

And you might have seen the sun, when it rose, declining to the right from their Cave, and when it set, turning away from them to the left.

_____________________________________

23.4  3AZABA (عَزَبَ) : to vanish from the sight because of going far away. And عازب refers to that man who goes away from his people in search of grass. عَزَّب means to disappear for a long time.

_____________________________________

Surah Yunus (Jonah)

10:61

وَمَا يَعْزُبُ عَن رَّبِّكَ مِن مِّثْقَالِ ذَرَّةٍ

And nothing is hidden from your Lord (so much as) the weight of an atom.

_____________________________________

23.5  WAQABA (وَقَبَ) : means to go into a hole and disappear. For the setting of the sun الشَّمْس وَقَبَت is used. الظلام وقب refers to the spreading of darkness. اَلْوَقْب means a hole made of stone in which water has collected. And وَقَبَ means to enter a hole.

_____________________________________

Surah Al-Falaq (The Daybreak)

113:3

وَمِن شَرِّغَاسِقٍ إِذَا وَقَبَ

And from the evil of the darkening (night) as it comes with its darkness.

_____________________________________

23.6  BATANA (بَطَنَ) : بطن means the stomach, or the internal part of something. As internal parts are completely hidden, بَطَنَ  is used for something that is completely hidden and its opposite is ظَهَرَ.

_____________________________________

Surah Al-A'raf (The Heights)

7:33

قُلْ إِنَّمَا حَرَّمَ رَبِّيَ الْفَوَاحِشَ مَا ظَهَرَ مِنْهَا وَمَا بَطَنَ

Say : the things that my Lord has indeed forbidden are Al-Fawâhish whether committed openly or secretly.

______________________________________

23.7  TAWAARAA (تَوَارى) : ورى means to hide the truth. يتوارى means to hide from people after************* by the truth.

_______________________________________

Surah An-Nahl (The Bees)

16:59

يَتَوَارَى مِنَ الْقَوْمِ مِن سُوءِ مَا بُشِّرَ بِهِ

He hides himself from the people because of the evil of that whereof he has been informed.

_______________________________________

23.8  KHAFIYA (خَفِىَ) : (opposite is بَدَا and عَلَنَ) the hiding of a thing. This word is even more common than غاب. It refers to the hiding of a thing which cannot even be imagined.

_______________________________________

Surah Ale-Imran (The Family of Imran)

3:5

إِنَّ اللّهَ لاَ يَخْفَىَ عَلَيْهِ شَيْءٌ فِي الأَرْضِ وَلاَ فِي السَّمَاء

Truly, nothing is hidden from Allâh, in the earth or in the heaven.

________________________________________

23.9  KANNA (كَنَّ) :  كِنّ means a safe place or a cave in a mountain where a human can seek refuge. Its plural words are اَكِنَّة and اَكْنَان. And  كَنَّ means a thing which is hidden in a safe place which is inaccessible to ordinary people.

________________________________________

Surah As-Saffaat (Those Arranged in Ranks)

37:49

كَأَنَّهُنَّ بَيْضٌ مَّكْنُونٌ

As if they were eggs which were kept in a safe place.

________________________________________

SUMMARY

GHABA: something which is out of the awareness of the human senses.

AFALA: the setting of the heavenly bodies.

GHARABA: the setting of the sun

3AZABA: to disappear due to going far away

WAQABA: to disappear due to climbing into a hole

BATANA: to be completely hidden.

YATWAARAA: to hide from others due to ******* from a truth

KHAFIYA: this word is more common than GHABA

KANNA: to hide in a safe place.
