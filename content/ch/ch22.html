22. CHOWKIDAAR

[ watchman ]

Synonyms: HARASA, RASAD, MU3AQIBAAT

25.1 HARASA (حَرَسَ) : means to take under protection, to take in custody, to guard over. To watch over a criminal. According to Ibn Alfaaris, two things are found in حرس . 1) protection 2) to watch over for a certain period of time. الملك حرس means royal protector, body guard.

___________________________________

Surah Al-Jinn (The Jinn)

72:8

وَأَنَّا لَمَسْنَا السَّمَاء فَوَجَدْنَاهَا مُلِئَتْ حَرَسًا شَدِيدًا وَشُهُبًا

And we have sought to reach the heaven; but found it filled with stern guards and flaming fires.

___________________________________

25.2 RASAD (رَصَد) : means to sit and watch over. To lay in wait. And مرصاد means the place where you lay in wait. And رَاصِد refers to the one who lies in wait.

___________________________________

Surah Al-Jinn (The Jinn)

72:27

إِلَّا مَنِ ارْتَضَى مِن رَّسُولٍ فَإِنَّهُ يَسْلُكُ مِن بَيْنِ يَدَيْهِ وَمِنْ خَلْفِهِ رَصَدًا

Except to a Messenger whom He has chosen, and then He makes a band of watching guards to march before him and behind him.

___________________________________

25.3 MU3AQIB (مُعَقِّبْ) : عَقَّب means to come behind, to walk behind. And مُعَقِّبَات means the one who walks behind. The one in charge, the one who protects, guard, watchman.

___________________________________

Surah Ar-Ra'd (The Thunder)

13:11

لَهُ مُعَقِّبَاتٌ مِّن بَيْنِ يَدَيْهِ وَمِنْ خَلْفِهِ يَحْفَظُونَهُ مِنْ أَمْرِ اللّهِ

For him, there are guards in succession, before and behind him. They guard him by the Command of Allâh.

___________________________________

SUMMARY

HARASA: the guard who protects and watches over someone for a certain period of time.

RASADA: the guard who sits and watches for when his prey will come and he will catch it.

MU3AQIBAAT: the guard or protector who stays with and protects and saves from problems.
