21 JAHAN,JAHAN KAHIYN [Where,Whereever]

SYNONYMS:HAYCU(حَيْثُ) HAYCUMA(حَيْثُمَا) AYNAMA(اَيْنَمَا)

21.1 HAYCU:use for a place unspecified.and the latter part of the sentence explains it.meaning WHERE. ALLAH said

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة

2:149

وَمِنْ حَيْثُ خَرَجْتَ فَوَلِّ وَجْهَكَ شَطْرَ الْمَسْجِدِ الْحَرَامِ ۖ وَإِنَّهُ لَلْحَقُّ مِن رَّبِّكَ ۗ وَمَا اللَّهُ بِغَافِلٍ عَمَّا تَعْمَلُونَ

So from wherever you go out [for prayer, O Muhammad] turn your face toward al- Masjid al-Haram, and indeed, it is the truth from your Lord. And Allah is not unaware of what you do.

21.2 HAYCUMA:meaning 'where ever'.the addition of MA(مَا) is only to give wide context to the meaning.ALLAH said

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة

2:149

وَمِنْ حَيْثُ خَرَجْتَ فَوَلِّ وَجْهَكَ شَطْرَ الْمَسْجِدِ الْحَرَامِ ۖ وَإِنَّهُ لَلْحَقُّ مِن رَّبِّكَ ۗ وَمَا اللَّهُ بِغَافِلٍ عَمَّا تَعْمَلُونَ

So from wherever you go out [for prayer, O Muhammad] turn your face toward al- Masjid al-Haram, and indeed, it is the truth from your Lord. And Allah is not unaware of what you do.

21.3 AYNAMA:AYNA(اَيْن) meaning 'where'- used to ask about some place.and AYNAMA meaning any place. that makes AYNAMA and HAYCUMA becomes having same meaning. yet in AYNAMA there exist meaning of condition.ALLAH said

Surat Al-'Aĥzāb (The Combined Forces) - سورة الأحزاب

33:61

مَّلْعُونِينَ ۖ أَيْنَمَا ثُقِفُوا أُخِذُوا وَقُتِّلُوا تَقْتِيلًا

Accursed wherever they are found, [being] seized and massacred completely.
