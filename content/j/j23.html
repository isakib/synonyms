23 JHUKNA [To Incline/Bow ]

SYNONYMS:JANAHA جَنَحَ MALA(MA-YA-LA)(مَالَ(ميل),i,3ANA(3A-NA-WA/3A-NA-YA)(عَنَا (عني/عنو) SAGHA (SA-GH-WA)صَغَا-صغو) RAKANA رَكَنَ MADA(MA-YA-DA) (مَادَ (ميد) KHASHA3A خَشَعَ ZALLA ذَلَّ,i, 3ALA عَالَ DANA دَنَي,JANAFA جَنَفَ SABA صَبَا

23.1 JANAHA:To incline to any one side.JANAHATI ASSAFIYNAH(جَنَحَتِ السَّفِيْنَة)-ship inclining to one side.and JANAHA(جَنَاح) meaning wing of a bird.side of a human (dual JANAHAYN جَنَاحَيْن)AND JANAHA(جَنَاحَ) inclination towards good.and JUNAHجُنَاح(sin)inclination towards evil.

ALLAH said

Surat Al-'Anfāl (The Spoils of War) - سورة الأنفال

8:61

وَإِن جَنَحُوا لِلسَّلْمِ فَاجْنَحْ لَهَا وَتَوَكَّلْ عَلَى اللَّهِ ۚ إِنَّهُ هُوَ السَّمِيعُ الْعَلِيمُ

And if they incline to peace, then incline to it [also] and rely upon Allah . Indeed, it is He who is the Hearing, the Knowing.

23.2 MALA:ALMAYL(الميل) meaning aptitude.relish.nature's inclination.inclination of nature towards something or some matter.this is a common word.In the QURAN

Surat An-Nisā' (The Women) - سورة النساء

4:129

وَلَن تَسْتَطِيعُوا أَن تَعْدِلُوا بَيْنَ النِّسَاءِ وَلَوْ حَرَصْتُمْ ۖ فَلَا تَمِيلُوا كُلَّ الْمَيْلِ فَتَذَرُوهَا كَالْمُعَلَّقَةِ ۚ وَإِن تُصْلِحُوا وَتَتَّقُوا فَإِنَّ اللَّهَ كَانَ غَفُورًا رَّحِيمًا

And you will never be able to be equal [in feeling] between wives, even if you should strive [to do so]. So do not incline completely [toward one] and leave another hanging. And if you amend [your affairs] and fear Allah - then indeed, Allah is ever Forgiving and Merciful.

23.3 3ANA(عَنَا):YA3NAW(يَعْنَوْا) meaning to be humiliated-to be low.to be obedient and 3ANA YA3NIY(عَنَا يَعْنِيْ) meaning to bear hardship.and 3ANIYA YA3NA(عَنِيَ يَعْنَي) meaning to be tired.so 3ANA YA3NIY(عَنِيَ يَعْنِي) to incline being helpless ,after being humiliated, or tired or bearing hardship.ALLAH said

Surat Ţāhā (Ta-Ha) - سورة طه

20:111

وَعَنَتِ الْوُجُوهُ لِلْحَيِّ الْقَيُّومِ ۖ وَقَدْ خَابَ مَنْ حَمَلَ ظُلْمًا

And [all] faces will be humbled before the Ever-Living, the Sustainer of existence. And he will have failed who carries injustice.

23.4 SAGHA(صَغَا):YASGHAW(يَصْغَوْا) bow down completely.SAGHATI ANNUJUWMU WA ASSHAMSU(صَغَتِ النُّجُوْمُ و الشَّمْسُ ) meaning sun or stars inclining towards setting.and SAGHAYTU ALINA-A(صَغَيْتُ الْاِنَاءِ)-I inclined the vessel so that its contents comes out.and ASGHA ILAYHI(اَصْغَي اِلَيْهِ) meaning inclining ears towards some side. In the QURAN

Surat At-Taĥrīm (The Prohibtiion) - سورة التحريم

66:4

إِن تَتُوبَا إِلَى اللَّهِ فَقَدْ صَغَتْ قُلُوبُكُمَا ۖ وَإِن تَظَاهَرَا عَلَيْهِ فَإِنَّ اللَّهَ هُوَ مَوْلَاهُ وَجِبْرِيلُ وَصَالِحُ الْمُؤْمِنِينَ ۖ وَالْمَلَائِكَةُ بَعْدَ ذَٰلِكَ ظَهِيرٌ

If you two [wives] repent to Allah , [it is best], for your hearts have deviated. But if you cooperate against him - then indeed Allah is his protector, and Gabriel and the righteous of the believers and the angels, moreover, are [his] assistants.

23.5 RAKANA:RUKN(رُكن) meaning power,domination,castle,strong side.and RAKANA meaning to trust someone and to incline towards that side from which power can be obtained.ALLAH said

Surat Al-'Isrā' (The Night Journey) - سورة الإسراء

17:74

وَلَوْلَا أَن ثَبَّتْنَاكَ لَقَدْ كِدتَّ تَرْكَنُ إِلَيْهِمْ شَيْئًا قَلِيلًا

And if We had not strengthened you, you would have almost inclined to them a little.

23.6 MADA:meaning start of movement in something and then it inclined.slight movement and then inclined to any side.to move, to rock,to shiver.MADAT ALGHASAN(مادت الغصن) meaning branch bow down. In the QURAN.

Surat Al-'Anbyā' (The Prophets) - سورة الأنبياء

21:31

وَجَعَلْنَا فِي الْأَرْضِ رَوَاسِيَ أَن تَمِيدَ بِهِمْ وَجَعَلْنَا فِيهَا فِجَاجًا سُبُلًا لَّعَلَّهُمْ يَهْتَدُونَ

And We placed within the earth firmly set mountains, lest it should shift with them, and We made therein [mountain] passes [as] roads that they might be guided.

23.7 KHASHA3A:to bend of humbleness.such a fear whose effect manifest on limbs.this is normally for lowering of eyes,face,voice,and heart.and it includes fear as well.for e.g

a) for voice:

20:108

يَوْمَئِذٍ يَتَّبِعُونَ الدَّاعِيَ لَا عِوَجَ لَهُ ۖ وَخَشَعَتِ الْأَصْوَاتُ لِلرَّحْمَٰنِ فَلَا تَسْمَعُ إِلَّا هَمْسًا

That Day, everyone will follow [the call of] the Caller [with] no deviation therefrom, and [all] voices will be stilled before the Most Merciful, so you will not hear except a whisper [of footsteps].

b)for eyes:

Surat Al-Ma`ārij (The Ascending Stairways) - سورة المعارج

70:44

خَاشِعَةً أَبْصَارُهُمْ تَرْهَقُهُمْ ذِلَّةٌ ۚ ذَٰلِكَ الْيَوْمُ الَّذِي كَانُوا يُوعَدُونَ

Their eyes humbled, humiliation will cover them. That is the Day which they had been promised.

23.8 ZALLA:meaning incline owing to force and wrath and pressure.and (its anonym is 3AZZA عَزَّ).to be insulted-neutralizing/settling of the hardness and sharpness of one;s nature by itself.ALLAH said

Surat Al-'Insān (The Man) - سورة الانسان

76:14

وَدَانِيَةً عَلَيْهِمْ ظِلَالُهَا وَذُلِّلَتْ قُطُوفُهَا تَذْلِيلًا

And near above them are its shades, and its [fruit] to be picked will be lowered in compliance.

23.9 3ALA:(3A-WA-LA)(عول) meaning to oppress-to get off the straight path.AL3AWL(اَلْعَوْل) use for all such thing that burdens human and cause him to be overburdened by it.it refers to such inclination which usurps some one's right.ALLAH said

Surat An-Nisā' (The Women) - سورة النساء

4:3

وَإِنْ خِفْتُمْ أَلَّا تُقْسِطُوا فِي الْيَتَامَىٰ فَانكِحُوا مَا طَابَ لَكُم مِّنَ النِّسَاءِ مَثْنَىٰ وَثُلَاثَ وَرُبَاعَ ۖ فَإِنْ خِفْتُمْ أَلَّا تَعْدِلُوا فَوَاحِدَةً أَوْ مَا مَلَكَتْ أَيْمَانُكُمْ ۚ ذَٰلِكَ أَدْنَىٰ أَلَّا تَعُولُوا

And if you fear that you will not deal justly with the orphan girls, then marry those that please you of [other] women, two or three or four. But if you fear that you will not be just, then [marry only] one or those your right hand possesses. That is more suitable that you may not incline [to injustice].

23.10 DANA:meaning to be near.to be close. and if it refers to some height then it means to close by incline.ALLAH said

Surat Ar-Raĥmān (The Beneficent) - سورة الرحمن

55:54

مُتَّكِئِينَ عَلَىٰ فُرُشٍ بَطَائِنُهَا مِنْ إِسْتَبْرَقٍ ۚ وَجَنَى الْجَنَّتَيْنِ دَانٍ

[They are] reclining on beds whose linings are of silk brocade, and the fruit of the two gardens is hanging low.

23.11 JANAFA:meaning to take side.to support.incline towards one of the party.ALLAH said

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة

2:182

فَمَنْ خَافَ مِن مُّوصٍ جَنَفًا أَوْ إِثْمًا فَأَصْلَحَ بَيْنَهُمْ فَلَا إِثْمَ عَلَيْهِ ۚ إِنَّ اللَّهَ غَفُورٌ رَّحِيمٌ

But if one fears from the bequeather [some] error or sin and corrects that which is between them, there is no sin upon him. Indeed, Allah is Forgiving and Merciful.

23.12 SABA:(SA-BA-WA) (صبو) SABIYUN( صَبِيٌ ) meaning kid,small boy(plural SIBYAN صِبْيَان)and SABA صَبَا meaning to incline towards childish behaviour.and TASABIYY(تَصَبِّي) meaning attrat towards play. so SABA-A صَبَاءَ is such an inclination which is via childhood.Joseph (PBUH) said

Surat Yūsuf (Joseph) - سورة يوسف

12:33

قَالَ رَبِّ السِّجْنُ أَحَبُّ إِلَيَّ مِمَّا يَدْعُونَنِي إِلَيْهِ ۖ وَإِلَّا تَصْرِفْ عَنِّي كَيْدَهُنَّ أَصْبُ إِلَيْهِنَّ وَأَكُن مِّنَ الْجَاهِلِينَ

He said, "My Lord, prison is more to my liking than that to which they invite me. And if You do not avert from me their plan, I might incline toward them and [thus] be of the ignorant."

SUMMARY:

23.1 MALA:inclination of nature and relish, is common usage

23.2 JANAHA:to incline towards good

23.3 3ANA:to bow being knackered owing to hardship

23.4 SAGHA:complete or excess inclination

23.5 RAKANA:to incline towards something for obtaining power or refuge

23.6 MADA:to low owing to movement or burden,to rock

23.7 KHASHA3A:lower of fear and humbleness which manifest in limbs evidently

23.8 ZALLA:to incline of some pressure.

23.9 3ALA:such an inclination which usurps other's right

23.10 DANA:incline to near

23.11 JANAFA:incline owing to taking side

23.12 SABA:to incline to something unethical/unpleasant owing to childhood
