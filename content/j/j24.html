24 JHUKANA [To Make Incline]

SYNONYMS:KHAFADAخَفَضَ GHADDAغَضَّ RAKA3Aرَكَعَ NAKASAنَكَسَ

24.1 KHAFADA:meaning to make incline-to make low- and its antonym is RAFA3A رافع meaning to make high,to raise.KHAFADA is used both as aparent and abstract.KHAFIDخافض and RAFA3 ,رَفَعَ both are also names and attributes of ALLAH. ALLAH said

Surat Al-'Isrā' (The Night Journey) - سورة الإسراء

17:24

وَاخْفِضْ لَهُمَا جَنَاحَ الذُّلِّ مِنَ الرَّحْمَةِ وَقُل رَّبِّ ارْحَمْهُمَا كَمَا رَبَّيَانِي صَغِيرًا

And lower to them the wing of humility out of mercy and say, "My Lord, have mercy upon them as they brought me up [when I was] small."

24.2 GHADDA:peculiar to lowering sight or voice.ALLAH said

Surat An-Nūr (The Light) - سورة النور

24:30

قُل لِّلْمُؤْمِنِينَ يَغُضُّوا مِنْ أَبْصَارِهِمْ وَيَحْفَظُوا فُرُوجَهُمْ ۚ ذَٰلِكَ أَزْكَىٰ لَهُمْ ۗ إِنَّ اللَّهَ خَبِيرٌ بِمَا يَصْنَعُونَ

Tell the believing men to reduce [some] of their vision and guard their private parts. That is purer for them. Indeed, Allah is Acquainted with what they do.

24.3 RAKA3A:meaning inclining body,to lower head,to curve back.to offer SALAH metaphorically.ALLAH said

Surat Al-Mursalāt (The Emissaries) - سورة المرسلات

77:48

وَإِذَا قِيلَ لَهُمُ ارْكَعُوا لَا يَرْكَعُونَ

And when it is said to them, "Bow [in prayer]," they do not bow.

24.4 NAKASA:meaning to upside down.and NAKASA RASAHU(نَكَسَ رَأَسَهُ) means to lower head.to give up.to lower head of shame and regret.and TANKKASA AL3ALAMA تَنَكَّسَ الْعَلَمَmeaning lowering of flag.and INTAKASAاِنْتَكَسَ meaning to fall on head.and NAKASA 3ALA RASIHI(نَكَسَ عَلَي رَاْسِهِ) is an idiom meaning to lower head of regret being outclassed.ALLAH said.

Surat Al-'Anbyā' (The Prophets) - سورة الأنبياء

21:65

ثُمَّ نُكِسُوا عَلَىٰ رُءُوسِهِمْ لَقَدْ عَلِمْتَ مَا هَٰؤُلَاءِ يَنطِقُونَ

Then they reversed themselves, [saying], "You have already known that these do not speak!"

SUMMARY:

24.1 KHAFADA:common word, for to make low to make incline

24.2 GHADDA:for sight and voice

24.3 RAKA3A:to lower body and to curve back

24.4 NAKASA:to low head of regret by being confuted
