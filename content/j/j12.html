12 JAMA3AT(JANWARWN KI) [Group (of Animals)]

SYNONYMS:RAKB(رَكْب) ALKHAYL(الْخَيْل) GHANAM(غَنَم) AABABIYL(اَبَابِيْل)

12.1 RAKB:meaning herd of camels and also caravan and cameleer it applies to all of them.ALLAH said

Surat Al-'Anfāl (The Spoils of War) - سورة الأنفال

8:42

إِذْ أَنتُم بِالْعُدْوَةِ الدُّنْيَا وَهُم بِالْعُدْوَةِ الْقُصْوَىٰ وَالرَّكْبُ أَسْفَلَ مِنكُمْ ۚ وَلَوْ تَوَاعَدتُّمْ لَاخْتَلَفْتُمْ فِي الْمِيعَادِ ۙ وَلَٰكِن لِّيَقْضِيَ اللَّهُ أَمْرًا كَانَ مَفْعُولًا لِّيَهْلِكَ مَنْ هَلَكَ عَن بَيِّنَةٍ وَيَحْيَىٰ مَنْ حَيَّ عَن بَيِّنَةٍ ۗ وَإِنَّ اللَّهَ لَسَمِيعٌ عَلِيمٌ

[Remember] when you were on the near side of the valley, and they were on the farther side, and the caravan was lower [in position] than you. If you had made an appointment [to meet], you would have missed the appointment. But [it was] so that Allah might accomplish a matter already destined - that those who perished [through disbelief] would perish upon evidence and those who lived [in faith] would live upon evidence; and indeed, Allah is Hearing and Knowing.

12.2 ALKHAYL:meaning herd of horses.and like RAKB it also applies to Horse rider meaning FARIS(فارس) as well.and also the collection of both horse and its rider.ALLAH said

Surat Al-'Anfāl (The Spoils of War) - سورة الأنفال

8:60

وَأَعِدُّوا لَهُم مَّا اسْتَطَعْتُم مِّن قُوَّةٍ وَمِن رِّبَاطِ الْخَيْلِ تُرْهِبُونَ بِهِ عَدُوَّ اللَّهِ وَعَدُوَّكُمْ وَآخَرِينَ مِن دُونِهِمْ لَا تَعْلَمُونَهُمُ اللَّهُ يَعْلَمُهُمْ ۚ وَمَا تُنفِقُوا مِن شَيْءٍ فِي سَبِيلِ اللَّهِ يُوَفَّ إِلَيْكُمْ وَأَنتُمْ لَا تُظْلَمُونَ

And prepare against them whatever you are able of power and of steeds of war by which you may terrify the enemy of Allah and your enemy and others besides them whom you do not know [but] whom Allah knows. And whatever you spend in the cause of Allah will be fully repaid to you, and you will not be wronged.

12.3 GHANAM:meaning flock of goats.In the QURAN

Surat Al-'Anbyā' (The Prophets) - سورة الأنبياء

21:78

وَدَاوُودَ وَسُلَيْمَانَ إِذْ يَحْكُمَانِ فِي الْحَرْثِ إِذْ نَفَشَتْ فِيهِ غَنَمُ الْقَوْمِ وَكُنَّا لِحُكْمِهِمْ شَاهِدِينَ

And [mention] David and Solomon, when they judged concerning the field - when the sheep of a people overran it [at night], and We were witness to their judgement.

12.4 AABABIYL:meaning flock of birds- and as per some AABABIYL applies to group of birds,camels and horses.yes if its TAYRAN ABABIYL(طَيْراً اَبَابِيْل) then it peculiarly means cluster of birds.

flock of birds.caboodle.ALLAH said

Surat Al-Fīl (The Elephant) - سورة الفيل

105:3

وَأَرْسَلَ عَلَيْهِمْ طَيْرًا أَبَابِيلَ

And He sent against them birds in flocks,

Surat Al-Fīl (The Elephant) - سورة الفيل

105:4

تَرْمِيهِم بِحِجَارَةٍ مِّن سِجِّيلٍ

Striking them with stones of hard clay,
