1 – Zaida [excess,extra]

Synonyms: AAFU, NAFILAH, DI’IF

1.1 AAFU (عَفْو): AFA has two important aspects: 1. To leave something, 2. To exceed. AFA-ASH-SHA’R (عَفََاالشَّعْر) means to leave hair and let it grow. The Prophet ﷺ said “قَصُّواالشَّوَارِبَ واعفوالحٰی ” translation: Clip then moustache and grow the beard. AFA-ASH-SHA (عَفَاالشَّیْ) means to exceed. AAFU (عَفْو) means excess or a superior thing. AAFU-MINAL-MAAL (عَفْومِنَ الْمَال) means wealth that is in excess and is easily dispensable. In the Quran

Surah Al-Baqarah (The cow): سورۃ البَقَرَۃ 2:219

وَيَسۡـَٔلُونَكَ مَاذَا يُنفِقُونَ قُلِ ٱلۡعَفۡوَ*ۗ

And they ask you about what they should give: say, “The surplus.” 

1.2 NAFILAH (نَافِلَۃ): NAFALA(نَفَلَ) means to donate. ANFAAL (اَنْفَالْ) means giving away spoils of war. NAFL (نَفْل) refers to anything that is in excess to requirement or dues. Similarly nawafil worship is what is intentionally performed apart from the fard or sunnah. It may be salah, charity, fasting or pilgrimage. In the Quran

Surah Al-Israa (The Night Journey): سورۃ الٕاسرَاء 17-79

وَمِنَ ٱلَّيۡلِ فَتَهَجَّدۡ بِهِۦ نَافِلَةً۬ لَّكَ

And keep vigil with it during parts of the night, as an extra prayer.

Another reference in Quran

Surah Al-Anbiya (the prophets): سورۃ الانَبیاء 21:72

وَوَهَبۡنَا لَهُ ۥۤ إِسۡحَـٰقَ وَيَعۡقُوبَ نَافِلَةً۬*ۖ

And We granted him Isaac and Jacob as a gift;

1.3 DI’IF (**ضِعْف): double the original amount. In the Quran

Surah Al-Israa (The Night Journey): سورۃ الٕاسرَاء 17-75

إِذً۬ا لَّأَذَقۡنَـٰكَ ضِعۡفَ ٱلۡحَيَوٰةِ وَضِعۡفَ ٱلۡمَمَاتِ

Then We would have made you taste double in life, and double at death;

The double of DI’IF(ضِعْف) is DI’IFAIN(ضِعْفِیْن) which means to increase. In the Quran

Surah Al-Baqarah (The cow): سورۃ البَقَرَۃ 2:265

كَمَثَلِ جَنَّةِۭ بِرَبۡوَةٍ أَصَابَهَا وَابِلٌ۬ فَـَٔاتَتۡ أُڪُلَهَا ضِعۡفَيۡنِ

Example is that of a garden on a hillside. If heavy rain falls on it, its produce is doubled

DA’AFA (ضَاعَفَ) means to exceed manifold. In the Quran

Surah Al-Baqarah (The cow): سورۃ البَقَرَۃ 2:261

وَٱللَّهُ يُضَـٰعِفُ لِمَن يَشَآءُ*ۗ

God multiplies for whom He wills

Summary:

1.1 AAFU: To exceed.

1.2 NAFILAH: anything that is in excess to requirement or dues.

1.3 DI’IF: double the original amount.
