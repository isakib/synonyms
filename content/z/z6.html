6 – To Level the Earth, To Destroy

 

The words دَكَّ and دَمدَمَ have been used in the holy Quran

 

6.1 DAKKA (دَكَّ): this term includes two things – to crush and to level, make plane and smooth.  So this term means to crush something into small bits so that it is leveled with the earth.

 

Surat Al-'A`rāf (The Heights) - الأعراف سورة

7: 143                                                                                  

فَلَمَّا تَجَلَّىٰ رَبُّهُ ۥ لِلۡجَبَلِ جَعَلَهُ ۥ دَڪًّ۬ا (﻿١٤٣﻿)

But when his Lord appeared to the mountain, He rendered it level (143)

 

And in another place the Almighty states:

 

Surat Al-Fajr (The Dawn) - لفجرا سورة

 89:21

 كَلَّآ إِذَا دُكَّتِ ٱلۡأَرۡضُ دَكًّ۬ا دَكًّ۬ا (﻿٢١﻿)

No! When the earth has been leveled - pounded and crushed (21)

 

6.2 DAMDAMA (دَمدَمَ): the phrase الشّئ دَمدَمُ refers to making something stick to the ground. The phrase عَلَيهِم اللّٰهُ دَمدَمَ means Allah destroyed them.  A dessert is also called a دَيمُومَة.

 

Surat Ash-Shams (The Sun) - الشمس سورة

91: 14

 فَدَمۡدَمَ عَلَيۡهِمۡ رَبُّهُم بِذَنۢبِهِمۡ فَسَوَّٮٰهَا (﻿١٤﻿)

So their Lord brought down upon them destruction for their sin and made it equal [upon all of them] (14)

 

Summary:

1.      دَكَّ: to demolish some lifeless object and level the earth

2.      دَمدَمَ: to punish through destruction and complete annihilation
