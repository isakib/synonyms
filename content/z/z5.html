5 ZAMEEN [earth,land]

ARAD (اَرْض) is commonly used to refer to the (physical) matter we live on and whose opposite is SAMAA’ (سماء) which means sky. The word is also used to mention descend or fall and in this manner SAMAA’ (سَمَاء) can be used to ascend or rise. In the Quran

Surah Al-Araf (The heights): سورۃ الأعرَاف 7:176

وَلَـٰكِنَّهُ ۥۤ أَخۡلَدَ إِلَى ٱلۡأَرۡضِ

but he clung to the ground,
