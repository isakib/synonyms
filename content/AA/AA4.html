4 UTAARNA UTARNA [ To Send Down , Descend]

UTARNA

4.1Synonyms: NAZALA,TANAZZALA,HALLA,HABATA

4.1.1 NAZALA(نَزَلَ): descend is well known word.anything coming down from height.and this word is of common usage. antonym of NAZOOL نزولis S3WD(صعود) as well as 3ROOJ(عروج). In the Holy Quran

Surat Al-Ĥadīd (The Iron) - سورة الحديد
57:4
هُوَ الَّذِي خَلَقَ السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضَ فِي سِتَّةِ أَيَّامٍ ثُمَّ اسْتَوَىٰ عَلَى الْعَرْشِ ۚ يَعْلَمُ مَا يَلِجُ فِي الْأَرْضِ وَمَا يَخْرُجُ مِنْهَا وَمَا يَنزِلُ مِنَ السَّمَاءِ وَمَا يَعْرُجُ فِيهَا ۖ وَهُوَ مَعَكُمْ أَيْنَ مَا كُنتُمْ ۚ وَاللَّهُ بِمَا تَعْمَلُونَ بَصِيرٌ
It is He who created the heavens and earth in six days and then established Himself above the Throne. He knows what penetrates into the earth and what emerges from it and what descends from the heaven and what ascends therein; and He is with you wherever you are. And Allah , of what you do, is Seeing.

and TANAZZALA BIHI (تَنَزَّلَ بِهِ) has same meaning as NAZALA BIHI(نَزَلَ بِهِ). and for angels and divine commandments descend both these words are used. ALLAH said

Surat Ash-Shu`arā' (The Poets) - سورة الشعراء
26:193
نَزَلَ بِهِ الرُّوحُ الْأَمِينُ
The Trustworthy Spirit has brought it down

Surat Al-Qadr (The Power) - سورة القدر
97:4
تَنَزَّلُ الْمَلَائِكَةُ وَالرُّوحُ فِيهَا بِإِذْنِ رَبِّهِم مِّن كُلِّ أَمْرٍ
The angels and the Spirit descend therein by permission of their Lord for every matter.

but for satanic revelations only TANAZZALA BIHI is used . ALLAH said

Surat Ash-Shu`arā' (The Poets) - سورة الشعراء
26:221
هَلْ أُنَبِّئُكُمْ عَلَىٰ مَن تَنَزَّلُ الشَّيَاطِينُ
Shall I inform you upon whom the devils descend?
Surat Ash-Shu`arā' (The Poets) - سورة الشعراء
26:222
تَنَزَّلُ عَلَىٰ كُلِّ أَفَّاكٍ أَثِيمٍ
They descend upon every sinful liar.


4.1.2 HALLA(حَلَّ): basic meaning is to untie knot. and its antonym is 3AQADA عَقَدَ meaning tying knot. It refers to travellers whose state of packing and un packing luggage is such that traveller pack it up when travelling and when subside then unpack it. therefore word HALLA is being used for subsiding and descend. just like traveller when stopover unpack his/her luggage.
likewise word HALLA is also used for result of anything . it is said MIN JARRAB ALMUJARRAB HALLAT BIHI ANNADAMAH(من جَرّب المُجَرَّب حَلَّت بِهِ النّدامة) meaning 'whoever put to test the one who has already been tested got remorse. In the Quran

Surat Hūd (Hud) - سورة هود
11:39
فَسَوْفَ تَعْلَمُونَ مَن يَأْتِيهِ عَذَابٌ يُخْزِيهِ وَيَحِلُّ عَلَيْهِ عَذَابٌ مُّقِيمٌ
And you are going to know who will get a punishment that will disgrace him [on earth] and upon whom will descend an enduring punishment [in the Hereafter]."

4.1.3 HABATA(هَبَطَ): something descending or falling or emerging ,involuntarily or forcibly like a stone falling of height. to emerge out involuntarily. to lower than one's status , to go lower from height. all of this is included in the meaning . now see some examples

a) to dismount from a ride

Surat Hūd (Hud) - سورة هود
11:48
قِيلَ يَا نُوحُ اهْبِطْ بِسَلَامٍ مِّنَّا وَبَرَكَاتٍ عَلَيْكَ وَعَلَىٰ أُمَمٍ مِّمَّن مَّعَكَ ۚ وَأُمَمٌ سَنُمَتِّعُهُمْ ثُمَّ يَمَسُّهُم مِّنَّا عَذَابٌ أَلِيمٌ
It was said, "O Noah, disembark in security from Us and blessings upon you and upon nations [descending] from those with you. But other nations [of them] We will grant enjoyment; then there will touch them from Us a painful punishment."

b) something descending invountarily or forcibly

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة
2:74
ثُمَّ قَسَتْ قُلُوبُكُم مِّن بَعْدِ ذَٰلِكَ فَهِيَ كَالْحِجَارَةِ أَوْ أَشَدُّ قَسْوَةً ۚ وَإِنَّ مِنَ الْحِجَارَةِ لَمَا يَتَفَجَّرُ مِنْهُ الْأَنْهَارُ ۚ وَإِنَّ مِنْهَا لَمَا يَشَّقَّقُ فَيَخْرُجُ مِنْهُ الْمَاءُ ۚ وَإِنَّ مِنْهَا لَمَا يَهْبِطُ مِنْ خَشْيَةِ اللَّهِ ۗ وَمَا اللَّهُ بِغَافِلٍ عَمَّا تَعْمَلُونَ
Then your hearts became hardened after that, being like stones or even harder. For indeed, there are stones from which rivers burst forth, and there are some of them that split open and water comes out, and there are some of them that fall down for fear of Allah . And Allah is not unaware of what you do.

c) to emerger by lowering from one's status

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة
2:36
فَأَزَلَّهُمَا الشَّيْطَانُ عَنْهَا فَأَخْرَجَهُمَا مِمَّا كَانَا فِيهِ ۖ وَقُلْنَا اهْبِطُوا بَعْضُكُمْ لِبَعْضٍ عَدُوٌّ ۖ وَلَكُمْ فِي الْأَرْضِ مُسْتَقَرٌّ وَمَتَاعٌ إِلَىٰ حِينٍ
But Satan caused them to slip out of it and removed them from that [condition] in which they had been. And We said, "Go down, [all of you], as enemies to one another, and you will have upon the earth a place of settlement and provision for a time."

4.2Synonyms: ANZAL (from NAZAL) and NAZZALA, AHALLA (from HALLA) and also WADA3 and KHALA3

4.2.1 ANZALA(انزَلَ) and NAZZALA(نَزَّلَ): some scholars thinks that word ANZALA is use to send downs something from height at once. as ALLAH said

Surat Al-Qadr (The Power) - سورة القدر
97:1
إِنَّا أَنزَلْنَاهُ فِي لَيْلَةِ الْقَدْرِ
Indeed, We sent the Qur'an down during the Night of Decree.

and it's obvious that Quran was not sent down at one . it's explaination is that Quran was sent down on Heaven of this word at once in that night. and then later on as per the occassion and need was sent down via revealation. second explaination is that (Fateh Muhammad Saheb) translated this ayah as " we have revealed this (Quran) in Night of QADR (start it) and TANZEEL means to send down something gradually or as per the need as ;

Surat Al-Furqān (The Criterian) - سورة الفرقان
25:1
تَبَارَكَ الَّذِي نَزَّلَ الْفُرْقَانَ عَلَىٰ عَبْدِهِ لِيَكُونَ لِلْعَالَمِينَ نَذِيرًا
Blessed is He who sent down the Criterion upon His Servant that he may be to the worlds a warner -

so Imam Raghib also shares this view point. he said "The literal difference between the two is that TANZEEL means some thing send down once and then after and differently. and ANZAAL is common word tat referes to something send down at once completely".
so as per Imam Raghib ,TANZEEL refers to gradual and ANZAAL refers to gradual and at once both. send down. but Quran doesn't confirm that for e.g.

Surat Al-Furqān (The Criterian) - سورة الفرقان
25:32
وَقَالَ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا لَوْلَا نُزِّلَ عَلَيْهِ الْقُرْآنُ جُمْلَةً وَاحِدَةً ۚ كَذَٰلِكَ لِنُثَبِّتَ بِهِ فُؤَادَكَ ۖ وَرَتَّلْنَاهُ تَرْتِيلًا
And those who disbelieve say, "Why was the Qur'an not revealed to him all at once?" Thus [it is] that We may strengthen thereby your heart. And We have spaced it distinctly.

here for TANZEEL is used to mean "at once"
and in reality these two words are so near in meaning that one can be informally used in place of other. only as per Limitation of words and explaination of sentence any of them is used by Quran.

4.2.2 AAHALLA(اَحَلَّ): to send down some one from one place to another. this results because of some deed of the one descending. and it can be for both good and bad. for e.g.

a) For Good

Surat Fāţir (Originator) - سورة فاطر
35:34
وَقَالُوا الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ الَّذِي أَذْهَبَ عَنَّا الْحَزَنَ ۖ إِنَّ رَبَّنَا لَغَفُورٌ شَكُورٌ
And they will say, "Praise to Allah , who has removed from us [all] sorrow. Indeed, our Lord is Forgiving and Appreciative -
Surat Fāţir (Originator) - سورة فاطر
35:35
الَّذِي أَحَلَّنَا دَارَ الْمُقَامَةِ مِن فَضْلِهِ لَا يَمَسُّنَا فِيهَا نَصَبٌ وَلَا يَمَسُّنَا فِيهَا لُغُوبٌ
He who has settled us in the home of duration out of His bounty. There touches us not in it any fatigue, and there touches us not in it weariness [of mind]."

b) For Bad

Surat 'Ibrāhīm (Abrahim) - سورة ابراهيم
14:28
أَلَمْ تَرَ إِلَى الَّذِينَ بَدَّلُوا نِعْمَتَ اللَّهِ كُفْرًا وَأَحَلُّوا قَوْمَهُمْ دَارَ الْبَوَارِ
Have you not considered those who exchanged the favor of Allah for disbelief and settled their people [in] the home of ruin?

Thus does Allah make clear to you the verses; and Allah is Knowing and Wise.
