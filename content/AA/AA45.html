45 AURHNA [To Cover]
Synonyms:ISTAGHSHA(GH-SH-YA) ,IDDACARA(DA-CA-RA) and IZZAMMALA(ZA-MA-LA)

45.1 ISTAGHSHA(استغشَي):GHASHY(غشي) meaning one thing to cover up another thing. and this word is used to cover from over the top or to put curtain.

Surat Hūd (Hud) - سورة هود
11:5
أَلَا إِنَّهُمْ يَثْنُونَ صُدُورَهُمْ لِيَسْتَخْفُوا مِنْهُ ۚ أَلَا حِينَ يَسْتَغْشُونَ ثِيَابَهُمْ يَعْلَمُ مَا يُسِرُّونَ وَمَا يُعْلِنُونَ ۚ إِنَّهُ عَلِيمٌ بِذَاتِ الصُّدُورِ
Unquestionably, they the disbelievers turn away their breasts to hide themselves from Him. Unquestionably, [even] when they cover themselves in their clothing, Allah knows what they conceal and what they declare. Indeed, He is Knowing of that within the breasts.

and this word is used the same way for apparent as well as abstract meaning .In the Quran

Surat Nūĥ (Noah) - سورة نوح
71:7
وَإِنِّي كُلَّمَا دَعَوْتُهُمْ لِتَغْفِرَ لَهُمْ جَعَلُوا أَصَابِعَهُمْ فِي آذَانِهِمْ وَاسْتَغْشَوْا ثِيَابَهُمْ وَأَصَرُّوا وَاسْتَكْبَرُوا اسْتِكْبَارًا
And indeed, every time I invited them that You may forgive them, they put their fingers in their ears, covered themselves with their garments, persisted, and were arrogant with [great] arrogance.

45.2 IDDACARA(اِدَّثَرَ):ADDACAR(الدْثار),is that warm cloth or blanket which is normally cover over dress. or the one going to sleep,cover it to sleep. and every cloth that is touched with body is SH3AR(شعَار) and any cloth that touch SH3AR is DACAR(دَثََار). and IDDACARA means cloth or blanket cover over the normal dress. and DACUR ALQALB(
دثور القلب) meaning heart covered with someone's rememberance. and DACIR(داثر) meaning one who is heedless. so IDDACARA means to sleep heedleesly by covering with cloth. ALLAH said

Surat Al-Muddaththir (The Cloaked One) - سورة المدثر
74:1
يَا أَيُّهَا الْمُدَّثِّرُ
O you who covers himself [with a garment],
74:2
قُمْ فَأَنذِرْ
Arise and warn

45.3 IZZAMMAL(اِزَّمَّل):ZAMMAL(زَمَّل) meaning to wrap up in cloth, to conceal oneself in cloth. and MUZZAMMAL( مُزَّمَّل) meaning the one wrapped up in cloth. and IZMAL(اِزْمَلْ) and ZUMYL(زُمَيْل) meaning weak,coward,fearful. and ZUMMAL(زُمَّل) meaning weak,terrified, and resentful. so MUZAMMIL is said to the one, cloth coverer who cover oneself with cloth and lay down owing to some weakness,fear ,or resentfulness. ALLAH said

Surat Al-Muzzammil (The Enshrouded One) - سورة المزمل
73:1
يَا أَيُّهَا الْمُزَّمِّلُ
O you who wraps himself [in clothing],
73:2
قُمِ اللَّيْلَ إِلَّا قَلِيلًا
Arise [to pray] the night, except for a little -

Summary:
45.1 ISTAGHSHA to cover up or wrap up around one self ,any cloth in any state .common usage
45.2 IDDACAR to sleep heedlessly by covering with cloth, or for the intention of deep sleep wrap up cloth
45.3 IZZAMML to cover cloth owing to some fear,weakness or resentfulness
