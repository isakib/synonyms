12 ACCHA,KHWB,BEHTER [GOOD,NICE,BETTER]

Synonyms: NI3MA,KHAIR,HASUNA,MUSLA,JAMEYL

12.1 NI3MA(نِعمَ): word of admiration which is used for all types of applause which means Bravo, Excellent. It's said on something when pleased. as per Ibn e Faris its real is Join and Inclination. meaning something when seen caused inclination towards it and to be favorable then NI3MA is said . its antonym is BI-SA() which word of protest and used for agitation.In the Quran

Surat An-Naĥl (The Bee) - سورة النحل
16:30
وَقِيلَ لِلَّذِينَ اتَّقَوْا مَاذَا أَنزَلَ رَبُّكُمْ ۚ قَالُوا خَيْرًا ۗ لِّلَّذِينَ أَحْسَنُوا فِي هَٰذِهِ الدُّنْيَا حَسَنَةٌ ۚ وَلَدَارُ الْآخِرَةِ خَيْرٌ ۚ وَلَنِعْمَ دَارُ الْمُتَّقِينَ
And it will be said to those who feared Allah , "What did your Lord send down?" They will say, "[That which is] good." For those who do good in this world is good; and the home of the Hereafter is better. And how excellent is the home of the righteous -


12.2 KHAYR(خَيْر): Every thing that is everyone's favourite for e.g justic,blessing,wisdom,and wealth. It's antonym is SHAR(شر). In the Quran

Surat Al-`Ādiyāt (The Courser) - سورة العاديات
100:8
وَإِنَّهُ لِحُبِّ الْخَيْرِ لَشَدِيدٌ
And indeed he is, in love of wealth, intense.

meaning KHAYR is every good discourse or deed and its plurl is KHAYRAT(خيرات) .ALLAH said

Surat 'Āli `Imrān (Family of Imran) - سورة آل عمران
3:114
يُؤْمِنُونَ بِاللَّهِ وَالْيَوْمِ الْآخِرِ وَيَأْمُرُونَ بِالْمَعْرُوفِ وَيَنْهَوْنَ عَنِ الْمُنكَرِ وَيُسَارِعُونَ فِي الْخَيْرَاتِ وَأُولَٰئِكَ مِنَ الصَّالِحِينَ
They believe in Allah and the Last Day, and they enjoin what is right and forbid what is wrong and hasten to good deeds. And those are among the righteous.

KHAYR is not only used for good discourse or good deed but also for good person and things as well. and for Faith ias well . Its plural in that case is AKHYAR(اخيار). for e.g.

Surat Şād (The Letter "Saad") - سورة ص
38:47
وَإِنَّهُمْ عِندَنَا لَمِنَ الْمُصْطَفَيْنَ الْأَخْيَارِ
And indeed they are, to Us, among the chosen and outstanding.

So KHAYR is used for good discourse or deed or person and NI3MA when happy by looking at some good thing .the ayah mentioned below clarify this

12.3 HASUNA(حَسُنَ): meaning relishing and pleasing thing. and its antonym is SA-A(سَاءَ). Then this word is used for visible beauty and for the improved facial beauty.as said

Surat Ar-Raĥmān (The Beneficent) - سورة الرحمن
55:70
فِيهِنَّ خَيْرَاتٌ حِسَانٌ
In them are good and beautiful women -

and in that case its antonym is QABUHA( قَبُحَ) . also this word is used to exhibit the fine morality in literal sense as said

Surat An-Nisā' (The Women) - سورة النساء
4:69
وَمَن يُطِعِ اللَّهَ وَالرَّسُولَ فَأُولَٰئِكَ مَعَ الَّذِينَ أَنْعَمَ اللَّهُ عَلَيْهِم مِّنَ النَّبِيِّينَ وَالصِّدِّيقِينَ وَالشُّهَدَاءِ وَالصَّالِحِينَ ۚ وَحَسُنَ أُولَٰئِكَ رَفِيقًا
And whoever obeys Allah and the Messenger - those will be with the ones upon whom Allah has bestowed favor of the prophets, the steadfast affirmers of truth, the martyrs and the righteous. And excellent are those as companions.

12.4 AMCAL اَمْثَل and MUCLA مُثْلََي : MACALAمَثَلَ means having a picture in front of eyes. and AMCAL meaning exemplary,ideal ,the best . as in the Quran.

Surat Ţāhā (Ta-Ha) - سورة طه
20:104
نَّحْنُ أَعْلَمُ بِمَا يَقُولُونَ إِذْ يَقُولُ أَمْثَلُهُمْ طَرِيقَةً إِن لَّبِثْتُمْ إِلَّا يَوْمًا
We are most knowing of what they say when the best of them in manner will say, "You remained not but one day."

and MUCLA is feminine from AMCALU. In the Quran

20:63

They said, "Indeed, these are two magicians who want to drive you out of your land with their magic and do away with your most exemplary way.


12.5 JAMIYL(جميل): from it real JAMAL جمال means manifest quality,grandeur,glory,luxury and beauty. as said

Surat An-Naĥl (The Bee) - سورة النحل
16:6
وَلَكُمْ فِيهَا جَمَالٌ حِينَ تُرِيحُونَ وَحِينَ تَسْرَحُونَ
And for you in them is [the enjoyment of] beauty when you bring them in [for the evening] and when you send them out [to pasture].

and AJMAL FI AL3MAL( اَجْمَلَ في العمل) meaning to perform something in an better way. and JAMYL is used to take better way. as JACOB(AS) said to his son
