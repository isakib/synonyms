10 UJAD[ Yahoo]

Synonyms: 3UTULL,FZZ,A3RAB

10.1 3UTULL(عُتُلّ):word 3ATALA عَتَلَ meaning to grab some one's head's hair and drag him with force and stern manner. ALLAH said

Surat Ad-Dukhān (The Smoke) - سورة الدخان
44:47
خُذُوهُ فَاعْتِلُوهُ إِلَىٰ سَوَاءِ الْجَحِيمِ
[It will be commanded], "Seize him and drag him into the midst of the Hellfire,

and 3UTULL meaning Yahoo and Yokel. ALLAH said

Surat Al-Qalam (The Pen) - سورة القلم
68:13
عُتُلٍّ بَعْدَ ذَٰلِكَ زَنِيمٍ
Cruel, moreover, and an illegitimate pretender.

10.2 FAZZ(فظّ) : meaning rude and immoral. and FAZZ meaning talk harshly and blunt. bitter of tongue.Antonym is LYN(لين) meaning soft tongue and kind manners

Surat 'Āli `Imrān (Family of Imran) - سورة آل عمران
3:159
فَبِمَا رَحْمَةٍ مِّنَ اللَّهِ لِنتَ لَهُمْ ۖ وَلَوْ كُنتَ فَظًّا غَلِيظَ الْقَلْبِ لَانفَضُّوا مِنْ حَوْلِكَ ۖ فَاعْفُ عَنْهُمْ وَاسْتَغْفِرْ لَهُمْ وَشَاوِرْهُمْ فِي الْأَمْرِ ۖ فَإِذَا عَزَمْتَ فَتَوَكَّلْ عَلَى اللَّهِ ۚ إِنَّ اللَّهَ يُحِبُّ الْمُتَوَكِّلِينَ
So by mercy from Allah , [O Muhammad], you were lenient with them. And if you had been rude [in speech] and harsh in heart, they would have disbanded from about you. So pardon them and ask forgiveness for them and consult them in the matter. And when you have decided, then rely upon Allah . Indeed, Allah loves those who rely [upon Him].


10.3 A3RAB(اعراب) : yokel- desert dwellers, wild ,jungle people, uncivilized, those who doesn't possess any mannerism and unable to converse in civilized way. ALLAH said

Surat At-Tawbah (The Repentance) - سورة التوبة
9:97
الْأَعْرَابُ أَشَدُّ كُفْرًا وَنِفَاقًا وَأَجْدَرُ أَلَّا يَعْلَمُوا حُدُودَ مَا أَنزَلَ اللَّهُ عَلَىٰ رَسُولِهِ ۗ وَاللَّهُ عَلِيمٌ حَكِيمٌ
The bedouins are stronger in disbelief and hypocrisy and more likely not to know the limits of what [laws] Allah has revealed to His Messenger. And Allah is Knowing and Wise.

Summary:
10.1 3TL rude and violent
10.2 FZ immoral and using derogatory speech
10.3 A3RAB hick ,those who don't even know the mannerism of speech
