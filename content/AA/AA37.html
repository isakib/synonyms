37 INTEZAR KARNA [To Wait]

Synonyms: INTAZARA,IRTAQABA,TARABBASA

37.1 INTAZARA(اِنتَظَرَ): NAZARA(نَظَرَ) basically has two meanings a) to inspect meaning to see something from eyes. b) TAAMMALI ALSHAYEE(تَامَّلِ الشَي). meaning to wait for something and respite. here concern is with second meaning. In the Holy Quran, NAZIRATUN(نَظِرَةٌ) is used in the same meaning. ALLAH said

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة
2:280
وَإِن كَانَ ذُو عُسْرَةٍ فَنَظِرَةٌ إِلَىٰ مَيْسَرَةٍ ۚ وَأَن تَصَدَّقُوا خَيْرٌ لَّكُمْ ۖ إِن كُنتُمْ تَعْلَمُونَ
And if someone is in hardship, then [let there be] postponement until [a time of] ease. But if you give [from your right as] charity, then it is better for you, if you only knew.

and INTAZARA means to spend time for fullfillment of a hope.and this word is common for 'wait'. and from this point of view NAZARAH and INTEZARإِنتِظَار are near in meaning. and INTEZAR for both good and evil. also in case of certainity and doubt is used. ALLAH said

Surat Hūd (Hud) - سورة هود
11:121
وَقُل لِّلَّذِينَ لَا يُؤْمِنُونَ اعْمَلُوا عَلَىٰ مَكَانَتِكُمْ إِنَّا عَامِلُونَ
And say to those who do not believe, "Work according to your position; indeed, we are working.
Surat Hūd (Hud) - سورة هود
11:122
وَانتَظِرُوا إِنَّا مُنتَظِرُونَ
And wait, indeed, we are waiting."

37.2 IRTAQABA(إِرتَقَبَ): RAQABATUN(رَقَبَةٌ) is neck, and RAQABA(رَقَبَ) means to keep an eye on someone's neck or to put one under surveillance. and RIQBATUN( رِقبَةٌ) meaning caution,protection,watch over, and fear. so IRTAQABA means such a wait in which a person remains alert and cautious,watching out over other movements and protection of self(self defense). In the Quran

Surat Hūd (Hud) - سورة هود
11:93
وَيَا قَوْمِ اعْمَلُوا عَلَىٰ مَكَانَتِكُمْ إِنِّي عَامِلٌ ۖ سَوْفَ تَعْلَمُونَ مَن يَأْتِيهِ عَذَابٌ يُخْزِيهِ وَمَنْ هُوَ كَاذِبٌ ۖ وَارْتَقِبُوا إِنِّي مَعَكُمْ رَقِيبٌ
And O my people, work according to your position; indeed, I am working. You are going to know to whom will come a punishment that will disgrace him and who is a liar. So watch; indeed, I am with you a watcher, [awaiting the outcome]."

37.3 TARABBUS(تَرَبَّص): RABASA(رَبَصَ) and TARABBUS are used in the same meaning.and this wait is either for a fixed time or till occurence or annulment of a matter/event which is expected. for e,g Trading goods appreciation and depreciation.TARRABUS BISIL3ATIN(تَرَبُّص بِسِلعَةٍ) meaning to wait for commodity to decline in value (become cheap). Likewise a woman waiting to complete 3DDAT عدّت is called TARABBUS, and meaning to wait to maximize benefit. ALLAH said

Surat At-Tawbah (The Repentance) - سورة التوبة
9:52
قُلْ هَلْ تَرَبَّصُونَ بِنَا إِلَّا إِحْدَى الْحُسْنَيَيْنِ ۖ وَنَحْنُ نَتَرَبَّصُ بِكُمْ أَن يُصِيبَكُمُ اللَّهُ بِعَذَابٍ مِّنْ عِندِهِ أَوْ بِأَيْدِينَا ۖ فَتَرَبَّصُوا إِنَّا مَعَكُم مُّتَرَبِّصُونَ
Say, "Do you await for us except one of the two best things while we await for you that Allah will afflict you with punishment from Himself or at our hands? So wait; indeed we, along with you, are waiting."

Summary:
37.1 INTEZAR To wait till a designated time of hope and this is common word. for good and bad,doubt and certainity in both the cases
37.2 TARRAQUB To wait and be alert. To surveil strictly. normally used for waiting for bad time
37.3 TARABBUS To wait for gaining more benefit out of a time or limited time
