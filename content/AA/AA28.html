28 IKHATTA KARNA -HONA [To Gather,To Congregate]

Synonyms: JAMA3A,IJTAMA3A,HASHARA,IDDAKHARA,KHAZANA,WASAQA,I TTASAQA,KAFATA,
LAMMA,HASSALA,MACABATA

28.1 JAMA3A(جَمَعَ): means different ingredients of same thing or to gather different things. whether those things are living or non living. for e.g.

A) for living

Surat 'Āli `Imrān (Family of Imran) - سورة آل عمران
3:173
الَّذِينَ قَالَ لَهُمُ النَّاسُ إِنَّ النَّاسَ قَدْ جَمَعُوا لَكُمْ فَاخْشَوْهُمْ فَزَادَهُمْ إِيمَانًا وَقَالُوا حَسْبُنَا اللَّهُ وَنِعْمَ الْوَكِيلُ
Those to whom hypocrites said, "Indeed, the people have gathered against you, so fear them." But it [merely] increased them in faith, and they said, "Sufficient for us is Allah , and [He is] the best Disposer of affairs."

B) for non-living

Surat Al-Humazah (The Traducer) - سورة الهمزة
104:2
الَّذِي جَمَعَ مَالًا وَعَدَّدَهُ
Who collects wealth and [continuously] counts it.

then this word is used for collecting apparent as well abstract things for e.g

Surat Ţāhā (Ta-Ha) - سورة طه
20:60
فَتَوَلَّىٰ فِرْعَوْنُ فَجَمَعَ كَيْدَهُ ثُمَّ أَتَىٰ
So Pharaoh went away, put together his plan, and then came [to Moses].

28.2 IJTAMA3A(اِجتَمَعَ): only sed to gather intelligent . whether they belong to only one kind or many for e.g

Surat Al-'Isrā' (The Night Journey) - سورة الإسراء
17:88
قُل لَّئِنِ اجْتَمَعَتِ الْإِنسُ وَالْجِنُّ عَلَىٰ أَن يَأْتُوا بِمِثْلِ هَٰذَا الْقُرْآنِ لَا يَأْتُونَ بِمِثْلِهِ وَلَوْ كَانَ بَعْضُهُمْ لِبَعْضٍ ظَهِيرًا
Say, "If mankind and the jinn gathered in order to produce the like of this Qur'an, they could not produce the like of it, even if they were to each other assistants."


28.3 HASHARA(حَشَرَ):comes only for living meaning to gather people from their places to some other place. and per ibn ul faris seconds it as ALHASHR ALJAMA3 M3 SWQ( الحشر الجمع مع سوق). In the Quran

Surat An-Nāzi`āt (Those who drag forth) - سورة النازعات
79:23
فَحَشَرَ فَنَادَىٰ
And he gathered [his people] and called out
Surat An-Nāzi`āt (Those who drag forth) - سورة النازعات
79:24
فَقَالَ أَنَا رَبُّكُمُ الْأَعْلَىٰ
And said, "I am your most exalted lord."

then this word is sometimes used only in the meaning of resurrection. meaning to bring up dead
to life or to send for a specific purpose. for meaning of resurrect see example below

Surat Ţāhā (Ta-Ha) - سورة طه
20:125
قَالَ رَبِّ لِمَ حَشَرْتَنِي أَعْمَىٰ وَقَدْ كُنتُ بَصِيرًا
He will say, "My Lord, why have you raised me blind while I was [once] seeing?"
and sometimes this word contains the meanin of TAJMM3(تجمّع),SUWQ(سوق) and B3C(بعث)
Surat Al-Kahf (The Cave) - سورة الكهف
18:47
وَيَوْمَ نُسَيِّرُ الْجِبَالَ وَتَرَى الْأَرْضَ بَارِزَةً وَحَشَرْنَاهُمْ فَلَمْ نُغَادِرْ مِنْهُمْ أَحَدًا
And [warn of] the Day when We will remove the mountains and you will see the earth prominent, and We will gather them and not leave behind from them anyone.

28.4 IDDAKHARA( اِدَّخَرَ):its root is (ZA-KHA-RA) ذخر which means to stock grains and grocery kind of things for the sake of future needs. to stock and this stock is also done by many living things other than human. Ibn Faris said it includes two things a) to gather b) to preserve. and IDDAKHARA comes in this meaning and gathered thing is called ZAKHR ذخر and ZAKHYRAH ذخيره. In the QURAN

Surat 'Āli `Imrān (Family of Imran) - سورة آل عمران
3:49
وَرَسُولًا إِلَىٰ بَنِي إِسْرَائِيلَ أَنِّي قَدْ جِئْتُكُم بِآيَةٍ مِّن رَّبِّكُمْ ۖ أَنِّي أَخْلُقُ لَكُم مِّنَ الطِّينِ كَهَيْئَةِ الطَّيْرِ فَأَنفُخُ فِيهِ فَيَكُونُ طَيْرًا بِإِذْنِ اللَّهِ ۖ وَأُبْرِئُ الْأَكْمَهَ وَالْأَبْرَصَ وَأُحْيِي الْمَوْتَىٰ بِإِذْنِ اللَّهِ ۖ وَأُنَبِّئُكُم بِمَا تَأْكُلُونَ وَمَا تَدَّخِرُونَ فِي بُيُوتِكُمْ ۚ إِنَّ فِي ذَٰلِكَ لَآيَةً لَّكُمْ إِن كُنتُم مُّؤْمِنِينَ
And [make him] a messenger to the Children of Israel, [who will say], 'Indeed I have come to you with a sign from your Lord in that I design for you from clay [that which is] like the form of a bird, then I breathe into it and it becomes a bird by permission of Allah . And I cure the blind and the leper, and I give life to the dead - by permission of Allah . And I inform you of what you eat and what you store in your houses. Indeed in that is a sign for you, if you are believers.

28.5 KHAZANA(خَزَنَ): ZAKHYRAH is normally used for eatables but KHAZANA is common than it and mostly used for gatheirng wealth (cash and kind) like KAZANTU ALDDARAHIMA(
خَزَنْتُ الدَّرَاهِمَ) but in its basic meaning securing it is more than gathering. it is said KHAZANTU ALSSIRRA(خَزَنْتُ السِّرّ) I guarded the secret. and the person who is guard of this gathered wealth is called KHAZIN( خَازِنْ) and its plural is KHAZANATUH(خَزَنَةُ). In the Quran

Surat Al-Mulk (The Sovereignty) - سورة الملك
67:8
تَكَادُ تَمَيَّزُ مِنَ الْغَيْظِ ۖ كُلَّمَا أُلْقِيَ فِيهَا فَوْجٌ سَأَلَهُمْ خَزَنَتُهَا أَلَمْ يَأْتِكُمْ نَذِيرٌ
It almost bursts with rage. Every time a company is thrown into it, its keepers ask them, "Did there not come to you a warner?"

and where something is gathered its called KHIZANA( خِزانه) KHAZENA(خَزِيْنه) and its plural is KHAZAIN( خزائن) . In the aforemnetioned Ayah ,gathered thing are sinner and Hell is KHIZANA , and its keepers or guards of this KHIZANA are KHAZIN

28.6 WASAQA(وَسَقَ) /ITTASAQA(اِتَّسَقَ) : meaning to gather things and bear its load. and AWSQ( اوسق) meaning to load up animal and WASAQTU ALSHAYEE(وَسَقْتُ الشَّيْءَ) means to gather miscellaneous ingredients of a thing. and ITTASAQA means that all ingredient of that things have ben gathered and it's now complete. In the Quran

Surat Al-'Inshiqāq (The Sundering) - سورة الإنشقاق
84:17
وَاللَّيْلِ وَمَا وَسَقَ
And [by] the night and what it envelops
Surat Al-'Inshiqāq (The Sundering) - سورة الإنشقاق
84:18
وَالْقَمَرِ إِذَا اتَّسَقَ
And [by] the moon when it becomes full

28.7 KAFATA(كَفَتَ): means to gather something and hold it. to took control of it. to fold it and KAFYT(كَفِيْت) meaning food cabinet where eatables and food are kept. and ALLAHUMA AKFIHI(الّلهُمَ اَكْفِهِ) meaning OH ALLAH ! take care of him or give him death. ALLAH said

Surat Al-Mursalāt (The Emissaries) - سورة المرسلات
77:25
أَلَمْ نَجْعَلِ الْأَرْضَ كِفَاتًا
Have We not made the earth a container
Surat Al-Mursalāt (The Emissaries) - سورة المرسلات
77:26
أَحْيَاءً وَأَمْوَاتًا
Of the living and the dead?

28.8 LAMMA( لَمَّ): meaning to gather something and to nurture and reform it . In the Quran

Surat Al-Fajr (The Dawn) - سورة الفجر
89:19
وَتَأْكُلُونَ التُّرَاثَ أَكْلًا لَّمًّا
And you consume inheritance, devouring [it] altogether,

28.9 HASSALA( حَصَّلَ): ALTAHSYL(التحصيل) meaning to get pulp out of skin (as fruit )and to take out nut (out of shell). so HASALA (حَصَلَ) means to things a) to take out 2) to gather. In the Quran

Surat Al-`Ādiyāt (The Courser) - سورة العاديات
100:10
وَحُصِّلَ مَا فِي الصُّدُورِ
And that within the breasts is obtained,
which means that whatever secrets are in the chest they will be taken out and gathered like a nut is taken out of shell.

28.10 MACABAH(مَثََابَة): CWB(ثوب) actual meaning is something returning to its original state. It is said CABA FULLANUN ALA DARH( ثَابَ فُلانٌ الَي داره) meaning that person returns to his home. and MACABAH مثابة is that place which is made near the mouth of well for drinking water. as people keep on returning to such a place for taking and drinking water and gathered there that's why it is called MACABAH . from this relationg ALLAH declared BAYT ULLAH( بيت الّله) as MACABAH, as people visit it time after time to quench their spiritual thirst and gathered
ALLAH said

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة
2:125
وَإِذْ جَعَلْنَا الْبَيْتَ مَثَابَةً لِّلنَّاسِ وَأَمْنًا وَاتَّخِذُوا مِن مَّقَامِ إِبْرَاهِيمَ مُصَلًّى ۖ وَعَهِدْنَا إِلَىٰ إِبْرَاهِيمَ وَإِسْمَاعِيلَ أَن طَهِّرَا بَيْتِيَ لِلطَّائِفِينَ وَالْعَاكِفِينَ وَالرُّكَّعِ السُّجُودِ
And [mention] when We made the House a place of return for the people and [a place of] security. And take, [O believers], from the standing place of Abraham a place of prayer. And We charged Abraham and Ishmael, [saying], "Purify My House for those who perform Tawaf and those who are staying [there] for worship and those who bow and prostrate [in prayer]."


Summary:

28.1 JAMA3 common usage for all types of things for apparent and abstract used in all cases.
28.2 IJTAM3 only for gathering living
28.3 HASHARA for living , taken from from their places to gather
28.4 ZAKHARA to gather things of need for future
28.5 KHAZANA to secure gathered things
28.6 WASAQA to gather pertinent ingredients of only one thing
28.7 KAFATA to gather, to hold and to fold (sweep off)
28.8 LAMMA to gather and to reform
28.9 HASSALA to take out and gather
