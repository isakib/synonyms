15 HANSNA

[to laugh]

Synonyms: DAHIKA(ضحك), TABASSAMA(تبسم)

15.1 dahika(ضحك): to laugh with an open mouth so that the teeth are visible. Allah states that:

________________

Surat Hud - سُوۡرَةُ هُود

11:71

وَٱمۡرَأَتُهُ ۥ قَآٮِٕمَةٌ۬ فَضَحِكَتۡ فَبَشَّرۡنَـٰهَا بِإِسۡحَـٰقَ

And his wife, standing by laughed when We gave her good tidings (of the birth) of Isaac, and, after Isaac, of Jacob.

________________

15.2 tabassama (تبسم): basama(بسم) and tabassama(تبسم) meaning to smile, to laugh lightly without any voice which shows signs of pleasure. It is stated in the Quran:

________________

Surat Al-Naml (The Ants) - سُوۡرَةُ النَّمل

27:19

فَتَبَسَّمَ ضَاحِكً۬ا مِّن قَوۡلِهَا

And (Solomon) smiled, laughing at her speech, and said: My Lord, arouse me to be thankful for Thy favour wherewith Thou hast favoured me and my parents, and to do good that shall be pleasing unto Thee, and include me in (the number of) Thy righteous slaves.
