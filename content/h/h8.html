8 HALKA HONA, KERNA

[light, to lighten]

Synonyms: KHAFFA(خف), KHAFFAFA (خفف), ISTAKHAFA(استخف), FATTARA(فتر), QASADA(قصد)

8.1 khaffafa(خفف): khaffa(خف) meaning to be light, (ant:saqula(ثقل) meaning to be heavy). Used both in literal and semantic senses. Khafeef(خفيف) means to be light in terms of weight as well as being energetic and positive. Saqeel(ثقيل) means heavy in terms of weight as well as heavy on the heart. Qaulan saqeelan(قولا ثقيلا)(73:5) means heavy on health, heavy responsibility. Allah states that:

________________

Surat Al-Araaf (The Heights) - سُوۡرَةُ الاٴعرَاف

7:9

وَمَنۡ خَفَّتۡ مَوَٲزِينُهُ ۥ فَأُوْلَـٰٓٮِٕكَ ٱلَّذِينَ خَسِرُوٓاْ أَنفُسَہُم

And as for those whose scale is light: those are they who lose their souls because they disbelived Our revelations.

________________

And khaffafa(خفف) means to decrease, to lighten the load or responsibility, to lighten. This is also used in all ways. Allah states that:

________________

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سُوۡرَةُ البَقَرَة

2:86

فَلَا يُخَفَّفُ عَنۡہُمُ ٱلۡعَذَابُ وَلَا هُمۡ يُنصَرُونَ

Such are those who buy the life of the world at the price of the Hereafter. Their punishment will not be lightened, neither will they have support.

________________

And istakhaffa(استخف) means to try to decrease the value and worth of something considerably. Allah states that:

________________

Surat Az-Zukhruf (Ornaments of Gold) - سُوۡرَةُ الزّخرُف

43:54

فَٱسۡتَخَفَّ قَوۡمَهُ ۥ فَأَطَاعُوهُ‌ۚ

Thus he persuaded his people to make light (of Moses), and they obeyed him. Lo! they were a wanton folk.

________________

8.2 fattara(فتر): fatara(فتر) means considerable decrease in something’s force or speed, weakness after strength, slow speed gradually after fast speed, and fatoor(فتور) means slowness or stationary after high speed, softness after hardness and weakness after strength. Fattara(فتر) means to considerably decrease the load or speed. Allah states that:

________________

Surat Az-Zukhruf (Ornaments of Gold) - سُوۡرَةُ الزّخرُف

43:74-75

لَا يُفَتَّرُ عَنۡهُمۡ وَهُمۡ فِيهِ مُبۡلِسُونَ فِى عَذَابِ جَهَنَّمَ خَـٰلِدُونَ

Lo! the guilty are immortal in hell's torment. (74) It is not relaxed for them, and they despair therein.

________________

8.3 qasada(قصد): one of its meanings is to adopt a middle way saving from extravagance and deflation. Iqtisaad(اقتصاد) means to adopt a middle way in the spending of money and other things. Allah states that:

31:19

وَٱقۡصِدۡ فِى مَشۡيِكَ

Be modest in thy bearing and subdue thy voice. Lo! the harshest of all voices is the voice of the ass.

Surat Al-Tawba (Repentance) - سُوۡرَةُ التّوبَة

9:42

لَوۡ كَانَ عَرَضً۬ا قَرِيبً۬ا وَسَفَرً۬ا قَاصِدً۬ا

Had it been a near adventure and an easy journey they had followed thee, but the distance seemed too far for them. Yet will they swear by Allah (saying): If we had been able we would surely have set out with you. They destroy their souls, and Allah knoweth that they verily are liars.

________________

here also, qaasidan(قاصدا) means very less or moderate distance, meaning not too far or near but it is used in the meaning of being lighter to avoid comparison and details.

 

Summary:

1. KHAFFAFA(خفف): meaning to lighten something by decreasing the weight or responsibility.

2. FATTARA(فتر): to decrease something’s strength.

3. QASADA(قصد): means middle way, moderate, easy as compared to difficult.
