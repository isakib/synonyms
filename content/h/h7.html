Summary:

1. HALAKA(هلك): to die, to destroy, common for living and non-living things.

2. BAADA(باد): changing of vegetation to desert and destruction.

3.  TARADDA(تردى): to die after falling in a well or pit.

4. BAKHA’A(بخع): to die out of grief.

5. DAMMARA(دمر): to annihilate, to wipe out.

6. DAMDAMA(دمدم): to destroy completely, to eradicate.

7. TABBABA(تبب): to move gradually towards destruction.

8. TABARRA(تبر): to annihilate and destroy.

9. BAARA(بار): to be destroyed after loss in merchandise.

10. AS-HATA(اسحت): destruction.

11. AUBAQA(أوبق): reaching such a point as if there is fire at one end and a pit at another.

12. QASAMA(قصم): to de-shape.

13. FANAA(فنى): to lose one’s existence.
