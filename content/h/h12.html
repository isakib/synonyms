12 HUMWAAR KERNA

[To level]

Synonyms: SATAH(سطح), DAKKA(دك), MARRAD(مرد)

12.1 satah(سطح): two things are included in the meaning of satah(سطح): (i)to spread, to stretch (ii) then to level it. Satah-ul-bait(سطح البيت) meaning to level the roof of a house and mistah(مسطح) means the instrument used to level something and satah(سطح) means the upper portion of the roof of a house. In the words of Imam Raghib: اعلى البيت جعل سويا Allah states that:

________________

Surat Al-Ghashiya (The Overwhelming) - سُوۡرَةُ الغَاشِیَة

88:20

وَإِلَى ٱلۡأَرۡضِ كَيۡفَ سُطِحَتۡ

And the earth, how it is spread?

________________

12.2 dakka (دك): basic meanings: (i)to crush (ii)then to level it i.e. to crush something and level it to the ground. Allah states that:

________________

Surat Al-Kahf (The Cave) - سُوۡرَةُ الکهف

18:98

فَإِذَا جَآءَ وَعۡدُ رَبِّى جَعَلَهُ ۥ دَكَّآءَ‌ۖ

He said: This is a mercy from my Lord; but when the promise of my Lord cometh to pass, He will lay it low, for the promise of my Lord is true.

________________

12.3 marrada (مرد): marid-al-ghulam(مرد الغلام) meaning man gone astray and marrad-al-fusn(مرد ألفصن) meaning cleaning the leaves of the branch and marrad-al-bana’a(مرد البناء) meaning to smooth a building and to dampen it. Allah states that:

________________

Surat Al-Naml (The Ant) - سُوۡرَةُ النَّمل

27:44

الَ إِنَّهُ ۥ صَرۡحٌ۬ مُّمَرَّدٌ۬ مِّن قَوَارِيرَ‌ۗ

It was said unto her: Enter the hall. And when she saw it she deemed it a pool and bared her legs. (Solomon) said: Lo! it is a hall, made smooth, of glass. She said: My Lord! Lo! I have wronged myself, and I surrender with Solomon unto Allah, the Lord of the Worlds.

________________

Summary:

1. SATAH(سطح): to spread and level it.  

2. DAKKA(دك): to level something by crushing it.

3. MARRADA(مرد): to level and dampen.
