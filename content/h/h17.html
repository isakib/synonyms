Kaana, “hona” P.900

{

Synonyms: KANA,ASBAHA,SADARA,ASDARA,WAQAA

1 KANA(كَانَ): meaning is,was KANA gives meaning of past and present. and its MUDHARI(مضارع) form is YAKUN(يكون) both for present and future. as per ibn al faris KANA means AKHBAR 3N HADOOS ALSHAYEE(اخبار عن حدوث الشي) that is to give news of some new matter happening

Surat Al-Baqarah [2:34] - The Noble Qur'an

إِلَّا إِبْلِيسَ أَبَىٰ وَاسْتَكْبَرَ وَكَانَ مِنَ الْكَافِرِينَ

except for Iblees. He refused and was arrogant and became of the disbelievers

Surat Al-'Insan [76:7] - The Noble Qur'an

وَيَخَافُونَ يَوْمًا كَانَ شَرُّهُ مُسْتَطِيرًا

and fear a Day whose evil will be widespread.

also KANA can come as defective verb. when it enters on past changes it to far past.KANA ZAHABA(كان ذَهَبَ) meaning he went. and when it enters on MUDHARI it becomes MAZI ISTIMRAARI

Surat Al-Baqarah [2:59] - The Noble Qur'an

فَأَنزَلْنَا عَلَى الَّذِينَ ظَلَمُوا رِجْزًا مِّنَ السَّمَاءِ بِمَا كَانُوا يَفْسُقُونَ

so We sent down upon those who wronged a punishment from the sky because they were defiantly disobeying

2 ASBAHA(اَصْبَحَ): meaning happened.change from one state to another or one attribute to another.ASBAH UL HAQQ(اَصْبَحَ الحقّ) meaning Truth became evident.

Surat Al-Qasas [28:10] - The Noble Qur'an

وَأَصْبَحَ فُؤَادُ أُمِّ مُوسَىٰ فَارِغًا ۖ

And the heart of Moses' mother became empty [of all else].

3 SADARA(صَدَرَ): [antonym WARADA(وَرَدَ)] to return after completion of something

Surat Al-Qasas [28:23] - The Noble Qur'an

قَالَتَا لَا نَسْقِي حَتَّىٰ يُصْدِرَ الرِّعَاءُ

They said, "We do not water until the shepherds dispatch [their flocks]

4 WAQAA(وَقَعَ):has basically two meanings a)to prove something b)to fall. and WAQIA(واقعة) is normally said to an intense and strong accident.In Quran this word is used for tribulation,distress}

Surat Al-Ma`arij [70:1] - The Noble Qur'an

سَأَلَ سَائِلٌ بِعَذَابٍ وَاقِعٍ 

A supplicant asked for a punishment bound to happen

Surat An-Naml [27:85] - The Noble Qur'anوَوَقَعَ الْقَوْلُ عَلَيْهِم بِمَا ظَلَمُواAnd the decree will befall them for the wrong they did

Summary:

1 KANA:To give news of Happening

2 ASBAHA: for change of state or attribute

3 SUDUR:to be properly relieved after completion of something

4 WAQAA:for some tragedy befalling

}
