Qatal kerna (to murder someone)

1. قَتَلَ (Qatala): This is used commonly. To kill. To separate the soul from the body either through execution or by other means.

Allah SWT says in Surah Nisaa (4:89): فَإِنلَّمْيَعْتَزِلُوكُمْوَيُلْقُواْإِلَيْكُمُالسَّلَمَوَيَكُفُّواْأَيْدِيَهُمْفَخُذُوهُمْوَاقْتُلُوهُمْحَيْثُثِقِفْتُمُوهُمْ“If they withdraw not and do not restraint themselves then take (hold of) them and kill them wherever you find them.”

2. سَفَكَ (Safa’ka): to spill water or innocent blood

· سَفُوْك: a liar

· سَفَّاك: a tyrant. Someone who murders innocent people and sheds their blood

Allah SWT says in Surah Al-Baqarah (2:30): قَالُواْأَتَجْعَلُفِيهَامَنيُفْسِدُفِيهَاوَيَسْفِكُالدِّمَآءَ“Angels said: "Will You place therein those who will make mischief therein and shed blood.”

3. حَسَّ (Hasaa): to pull something from its roots. In dictionary terms it means how severely somebody is killed. Therefore, it means to kill somebody so brutally or kill so precisely that enemy gets completely annihilated and rooted out.

Allah SWT says in Surah Al-Imran (3:152): وَلَقَدْصَدَقَكُمُاللَّهُوَعْدَهُإِذْتَحُسُّونَهُمبِإِذْنِهِ “And Allah did indeed fulfill His promise to you when you were killing them(your enemy) with His permission.”

4. اَثْخَنَ (Askha’an): to be harsh while killing or murdering someone, mass murder or genocide.

· ثَخَنَ: to weigh something while carrying

· ثَخُنَ: thick and sturdy

· اثخنفياَلارض: transgressing the limits in shedding blood

Allah SWT says in Surah Muhammad (47:4) فَإِذَالَقِيتُمُالَّذِينَكَفَرُواْفَضَرْبَالرِّقَابِحَتَّىإِذَآأَثْخَنتُمُوهُمْفَشُدُّواْالْوَثَاقَ “So, when you meet those who disbelieve (in battle), smite (their) necksuntil you have fully defeated them, then tighten their bonds.”

Summary:

1. قَتَلَ: This is used commonly. Murder

2. سَفَكَ: to spill innocent blood

3. حَسَّ: to root out the enemy

4. اَثْخَنَ: to be harsh while killing or murdering someone, mass murder or genocide.
