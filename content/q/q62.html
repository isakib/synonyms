62 – Hunger

 

The words جُوع, مسغَبَة, مَخمَصة and خَصَاصَة have been used for hunger

 

62.1 جُوع: to have the need for food, its antonym is شَبَعَ (to be satiated after eating).  This is an initial stage of hunger. For example, Allah (the exalted) states:

 

Surat Quraysh (Quraysh) -  قريش سورة

106:4

  ٱلَّذِىٓ أَطۡعَمَهُم مِّن جُوعٍ۬ وَءَامَنَهُم مِّنۡ خَوۡفِۭ (﻿٤﻿)

Who has fed them, [saving them] from hunger and made them safe, [saving them] from fear (4).

____________________________________

 

62.2  مسغَبَة: سَغَب means to be hungry and اَسغَب refers to a nation being in a state of drought and

سغَاب refers to hunger.  This is the second stage of hunger.  Allah (the exalted) states:

 

Surat Al-Balad (The City) - البلد سورة

90:14

 أَوۡ إِطۡعَـٰمٌ۬ فِى يَوۡمٍ۬ ذِى مَسۡغَبَةٍ۬ (﻿١٤﻿)

Or feeding on a day of severe hunger (14).

____________________________________

 

62.3 مَخمَصة: الجُوع خَمَصَهُ refers to someone having a slender stomach due to hunger and مَخمَصة means to have an empty stomach, to get weak because of a combination of hard work and insufficient food and the shrinking/squeezing of the stomach.  This is the third level of hunger.  For example, Allah (the exalted) states:

 

Surat Al-Mā'idah (The Table Spread) - المائدة سورة

5:3

(﻿٣﻿)رَّحِيمٌ۬ غَفُورٌ۬ٱللَّهَ فَإِنَّ لِّإِثۡمٍ۬‌ۙ مُتَجَانِفٍ۬ غَيرَ مَخۡمَصَةٍ فِى ٱضۡطُرَّ فَمَنِ

But whoever is forced by severe hunger with no inclination to sin - then indeed, Allah is Forgiving and Merciful (3).

____________________________________

 

62.4 خَصَاصَة: خَصَّ refers to be destitute or needy and خَصَاصَة is a verbal noun (مصدر).  خَصَاصَة includes the meaning of less or very little.  Overall it means to be on the verge of starvation due to being needy and destitute.  In the holy Quran, Allah (the exalted) states:

 

Surat Al-Ĥashr (The Exile) - سورة الحشر

59:9

‌(﻿٩﻿) خَصَاصَةٌ۬ بِہِمۡ  كَانَ وَلَوۡ أَنفُسِہِمۡ علَى وَيُؤۡثِرُونَ

And they give [them] preference over themselves, even though they are in privation (9).

________________________________________

 

Summary:

1.      جُوع: the state in which one feel the need for food

2.      مسغَبَة: to be in a state of drought

3.      مَخمَصة: to be weak due to a lack of food

خَصَاصَة: to be in or near a state of starvation due to poverty
