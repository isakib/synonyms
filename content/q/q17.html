17. QAID KHANA [Prison, Jail]

Synonyms: SIJN, HASEER

17.1 SIJN (سِجْن): A famous word for prison or jail. An institution where persons are confined for punishment by the order of court. In the Quran

Surah Yusuf (Joseph): سورۃ یُوسُف 12:42

 فَلَبِثَ فِى ٱلسِّجۡنِ بِضۡعَ سِنِينَ

 So he remained in prison for several years.

 

17.2 HASEER (حَصِیْر): HASARA (حَصَرَ) means to surround from all the sides, to besiege or circumvent. HASEER (حَصِیْر) is a place to detain or confine a prison. In the Quran

Surah Al-Israa (The Night Journey): سورۃ الٕاسرَاء  17:8

وَجَعَلۡنَا جَهَنَّمَ لِلۡكَـٰفِرِينَ حَصِيرًا

We have made Hell a prison for the disbelievers
