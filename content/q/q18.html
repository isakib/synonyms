18. QAID KARNA – QAIDI BANANA [to imprison, to hold captive]

Synonyms: HABASA, ATHBATA, ASARA, SAJANA

18.1 HABASA (حَبَسَ): To stop someone from getting up. HABAS-ASH-SHAI (حَبَسَ الشَّیْ) means to guard something. MAHBASA (مَحْبَسَۃ) means a prison. Also the place of meditation or reclusion of saints. HABASA (حَبَسَ) means to detain someone, or keep in custody (dictionary Al Manjad). And HABASA (حَبَسَ) also means MANA’-AT-TASARAF (منع عن التصرّف) means to stop someone to use his/her property. MAHBAS (مَحْبَسَ) means jail or prison where criminals are kept during interrogation. Allah says in the Holy Quran

Surah Al-Maeda (The table spread): سورۃ المَائدہ 5:106

 تَحۡبِسُونَهُمَا مِنۢ بَعۡدِ ٱلصَّلَوٰةِ فَيُقۡسِمَانِ بِٱللَّهِ

Detain them both after prayer, and let them both swear by Allah:

 

18.2 ATHBATA (اَثْبَتَ): To tie someone up with lace or rope (dictionary Al Manjad), to lash someone up and stop his/her movement and keep him in ones custody. In the Quran

Surah Al-Anfal (The Spoils of War): سُوۡرَةُ الاٴنفَال 8:30

وَإِذۡ يَمۡكُرُ بِكَ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُواْ لِيُثۡبِتُوكَ أَوۡ يَقۡتُلُوكَ أَوۡ يُخۡرِجُوكَ‌ۚ

When the disbelievers plotted against you, to imprison you, or kill you, or expel you.

 

18.3 ASARA (اَسَرَ): Also means to tie someone up with a rope. The difference between ASARA (اَسَرَ) and ATHBATA (اَثْبَتَ) is that ASARA (اَسَرَ) means to capture during a battle. ASEER means war prisoner (plural ASRAA (اَسْرٰی), USARAA (اُسَارٰی)). Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Baqarah (The cow): سورۃ البَقَرَۃ  2:85

 وَإِن يَأۡتُوكُمۡ أُسَـٰرَىٰ تُفَـٰدُوهُمۡ وَهُوَ مُحَرَّمٌ عَلَيۡڪُمۡ إِخۡرَاجُهُمۡ‌ۚ

 And if they come to you as captives, you ransom them, although it was forbidden to you.

 

In Another Reference in Holy Quran

Suran Al-Ahzaab (The clans): سورۃ الأحزَاب 33:26

 فَرِيقً۬ا تَقۡتُلُونَ وَتَأۡسِرُونَ فَرِيقً۬ا

 Some of them you killed, and others you took captive.

18.4 SAJANA (سَجَنَ): To imprison someone after finding him guilty of charges against him by the court. To send to prison, prison someone for some crime by the leader. In the Quran

Surah Yusuf (Joseph): سورۃ یُوسُف 12:25

قَالَتۡ مَا جَزَآءُ مَنۡ أَرَادَ بِأَهۡلِكَ سُوٓءًا إِلَّآ أَن يُسۡجَنَ أَوۡ عَذَابٌ أَلِيمٌ۬

She said, “What is the penalty for him who desired to dishonor your wife, except imprisonment or a painful punishment?”

Summary:

18.1 HABASA: Stop someone to use his/her property.

18.2 ATHBATA: To lash someone up and stop his/her movement

18.3 ASARA: Capture during a battle

18.4 SAJANA: Imprison someone after finding him guilty of charges against him by the court
