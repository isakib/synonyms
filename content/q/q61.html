And إمتلَأ in the meaning of filling something.  Allah (the exalted) states:

 

Surat Qāf (The Letter "Qaf") - ق سورة

50:30

 يَوۡمَ نَقُولُ لِجَهَنَّمَ هَلِ ٱمۡتَلَأۡتِ وَتَقُولُ هَلۡ مِن مَّزِيدٍ۬ (﻿٣٠﻿)

On the Day We will say to Hell, "Have you been filled?" and it will say, "Are there some more"(30)

61.2 دَهَقَ: used for filling a utensil (such as a bowl) with a drink.  Allah (the exalted) states:

 

Surat An-Naba' (The Tidings) - النبإ سورة

78:31-34

 إِنَّ لِلۡمُتَّقِينَ مَفَازًا (﻿٣١﻿) حَدَآٮِٕقَ وَأَعۡنَـٰبً۬ا (﻿٣٢﻿) وَكَوَاعِبَ أَتۡرَابً۬ا (﻿٣٣﻿) وَكَأۡسً۬ا دِهَاقً۬ا (﻿٣٤﻿)

Indeed, for the righteous is attainment –(31) gardens and grapevines (32) and full-breasted [companions] of equal age (33) and a full cup (34).

____________________________________

 

61.3 شَحَنَ: to load a boat or airplane with people or things.  Allah (the exalted) states:

 

Surat Aş-Şāffāt (Those who set the Ranks) -  الصافات سورة

37:140

 إِذۡ أَبَقَ إِلَى ٱلۡفُلۡكِ ٱلۡمَشۡحُونِ (﻿١٤٠﻿)

[Mention] when he (Jonah, pbuh) ran away to the laden ship (140).

____________________________________

 

Summary:

1.      دَهَقَ: to fill a bowl

2.      شَحَنَ: to load a boat

3.      مَلَاَ for knowledge
