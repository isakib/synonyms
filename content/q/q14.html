14. QAMEES [Shirt]

Synonyms: QAMEES, SARABEEL

14.1 QAMEES (قَمِیْص): A famous type of garment, a shirt or a kurta (long shirt). In the Quran

Surah Yusuf (Joseph): سورۃ یُوسُف 12:27

 وَإِن كَانَ قَمِيصُهُ ۥ قُدَّ مِن دُبُرٍ۬ فَكَذَبَتۡ

But if his shirt is torn from the back: then she has lied

 

14.2 SARABEEL (سَرَابِیْل): (singular SARBAL (سربال)) The word is used for both shirt and trouser. Shirt or anything that can be worn (dictionary Al Manjad).

Surat An-Nahl (The Bee): سورۃ النّحل16:81

 وَجَعَلَ لَكُمۡ سَرَٲبِيلَ تَقِيڪُمُ ٱلۡحَرَّ

Has given you garments to protect you from the heat,
