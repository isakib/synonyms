65 – To Send

 

Two words have been used: اَرسَلَ and بَعَثَ

 

65.1 اَرسَلَ: to send a message, letter or order or to send a person.  اِلَيهِ بِهِ اَرسَلَ refers to sending someone with a message to someone.  رسول refers to a messenger which can be human or from the angels.  In the holy Quran, Allah (the exalted) states:

 

Surat Al-'A`rāf (The Heights) - سورة الأعراف

7:111

 قَالُوٓاْ أَرۡجِهۡ وَأَخَاهُ وَأَرۡسِلۡ فِى ٱلۡمَدَآٮِٕنِ حَـٰشِرِينَ (﻿١١١﻿)

They said, "Postpone [the matter of] him and his brother and send among the cities gatherers (111)

______________________________________

 

65.2 بَعَثَ: originally it has two meanings: to raise and to send alone.  These meanings can sometimes be combined, for example البَعِير بَعَثتُ means ‘I raised the camel and let it go free’.  When it is used in the meaning of sending, it refers to sending with a purpose or for the completion of a task. Allah (the exalted) states:

 

Surat Al-Mā'idah (The Table Spread) - المائدة سورة

5:31

 فَبَعَثَ ٱللَّهُ غُرَابً۬ا يَبۡحَثُ فِى ٱلۡأَرۡضِ (﻿٣١﻿)   

Then Allah sent a crow searching (digging) in the ground (31).

 

And in another place, Allah (the exalted) states:

 

Surat Al-Jumu`ah (The Congregation, Friday) - الجمعة سورة

62:2

 هُوَ ٱلَّذِى بَعَثَ فِى ٱلۡأُمِّيِّـۧنَ رَسُولاً۬ مِّنۡہُمۡ يَتۡلُواْ عَلَيۡہِمۡ ءَايَـٰتِهِۦ وَيُزَكِّيہِمۡ۬ (٢)﻿

It is He who has sent among the unlettered a Messenger from themselves reciting to them His verses and purifying them (2)

 

Summary:

1-) اَرسَلَ: to send someone with a message

2-) بَعَثَ: to send someone alone for a purpose
