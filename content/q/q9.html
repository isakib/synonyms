9- QURAN KE MUKHTALIF NAAM [Different Names of Quran]

 

The words are used like QURAN (قران ), FURQAN (فرقان), ZIKR (ذكر), TAZKIRA (تذكرة), KITABUN-MUBIN (كتاب مبين) and HADEES (حديث ) which describe its different aspects.

 

9.1 QURAN (قران ): QARA (قر) means from read to exaggerate means repeatedly, frequently and the book that is always recited. Allah says:

 

Surat Qāf (The Letter "Qaf") - سورة ق 50:1

 

قۗ ڗوَالْقُرْاٰنِ الْمَجِيْدِ

Qaf. By the honored Qur'an.

 

9.2 FURQAN (فرقان): FARAQA (فرق) means to separate, to disjoint from exaggerate means such book which distinguishes and discriminates between each and every aspect of right and wrong. Allah says:

 

Surat Al-Furqān (The Criterian) - سورة الفرقان 25:1

 

تَبٰرَكَ الَّذِيْ نَزَّلَ الْفُرْقَانَ عَلٰي عَبْدِهٖ

Blessed is He who sent down the Criterion upon His Servant.

 

9.3 ZIKR (ذكر) and TAZKIRA (تذكرة): The antonym of ZIKR (ذكر) is NASSIA (نسي). ZAKAR (ذكر) means to remember and to be remembered which is opposite of forgetting.

And Quran is called ZIKR (ذكر) for the reason that it recalls the natural offering of mans goodness also of AHED-E-ALSAT (عہد الست). Hence it is also an advice. And similarly the meaning of TAZKIRA (تذكرة) is also advice and remembering as well. And the ticket of some ride such as rail, airplane  is called TAZKIRA (تذكرة) and also to certificate and passport. Allah says:

 

Surat Şād (The Letter "Saad") - سورة ص 38:8

 

ءَاُنْزِلَ عَلَيْهِ الذِّكْرُ مِنْۢ بَيْنِنَا

Has the message been revealed to him out of [all of] us? And said:

 

Surat Al-Muzzammil (The Enshrouded One) - سورة المزمل 73:19

 

اِنَّ هٰذِهٖ تَذْكِرَةٌ

Indeed, this is a reminder.

 

9.4 KITABUN-MUBIN (كتاب مبين): BANA (بان) means to get away or to separate. Three things are found in BRAIN (بين)  (1) IFTRAAQ (افتراق), (2) BUAD (بعد), (3) WUJUU (وضو ح) and BAYANA (بين) means to openly describe something. And KITABUN-MUBIN (كتاب مبين) means such book in which everything about the guidance is explained with complete explanation and interpretation. Clear and shinning book i.e. QURAN-KAREEM.  Allah says:

 

Surat Al-Mā'idah (The Table Spread) - سورة المائدة 5:15

 

قَدْ جَاۗءَكُمْ مِّنَ اللّٰهِ نُوْرٌ وَّكِتٰبٌ مُّبِيْنٌ

There has come to you from Allah a light and a clear Book.

 

9.5 HADEES (حديث ): The antonym of HADAS (حدث) is ADAM (عدم). And HADASA (حدث) means the emergence of a new thing. And HADEES (حديث) means everything that was not before and emerges entirely new. New thing. Like said:

 

Surat Aţ-Ţalāq (The Divorce) - سورة الطلاق 65:1

 

لَعَلَّ اللّٰهَ يُحْدِثُ بَعْدَ ذٰلِكَ اَمْرًا

Perhaps Allah will bring about after that a [different] matter.

 

And HADEES (حديث) also applies to everything that was present before but have been erased from peoples mind due to the passing of time. if it becomes alive again then HADEES (حديث) will also be applicable on it. New thing. Quran is called HADEES (حديث) in the same sense. Allah says:

 

Surat Az-Zumar (The Troops) - سورة الزمر 39:23

 

اَللّٰهُ نَزَّلَ اَحْسَنَ الْحَدِيْثِ

Allah has sent down the best statement.
