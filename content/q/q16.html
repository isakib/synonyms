16 QAYAMAT [QAYAMAH (Day of resurrection) and its Synonyms]

Synonyms: QIYAMAH, ASSA’ATA, YAWM-AD-DEEN, YAWM-AL-KHUROOJ, YAWM-AL-HISAAB, YAWM-AL-FASAL, GHASHIA, WAQIAH, AL-HAQQAH, SAKH-KHATA, AZIFAH, QARIAH, TA’AMMA-TIL-KUBRA

16.1 QIYAMAH (قِیَامَۃ): something that will be certainly established. A day or time that will be established for the implementation of truth and justice. The different names of this day in the Quran are due to the different aspects and conditions of this day. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Ale- Imran (The family of Imran): سورۃ آل عِمْرن 3:185

كُلُّ نَفۡسٍ۬ ذَآٮِٕقَةُ ٱلۡمَوۡتِ‌ۗ وَإِنَّمَا تُوَفَّوۡنَ أُجُورَڪُمۡ يَوۡمَ ٱلۡقِيَـٰمَةِ‌ۖ

Every soul will have a taste of death, and you will receive your recompense on the Day of Resurrection.

16.2 ASSA’ATA (السَّاعَۃ): SA’ATA (سَاعَۃ) means time. And ASSA’ATA  (السَّاعَۃ) means a specific time. In Quran ASSA’ATA (السَّاعَۃ) refers to the time when the trumpets will be blown for the first time. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Ana’am (The Cattle): سورۃ الانعام 6:31

 إِذَا جَآءَتۡہُمُ ٱلسَّاعَةُ بَغۡتَةً۬ قَالُواْ يَـٰحَسۡرَتَنَا عَلَىٰ مَا فَرَّطۡنَا فِيہَا

 Then, when the Hour comes upon them suddenly, they will say, “Alas for us, how we have neglected it.”

 

16.3 YAWM-UD-DEEN (یَوْمُ الدِّیْن): The day when everybody will be punished and awarded for their bad and good deeds respectively. The Day of Judgment or AL-QIYAMA. In the Quran

Surah Fatiha (The Opening) : سُوۡرَةُ الفَاتِحَة 1:4

 مَـٰلِكِ يَوۡمِ ٱلدِّينِ

Master of the Day of Judgment.

16.4 YAWM-UL-KHUROOJ (یَوْمُ الخُرُوْج): The day everybody will come out of their graves. When the trumpet will be blown the second time, the dead will rise from their graves. Day of Resurrection.

Surah Qaf (Qaf): سُوۡرَةُ قٓ 50:42

يَوۡمَ يَسۡمَعُونَ ٱلصَّيۡحَةَ بِٱلۡحَقِّ‌ۚ ذَٲلِكَ يَوۡمُ ٱلۡخُرُوجِ

The Day when they will hear a (mighty) Blast in (very) truth: that will be the Day of Resurrection.

 

16.5 YAWM-UL-HISAAB (یَوْمُ الْحِسَاب): The day when everyone will be held accountable for their deeds and will to be judged by Allah personally.  In the Quran

Surah Al-Ghashiya (The overwhelming): سُوۡرَةُ الغَاشِیَة 88:25

 إِنَّ إِلَيۡنَآ إِيَابَہُمۡ  ثُمَّ إِنَّ عَلَيۡنَا حِسَابَہُم

 To Us is their return. Then upon Us rests their reckoning.

 

Another reference in Holy Quran:

Surah Saad (Saad): سورۃ ص 38:16

 وَقَالُواْ رَبَّنَا عَجِّل لَّنَا قِطَّنَا قَبۡلَ يَوۡمِ ٱلۡحِسَابِ

And they say, “Our Lord, hasten Your writ upon us, before the Day of Account.”

 

16.6 YAWM-UL-FASAL (یَوْمُ الْفَصْل): Day of separation, Separation of believers from non-believers. This will happen after the judgment. People of Paradise will be separated from People of hell. Allah says in the Holy Quran

Surah Al-Mursalat (The Emissaries): سُوۡرَةُ المُرسَلات 77:38

هَـٰذَا يَوۡمُ ٱلۡفَصۡلِ‌ۖ جَمَعۡنَـٰكُمۡ وَٱلۡأَوَّلِينَ

This is the Day of Separation; We have gathered you, together with the ancients.

 

16.7 GHASHIA (غَاشِیَۃ): GHASHI (غَشِی) means something covering other thing. And GHASHI (غَشِی) is also a condition when a person is not in senses. GHASHIA (غَاشِیَۃ) means something whose terror and dreadfulness covers everything. Causing loss of senses and mind. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Ghashiya (The overwhelming): سُوۡرَةُ الغَاشِیَة 88:1

هَلۡ أَتَٮٰكَ حَدِيثُ ٱلۡغَـٰشِيَةِ

 Has the story reached thee of the overwhelming (Event)?

16.8 AL-WAQIAH (اَلْوَاقِعَۃ): Something that will happen for sure. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Waqiah (The Inevitable): سُوۡرَةُ الواقِعَة 56:1

إِذَا وَقَعَتِ ٱلۡوَاقِعَةُ لَيۡسَ لِوَقۡعَتِہَا كَاذِبَةٌ

When the inevitable occurs. Of its occurrence, there is no denial.

 

16.9 AL-HAQQAH (اَلْحَاقَّۃ): HAQQA (حَقَّ) means fact or truth (dictionary Al Manjad). HAQQAH (حَاقَّۃ) whose establishment is a fact. Imperishable reality. In the Quran

Surah Al-Haqqah (The reality): سورۃ الحَاقَّۃ 69:3

 وَمَآ أَدۡرَٮٰكَ مَا ٱلۡحَآقَّةُ

What will make you understand what the Reality is?

 

16.10 SAKH-KHA (صَآخَّۃ): SAKH (صَخَّ) means a deafening noise or sound. An earsplitting loud noise. This condition will befall when the trumpets will be blown. In the Quran

Surah Abasa (He frowned): سورۃعَبَسَ 80:33

فَإِذَا جَآءَتِ ٱلصَّآخَّةُ

But when the Deafening Noise comes to pass.

16.11 AZIFAH (اٰزِفَۃ): AZIFA (اَزِفَ) refers to running low on time, like the time of train departure is running low. AZIFAH (اٰزِفَۃ) means a time that is near. Allah says in the Holy Quran

Surah Al-Ghafir (The forgiver): سورۃ غافِر 40:18

 وَأَنذِرۡهُمۡ يَوۡمَ ٱلۡأَزِفَةِ

 Warn them of the Day that is (ever) drawing near

 

16.14 QARIAH (قَارِعَۃ): QARA’ (قَرَعَ) means one thing hitting another and creating noise. QARA-AL-BAAB (قَرَعَ الْبَاب) means a door knock. QARIAH (قَارِعَۃ) means a rumbling time. According to Ibn-Al-Faris it means something that comes over man with severity is called QARIAH (قَارِعَۃ). Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Qaria (The Calamity): سُوۡرَةُ القَارعَة 101:3

 وَمَآ أَدۡرَٮٰكَ مَا ٱلۡقَارِعَةُ

And what will explain to thee what the (Day) of Noise and Clamour is?

 

16.13 TA’AMMA-TIL-KUBRA (طَٔٓامَّۃُ الْکُبْرٰی): AT-TAMMU means a sea filled with water. TAMMA (طَمَّ) means to fill. TAMM-AL-BI’RA (طَمَّ الْبِئْرَ) means to fill a well with earth. TAMMAH (طَٔٓامَّۃُ) means a calamity that overpowers all other tribulations. In the Quran

Surah An-Nazi'at (Those who drag forth): سورۃ النَّازعَات 79:34

فَإِذَا جَآءَتِ ٱلطَّآمَّةُ ٱلۡكُبۡرَىٰ
