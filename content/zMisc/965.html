Yateem (orphan)

Pg. 965

Pdf pg. 983

Yateem: A male or female child whose father has passed away before they reached puberty. Literal meaning of yateem is to be different from anything else, alone, unparalleled, unequalled.

Duryateem: Unmatched

Yatama: In animals that kid whose mother died before it (the kid) reached maturity.

Allah SWT says (4:5): “And test (examine their behavior) the orphans until they reach the age of marriage (puberty). “
