ڈالنا- to throw- pour

These words   القى  سلك  نبذ قذف افرغ  اوقع     have been used in the Quran

1.     القى – لقى means two opposing things which come in front of each other, to meet up, but when it is used with baab ifaal then the root meaning changes القى – to throw or put something in such a way that it becomes apparent in front of the other person.

 

In Surah Al-Aaaraaf, (7;107)

 

فَأَلْقَىٰ عَصَاهُ فَإِذَا هِيَ ثُعْبَانٌ مُّبِينٌ

 

So Moses threw his staff, and suddenly it was a serpent, manifest.

 

And you can also use the word metaphorically as:  In Surah Taha:  (20:39):

 

وَأَلْقَيْتُ عَلَيْكَ مَحَبَّةً مِّنِّي 

 

 And I bestowed upon you love from Me

 

This word is also used for, to inspire which is called القاء (ilqaa) refer to دل میں بات ڈالنا

 

2.         سلك- the discussion has took place under the word داخل کرنا , the root meaning is to pull something  to put something in another, therefore according to that sometimes it means to put/to throw.

 

In Surah Qasas: (28:32):

 

اسْلُكْ يَدَكَ فِي جَيْبِكَ

 

Insert your hand into the opening of your garment;

 

 

3.      نبذ – the root meaning ‘to throw something which is of no interest/value, or to throw something behind your back, (the discussion has took place under the word پھینکنا , similar to the word سلك it is used also for ‘to put/to throw’).

 

In Surah Saafaat:   (37:145):

 

فَنَبَذْنَاهُ بِالْعَرَاءِ وَهُوَ سَقِيمٌ

 

But We threw him onto the open shore while he was ill

 

 

4.   قذف   -  to throw from faraway, but sometimes it is used for,  to put or to throw.

 

In Surah Hashar:  (59: 2):

 

وَقَذَفَ فِي قُلُوبِهِمُ الرُّعْبَ

and He cast terror into their hearts.

 

5.     افرغ- فرغ – to finish or complete a job/task شغل or to be empty. افرغ الدلو – means to pour water from the bucket whilst emptying it (MF) -  to put or pour something slowly.

 

In Surah Baqarah: (2:250):

 

رَبَّنَا أَفْرِغْ عَلَيْنَا صَبْرًا وَثَبِّتْ أَقْدَامَنَا 

 

"Our Lord, pour upon us patience and plant firmly our feet

 

In Surah Kahaf: (18:96):

 

آتُونِي أُفْرِغْ عَلَيْهِ قِطْرًا

 

"Bring me, that I may pour over it molten copper."

 

6.     اوقع- وقع there are two root meanings , to stay firm and to fall down, and اوقع means to befall/to make it happen  (MJD) means to place something and then make it firm.

 

In Surah Maidah: (5:91):

 

إِنَّمَا يُرِيدُ الشَّيْطَانُ أَن يُوقِعَ بَيْنَكُمُ الْعَدَاوَةَ وَالْبَغْضَاءَ 

Satan only wants to cause between you animosity and hatred

Summary:

1.             القى – to put/place something with ease which others can see

2.             سلك- to put/place something in another

3.               نبذ- to throw something away thinking it not worth of attention

4.             قذف- to place or to put with intensity and strength

5.             افرغ  - to place or put in such a manner similar to pouring water slowly from a bucket.

6.             اوقع- to place something and then to consolidate it.
