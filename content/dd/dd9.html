9- DHOONDNA [Search]

Qur’an has referred them by the words TALABA (طلب ) IBTAGHA (ابتغاء ) TAHASAS (تحسس ) TAJASASA (تجسس ) ILTAMASA (التمس ) JAASA (جاس )  BASARA (بعژا ) and TAHERA (تحرى ).

 

 

9.1    TALABA (طلب ): means to desire, want, find and means to search and quest to find and get something. It is used both in physical and spiritual sense.   It is mentioned in Qur’an:

Surat Al-Kahf (The Cave) - سورة الكهف 18:41

 اَوْ يُصْبِحَ مَاۗؤُهَا غَوْرًا فَلَنْ تَسْتَطِيْعَ لَهٗ طَلَبًا        

 Or its water will become sunken [into the earth], so you would never be able to seek it.

 

9.2   IBTAGHA (ابتغى ):  BAGHA (بغى ): Ibn-al-faras has told two meanings of BAGHA (بغى ):: TALABASHEE (طلب اشي ) JINS MINAL FASAD (جنس من الفساد ) means desire for a thing and a form of violence i.e. disobedience and rebellion etc. Imam Raghib combines these two terms and define it as “to go over or cross the limit of moderation to get something. And IBTAGHA (ابتغاء )is used in terms of seeking something by trying. Allah says:

Surat Al-'An`ām (The Cattle) - سورة الأنعام 6:35

 فَاِنِ اسْتَطَعْتَ اَنْ تَبْتَغِيَ نَفَقًا فِي الْاَرْضِ اَوْ سُلَّمًا فِي السَّمَاۗءِ

 If you are able to seek a tunnel into the earth or a stairway into the sky to bring them a sign.

 

 

9.3   TAHASASA  (تحسس ):   AHASA (احس ): means to feel something and TAHASASA (تحسس ): means to make full use of five senses in search of something. Try hard to find a missing thing. This is mehmood attribute. It is mentioned in Qur’an:

Surat Yūsuf (Joseph) - سورة يوسف 12:87

   يٰبَنِيَّ اذْهَبُوْا فَتَحَسَّسُوْا مِنْ يُّوْسُفَ وَاَخِيْهِ

 O my sons, go and find out about Youssef and his brother.

9.4  TAJASASA (تجسس ): JASSA (جس ): means to know the inner condition of the patient by checking his pulse. It is specific with HASSA (حس ): in HASSA (حس ):, the five senses are utilized to unfold the outside conditions while JASSA (جس )is called as finding inner situations.  And the word JASOOS (جاسوس ) is also related to it. And TAJASASA (تجسس )means to fossick/search something secret, reconnoitre, spying and trying to find someone's faults and weaknesses. Then inform the others. And this attribute is mazmum. Allah says:

Surat Al-Ĥujurāt (The Rooms) - سورة الحجرات 49:12

وَّلَا تَجَسَّسُوْا وَلَا يَغْتَبْ بَّعْضُكُمْ بَعْضًا  

 And do not spy or backbite each other.

9.5          ILTAMASA  (التمس ):  LAMASA (لمس ): means to fossick something with hands or fingers and ILTAMASA  (التمس )means to find an object by touching it. Demand an object repeatedly, continuously try to search. Allah says:

Surat Al-Ĥadīd (The Iron) - سورة الحديد 57:13

 قِيْلَ ارْجِعُوْا وَرَاۗءَكُمْ فَالْتَمِسُوْا نُوْرًاۭ

“It will be said, "Go back behind you and seek light.”

 

9.6  JASA  (جا س ) : means reaching an extreme in desire of something, greedily find out something:

Surat-Bani Israel ( سورة بنی اسراءیل) 17:5

  بَعَثْنَا عَلَيْكُمْ عِبَادًا لَّنَآ اُولِيْ بَاْسٍ شَدِيْدٍ فَجَاسُوْا خِلٰلَ الدِّيَار

 We sent against you servants of Ours - those of great military might, and they probed [even] into the homes.

9.7      BASARA  (بعژا ): means to find out and BASARAL MATTAA ( بعژالمتاع) means to overturn the stuff and BUSIRA (بعژ )word is a mixture of BUESA (بعث )and USERA (عژ )which contain the meanings of both verbs i.e. to raise the dead by overturning them.

Surat Al-Ĥujurāt (The Rooms) - سورة الحجرات 82:4

            وَاِذَا الْقُبُوْرُ بُعْثِرَتْ                          

And when the [contents of] graves are scattered.

 

And at another place is

Surat Al-`Ādiyāt (The Courser) - سورة العاديات 100:9

          اَفَلَا يَعْلَمُ اِذَا بُعْثِرَ مَا فِي الْقُبُوْرِ                    

But does he not know that when the contents of the graves are scattered.

 

 

9.8          TAHERA (  تحرى ): AL-HARA (الأحرى ) means more efficient, more appropriate, more better. And TAHERA (تحرى ) means to use the one which is more appropriate and efficient. To demand  the one which is better between the two things.

Surat Al-Jinn (The Jinn) - سورة الجن  72:14

           وَّاَنَّا مِنَّا الْمُسْلِمُوْنَ وَمِنَّا الْقٰسِطُوْنَ ۭ فَمَنْ اَسْلَمَ فَاُولٰۗىِٕكَ تَحَرَّوْا رَشَدًا                         

And among us are Muslims [in submission to Allah ], and among us are the unjust. And whoever has become Muslim - those have sought out the right course.

Summary:

9.1          TALB: is a common word. To demand something. To desire, to find.

9.2          IBTIGHA: to try hard in seeking something.

9.3          TAHASAS: to use five senses in searching something which is related to outside conditions.

9.4          ILTAMASA: repeatedly try to search something.  

9.5          JAASA: reaching an extreme in desire of something, greedily find out something

9.6          BASARA: find out something by overturning.

9.7          TAHARA: to search for better and most appropriate.
