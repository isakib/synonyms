8- DHALNA [Descend]

 

Qur’an has referred them by the words ZAWAAL (زوال )  and DULUK (دلوك ).

 

Page-524

8.1     ZAAL (زال ) :  ZOL (زول ) ZAALATISHAMS (زالتيشمش ) downward movement of sun. and ZAWAAL (زوال )means downward movement of sun from the head and the time of downward movement. And ZAWAAL (زوال ) is also used for an object reaching to its extreme height and then coming down slowly. Antonym is UROOJH and ZAWAAL (زوال )also refers to a thing that is drawn to a specific side after leaving its right direction. And ZAALA UNHO MULKOHU (زال عنه ملكه ) means his government has been dissolved/erased. Allah says:

Surat 'Ibrāhīm (Abrahim) - سورة ابراهيم 14:44

 اَوَلَمْ تَكُوْنُوْٓا اَقْسَمْتُمْ مِّنْ قَبْلُ مَا لَكُمْ مِّنْ زَوَالٍ  

Had you not sworn, before, that for you there would be no cessation?

 

8.2 DULUK (دلو ك ) : DALKAT ISHAMSO DULUKA (دلکت اشمس دلوکا ) Bend down/bow down of the sun during sunset and ADALKA (ادلک ):means looseness:

(Surat-Bani Israel) سورة بنی اسراءیل 17:78

 اَقِمِ الصَّلٰوةَ لِدُلُوْكِ الشَّمْسِ اِلٰى غَسَقِ الَّيْلِ وَقُرْاٰنَ الْفَجْر

Establish prayer at the decline of the sun [from its meridian] until the darkness of the night and [also] the Qur'an of dawn.

Summary:

ZAWAAL (زوال ) : Is used for coming down from height and DULUK (دلوك ): shows a strong notion of strength and looseness.
