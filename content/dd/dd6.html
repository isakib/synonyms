6- DHAAL [Shield]

Qur’an has included the words JUNAH (جنة ), and URZA (عرضثہ).

 

 

 

6.1  JUNA (جنة ): DHAAL: that known weapon which is used to prevent enemy attack, to stop the enemy attack by putting it before his weapon and to rescue yourself. It is used in both physical and spiritual manners.

Surat Al-Mujādila (The Pleading Woman) - سورة المجادلة 58:16

 اِتَّخَذُوْٓا اَيْمَانَهُمْ جُنَّةً فَصَدُّوْا عَنْ سَبِيْلِ اللّٰه

They took their [false] oaths as a cover, so they averted [people] from the way of Allah.

 

 

6.2  URZATUN (عر ظة ) :Araza: means to present and URZA (عرضثہ) Is everything which can save you by putting in front, it can be a shield or any other thing. and URZA (عرضثہ) means target. It is said that and HOWA URZATUN LINAASE (عرضثہ هوا)   means he has been targeted by peoples slandering and defamation. HOWA URZATUN LILKALAAM (للكلام عرضثہ هوا) means he is the target of objections. Hence ARZA (عرضثہ)  is used in deeper concept than JUNAH (جنة ). Allah says:

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة 2:224

 وَلَا تَجْعَلُوا اللّٰهَ عُرْضَةً لِّاَيْمَانِكُمٌ        

And do not make [your oath by] Allah a target.
