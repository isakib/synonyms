To plan-8

Synonyms are:

1)    دَبَّرَ

2)    كاد

(كيد)

3)    مَكَرَ

4)    حيلَة

1)dabara دَبَّرَ:

dubr دُبَر means back or the

backside of anything. And dabara دَبَّرَ

means to focus on the result of a task to ponder over it and to sort out way

for it Mufraddat – مفردات .

Allah says:

وَمَن يُدَبِّرُ الْأَمْرَ ۚ

فَسَيَقُولُونَ اللَّـهُ

10:31 (surah 10 yoonas verse

31)

Translation:

And Who plans all matters?” So they will now say, “Allah.

2)keed

كَيْد: plan secretly to  accomplish a

task .to play or to trick Mufraddat – مفردات ..and keedo

sahiriin كَيْدُسَاحِرٍ means trick of sorcerer. If the aim of such plan is

right and virtues than this kind of plan is correct and allowed.

كَذَٰلِكَ كِدْنَا لِيُوسُفَ

12:76 (surah 12

yousaf verse 76)

Translation: Thus did We plan for Joseph.

And if the aim is wrong than it is forbidden.

Allah says:

فَإِن

كَانَ لَكُمْ كَيْدٌ فَكِيدُونِ

77:39 (surah 77

al mursallat  verse 39)

Translation: So if you have a plan, then plan against Me.

3)makara مَكَرَ:  deceive and lie(artful) to accomplish a task

Maqaayeesul-lughat .and it also means to secretly

introduce sumthing abhorrent to sumone Alfurooqul-lughwiyyah

214. This word  usually gives a negative meaning(interpretation).makar

مكر  means cheat and makaar مَكّار means

cheater(deciver) and ill natured(one with bad reputation)

Maqaayeesul-lughat.and imam Raghib tells the

meaning of this word as to turn a person away from his/her aim through

 pretext and excuse. And if it is attributed

to Allah than it means to take an action against deceitful people.

like Allah says اللَّـهُ

يَسْتَهْزِئُ بِهِمْ “Allah mocks them” 2:15 (surah baqarah verse

15).and in case of competing/opposing this is permitted.

Allah says:

وَمَكَرُوا وَمَكَرَ اللَّـهُ ۖ وَاللَّـهُ خَيْرُ الْمَاكِرِينَ

3:54 (surah 3 al

e imraan verse 54)

Translation: And

the disbelievers planned, but Allah planned. And Allah is the best of planners

4)heelah حيلَة :from hool حول means cleverness, far sightedness. The

ability to Seek to

obtain (something, typically food or money) at the expense or through the

generosity of others or by stealth.its plural is heial حِيَل or hiwal

حِوَل and it means cheat and deceive Miraatul Quraan. Heelaa حيله

actually is a task perform

in such a way to derive benefit or to repel harm so that one can escape from

the grip of law and people.

For example before

the start of New Year to gift ones asset to wife so that he don’t have to pay

zakat. and then after some time ask wife to gift it back to him.

This is cleverness

and is cheating and also planning but not like maker مكر or kaiid كَيْد  it’s

not a hidden plan.

Allah says:

وَالنِّسَاءِ وَالْوِلْدَانِ لَا يَسْتَطِيعُونَ

حِيلَةً وَلَا يَهْتَدُونَ سَبِيلًا

4:98 (surah 4 an nisa verse  98)

Translation: women

and children who cannot devise a plan nor are they directed to a way

Summary:

1)dabra:to keep the

result of some task in front and ponder over it.

2)keeidd:a hidden plan

to introduce something condemn to some one.And if cheat is on low scale than it

is called makaar and if on large scale than it is kaeeid.

3)maker:if it is

for a good aim then it is correct and if the aim is wrong then it is forbidden

and usually maker word is taken in negative senses.

4)heela:

through  cleverness and in cunning way steal something.
