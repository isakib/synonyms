25- Giving strength/ability توفيق دينا /هونا

 

 

For this وَفَقَ   and اَوزَعَ        have been used in the Quran.

 

1- وَفَقَ means   harmony and compatibility between two things and        means to make suitable. And وَفَقَ الله   means   making a thing suitable for some one by Almighty Allah .He says;

 

اِن يُرِيدَآاِصلاَحاًيُوَفِّقِ اللهُ بَينَهُمَا

If they both desire reconciliation, Allah will cause it  between them. (4:35)

 

1-             اَوزَعَ  وَزَعَ means to restrict, and وَزَعَ الجيش  means keeping the army in their line and length and وزَّعَ and اَوزَعَ  means to divide, derange and اَوزَعَ الشَّي  means to get revelation, and استوزع الله شكره  asking Allah for the ability of gratitude, thankfulness. And اوزع الله فلانا means Allah has given that some one the ability to be thankful for what he has, Allah Says;

 

قَاَلَ رَبِي اَوزِعنِي اَن اَشكُرَنِعمَتِكَ الَّتِي اَنعَمتَ عَليَّ وَ عَلى وَالِدَىَّ

My Lord, enable me to be grateful for Your favor which You have bestowed upon me and upon my parents. (46:15)

 

 

 

Summary:

 

1-Wafaqa Allah وَ فَّقَ الله :  Allah making suitable/ favorable circumstances for some one .

 

2-Aao’za Allah  اَوزَعَ الله : Allah making some one’s heart to be convinced /inclined for some task.
