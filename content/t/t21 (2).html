21=Poverty  تنگدستي

 

 

فَقَرَ,  مَسْكَنَة, عَيْلَةً , عيل , اِملآق , قَتَر ,  باسآء ,and مَترَبَة  are used for this,

 

1- فَقَرَ means poorness and beggary         فَقَرَ     and   فَقَرَه  are used to calling back of the spinal cord and its plural is فِقار , فَقَرَه  means spinal cord—and  فَقْرُ is used for breaking the trail of back bone or spinal cord. فَقْرته الداهيَةُ is used when saying such misery has fallen upon him which in turn broke his back bone. Therefore فَقَرَ , poorness is defined as something which breaks the backbone or extreme indigence. Allah SWT says;

 

اِنٌ يَكُونُو فُقرآءَ يُغنِهِمُ اللهُ مِنٌ فَضٌلِه

 If they should be poor, Allah will enrich them from His bounty   (24:32)

 

 

2- مَسكَنَه , مَسكِينis a person who doesn’t have sufficient necessities of life ,For some people مَسكِين are those who make their ends meet with great difficulty and is the owner of nothing not even a place to live. There has been a disagreement on his word among lexicographers as to whether economically, a beggar is much weaker then a poor. According to Quran;

 

اِنّمَا الصّدَقاَتُ لِلْفُقَرآءِ وَالْمَسكِينْ

Zakah expenditures are only for the poor and for the needy  (9:60)

 

It is apparent that poor is much needy and deprived then the other. Allah SWT says;

 

اَمّاَ السَّفِيْنَةُ فَكاَنَتْ لِمسكِيْنَ يَعْمَلُونَ فِي الْبَحْر

As for the ship, it belonged to poor people working at sea.  (17:80)

 

In the above quoted verse coming of word beggar before poor stresses on the same thing and following verse shows that poor, مَسكِين could be an owner.

 

 

3- عَيْلَةً  is something which makes a human being dependable/burdened and would become afflicted. And عيل  refers to such people who are afflicted because of which a person is under a lot of responsibilities to support them for their expenses to make a living. So عيلة is such poorness and dependability which is caused by his family members of dependeds.Allah SWT says;

 

فَاِنْ خِفْتُمْ عَيْلَةً فَسَوفَ يُغْنِيكُمْ اللهُ مِنْ فَضلِه

And if you fear privation, Allah will enrich you from His bounty  (9:28)

 

 

 

4- املاق: is not actually poorness or dependency ,it is actually the doubt of having to loose one’s wealth or savings ,It is also said  املق الدهرماله ,time had lost his wealth, and اَملَقَ   means loosing one’s wealth currently owned, Allah SWT says;

 

وَلاَ تَقْتُلُوآ اَوْلادَكُمْ خَشْيَةَاِمْلَاقٍ

And do not kill your children for fear of poverty.  (17:31)

 

And it has also been used in the same manner   خشيته انفاق in the Quran.

 

 

5-      قتر  meaning needy, resource less, impoverished, are used in the Quran for this, for example :

 

وَمَتِّعُوهُنَّ عَلَئ الْمُوسِعِ قَدَرُه وَعَلَى الْمُقْتِرِقَدَرُه

But lexicographers take their meaning as limiting living resources for their family, like مقتر  is a man who is himself not resource less but doesn’t spend much on his dependents, according to his economical status..

 

وَاْلَذِينَ اِذآ اَنفَقُوا لَمْ يٌسرَفُو وَ لَم يَقتُرُو وَ كَانَ بَيْنَ ذلِكَ قَوَّاماً

And [they are] those who, when they spend, do so not excessively or sparingly but are ever, between that, [justly] moderate  (25:67)

 

 

 

6-  بَاسآء :  بَءِسَ يَبْءِسَ  means being  in a great need, and باس  means disaster, war and hunger and   usually باسآء  is used for economical crisis and starvation, and its opposite /antonym is  سراء prosperity and period of happiness, meaning مسرة في اليسر     and  بَاسآء  means such a problem which also involved terror, Allah SWT says;

 

وَاَطْعِمُو الْباَءِسَ الْفَقِيرْ

feed the miserable and poor.  (22:28)

 

Similarly   مَسّتْهُمُ الْباَسآءُوَالْضَرّآءُ ,  باسآ  means economical crisis, and  ضرآء  means other difficulties.

 

 

7-  مَترَبَه  :  تُرَاب  means dry mud,and تَرِب   يَترُبُ تَرْباً مَترَباً  means a man is so poor that he was dusted(not literally),Quran says;

 

اَوْ اِطْعاَمُ فِيْ يَوْمٍ ذِيْ مَسْفَبَةٍ يَتِيماً ذَا مَقْرَبَةٍ اَوْ مِسْكِيناً ذَا مَترَبَةٍ

Or feeding on a day of severe hunger, An orphan of near relationship, Or a needy person in misery  (90:14-16)

 

 

Summary:

 

Faqara فقر :   Extreme poverty

 

Muskanat مسكنت   To make ends meet

 

Ailah  عيلة   Burdened due to the expenses of family/dependents.

 

Imlaaq املاق   Worrying/Concerned about being broke

 

Qatr قتر        being miser in spending on family

 

Ba’saa بَاسآء   Phase of starvation

 

Matraba مَترَبَه Disgusted because of poverty
