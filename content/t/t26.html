26-Layers /Over lapped ته بر ته

 

 

For this طِباَق (طَبَق ) مركوم رُكَام  رُكَم  مُتَرَاكِبَاً  رَكَب نَضِيْد مَنضُودْ and كِسَف have been used.

 

 

1-           طَبَق Placing one thing over or besides another thing, so that they would fit together, making it looking equal in size. Allah SWT says;

 

اَلَّذِي خَلَقَ سَبْعَ سَمَواتٍ طِباَقاً

[And] who created seven heavens in layers. (67:3)

 

 

2-           رَكَمَ  means accumulation of something, heap, without any order, putting things together with out any manner or specific order, placing them left right centre and رُكام and مركوم  means layers of clouds. Allah SWT says ;

 

اَلَم تَرَاَنَّ اللهَ يُزجِيْ سَحاَباً ثُمَ يُوءَلِّفُ بَيْنَهُ ثُمَّ يَجْعَلُهُ رُكاَماً

Do you not see that Allah drives clouds? Then He brings them together, then He makes them into a mass. (24:43)

 

At other place it is Said;

وَ يَجْعَلَ الْخَبِيثَ بَعْضَهُ عَلي بَعْضٍ فَيَرْكُمَهُ جَمِيعاً

and place the wicked some of them upon others and heap them all together. (8:37)

 

 

3-           تراكب  : ركب means to take a ride, and ركييب means the back rider amongst the two .Or something which is conjoined like  pearls in the ring and تراكب means over lapping in such a way that there is some order or arrangement. And متراكب  means associated, adjoined, linked .Allah says;

 

فَاَخْرَجْناَ مِنْهُ خَضِراً نَخْرِجُ مِنْهُ حَباً مُتَرَاكِباً

We produce from it greenery from which We produce grains arranged in layers. (6:99)

4-           نَّضِيْد : نَضَدَ means gradually collecting a mass, or picking a quantity of something house hold stuff etc. and تنضدت اللنسان means arrangement of the teeth, meaning accumulating in such a way that it has some nice distinct arrangement. Allah SWT says;

 

وَانَّخْلُ يسِقتٍ لَهاَ طَلْعُ نَّضِيْد

And lofty palm trees having fruit arranged in layers – (59:10)

 

 

5-           كِسَف كِسْفَةُ means something feathery , like a cloud or cotton bud  ,whose plural is كِسْفُ  and كِسَفاً  are those feather like  pieces folded on top of each other, big or small.Allah SWT says;

 

اَللهُ اَّلذِي يُرسِلُ الِّريحَ فَتُثِيرُ سَحَاباً فَيَبسُطُهُ ِفي َالَّسمآءِ كَيْفَ يَشآءُ وَ يَجْعَلُهُ كِسَفاً

It is Allah who sends the winds, and they stir the clouds and spread them in the sky however He wills, and He makes them fragments.  (30:48)

 

 

 

 

Summary:

 

1-Tabaaq  طِباَق : Placing something above something, of same size.

 

2-Rakam  ركم : Accumulating things left right centre up down without any specific order.

 

3-Tarakib تراكب : Placing things in a proper arrangement and in such a manner that they would join together.

 

4-Nazad نَضَد :  Arranging things in a nice decorative manner.

 

5-Kisafa كِسَف : Placing feathery matter .such as clouds, cotton buds

 in layers.
