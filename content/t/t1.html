Star (types)-1

Synonyms are

1) نجم

2) كوكب

1)najam نجم:basically it is used in two ways (a)for the plants that have no stems or herbs and shrubs and bushes.(b) planets that rise and set. And its plural is najom نجوم.and it is used as a generic noun.

Allah says

وَبِالنَّجْمِ هُمْ يَهْتَدُونَ

16:16 (surah 16 an nahl,verse 16)

Transaltion: and by the stars (men) guide themselves.

2)kawakib كوكب: كوكبة kokabaah a name of a big star Munjid - منجدand all big stars are called kokab كوكب even if it is a planet or a star Fiqhul lughat - فقه اللغة and its plural Is kawakib كواكبand kokab al hadeed كوكب الحديد means the glitter of iron, shining or glowing Munjid - منجد. so a kokab is a star which is huge and also radiant/bright.

ALLAh says in quran:

إِنَّا زَيَّنَّا السَّمَاءَ الدُّنْيَا بِزِينَةٍ الْكَوَاكِبِ

37:6 (surah 37 as saffat,verse 6)

Transaltion: Verily We have adorned the near heaven with the stars (for beauty).

In quran following stars are mentioned

1)shiraa شِعرٰئ: a name of a bright/اustorous star which rises in very hot weather Mufraddat – مفردات.in the times of ignorance people use to worship it(for more details check ض 1/5 معبودان باطل)

ALLAh says to unbelievers in quran:

وَأَنَّهُ هُوَ رَبُّ الشِّعْرَىٰ

53:29 (Surah 53 an najm,verse 29)

Transaltion: That He is the Lord of Sirius (the Mighty Star);

2)khanusa خُنَّسَ :means moving back and shrinking Munjid - منجد.walking straight and then talking a step behind Mufraddat – مفردات .to hide and move back Maqaayeesul-lughat - مقائيس اللغة.khanis خانِس means the one who moves back and khunas خُنَّسَ is its plural.so khunas are those planets that moves in straight way and then starts moving behind.

Accordind to some people

Venus,Jupiter,Saturn,mars and mercury are those planets Munjid - منجد thts why they are called khamsa mutaheera خمسہ متحیرہ (five sumthing )

3)jawar-jaar -joraan جوار- جار -جوراً:to move on a side from the way.and jawaar جوار means those planets which keeps on moving in a straight way and sometimes change a little bit direction.

4)kunass كنَّس menas to hide to disappear and kanas كناس menas the shelter of a dear and kaanis كانس means the dear who enters in the shelter and the plural of kanis is kunass كنَّس which means planets that suddenly disappear during their rotation.

ALLAH SAYS

فَلَا أُقْسِمُ بِالْخُنَّسِ الْجَوَارِ الْكُنَّسِ

81:15-16 (surah 81 at takweer,verse 15-16)

Translation: So verily I call to witness the planets - that recede Go straight, or hide.
