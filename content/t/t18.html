مَعَرة  is the disease of itching to the entire body ,similarly مَعَرة  is spoken when speaking of ignorance/ harms and lexicographers interpret this word as sin, inconvenience, flaw, evil, guilt ,fault, bad mouthing, and rudeness etc. So this word has very vast meanings and interpretations, which describes every kind of suffering, grief and sadness. Allah SWT says in the Quran;

 

فَتُصِيَبكُمْ مِنْهُمْ مَعَرّةُ بِغَيْرِ عِلْمٍ

And there would befall you because of them dishonor without [your] knowledge -  (48:25)

 

 

Summary

 

 

1-Zarra ضرَّ = Taken as loss. Opposite of benefit,.

 

2-Zurr ضُرِ = for physical illness and problems

 

3-Zarra ضَرّآء= for the phase of temporary, superficial hardships.

 

4-azzan اذن = for minor troubles

 

5-Kurhan كُرهاً = is used for forced troubles

 

6- Ma’aaraa مَعَرَّة = is used of every kind of trouble and nuisance.
