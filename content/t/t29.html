29: Less:

Qaleel  قَليل ,fateel   فتيل ,naqeer نَقير ,qatmeer  قطمير,fawaaq   فواق are the words used.

1.        Qaleel  قَليل : Opposite is qaseer. Meaning less as compared to more. Its a commonly used word. May it be quantity,weight,distance,duration  etc.It is used for all.

 

Allah says in the Quran: 2:41 : Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة

وَآمِنُوا بِمَا أَنزَلْتُ مُصَدِّقًا لِّمَا مَعَكُمْ وَلَا تَكُونُوا أَوَّلَ كَافِرٍ بِهِ ۖ وَلَا تَشْتَرُوا بِآيَاتِي ثَمَنًا قَلِيلًا وَإِيَّايَ فَاتَّقُونِ

And believe in what I have sent down confirming that which is [already] with you, and be not the first to disbelieve in it. And do not exchange My signs for a small price, and fear [only] Me.

2.        Fateel   فتيل: fatal means dividing/twisted rope etc. Fateel   فتيل is divided/twisted rope. Its feminine is fateela فتيله .( it is that small twisted rope used in a oil lamp. Fateelaفتيله   is also used for that very thin thread which is in the middle of the seed of the date(fruit). This word is basically used for very small quantity/amount etc.

 

In the Quran: 4:49: Surat An-Nisā' (The Women) - سورة النساء

أَلَمْ تَرَ إِلَى الَّذِينَ يُزَكُّونَ أَنفُسَهُم ۚ بَلِ اللَّهُ يُزَكِّي مَن يَشَاءُ وَلَا يُظْلَمُونَ فَتِيلًا

Have you not seen those who claim themselves to be pure? Rather, Allah purifies whom He wills, and injustice is not done to them, [even] as much as a thread [inside a date seed].

3.        Naqeer  نَقير : Naqar al teer  نقر الطير means the bird touched/pecked with its beak. & naqar alteayr alhab  نقر الطير الحب means bird picked seeds with its beak. Mankaar  منقار means beak & naqeer  نَقير  is the small mark on the seed of the date fruit. (because it looks like a bird touched/pecked it with its beak) & naqeer  نَقير also signifies very small amount of something.

Allah says in the Quran:4:53: Surat An-Nisā' (The Women) - سورة النساء

أَمْ لَهُمْ نَصِيبٌ مِّنَ الْمُلْكِ فَإِذًا لَّا يُؤْتُونَ النَّاسَ نَقِيرًا

Or have they a share of dominion? Then [if that were so], they would not give the people [even as much as] the speck on a date seed.

4.        Qitmeer   قطمير: That very fine/thin membrane on date seed which is very thin & almost like nothing. There is a proverb, مَا اَصَبتُ عَنهُ قِطميرًا meaning i didn’t get anything out of him.

Allah says in the Quran:35:13 : Surat Fāţir (Originator) - سورة فاطر

يُولِجُ اللَّيْلَ فِي النَّهَارِ وَيُولِجُ النَّهَارَ فِي اللَّيْلِ وَسَخَّرَ الشَّمْسَ وَالْقَمَرَ كُلٌّ يَجْرِي لِأَجَلٍ مُّسَمًّى ۚ ذَٰلِكُمُ اللَّهُ رَبُّكُمْ لَهُ الْمُلْكُ ۚ وَالَّذِينَ تَدْعُونَ مِن دُونِهِ مَا يَمْلِكُونَ مِن قِطْمِيرٍ

He causes the night to enter the day, and He causes the day to enter the night and has subjected the sun and the moon - each running [its course] for a specified term. That is Allah , your Lord; to Him belongs sovereignty. And those whom you invoke other than Him do not possess [as much as] the membrane of a date seed.

5.        Fawaaq فواق :The duration between milking of a cow /other animal. Meaning the duration between the opening & closing/pressing of hands of the milk man on the udders. This describes very less duration.

Allah says in the Quran: 38:15 : Surat Şād (The Letter "Saad") - سورة ص

وَمَا يَنظُرُ هَٰؤُلَاءِ إِلَّا صَيْحَةً وَاحِدَةً مَّا لَهَا مِن فَوَاقٍ

And these [disbelievers] await not but one blast [of the Horn]; for it there will be no delay.

 

 

Summary:

1.        Qaleel  قَليل: Less quantity in comparison of more (of anything).COmonly used word

2.        Fateel فتيل : for a small quantity/amount

3.        Naqeer  نَقير . For a very small amount/quantity

4.        Qitmeer   قطمير: That small quantity which is almost like none.

5.        Fawaaq فواق : Used for a small duration.

 

Note: Similarly, khar dill (seed of rai) & zarra are also used to describe small quantities.
