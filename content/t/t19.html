19 = having or giving Trouble/Difficulty  تكليف اتهانا

 

اَذًى , اذى , يُوْذِي ,and ضَرّ , ضُرّ , يَضُرّ and from كُرْهاً  to اَكْرَهَ , يُكْرِهُ have been used to give the meaning of trouble and inconvenience in Quran. اذي , يوذي are narrated previously now rest of the examples are as follows;

 

1-  وَلَا تكون كَالذين اذو مُوسى

Like those who abused Moses         (33:69)

 

 

2- و إِن تَصبِرو و تّتقو لَا يَضروكم كيدوهم شيَا

If you are patient and fear Allah, their plot will not harm you at all.         (3:120)

 

 

And ضَار (يضَار) means to hurt one another trouble or harm each other, Quran says;

 

لَا تضَار وَالدة بِوَالدِهَا وَلَامولودله بوَالده

No mother should be harmed through her child, and no father through his child. And upon the [father's] heir is [a duty] like that [of the father].          (2:233)

 

3- كُرهُ Means forced drudgery and اكرة   means involving some one in some kind of hardship/exertion which is against his will /nature.

 

Forcing some one to do something which he is not willing to do. Allah SWT says;

وَلَاتُكرِهُو فَتَىتِكُمٌ عَلَى الْبِغاءِ

And do not compel your slave girls to prostitution.  (24:33)

 

 

Other than this, كَلفَ   شق  عَنَتَ  and سَام  are used in the similar way.

 

4-Kallafa كَلفَ : means utilizing something beyond its capacity, or obliging on  something harder to follow, Allah SWT says;

 

لآيُكلِفُ اللهُ نفْساً اِلآ وُسْعَها

Allah does not charge a soul except [with that within] its capacity.  (2:286)

 

And trouble is both liked and imposed/disgraceful. Liked trouble is, doing a thing in such a way that it becomes easier for him and also becomes one of the loved tasks, and this very thing is illegal botheration. Disgraceful trouble is called fakery, meaning pretence/dramatizing of trouble.

 

 

5- شق means tearing apart شق, شقَّmeaning utilizing some one more then he could put his efforts into ,or involving some one in hard labour.Quran says;

 

وَ مآ اُرِيدُ انْ اشقَّ عَليكَ

And I do not wish to put you in difficulty.  (28:27)

 

 

6- عَنَتَ being in such a trouble which could lead to assassination, meaning extreme harm or great difficulty. Allah talah says;

 

ياايُهاَالْذِيْنَ امَنُوْ لآ تَتَّخِذُوْا بِطَانَةً مِنْ دُوْنِكُمْ لآ يَاْلُوْنَكُمْ خُباَلاً وَدُّوْا ماَ عَنِتُمْ

O you who have believed, do not take as intimates those other than yourselves, for they will not spare you [any] ruin. They wish you would have hardship. (3:118)

 

 

And اعُنَتَ means imposing some one trouble. Allah SWT says;

 

وَلو شآءَاللهُ

لَاَعْنَتِكُم

And if Allah had willed, He could have put you in difficulty. (2:220)

 

7- سَامَ means harming some one and سَامَخَسُفأ is an idiom which means demoralizing some one, سَامَ means such a trouble which is also embarrassing and undermining the morale, سَامَ depicts a trouble which involves dishonoring and discomposure. Quran says;

 

يَسومُونَكُمْ سُوْءالعذابِ يذبِحونَ اَبْناءَكُمْ وَ يَسْتَحْيُونَ نِسآءَكُمْ

People of Pharaoh, who afflicted you with the worst torment, slaughtering your [newborn] sons and keeping your females alive.  (2:49)

 

 

Summary:

 

1-      اذي   Azza         For minor suffering, mental or physical.

2-      ضَرَ  Zarra       Major trouble and mishap.

3-      اكرة  Akra         Forced suffering, torture.

4-      تَكليف  Taklef     Utilizing according to capacity.

5-      مشقَ   mashaq   Utilizing beyond capacity

6-      عَنَتَ   Aanata    a kind of trouble which leads to killings.

7-      سَامَ    Samaa     A kind of trouble which is demoralizing to one’s character.
