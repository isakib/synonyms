Throne-6

Synonyms are:

1)      عرش

2)      ارآ

ئكّ is plural of اريكه

3)      سُرُر

is plural of سرير

1)عرش aarsh:

means royal throne سريرالملك (throne

of king) . Fiqhul lughat 230

Allah says:

 وَرَفَعَ أَبَوَيْهِ عَلَى الْعَرْشِ

12:100 (surah 12

yousaf verse 100)

Translation: And

he raised his parents upon the throne.

عَرَش الْبَيْت arsh albeit  Means

roof of house. عَرَشَ يَعْرِشُ  aarsh yaresh to build a wooden house

so عرش البيت aarsh albeit  means a roof that is made up of log(beam)

and

hair.

2)ارآ ئكّ

aarayik:plural of اريكه arikha

it means tent bed or canopy bed or throne . Fiqhul

lughat - فقه اللغة comfortable

chair or a throne where one can recline .A decorated and adorned

throne. Munjid – منجد.

Allah says:

مُّتَّكِئِينَ فِيهَا عَلَى الْأَرَائِكِ

76:13 (surah 76

insaan verse 13)

Translation: They

will be] reclining therein on adorned couches

4)سُرُر

suror singular is  سرير sareer it means a bed without

canopy

or tent Fiqhul lughat - فقه اللغة 30  sareer almaiit سَريرُالْمَيِّت means bed

on which corpse are carried. And as per the author of munjid سرير

sareer word is also mostly used

for royal throne. And according to imam Raghib it means a throne (seat) which

well established people make for sitting on in a grand manner

Mufraddat – مفردات

Allah says :

  عَلَىٰ سُرُرٍ مَّوْضُونَةٍ مُّتَّكِئِينَ عَلَيْهَا مُتَقَابِلِينَ

56:15 (surah 56 al waqiyaah

15 verse)

Translation:

On thrones woven [with ornament], Reclining on them, facing each other.

And in above verse the word modunaah مَوضُوْنَةٍ has the word wadan

وَضَنَ means gold-lace work means to weave with gold and silver

wires Mufraddat – مفردات . so the above

verse translation can also be: on thrones woven with gold and silver wires.

Summary

1)aarsh:royal throne.

2)arekah:throne on which one can recline.

3)sareer: a throne on which well settled people sit grandly.
