28- Stop

Sakan سَكَنَ ,sakat, & rahwann are the words used.

1.        Sakan سَكَنَ : stopping after a movement is called sakoon سكون . Used for both obvious & for its meaning. Its opposite is harkat        حركت& iztarab اضطراب  .

Allah says in the Quran: ( 42:33): Surat Ash-Shūraá (The Consultation) - سورة الشورى

إِن يَشَأْ يُسْكِنِ الرِّيحَ فَيَظْلَلْنَ رَوَاكِدَ عَلَىٰ ظَهْرِهِ ۚ إِنَّ فِي ذَٰلِكَ لَآيَاتٍ لِّكُلِّ صَبَّارٍ شَكُورٍ

If He willed, He could still the wind, and they would remain motionless on its surface. Indeed in that are signs for everyone patient and grateful.

2.        Sakat  سَكَتَ : to be quiet. To stop talking/be quiet while talking. Sakeet سَكيت & saakoot  سَاكُوت is such a person who is stays quiet for most of his time. Sakat al ghazab  سَكَتَ الغَضَب means subsiding of anger.

Allah says in the Quran:7:154 : Surat Al-'A`rāf (The Heights) - سورة الأعراف

وَلَمَّا سَكَتَ عَن مُّوسَى الْغَضَبُ أَخَذَ الْأَلْوَاحَ ۖ وَفِي نُسْخَتِهَا هُدًى وَرَحْمَةٌ لِّلَّذِينَ هُمْ لِرَبِّهِمْ يَرْهَبُونَ

And when the anger subsided in Moses, he took up the tablets; and in their inscription was guidance and mercy for those who are fearful of their Lord.

3.        Rahwann  رَهوًا : raha yarhoo   رها يرهو means walk slowly. Raha al bahr   رَهَا البَحر means quietness/calmness/stillness of the sea. In other words the waves stop moving fast in the sea.(the sea becomes calm/still) . It also means water puddle/water collection on a flat ground.

 

Quran says: (44:24) Surat Ad-Dukhān (The Smoke) - سورة الدخان

وَاتْرُكِ الْبَحْرَ رَهْوًا ۖ إِنَّهُمْ جُندٌ مُّغْرَقُونَ

And leave the sea in stillness. Indeed, they are an army to be drowned."

Summary:

1.        Sakan  سَكَنَ: commonly used word. Whenever soething stops after moving then it is called sukoon.

2.        Sakat   سَكَتَ: to stop talking in the middle of conversation/talking

3.        Rahwann  رَهوًا : stillness of sea. When the waves stop moving.
