So

that-4

Synonyms are:

1)   لِ

2)   كَى

3)   اَنْ

4)   كَى

لا

5)   لِكَيْلا

6)   لِئَلا

These words are added with

مضارع madaray(the verb in the present form) and give a sense of

reasoning in it. First

three among them give a positive impression. لِ lii, كَى kaii are used

in the start of sentence whereas اَنْ  ann comes in between and last

three with

the addition of laa are compound  together

for giving a  negative impression.

For example:

1) لِ lii:

لِيَغْفِرَ

لَكَ اللَّهُ مَا تَقَدَّمَ مِنْ ذَنْبِكَ وَمَا تَأَخَّرَ

48:2 (surah 48 al fath

verse 2)

Translation: That

Allah may forgive for you what preceded of your sin and what will follow.

2) كَى  kaai:

كَيْ

تَقَرَّ عَيْنُهَا وَلَا تَحْزَنَ

20:40 (surah 20 taa haa verse 40)

Translation: that her eye might be cooled and she

should not grieve.

3)

اَنْ  ann

ذَٰلِكَ أَدْنَىٰ أَنْ تَقَرَّ

أَعْيُنُهُنَّ

33:51 (surah 33 al ahzaab verse 51)

Translation: this is most proper, so that their eyes

may be cool

4) كَى لا

kaai laa:

 كَيْ لَا يَكُونَ دُولَةً

بَيْنَ الْأَغْنِيَاءِ مِنْكُمْ

59:7 (surah 59 hashar verse 7)

Translation: so that it will not be a perpetual

distribution among the rich from among you

5) لِكَيْل likiala:

لِكَيْلَا تَحْزَنُوا عَلَىٰ مَا

فَاتَكُمْ

3:153 (surah 3 aal imraan verse 153)

Translation: so that you might not grieve at what

had escaped you.

لِئَلا lialla:

لِئَلَّا

يَكُونَ لِلنَّاسِ عَلَيْكُمْ حُجَّةٌ

2:150 (surah 2 al baqarah

verse 150)

Translation: so that

people shall have no accusation against you
