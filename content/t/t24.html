24-Breaking تورنا

 

 

For this نَكَثَ, نَقَضَ, انْقَضَ, فَقَرَ, جَدَّ are used in the Quran.

 

1- نَكَثَ means tearing something made of weave, and نَكَثَ

 Means unknotting a blanket or a camp cloth to weave/knit it again and is a person who disjoint something joint already, or something which is woven, it is often used for breaking promises, commitments, means breaking a promise, disobeying an obligation.

 

 

اَلاَ تُقَاتِلُونَ قَوْماً نَكَثُوآ اَيماَنَهُم

Would you not fight a people who broke their oaths (9:12)

 

 

2- نَقَضَ means falling apart, and its antonym is ابرم  meaning strengthening some issue/matter and this word is more common then نكث  like نقضت البنا   means demolishing some building, الْعَظمُ   breaking bone  , العقد untying a Knott,

Similarly نقضت الحبل        means straightening the curves of a rope then    نَقَضَis above then نكث  and this is used for breaking a firmly determined promise or commitment .Allah SWT says;

 

وَاَوفُو بِعَهدِاللهِ اِذاَ عَاهَدّْتُمْ وَلاَ تَنقُضُوا الْاَيْمَانَ بَعدَ تَوكِيدِهاَ

And fulfill the covenant of Allah when you have taken it, [O believers], and do not break oaths after their confirmation (16:91)

 

And at another place it is written as

 

اَلذِينَ يَنقُضُونَ عَهدِاللهَ مِن بَعدِ مِيثاَقِه

Who break the covenant of Allah after contracting it (2:27)

 

3- انْقَضَ means breaking something in a way that it would produce a loud sound, then it was started applying on different occasions, for example  اَنقَضَتِ الْدُجاَجَه   meaning a chicken producing sound while laying eggs   اَلاَدِيم leather squishing sound   اَنقَضَ اَصَابِعَهُ   means cracking the knuckles, اَنقَضَ اَلْحَمَلُ اَلْظُهَر        means having so much weight on the shoulders that it would start to hurt. Almighty Says;

 

وَوَضَعناَ عَنكَ وِزرَك الَّذِي اَنقَضَ ظَهرَك

 And We removed from you your burden, Which had weighed upon your back (94:3)

 

 

4- فَقَرَ this word is taken from انْقَضَ which is only used as breaking the back bone, however انْقَضَ

 is very common, الفقرةُ  and الفِقرةُ  are used to call the base of the spinal cord which has whole in it, and its plural is فقار  and   فقرات .and  فقرالخرز means  to have a whole for sewing the beads through it.and فاقرةُ means such a misery which in turn would break the back bone/spinal cord.

 

وَوُجُوهُ يَومَءِذٍ ابَاسِرَةُ تَظُنُ اَنْ يُفعَلَ بِهاَ فَاقِرَه

And [some] faces, that Day, will be contorted, Expecting that there will be done to them [something] backbreaking. (75:25)

 

 

5- جَدَّ breaking a hard thing into pieces.

 

 

 

Summary:

 

1-Nakasa  نَكَثَ :  Breaking or disobeying a promise or commitment etc, OR disjointing a thing which is   already joint .

 

2- Naqaza  نَقَضَ: Tearing something, falling apart, and breaking a firm decision or promise or spoiling it.

 

3-Anqaza  انْقَضَ: Breaking something in such a manner that it would produce a sound.

 

4-Faqara  فَقَرَ: Breaking the back bone

 

5-Jadda  جَدَّ: Breaking a hard thing into pieces.
