22- Indigence تنگي

 

For this عُسر حَرَج ضَنْكَ ضَيْق حَاجَة have been used,

 

1-            عُسر Usrun : means time of need, needy, destitution, and extreme difficulties be it economical or other wise. This word is commonly used and its antonym is  يُسر means ease, prosperity, contentment. Allah SWT says;

 

اِنِّ مَعَ الْعُسْرِ يُسْرًا  

Indeed, with hardship [will be] ease. (94:6)

 

 

2- حَرَج Haraj : Congestion in the heart, suffocation because of undue restrictions

 

وَمَا جَعَلَ عَلَيْكُمْ فِي الْدِيْنِ مِنْ حَرَج

and has not placed upon you in the religion any difficulty  (22:78)

 

 

3- ضَنْكَ Zanka : means difficulties in day to day lives, discontentment of the heart, stressed, ضناكة   means suffocated life.

 

وَمَنْ اَعْرَضَ عَنْ ذِكْرِي فَاِنَ لَهُ مَعِيشَة ًضَنْكاً

And whoever turns away from My remembrance - indeed, he will have a depressed life, (20:124)

 

 

 

4- ضَيْق Zayq : is used for the shortage of the place of living, and its antonym is distention ,spacious. Almighty Allah says in the Quran;

 

 

اِذآ اُلْقُوا مِنْهاَمَكَاناً ضَيِقاً

And when they are thrown into a narrow place (25:13)

 

Some times its opposite comes as رَحُب   as well like;

 

حَتى اِذَا ضَاقَتْ عَلَيهَمُ الْاَرْضُ بِماَ رَحُبَت

that the earth closed in on them in spite of its vastness, ( 9:118)

 

Then on other times   ضَيْق   is used for حَرَج , that is, suffocation of the heart, this also imply the meaning for ضَيْق   , such as,

 

وَلاَ تَكُ فِي ضَيْقٍ مِّمَّا يَمْكُرُونْ

And do not grieve over them and do not be in distress over what they conspire.  (16:127)

 

 

 

5-               حَاجَة Hajja : الحوج  means dependency, starvation ,poverty, and حاج  means to be dependent ,need, needy –Such hopeless feeling which is produced because of unfulfilled wishes or need, Allah SWT says;

 

وَلاَ يَجِدُونَ فِى صُدُورِهِمْ حَاجَةً مِمآ اُوتُو

They love those who emigrated to them and find not any want in their breasts. (59:9)

 

 

Summary:

 

1- Usra عسر: For the time of need

 

2- Haraja حَرَج Discontentment of the heart

 

3-Zanka ضَنْكَ Problems and stresses of life

 

4-Zayq ضَيْق Shortage of space

 

5-Hajja حَاجَة  Unsatisfied because of not getting what is desired or needed.
