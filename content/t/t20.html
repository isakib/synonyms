20= To be/Make healthy تندرست كرنا/ هونا

 

For this  شفاء   and اَبْرَءَ have been used,

 

 

1-           شفاء means getting rid of an illness, being composed, and means getting cured from a disease ,making some one healthy, Quran says;

 

وَاِذَا مَرِضتُ فَهُوَ يَشفِين

And when I fall sick, You are one who cure me .

 

and the way شَفى   is used for bodily diseases, it also been used for illnesses of soul and its relief, Allah SWT says;

 

وَ يَشْفِ صُدُورَ قَوْمٍ مُومِنِينْ    

and satisfy the breasts of a believing people        (9:14 )

 

 

2-   اَبْرَءَ        means discontented/dissatisfied and بُرَءُ  means getting rid of something detestable and  رَجُلُ بَرِىُ  means pious and righteous man, and بَرَءَ مِنَ الْمَرضْ   means getting healed, getting cured from an illness and  اَبْرَءَ الْمَرِيْض        means giving sufferer/patient relief from his illness.Quran says;

 

وَ اُبرِىءٌ الْاَكْمَهَ وَالْاَبْرَصَ وَاُخِي الْمَوْتى بِاِذنِ الله

 And I cure the blind and the leper, and I give life to the dead - by permission of Allah. (3:49)

 

 

3- اَفَاقَ   means coming back to conscious state after being in an state of unconscious/doped OR regaining strength from a feeble condition of health and according to lexicographers, اَفَاقَ   word is used only for becoming conscious from an unconscious state, Allah SWT says;

 

فَلَمَّا تَجَلّى رَبُّه ُلِلْجَبَلِ جَعَلُهُ دَكَّا وَ خَرَّ مُوسى صَعِقاً -فَلَمآ اَفَاقَ قَالَ سُبحنَكَ

And when Moses arrived at Our appointed time and his Lord spoke to him, he said, "My Lord, show me [Yourself] that I may look at You." [ Allah ] said, "You will not see Me, but look at the mountain; if it should remain in place, then you will see Me." But when his Lord appeared to the mountain, He rendered it level, and Moses fell unconscious. And when he awoke, he said, "Exalted are You! I have repented to You   (7:143)

 

 

Summary:

 

Shafaa   شَفى     Getting cured from an ailment.

 

Abraaa   اَبْرَءَ        Becoming conscious from an unconscious state

 

 

Aafaqa   اَفَاقَ   Getting rid from an extreme illness
