16. To respect/honour:

 

Azzam   عَظَّم& waqqar  وَقَّر are the words used.

1.        Azzam عَظَّم : Azm عَظم means grow as well as bone. i.e Azm عَظم has the meaning of  being grown up/big as well as strength and hardness. Azzam  عَظَّم means to think of someone as respectful & respect/honour the by heart. It is used for both living as well as non-living things.

Allah says in the Quran:22:32: Surat Al-Ĥaj (The Pilgrimage) - سورة الحج

 

ذَٰلِكَ وَمَن يُعَظِّمْ شَعَائِرَ اللَّهِ فَإِنَّهَا مِن تَقْوَى الْقُلُوبِ

That [is so]. And whoever honors the symbols of Allah - indeed, it is from the piety of hearts.

 

2.        Waqqar  وَقَّر  : Waqaar  وقار means glory/grandeur and seriousness. According to people of vocabulary,it eans seriousness as well as knowledge. Ibn e Faaris also writes its meaning as al hilm war razanat  الحلم والرّزانة. Waqqar   وَقَّر means to give respect /honour someone with a status/knowledge & do not do anything against his will. This word is only used for people.

As per Quran: 48:9: Surat Al-Fatĥ (The Victory) - سورة الفتح

لِّتُؤْمِنُوا بِاللَّهِ وَرَسُولِهِ وَتُعَزِّرُوهُ وَتُوَقِّرُوهُ وَتُسَبِّحُوهُ بُكْرَةً وَأَصِيلًا

That you [people] may believe in Allah and His Messenger and honor him and respect the Prophet and exalt Allah morning and afternoon.

 

Summary:

 

Azzam  عَظَّم is a common word whereas Waqqar   وَقَّر is only used for people/humans.
