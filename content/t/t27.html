27-Tired تهكنا

 

سَءَمَ  ,نَصِبَ,  ,عَيَّ  لَغَبَ  ,حَسَرَ ادَ (اود) have been used for this ;

 

1-            سَءَمَ  Being disheartened or fed up of bearing a thing for a very long time, Fed up/annoyed/bored of doing a thing repeatedly, this would be initial phase of tiredness. Allah says;

 

لَا يَسءَمُ الْاِنساَنُ مِن دُعآءِ الْخَيرْ

Man is not weary of supplication for good [things]. (41:49)

 

 

2-            نَصَبَ means to bury, and if it is  علم يعلم then it would produce a reverse meaning, that is, a man cant keep his balance due to tiredness .To work really hard and tiredness, exhaustion, because of hard work. Quran says;

 

فَلَمّاَ جَاوَزَا قَالَ لِفَتهُ اتِنَا غَدَآءَناَ لَقَد لَقِيناَ مِن سَفَرِناَهَذاَ نَصَباً

So when they had passed beyond it, [Moses] said to his boy, "Bring us our morning meal. We have certainly suffered in this, our journey, [much] fatigue."  (18:62)

 

 

 

3-             Getting weak and tired because of excessive hard work. Allah talah says;

 

وَ لَقَد خَلَقْناَالْسَموَاتِ وَالْاَرْضَ وَماَ بَيْنَهُماَ فِي سِتَّةِ اَيَّامٍ وَّماَ مَسَّناَ مِنْ لُغُوبٍ

And We did certainly create the heavens and earth and what is between them in six days, and there touched Us no weariness. (50:38)

 

 

 

4- عَيَّ Not being able to work more due to tiredness/ fatigue. اَلْعَيُّ  And اَلْعَيَّان   is called tired and دَاءُ عَياَءُ   , untreatable illness .Allah SWT says;

 

اَوَلَم يَرَوْا اَنَّ اللهَ الْذِيْ خَلَقَ الْسَموَاتِ وَالْاَرضَ وَلَم يَعْىَ بِخَلْقِهَنَّ

Do they not see that Allah , who created the heavens and earth and did not fail in their creation. (46:33)

 

 

 

 

5- حَسَرَ is the next level to اَلْعَيَّ  that is, feeling so restricted to move because of tiredness/fatigue.

 

 ثُمَّ اَرْجِعِ الْبَصَرَ خَاسِءًا وَّهُوَ حَسِيرْ

Then return [your] vision twice again. [Your] vision will return to you humbled while it is fatigued. (67:4)

 

 

 

 

1-           ادَ يَءُود  Real meaning is burden and being burdened, and due to which deviating from its path. And in common words, its meaning are getting tired because of burden. Allah SWT says;

 

وَسِعَ كُرْسِيُّهُ الْسَموَاتِ الْاَرْضَ وَلاَ يَءُدُهُ حِفضُهُماَ

His Kursi extends over the heavens and the earth, and their preservation tires Him not. (2:255)

 

 

 

 

 

Summary:

 

1-Sa’aa maa سَءَمَ Getting bored of some task.

 

 

2-Nasaba نَصِبَ   disorientation because of fatigue in the body.

 

 

3-Laghaba لَغَبَ  Getting weak because of tiredness.

 

 

4-Aayaa عَيَّ Not being able to work any more because of tiredness.

 

 

5-Hasara حَسَرَ  Next level of tiredness and fatigue to عَيَّ

 

 

7-            Aadaa ادَ  Getting tired because of burdens
