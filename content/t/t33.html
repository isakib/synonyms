33. Oil :

 

 

Zait زيت , duhan دُهن, & dihaan دِهان are the words used.

1.        Zait زيت : fruit from the olive tree tree is called zaitoona زيتونة & the oil from the fruit is called zait زيت . Zaat al taa’am زات الطعام means putting olive oil in food or brush olive oil on food. This oil ,for the purpose of burning is best in quality.

 

Allah says in the Quran: 24:35 : Surat An-Nūr (The Light) - سورة النور

 

اللَّهُ نُورُ السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضِ ۚ مَثَلُ نُورِهِ كَمِشْكَاةٍ فِيهَا مِصْبَاحٌ ۖ الْمِصْبَاحُ فِي زُجَاجَةٍ ۖ الزُّجَاجَةُ كَأَنَّهَا كَوْكَبٌ دُرِّيٌّ يُوقَدُ مِن شَجَرَةٍ مُّبَارَكَةٍ زَيْتُونَةٍ لَّا شَرْقِيَّةٍ وَلَا غَرْبِيَّةٍ يَكَادُ زَيْتُهَا يُضِيءُ وَلَوْ لَمْ تَمْسَسْهُ نَارٌ ۚ نُّورٌ عَلَىٰ نُورٍ ۗ يَهْدِي اللَّهُ لِنُورِهِ مَن يَشَاءُ ۚ وَيَضْرِبُ اللَّهُ الْأَمْثَالَ لِلنَّاسِ ۗ وَاللَّهُ بِكُلِّ شَيْءٍ عَلِيمٌ

Allah is the Light of the heavens and the earth. The example of His light is like a niche within which is a lamp, the lamp is within glass, the glass as if it were a pearly [white] star lit from [the oil of] a blessed olive tree, neither of the east nor of the west, whose oil would almost glow even if untouched by fire. Light upon light. Allah guides to His light whom He wills. And Allah presents examples for the people, and Allah is Knowing of all things.

 

2.        Duhn دُهن : It means oil,fat. This is a common word. May this oil/fat be of any kind like ,plant based, animal based. & dahan دَهَنَ & adhan اَدهَنَ means to fool someone,cheat someone. Taddahan تَدَهَّنَ means to make something slippery/oily with oil/fat.

 

Allah says in the Quran: 23:20 : Surat Al-Mu'minūn (The Believers) - سورة المؤمنون

 

وَشَجَرَةً تَخْرُجُ مِن طُورِ سَيْنَاءَ تَنبُتُ بِالدُّهْنِ وَصِبْغٍ لِّلْآكِلِينَ

And [We brought forth] a tree issuing from Mount Sinai which produces oil and food for those who eat.

3.        Dihaan دِهان : This means lees/scum of oil. slippery place, paint,warmish etc.

 

Allah says in the Quran: 55:37: Surat Ar-Raĥmān (The Beneficent) - سورة الرحمن

 

فَإِذَا انشَقَّتِ السَّمَاءُ فَكَانَتْ وَرْدَةً كَالدِّهَانِ

And when the heaven is split open and becomes rose-colored like oil –

 

Summary:

Zait  زيت means oil derived from olive. & duhn means every oil including olive oil. & may the oil be of any type, its lees/scum is dihaan دِهان.
