1- ZAJH KERNA [To Slaughter]

Qur’an has referred them by the words ZABAHA (ذبح ) ZAKAA (ذكى ) and NAHARA (نحر ).

 

 

1.1   ZABAHA (ذبح ): Running knife at the throat of an animal by following renowned sharia method. And to expel “hararat-e-Gherazi”. It is mentioned in Qur’an:

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة 2:71

           فَذَبَحُوْھَا وَمَا كَادُوْا يَفْعَلُوْنَ

  So they slaughtered her, but they could hardly do it.

1.2 ZAKAA (زكى ): to slaughter an animal in a manner that her life comes out quickly and comfortably. And means to read TAKBEER (تكبير ) on hunting animal and leave it. After that, slaughter it by following specific sharia method. Allah says:

Surat Al-Mā'idah (The Table Spread) - سورة المائدة 5:3        

   وَمَآ اَ كَلَ السَّبُعُ اِلَّا مَا ذَكَّيْتُمْ  

And those from which a wild animal has eaten, except what you [are able to] slaughter [before its death].

1.3    NAHAR (نحر ): means to strike on the chest, to slaughter. And NAHAR (نحر ) means upper part of chest and INTAHARA (انتحر ) means to attempt suicide. And YOUMALNAHR (انحر) means the day of sacrifice i.e. 10th date of ZILHAJ MONTH (ذي الحجة ). And NAHARA (نحر ) means to perform sacrifice or cut the throat or to contest. It means that the real meaning of NAHAR (نحر ) is to cut throat whether it is in the shape of slaughter or sacrifice. Whether it is done by knife or Burcha or the animal is cut in standing or lying.   It is mentioned in Qur’an:

Surat Al-Kawthar (The Abundance) - سورة الكوثر  108:3

   فَصَلِّ لِرَبِّكَ وَانْحَرْ

So pray to your Lord and sacrifice [to Him alone].

Summary:

1.1  ZABAHA: to slaughter an animal by following renowned Sharia method.

1.2  ZAKAH:  to take care of animals comfort while performing sacrifice.

1.3  NAHARA: Any way of cutting throat, whether it is sacrifice, slaughter or any other case.
