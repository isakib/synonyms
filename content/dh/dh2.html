2- ZERIA [Source]

Qur’an has referred them by the words SABAB and “WASEELA”.

  

2.1   SABAB (سبب  ): It is a rope by which we climb and descend from the palm tree: it is due to this association, anything which is used to get to other thing is called as SABAB (سبب  ) Then this word also applies to the path and the path-related equipment as well. Allah says:

Surat Al-Kahf (The Cave) - سورة الكهف 18:84

 فَاَتْبَعَ سَبَبًا وَاٰتَيْنٰهُ مِنْ كُلِّ شَيْءٍ سَبَبًا

 And We gave him to everything a way. So he followed a way.

2.2   WASEELA (وسيلة ): WASALA (وسل )means to reach a thing with allure. And WASAL-ILLALAH (الی اللہ وسل  ) means getting closer to Allah. And AL-WASTA (الواسطه ) and AL-WASEELA (الوسيله ) means source to get closer, status, level. Allah says

Surat Al-Mā'idah (The Table Spread) - سورة المائدة 5:35

 وَابْتَغُوْٓا اِلَيْهِ الْوَسِيْلَةَ وَجَاهِدُوْا فِيْ سَبِيْلِهٖ

 And seek the means [of nearness] to Him and strive in His cause.

 

Summary:

The word SABAB (سبب  ) is common for Source: and the source which is opted to draw closer, it is WASEELA (وسيلة ).
