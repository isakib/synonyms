3- ZILLAT [Disgrace]

 

Qur’an has referred them by the words ZILLA (زلة ) SAGHAR (صغار ) KHIZI (خزي ) and HON (هون )

 

 

3.1   ZILLA (زلة ) : means inferiority (antonym is superiority), weakness, to succumb to power and force. Allah says:

Surat 'Āli `Imrān (Family of Imran) - سورة آل عمران 3:112

 ضُرِبَتْ عَلَيْهِمُ الذِّلَّةُ اَيْنَ مَا ثُـقِفُوْٓا

They have been put under humiliation [by Allah ] wherever they are overtaken.

3.2   SAGHAR (صغار ): means the humiliation of becoming inferior after supremacy and its acceptance and acknowledgement. To accept the humiliation of becoming subdued after being ruler or independent.  Allah says

Surat Al-'An`ām (The Cattle) - سورة الأنعام 6:135

   ۭسَيُصِيْبُ الَّذِيْنَ اَجْرَمُوْا صَغَارٌ عِنْدَ اللّٰهِ

 There will afflict those who committed crimes debasement before Allah.

 

3.3   KHIZI  (خزی): means disgrace and shamefaced. Go down in the eyes of others because of evil deeds and if it is limited to oneself then it is called as Regret. Allah says

Surat Ţāhā (Ta-Ha) - سورة طه  20:134 

        لَقَالُوْا رَبَّنَا لَوْلَآ اَرْسَلْتَ اِلَيْنَا رَسُوْلًا فَنَتَّبِعَ اٰيٰتِكَ مِنْ قَبْلِ اَنْ نَّذِلَّ وَنَخْزٰى  

They would have said, "Our Lord, why did You not send to us a messenger so we could have followed Your verses before we were humiliated and disgraced?"

3.4   HON (  ھون ): means insignificance, humiliation and disgrace, insult. It is in Qur’an

Surat An-Naĥl (The Bee) - سورة النحل 16:59

  يَتَوَارٰى مِنَ الْقَوْمِ مِنْ سُوْۗءِ مَا بُشِّرَ بِهٖ  ۭ اَيُمْسِكُهٗ عَلٰي هُوْنٍ اَمْ يَدُسُّهٗ فِي التُّرَابِ  ۭ

 He hides himself from the people because of the ill of which he has been informed. Should he keep it in humiliation or bury it in the ground?

         If HON (هون ) contains fatah on H then it means softness and this attribute is Mehmood. Just like Allah says while describing the qualities of true believers:

 

Surat Al-Furqān (The Criterian) - سورة الفرقان 25:63

  وَعِبَادُ الرَّحْمٰنِ الَّذِيْنَ يَمْشُوْنَ عَلَي الْاَرْضِ هَوْنًا ۭ

And the servants of the Most Merciful are those who walk upon the earth easily.

 

Summary:

3.1          ZILLA: inferiority and weakness.

3.2          SAGHAR: humiliation of becoming inferior after supremacy and its acceptance and acknowledgement.

3.3          KIZI: Go down in the eyes of people, disgrace.

3.4          HON: Humiliation because of being inferior.
