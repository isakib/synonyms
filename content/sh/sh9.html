9. Flame:

Lahab  لَهَب,shawaaz شَواظ ,nuhhaas نُحَّاس ,maarij  مَارِّج & sharar  شَرَر are the words used in the Quran.

1.        Lahab لَهَب: This means movement of the flame/fire & get higher.in other words,high flame. It is a commonly used word for flame.

Allah says in the Quran: (111:3) : Surat Al-Masad (The Palm Fiber, Flame) - سورة المسد

سَيَصْلَىٰ نَارًا ذَاتَ لَهَبٍ

He will [enter to] burn in a Fire of [blazing] flame

2.        Shawaaz شَواظ/Nuhhaas نُحَّاس : Shawaaz  شَواظ is such a flame that does not have smoke with it. & when some smoke accompanies that flame, then it is termed as Nuhhaas  نُحَّاس.(provided smoke is less than fire/fire is higher & has some smoke with it). It is called Nuhhaas نُحَّاس because the colour of the flame is more like copper.(Nuhhaas نُحَّاس means copper)

Allah says in the Quran:55:35: Surat Ar-Raĥmān (The Beneficent) - سورة الرحمن

يُرْسَلُ عَلَيْكُمَا شُوَاظٌ مِّن نَّارٍ وَنُحَاسٌ فَلَا تَنتَصِرَانِ

There will be sent upon you a flame of fire and smoke, and you will not defend yourselves.

3.        Maarij مَارِّج : The highest point of flame ,which is extremely hot & is without any smoke. Or engulfed/surrounded by fire.

 

Allah says in the Quran: 55:15: Surat Ar-Raĥmān (The Beneficent) - سورة الرحمن

وَخَلَقَ الْجَانَّ مِن مَّارِجٍ مِّن نَّارٍ

And He created the jinn from a smokeless flame of fire.

4.        Sharar  شَرَر : small particles of fire coming off a bigger/higher fire/flame. Sparks.(tiny bright material that flies up of something that is burning)

Allah says in the Quran: 77:32: Surat Al-Mursalāt (The Emissaries) - سورة المرسلات

إِنَّهَا تَرْمِي بِشَرَرٍ كَالْقَصْرِ

Indeed, it throws sparks [as huge] as a fortress,

Summary

1.        Lahab  لَهَب: Commonly used word for flame.

2.        Shuwaaz شَواظ : smokeless flame

3.        Nuhhaas نُحَّاس : fire with smoke(provided smoke is less than fire)

4.        Maarij مَارِّج : the highest & hottest part of flame.

5.        Sharar  شَرَر : tiny particles flying from something burning/spark.
