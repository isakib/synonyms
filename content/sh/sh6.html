6.To start/initiate:

 

 Bada’a بَدَاَ  & tafiqa  طَفِقَ  are the words used.

 

1.        Bada’a  بَدَاَ : It has two meanings. a ) to start something. To initiate/inaugurate. B) to do something first.(give something priority. If this is associated to Allah, then it means to do it first time.

Allah says in the Quran: 12:76 Surat Yūsuf (Joseph) - سورة يوسف

فَبَدَأَ بِأَوْعِيَتِهِمْ قَبْلَ وِعَاءِ أَخِيهِ ثُمَّ اسْتَخْرَجَهَا مِن وِعَاءِ أَخِيهِ ۚ كَذَٰلِكَ كِدْنَا لِيُوسُفَ ۖ مَا كَانَ لِيَأْخُذَ أَخَاهُ فِي دِينِ الْمَلِكِ إِلَّا أَن يَشَاءَ اللَّهُ ۚ نَرْفَعُ دَرَجَاتٍ مَّن نَّشَاءُ ۗ وَفَوْقَ كُلِّ ذِي عِلْمٍ عَلِيمٌ

So he began [the search] with their bags before the bag of his brother; then he extracted it from the bag of his brother. Thus did We plan for Joseph. He could not have taken his brother within the religion of the king except that Allah willed. We raise in degrees whom We will, but over every possessor of knowledge is one [more] knowing.

 

The above aayat used both the meanings of bada’a بَدَاَ . First meaning is to start search with the bag of other brothers & secondly, search the bag of others before he searched for his brothers bag.

Allah also said in 85:13:Surat Al-Burūj (The Mansions of the Stars) - سورة البروج

إِنَّهُ هُوَ يُبْدِئُ وَيُعِيدُ

Indeed, it is He who originates [creation] and repeats.

 

2.        Tafiqa طَفِقَ  : This word/verb needs another word to complete the meaning.(adverb). It generally gives a positive meaning. It means to start /do something.

 

Allah says in the Quran: 7:22 : Surat Al-'A`rāf (The Heights) - سورة الأعراف

فَدَلَّاهُمَا بِغُرُورٍ ۚ فَلَمَّا ذَاقَا الشَّجَرَةَ بَدَتْ لَهُمَا سَوْآتُهُمَا وَطَفِقَا يَخْصِفَانِ عَلَيْهِمَا مِن وَرَقِ الْجَنَّةِ ۖ وَنَادَاهُمَا رَبُّهُمَا أَلَمْ أَنْهَكُمَا عَن تِلْكُمَا الشَّجَرَةِ وَأَقُل لَّكُمَا إِنَّ الشَّيْطَانَ لَكُمَا عَدُوٌّ مُّبِينٌ

So he made them fall, through deception. And when they tasted of the tree, their private parts became apparent to them, and they began to fasten together over themselves from the leaves of Paradise. And their Lord called to them, "Did I not forbid you from that tree and tell you that Satan is to you a clear enemy?"

In another verse, Allah says: 38:33: Surat Şād (The Letter "Saad") - سورة ص

رُدُّوهَا عَلَيَّ ۖ فَطَفِقَ مَسْحًا بِالسُّوقِ وَالْأَعْنَاقِ

[He said], "Return them to me," and set about striking [their] legs and necks.

 

Summary:

Bada’a  بَدَاَ means to do something first or start something. Tafiqa طَفِقَ  joins with another word to express its meaning that it has started. i.e the original work is something else.Only when this word is joined, then it completes the meaning.
