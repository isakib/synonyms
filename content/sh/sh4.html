4.Alcohol/Intoxicants:

 

  Khamr خَمر ,maeen مَعِين ,raheeq رَحيق  are the words used for alcohol in the Quran:

 

1.        Khamr  خَمر : Its a noun for Alcohol. Khamara  خَمَرَ means to cover. & khimaar  خِمار means some fabric used for covering (scarf/dupatta)-used to cover face etc. Alcohol is called khamar  خَمر because it covers the wisdom or mind /thinking of a person.

Allah says in the Quran: 5:90 : Surat Al-Mā'idah (The Table Spread) - سورة المائدة

يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا إِنَّمَا الْخَمْرُ وَالْمَيْسِرُ وَالْأَنصَابُ وَالْأَزْلَامُ رِجْسٌ مِّنْ عَمَلِ الشَّيْطَانِ فَاجْتَنِبُوهُ لَعَلَّكُمْ تُفْلِحُونَ

O you who have believed, indeed, intoxicants, gambling, [sacrificing on] stone alters [to other than Allah ], and divining arrows are but defilement from the work of Satan, so avoid it that you may be successful.

2.        Maeen  مَعِين : ma’anal maa  مَعَنَ الماء means water flowing slowly on the ground. Maanan nazar fel amar  مَعَنَ النَّظر فى الامر  means think deeply on some matter. Maeen  مَعِين is such a water that is sweet,delicious & clean & clear.

Allah says in the Quran: 67:30: Surat Al-Mulk (The Sovereignty) - سورة الملك

قُلْ أَرَأَيْتُمْ إِنْ أَصْبَحَ مَاؤُكُمْ غَوْرًا فَمَن يَأْتِيكُم بِمَاءٍ مَّعِينٍ

 

Say, "Have you considered: if your water was to become sunken [into the earth], then who could bring you flowing water?"

But the word kaas كَاس , when used in the above aayat makes the meaning of maeen  مَعِين as pleasant alcohol. Such alcohol which is devoid of unpleasant effects of khamr  خَمر  . Because kaas كَاس  is used generally used for a glass /container filled with alcohol & even for alcohol.

As Allah says in the Quran:56:18: Surat Al-Wāqi`ah (The Inevitable) - سورة الواقعة

بِأَكْوَابٍ وَأَبَارِيقَ وَكَأْسٍ مِّن مَّعِينٍ

With vessels, pitchers and a cup [of wine] from a flowing spring 

2.        Raheeq رَحيق : Means pure,clear &  pleasant smelling alcohol.(which is clear of any particles)

Allah says in the Quran:83:25: Surat Al-Muţaffifīn (The Defrauding) - سورة المطففين

يُسْقَوْنَ مِن رَّحِيقٍ مَّخْتُومٍ

They will be given to drink [pure] wine [which was] sealed.

 

Summary:

1. Khamar خَمر : alcohol that makes one lose his control/mind.It is used for all kinds of alcohol.

2. Maeen مَعِين : Pleasant tasting & smelling drink which is devoid of all impurities & which will be given in heaven .

3. Raheeq رَحيق  : the best quality/type of drink ,fragrant & delicious available in heaven.
