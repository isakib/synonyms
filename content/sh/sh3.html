3.Probable/May be:

 

Asa عَسى  & la ‘alla لَعَلَّ  are the words used.

1.Asa عَسى : it is from af’aal muqarba أفعال المقاربة (verb of approach & hope) & it is indeclinable/frozen. It is only used with Madi ( ماضي past) & not used with mudari (مضارع present/future)

It is used for ‘Hope’ amongst desired things & for ‘fear’ amongst hated/disliked things. For example, amongst desired/favourable things, it is used for dominating hope.

 

As per the Quran: 28:22: Surat Al-Qaşaş (The Stories) - سورة القصص

وَلَمَّا تَوَجَّهَ تِلْقَاءَ مَدْيَنَ قَالَ عَسَىٰ رَبِّي أَن يَهْدِيَنِي سَوَاءَ السَّبِيلِ

And when he directed himself toward Madyan, he said, "Perhaps my Lord will guide me to the sound way."

 

& ‘fear’  amongst the prohibited/disliked things.

Allah says in the Quran: 47:22 : Surat Muĥammad (Muhammad) - سورة محمد

فَهَلْ عَسَيْتُمْ إِن تَوَلَّيْتُمْ أَن تُفْسِدُوا فِي الْأَرْضِ وَتُقَطِّعُوا أَرْحَامَكُمْ

 

So would you perhaps, if you turned away, cause corruption on earth and sever your [ties of] relationship?

 

2. La’alla لَعَلَّ : a verb used for dominating probability.

Allah says in the Quran: 33:63: Surat Al-'Aĥzāb (The Combined Forces) - سورة الأحزاب

يَسْأَلُكَ النَّاسُ عَنِ السَّاعَةِ ۖ قُلْ إِنَّمَا عِلْمُهَا عِندَ اللَّهِ ۚ وَمَا يُدْرِيكَ لَعَلَّ السَّاعَةَ تَكُونُ قَرِيبًا

People ask you concerning the Hour. Say," Knowledge of it is only with Allah . And what may make you perceive? Perhaps the Hour is near."

 

 & when this is related to Allah, then it means allowable.

As Allah says in the Quran: 65:1 : Surat Aţ-Ţalāq (The Divorce) - سورة الطلاق

يَا أَيُّهَا النَّبِيُّ إِذَا طَلَّقْتُمُ النِّسَاءَ فَطَلِّقُوهُنَّ لِعِدَّتِهِنَّ وَأَحْصُوا الْعِدَّةَ ۖ وَاتَّقُوا اللَّهَ رَبَّكُمْ ۖ لَا تُخْرِجُوهُنَّ مِن بُيُوتِهِنَّ وَلَا يَخْرُجْنَ إِلَّا أَن يَأْتِينَ بِفَاحِشَةٍ مُّبَيِّنَةٍ ۚ وَتِلْكَ حُدُودُ اللَّهِ ۚ وَمَن يَتَعَدَّ حُدُودَ اللَّهِ فَقَدْ ظَلَمَ نَفْسَهُ ۚ لَا تَدْرِي لَعَلَّ اللَّهَ يُحْدِثُ بَعْدَ ذَٰلِكَ أَمْرًا

O Prophet, when you [Muslims] divorce women, divorce them for [the commencement of] their waiting period and keep count of the waiting period, and fear Allah , your Lord. Do not turn them out of their [husbands'] houses, nor should they [themselves] leave [during that period] unless they are committing a clear immorality. And those are the limits [set by] Allah . And whoever transgresses the limits of Allah has certainly wronged himself. You know not; perhaps Allah will bring about after that a [different] matter.

 

Summary:

Asaa عَسى  is used for dominating hope ,whereas la’alla لَعَلَّ  is used for dominating probability
