Hardship – Severity 

In the Quran, the words غِلظَة, قَسوَة, بَاسَاء (بوس يَا بِأَس), كَبَدَ

1-2 غِلظَةand قَسوَة

These two have been discussed previously. قَسوَة is having a stone cold heart. 

ثُمَّ قَسَتۡ قُلُوبُكُم مِّنۢ بَعۡدِ ذَٲلِكَ فَهِىَ كَٱلۡحِجَارَةِ أَوۡ أَشَدُّ قَسۡوَةً۬*ۚ 

“Then, even after that, your hearts were hardened and became as rocks” 2/74

غِلظَة is the combination of rudeness and ruthlessness when it is used in the context of one's heart.

يَـٰٓأَيُّہَا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُواْ قَـٰتِلُواْ ٱلَّذِينَ يَلُونَكُم مِّنَ ٱلۡڪُفَّارِ وَلۡيَجِدُواْ فِيكُمۡ غِلۡظَةً۬*ۚ 

“O ye who believe! Fight those of the disbelievers who are near to you, and let them find harshness in you” 9/123

3- بَاسَاء

بوءس carries the meaning of severity whether it results from starvation or from war and it has been used in the Quran in the meanings of war:

وَحِينَ ٱلۡبَأۡسِ*ۗ 

“And at time of stress” 2/177 

And in the meanings of punishment: 

إِذۡ جَآءَهُم بَأۡسُنَا 

“When Our disaster came on them” 6/43

For a calamity, بَاءساً شَدِيداًall carry the meaning of severity

بَأۡسً۬ا شَدِيدً۬ا 

“Stern punishment”18/2. 

بَاسَاء is a long period of severity which could involve war and fighting.

وَلَقَدۡ أَرۡسَلۡنَآ إِلَىٰٓ أُمَمٍ۬ مِّن قَبۡلِكَ فَأَخَذۡنَـٰهُم بِٱلۡبَأۡسَآءِ وَٱلضَّرَّآءِ لَعَلَّهُمۡ يَتَضَرَّعُونَ 

“We have sent already unto peoples that were before thee, and We visited them with tribulation and adversity, in order that they might grow humble” 6/42

4- كَبَدَ

It means liver and points towards might and toughness. تَكَبَّدand كَابَدmean to endure pain. فِيكَبَدَis used in a proverbial context and depicts human nature. Man desires for something and to get it he endures through a lot but before even the fulfilment of that desire, more desires start sprouting in his heart. This, man then embarks on fulfilling his new desires and the same cycle continues until he dies.

لَقَدۡ خَلَقۡنَا ٱلۡإِنسَـٰنَ فِى كَبَدٍ 

“We verily have created man in an atmosphere[pointing towards his nature as explained above] “90/4

Inference 

1- قَسوَة: Discussed earlier

2- غِلظَة: Discussed earlier

3- بَاسَاء: A time of adversity

4- كَبَدَ: Human nature to endure severity until death
