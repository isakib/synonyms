To Understand – To Enlighten – To Elucidate 

In the Quran, the words عَقَلَ, فَهَمَ, فَقَهَand شَعَرَhave been used.

1- شَعَرَ

اَشَّعَر means hair and شَعَرَmeans to gain comprehensive knowledge like a fine hair, to comprehend the soul and true meaning of a matter.

وَمَا يَخۡدَعُونَ إِلَّآ أَنفُسَهُمۡ وَمَا يَشۡعُرُونَ 

“And they beguile none save themselves; but they perceive not” 2:9

2- فَهَمَ

اَلفَهم is the capability of a human being by which he comprehends and understands the gist and the meanings and فَهَمَmeans to fully understand something and also in the meaning of understanding immediately as soon as one hears something. سَرِيعُالفَهمis to understand quickly and سئالفهمmeans a dull mind and فَهَّمَmeans to elucidate the reality to someone else.

وَڪُنَّا لِحُكۡمِهِمۡ شَـٰهِدِينَ (﻿٧٨﻿) فَفَهَّمۡنَـٰهَا سُلَيۡمَـٰنَ*ۚ

“And We were witnesses to their judgement. (78) And We made Solomon to understand” 21:78

3- فَقَهَ

اَلفِقَه is the knowledge or the study of deriving inferences or arriving at decisions while taking into consideration numerous other verdicts surrounding that particular matter.

ٱنظُرۡ كَيۡفَ نُصَرِّفُ ٱلۡأَيَـٰتِ لَعَلَّهُمۡ يَفۡقَهُونَ 

“See how We display the revelations so that they may understand” 6:65

Also, تَفَقَّهَmeans to find the deeper and true meaning of a matter.

فَلَوۡلَا نَفَرَ مِن كُلِّ فِرۡقَةٍ۬ مِّنۡہُمۡ طَآٮِٕفَةٌ۬ لِّيَتَفَقَّهُواْ فِى ٱلدِّينِ 

“A party only should go forth, that they (who are left behind) may gain sound knowledge in religion” 9:122

4- عَقَلَ

Means to be wise and judicious. A person who can comprehend mistakes and errors and عَقَلَmeans to stop, to halt. عِقَالis that which is used to arrest the foot of a camel. اَلمعقلis a refuge, hills or a citadel for taking refuge. Hence, wisdom helps a person to discern between loss and gain and to discover ways to desist from doing harm and to be receptive and centripetal to gaining knowledge. Also, means to reflect and ponder.

أَتَأۡمُرُونَ ٱلنَّاسَ بِٱلۡبِرِّ وَتَنسَوۡنَ أَنفُسَكُمۡ وَأَنتُمۡ تَتۡلُونَ ٱلۡكِتَـٰبَ*ۚ أَفَلَا تَعۡقِلُونَ 

“Enjoin ye righteousness upon mankind while ye yourselves forget (to practise it)? And ye are readers of the Scripture! Have ye then no sense? “ 2:44

Inference 

1- شَعَرَ: To comprehend the true meaning

2- فَهَمَ: To grasp the meaning of, to understand

3- فَقَهَ: To derive decisions from similar examples of the past

4- عَقَلَ: To understand about one's gains and losses and to find ways of preventing loss.
