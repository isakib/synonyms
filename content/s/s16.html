16 SARGOSHI KERNA [to whisper]

Synonyms: TAKHAFAT, TANAJA-NAJWA

16.1 TAKHAFAT (تَخَافَتَ): KHAFAT (خَفَتَ) means muffled voice, to murmur; TAKHAFAT (تَخَافَتَ) means to talk in a low voice (dictionary Al Manjad). To whisper to others even if others can hear it. In the Quran

Surah Ta-Ha (Ta-ha): سورۃ طٰھٰ  20:103

يَتَخَـٰفَتُونَ بَيۡنَہُمۡ إِن لَّبِثۡتُمۡ إِلَّا عَشۡرً۬ا

 Murmuring among themselves: “You have lingered only for ten.”

16.2 TANAJA – NAJWA (تَنَاجٰی نَجْوٰی): NAJA’A (نَجَاَ) means to whisper, to talk about secrecy, TANAJA (تَنَاجٰی) means to make a confidant (dictionary Al Manjad). To hold a secret meeting, to talk about secrets separately. Allah says in the Holy Quran  

Surah Al-Mujadila (The pleading Women): سُوۡرَةُ المجَادلة 58:10

 إِنَّمَا ٱلنَّجۡوَىٰ مِنَ ٱلشَّيۡطَـٰنِ لِيَحۡزُنَ ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُواْ

 Secret counsels are only (inspired) by the Evil One, in order that he may cause grief to the Believers

In Another Place:

يَـٰٓأَيُّہَا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوٓاْ إِذَا تَنَـٰجَيۡتُمۡ فَلَا تَتَنَـٰجَوۡاْ بِٱلۡإِثۡمِ وَٱلۡعُدۡوَٲنِ وَمَعۡصِيَتِ ٱلرَّسُولِ وَتَنَـٰجَوۡاْ بِٱلۡبِرِّ وَٱلتَّقۡوَىٰ‌ۖ

O you who believe! When you converse secretly, do not converse in sin, and aggression, and disobedience of the Messenger; but converse in virtue and piety

Summary:

16.1 TAKHAFAT: To converse in a low voice.

16.2 NAJWA: To converse about secrets in privacy
