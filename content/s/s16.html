To Whisper 

In the Quran, the words تَنَجَي, نَجوَاي (نجِي), تَخَافَتhave been used.

1- تَخَافَت

خَافَتَ means low voice or talking in undertones, to whisper among others, regardless of the fact that other could also listen.

يَتَخَـٰفَتُونَ بَيۡنَہُمۡ إِن لَّبِثۡتُمۡ إِلَّا عَشۡرً۬ا 

“Murmuring among themselves: Ye have tarried but ten (days) “ 20/103

2- تَنَجَي, نَجوَاي

نَجَا means to whisper, to talk secretively. تَنَاجَيmeans to take someone as a confidant, to hold a secret meeting in a separate place.

إِنَّمَا ٱلنَّجۡوَىٰ مِنَ ٱلشَّيۡطَـٰنِ لِيَحۡزُنَ ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُواْ 

“Lo! Conspiracy is only of the devil, that he may vex those who believe “58/10

And again 

يَـٰٓأَيُّہَا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوٓاْ إِذَا تَنَـٰجَيۡتُمۡ فَلَا تَتَنَـٰجَوۡاْ بِٱلۡإِثۡمِ وَٱلۡعُدۡوَٲنِ وَمَعۡصِيَتِ ٱلرَّسُولِ وَتَنَـٰجَوۡاْ بِٱلۡبِرِّ وَٱلتَّقۡوَىٰ*ۖ 

“O ye who believe! When ye conspire together, conspire not together for crime and wrongdoing and disobedience toward the messenger, but conspire together for righteousness and piety” 58/9

Inference 

1- تَخَافَت: To speak in undertones

2- نَجوَاي: To speak secretively in a separate setting
