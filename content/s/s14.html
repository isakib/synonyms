14 SARD,SARDI [winter, cold]

 Synonyms: SHITAA’, SIRR, SARSAR, NAFHAH, ZAMHAREER

14.1 SHITAA’ (شِتَآء): means winter season (opposite SAIF (صَیْف) summer season), SHATWII (شَتْوِیّ) means rain in winter season (dictionary Al Manjad). Allah says in the Quran

Surah Quraish (Quraish): سورۃ قُرَیْش 106:1-2

لِإِيلَـٰفِ قُرَيۡشٍ إِیلَـٰفِهِمۡ رِحۡلَةَ ٱلشِّتَآءِ وَٱلصَّيۡفِ

For the security of Quraish. Their security during winter and summer journeys.

14.2 SIRR (صِرّ): SURR-AL-BANAT (صُرَّالْبَنَات) means vegetation destroyed due to cold winds (dictionary Al Manjad). SIRR (صِرّ) means severe cold, frost or haze. Allah says in the Holy Quran.

Surah Ale- Imran (The family of Imran): سورۃ آل عِمْرن 3:117

ڪَمَثَلِ رِيحٍ۬ فِيہَا صِرٌّ أَصَابَتۡ حَرۡثَ قَوۡمٍ۬ ظَلَمُوٓاْ أَنفُسَهُمۡ فَأَهۡلَڪَتۡهُ‌ۚ

 May be likened to a wind which brings a nipping frost: It strikes and destroys the harvest of men who have wronged their own souls:

14.3 SARSAR (صَرْصَر): means swift cold wind. Wind that is cold and creates sound. Whistling cold wind. Allah says in the Holy Quran

Surah Al-Haqqah (The reality): سورۃ الحَاقَّۃ 69:6

 وَأَمَّا عَادٌ۬ فَأُهۡلِڪُواْ بِرِيحٍ۬ صَرۡصَرٍ عَاتِيَةٍ۬

 And as for Aad; they were annihilated by a furious, roaring wind.

14.4 NAFHA (نَفْحَۃ): NAFAHA (نَفَحَ) means blowing of wind in winter, blowing of chilly wind, NAFHA (نَفْحَۃ) is an instant noun. Like a rush of cold wind. (Opposite LAFHA (لَفْحَۃ) means a rush of warm wind).Allah says in the Holy Quran

Surah Al-Anbiya (the prophets): سورۃ الانَبیاء 21:46

 وَلَٮِٕن مَّسَّتۡهُمۡ نَفۡحَةٌ۬ مِّنۡ عَذَابِ رَبِّكَ

 And when a breath of your Lord’s punishment touches them, they say

14.5 ZAMHAREER (زَمْھَرِیْر): IZMAHARR-AL-YAUM (اِزْمَھَرَّالیوم) means a very cold day. ZAMHAREER (زَمْھَرِیْر) means severe cold and also a place where there is severe cold (dictionary Al Manjad). Allah says in the Holy Quran.

Surah Al-Insan (The Man): سورۃ الانْسان 76:13

 لَا يَرَوۡنَ فِيہَا شَمۡسً۬ا وَلَا زَمۡهَرِيرً۬ا

They will see there neither the sun´s (excessive heat) nor (the moon´s) excessive cold.

SUMMARY:

14.1 SHITAA’ : Winter

14.2 SIRR: Haze or frost

14.3 SARSAR: Clattering swift cold wind.

14.4 NAFHA: A single rush of cold wind

14.5 ZAMHAREER: Severe cold, or area which is cold.
