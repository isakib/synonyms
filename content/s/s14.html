Cold – Winter 

For this the Quran has used the following words: زَمهَرِير, نَفحَة, صَرصَر, صِرّ, and شِتَأ

1- شِتَأ

Means winter season(Ant: صَيف) and شِتوِيّmeans winter rain.

لِإِيلَـٰفِ قُرَيۡشٍ (﻿١﻿) إِیلَـٰفِهِمۡ رِحۡلَةَ ٱلشِّتَآءِ وَٱلصَّيۡفِ

“For the taming of Qureysh. (1) For their taming (We cause) the caravans to set forth in winter and summer” 

106/1-2 

2- صِرّ

صُرَّ النَبَاتmeans frost that kills plants and صِرّmeans means frost.

ڪَمَثَلِ رِيحٍ۬ فِيہَا صِرٌّ أَصَابَتۡ حَرۡثَ قَوۡمٍ۬ ظَلَمُوٓاْ أَنفُسَهُمۡ فَأَهۡلَڪَتۡهُ*ۚ 

“As the likeness of a biting, icy wind which smiteth the harvest of a people who have wronged themselves” 

3/117 

3- صَرصَر

This is extremely cold wind which also makes a howling noise. 

وَأَمَّا عَادٌ۬ فَأُهۡلِڪُواْ بِرِيحٍ۬ صَرۡصَرٍ عَاتِيَةٍ۬ 

“And as for A'ad, they were destroyed by a fierce roaring wind” 69/6

4- نَفحَة

نَفَحَ means the wind blowing in winters or blowing of the cold wind. نَفحَةis a singular noun which means a single gust of cold wind(Ant: لَفحَة)

وَلَٮِٕن مَّسَّتۡهُمۡ نَفۡحَةٌ۬ مِّنۡ عَذَابِ رَبِّكَ 

“ And if a breath of thy Lord's punishment were to touchthem” 21/46

5- زَمهَرِير

إِز مَهَرَّmeans a hard day and زَمهَرِيرmeans extreme cold and also means a setting where the weather is bitterly cold.

لَا يَرَوۡنَ فِيہَا شَمۡسً۬ا وَلَا زَمۡهَرِيرً۬ا 

“They will find there neither (heat of) a sun nor bitter cold” 76/13

Inference 

1- شِتَأ: Winter season

2- صِرّ: Frost

3- صَرصَر: Cold and strong wind

4- نَفحَة: A gust of cold wind

5- زَمهَرِير: An extremely cold region or extreme cold
