Except – Exclude 

For this term, the words إِ لاَّ, دُون, غَيرَ, وَرَاءand إستِثنَاhave been used in the Quran.

1- إِلاَّ

Means but, except and in this form it can be used in a universal scope as the ayah below will make it clear. 

لَّآ إِلَـٰهَ إِلَّا هُوَ ٱلرَّحۡمَـٰنُ ٱلرَّحِيمُ 

“There is no God save Him, the Beneficent, the Merciful” 2:163

Furthermore, this word also gives the meaning of حَصَرَ

وَمَا يَخۡدَعُونَ إِلَّآ أَنفُسَهُمۡ 

“And they beguile none save themselves” 2:9

2- غَيرَ

It is sometimes used simply for negation, just like 'not' in English as in 

وَهُوَ فِى ٱلۡخِصَامِ غَيۡرُ مُبِينٍ۬ 

“And in dispute cannot make itself plain?” 43:18

On another occasion, it is used in the context of drawing an exception, in which case its scope becomes universal or absolute. 

هَلۡ مِنۡ خَـٰلِقٍ غَيۡرُ ٱللَّهِ 

“Is there any creator other than Allah” 35:3

3- دُون

دَان means low, cheap, flimsy and شَيٌدُونٌmeans a cheap item and دُونalso means something devoid of or lacking and when دُونgives the meaning of exclusion, than it is used in the context of cheapness and weakness.

يَـٰٓأَيُّہَا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُواْ لَا تَتَّخِذُواْ بِطَانَةً۬ مِّن دُونِكُمۡ 

“O ye who believe! Take not for intimates others than your own folk” 3:118

In this context, deficiency in other religions, except Islam is denoted. 

On another occasion, 

إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ لَا يَغۡفِرُ أَن يُشۡرَكَ بِهِۦ وَيَغۡفِرُ مَا دُونَ ذَٲلِكَ لِمَن يَشَآءُ*ۚ 

“Lo! Allah forgiveth not that a partner should be ascribed unto Him. He forgiveth (all) save that to whom He will “ 4:48

and in this ayah, other sins apart from shirk are intended. Similarly 2:108 

4- وَرَاء

Means that whenever its used, situation on either side, that surround it are meant to be opposed or negated. This word means literally, on either side. In such situations, it also gives the meaning of exclusion. 

فَمَنِ ٱبۡتَغَىٰ وَرَآءَ ذَٲلِكَ فَأُوْلَـٰٓٮِٕكَ هُمُ ٱلۡعَادُونَ 

“But whoso craveth beyond that, such are transgressors “ 38:7

which actually means that whoever tries to find any other way, apart from the commandment of Allah is a sinner. 

5- إستِثنَا

ثَنِي means to exclude something from a common decree, for this usually إِلاَّis used but for excluding something according to he commandment of Allah.

وَلَا تَقُولَنَّ لِشَاْىۡءٍ إِنِّى فَاعِلٌ۬ ذَٲلِكَ غَدًا (﻿٢٣﻿) إِلَّآ أَن يَشَآءَ ٱللَّهُ*ۚ

“And say not of anything: Lo! I shall do that tomorrow, (23) Except if Allah will “ 18:24

And إستِثنَاis used in place of saying إِنشَاالله.

إِذۡ أَقۡسَمُواْ لَيَصۡرِمُنَّہَا مُصۡبِحِينَ (﻿١٧﻿) وَلَا يَسۡتَثۡنُونَ

“When they vowed they would pluck its fruit next morning, (17) And made no exception” 68:17-18

Inference 

1- إِلاَّ: Exclusion, also in the meaning of حَصَرَ

2- غَيرَ: Used for negation

3- دُون: Exclusion in the meaning of lacking

4- وَرَاء: Absolute exclusion

5- إستِثنَا: To exclude something from a common decree by saying إِنشَا الله.
