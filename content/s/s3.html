Year

The words عم )عام , ( سنو) سِنِين, حول, حج) have been used.

1- عَام

عَامis considered a year of prosperity and thriving. It says in the Quran:

ثُمَّ يَأۡتِى مِنۢ بَعۡدِ ذَٲلِكَ عَامٌ۬ فِيهِ يُغَاثُ ٱلنَّاسُ وَفِيهِ يَعۡصِرُونَ

“Then, after that, will come a year when the people will have plenteous crops and when they will press (wine and oil)” 12/49

In a similar context, the word عَام has been used as a word of دعَا or prayer. The Arabs used to greet each other on Eid with a prayer saying:

اَنتُم وَّ كُلَّ عََامٍ بِخَير

“May this year bring you peace and tranquillity”

2- سَنَة(Pl: سِنِين)

It means a year of adversity. Also, the same word has been used for drought and famine.

وَلَقَدۡ أَخَذۡنَآ ءَالَ فِرۡعَوۡنَ بِٱلسِّنِينَ

“And we straitened Pharaoh's folk with famine and dearth“ 7/130

فَلَبِثَ فِيهِمۡ أَلۡفَ سَنَةٍ إِلَّا خَمۡسِينَ عَامً۬ا

“He continued with them for a thousand years save fifty years“ 29/14

In this ayah, the 950 years that Nooh (Noah) spent amongst his people in extreme hardship and opposition have been denoted by the word سِنِينand the 50 years before prophet hood, where there was no opposition from his people are denoted by عَام.

The word سنةis normally used to keep and account of time or a period which also points towards the fact that the periods of prosperity are normally a lot less than the periods of adversity.

هُوَ ٱلَّذِى جَعَلَ ٱلشَّمۡسَ ضِيَآءً۬ وَٱلۡقَمَرَ نُورً۬ا وَقَدَّرَهُ ۥ مَنَازِلَ لِتَعۡلَمُواْ عَدَدَ ٱلسِّنِينَ وَٱلۡحِسَابَ*ۚ

“He it is Who appointed the sun a splendour and the moon a light, and measured for her stages, that ye might know the number of the years” 10/5

3- حول

حَالَmeans the transition of times from one state to another. (حَال Pl: حَالاَت).

حََالاَت الدَّهَرmeans the passing of days or transition from day to day. حال الحولmeans the passing of a year and حولmeans one complete rotation of the year from one particular date in a year to the next time or date after one full year. زكوةis calculated and paid by حولand divorce and the period of suckling are also determined by it.

وَصِيَّةً۬ لِّأَزۡوَٲجِهِم مَّتَـٰعًا إِلَى ٱلۡحَوۡلِ غَيۡرَ إِخۡرَاجٍ۬*ۚ

“They should bequeath unto their wives a provision for the year without turning them out” 2/240

4- حِجّج(sing: حَجَّة)

It means something that comes again and again, some even that repeats, that comes a lot and because Hajj comes once a year, every year, hence the year is called Hajj. In old times there were no calendars to account for days, months or even years so people, would commonly count years with reference to the number of Hajjs.

قَالَ إِنِّىٓ أُرِيدُ أَنۡ أُنكِحَكَ إِحۡدَى ٱبۡنَتَىَّ هَـٰتَيۡنِ عَلَىٰٓ أَن تَأۡجُرَنِى ثَمَـٰنِىَ حِجَجٍ۬*ۖ

“He said: Lo! I fain would marry thee to one of these two daughters of mine on condition that thou hirest thyself to me for (the term of) eight pilgrimages” 28/27

Inference

1- عَام: The year of prosperity

2- حول: A whole rotation of the year, starting from a particular date (lunar)

3- سَنَةَ: The year of drought

4- حِجَج: A common way of counting years
