سیدھا سیدھی   - Straight

These words مستقيم سواء (سوى) قصد سديد  have been used in the Quran

1.         مستقيم – قام على الامر  means to stay firm and to continue as before, and اقام الشئ means to stand something up and to straighten it and مستقيم is something which is straight and moderate, even.

 

In Surah Israa: (17:35):

 

وَزِنُوا بِالْقِسْطَاسِ الْمُسْتَقِيمِ ۚ

 

and weigh with an even balance

 

This is because the beam of the scale is always straight.  If this beam is not straight then the weighing will not be balanced correctly.  The beam of the scale is always straight even if one side of the scale is lower down;  therefore here “to be straight” means as well as horizontal, it can also be like the different positions of the beam, regardless of the balance on either side. According to geometry the minimal difference between two points, two places is called خط مستقیم, so to say صراط مستقیم  is that straight path which has no crease and  bend, free from excessiveness and lower extremity. In the Quran we have been taught this prayer:

 

In Surah Faatiha: (1:4):

 

اهْدِنَا الصِّرَاطَ الْمُسْتَقِيمَ

 

Guide us to the straight path.

 

2.      سواء (سوى) – (sawaa & Saweeyya) – سوى يسوى (saweya yaswee)-  be corrected and سوى (sawwaa) to correct, straighten something and سوى (Saweeyya) means  straight, correct , healthy.

 

In Surah Maryam: (19:17):

 

فَتَمَثَّلَ لَهَا بَشَرًا سَوِيًّا

 

and he represented himself to her as a well-proportioned man.

 

And صراطا سويا means a path, which is straight, balanced and even. No inequality, highs or lows, no bends.

 

In Surah Maryam: (19:43):

 

 فَاتَّبِعْنِي أَهْدِكَ صِرَاطًا سَوِيًّا

 

so follow me; I will guide you to an even path.

 

The word سواء is used for equal and even. If this evenness from both sides is according to the distance or number then the word وسط is used, but if the evenness is according to all things and sides then the word سواء is used, therefore it means centre point, middle point.

 

In Surah Dukhaan:  (44:47):

 

 خُذُوهُ فَاعْتِلُوهُ إِلَىٰ سَوَاءِ الْجَحِيمِ

 

[It will be commanded], "Seize him and drag him into the midst of the Hellfire,

 

And سواء السبيل  means a straight path which comes out leaving behind all base acts, all misguidance and paganism.

 

In Surah Qasas: (28:22):

 

وَلَمَّا تَوَجَّهَ تِلْقَاءَ مَدْيَنَ قَالَ عَسَىٰ رَبِّي أَن يَهْدِيَنِي سَوَاءَ السَّبِيلِ

 

And when he directed himself toward Madyan, he said, "Perhaps my Lord will guide me to the sound way."

 

3.          قصد- to use something in a permissible manner ie  If a man can have a bath in one bucket of water, then to use one bucket of water for a bath is قصد.  To use more than one bucket is   اسراف – excessive,  and to use less than a bucket is بخل - miser. To consider this in each situation and to make it in to a habit is called اقتصاد.

 

Now looking at the meaning not all things and matters are the same, sometimes you have to be harsh, sometimes soft, to attain or reach a certain goal, aim and destination and to elaborate on this, is the function of Islamic jurisprudence.

 

In Surah An-Nahl: (16:9):

 

وَعَلَى اللَّهِ قَصْدُ السَّبِيلِ وَمِنْهَا جَائِرٌ

 

And upon Allah is the direction of the [right] way, and among the various paths are those deviating.

 

When the word قصد is translated with ‘central or even’ the connotation is not correct. That is why it is translated with ‘straight’.

 

4.          سديد  - سد (sadd) means barrier, or wall which is made between two objects and  سد (sadda) means to fill the crack with stones and سداد means the mixture which you put on cracks and holes ie cement. قول سديد  - speech which has no faults, defects or mistakes:  sound and truthful speech, that has integrity, the opposite is قول الزور which means much more than just  a lie.

 

In Surah Nisaa: (4:9):

 

 فَلْيَتَّقُوا اللَّهَ وَلْيَقُولُوا قَوْلًا سَدِيدًا

 

So let them fear Allah and speak words of appropriate justice.

 

 

Summary:

1.      مستقيم  - even ,equal and straight

2.        (سوى) level, straight and correct and سواء – something that passes in the middle and straight

3.      قصد- someone who correctly defines the values and it is free from excessive and lower extremity.

4.      سديد-  an object which has no cracks faults , strong, clean and straight.
