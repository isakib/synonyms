Hard 

In the Quran, the words عَرِمَ, غَلِيظ, قََا سِيه (قسو), قَمطَرِير (قمطر), عَصِيب, رَابِيَه (ربو), اَد هَاَى, اَشَدَّ, اَشَقَّhave been used.

1- اَشَقَّ

شَقَّ الاَمرmeans something difficult. شَقَّعَلَيفُلاَنmeans to make things difficult for someone and اَشَقَّis commonly used for a difficulty which is beyond the strength of the person. To be extremely stressful.

وَلَعَذَابُ ٱلۡأَخِرَةِ أَشَقُّ*ۖ 

“and verily the doom of the Hereafter is more painful “13/34

2- اَشَدَّ

قُوَّةَ فِيشَئٍmeans that something itself is hard and strong. Its commonly used for a very strong body, immense strength and torment.

يُرَدُّونَ إِلَىٰٓ أَشَدِّ ٱلۡعَذَابِ*ۗ 

“On the Day of Resurrection they will be consigned to the most grievous doom “2/85

3- اَدهَاَى

دَهِيَ means to torture someone and دَاهِيَmeans a very grave matter and دَوَاهِيالدَّهرmeans accidents that happen in life and اَدهَاَىmeans grave difficulties.

وَٱلسَّاعَةُ أَدۡهَىٰ وَأَمَرُّ 

“And the Hour will be more wretched and more bitter” 54/46

4- رَابِيَه

رَبَا means to grow, flourish, proliferate and رَابِيَهmeans something that constantly flourishes or increases.

فَعَصَوۡاْ رَسُولَ رَبِّہِمۡ فَأَخَذَهُمۡ أَخۡذَةً۬ رَّابِيَةً 

“And they disobeyed the messenger of their Lord, therefore did He grip them with a tightening grip “69/10

5- عَصِيب

عَصَبَ has 2 aspects. 1, the connection between two things and 2, strength. عَصبَة(Pl: اَعصَاب) means muscles. Hence, عَصبَةis that strong party whose numbers are 10 or greater and they are well connected among themselves, which makes them very strong. As the brothers of Yousuf (AS) said:

وَنَحۡنُ عُصۡبَةٌ 

“And we are a strong band”12/24 

تَعصُب is bigotry, not accepting the manifest truth and عَصِيبis something that is hard as well as evil.

وَلَمَّا جَآءَتۡ رُسُلُنَا لُوطً۬ا سِىٓءَ بِہِمۡ وَضَاقَ بِہِمۡ ذَرۡعً۬ا وَقَالَ هَـٰذَا يَوۡمٌ عَصِيبٌ۬ 

“And when Our messengers came unto Lot, he was distressed and knew not how to protect them. He said: This is a distressful day “11/77

6- قَمطَرِير

It means something that fills the heart with dread. قِمطَرis the wood used for stretching the feet of and punishing criminals. قَمطَرِيرmeans serious fear and excessively hard times.

إِنَّا نَخَافُ مِن رَّبِّنَا يَوۡمًا عَبُوسً۬ا قَمۡطَرِيرً۬ا 

“Lo! we fear from our Lord a day of frowning and of fate” 76/10

7- قََاسِيه

قسو points towards the quality of extremeness, meaning utterly strong and solid. هَجَرٌقَاسٍmeans hard stone. اَلقَسوَةmeans having a heart of stone as in a heart devoid or mercy and compassion. اَلذَنبٌ مُسقَاةٌ لِلقَلبِmeans that sins harden the hearts.

فَ وَيۡلٌ۬ لِّلۡقَـٰسِيَةِ قُلُوبُہُم مِّن ذِكۡرِ ٱللَّهِ*ۚ

“Then woe unto those whose hearts are hardened against remembrance of Allah “39/22

8- غَلِيظ

غَلَظَ means thick, flat, strong and abrasive or uneven and غَلَظَ الرَّجُلmeans an abrasive man. غَلِيظ(Ant: لِِينٌ) carries the same meaning as above. غَلِيظُ القَلبmeans a person who is both abrasive and rude. (Pl: غِلاَظ)

عَلَيۡہَا مَلَـٰٓٮِٕكَةٌ غِلَاظٌ۬ شِدَادٌ۬ لَّا يَعۡصُونَ ٱللَّهَ مَآ أَمَرَهُمۡ 

“Over which are set angels strong, severe, who resist not Allah in that which He commandeth them “66/6

9- عَرِمَ

عُرَامَ means harshness of a person which is also reflected in one's actions and عُرَامَُالجَيشmeans the strength and harshness of an army.

فَأَرۡسَلۡنَا عَلَيۡہِمۡ سَيۡلَ ٱلۡعَرِمِ 

“So We sent on them the flood of 'Iram” 34/16

Inference 

1- اَشَقَّ: Pain beyond ability

2- اَشَدَّ: To be something hard in itself

3- اَدهَي: Hard in terms of affliction

4- رَابِيَه: The thing that increases by the second

5- عَصِيب: Hard and evil

6- قَمطَرِِير: Hard, in terms of fear and evil

7- قَاسِيه: Hardness of heart

8- غَلِيظ: Cold and rude

9- عَرِمَ: Harshness which is reflected in one's character
