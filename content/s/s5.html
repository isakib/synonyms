5. SAMANE ANA [to come forth, to appear]

Synonyms: AQBALA, BARAZA

5.1 AQBALA (اَقْبَلَ): QABAL-AL-MAKAAN (قَبَلَ المَکَان) means to head for a place and QABAL (قْابَل) means two things coming face to face, competing. QABBALA (قْبَّلَ) means to embrace and AQBALA (اَقْبَلَ) means to stand/come in front of someone facing it. Or face something. To come from the front (dictionary Al Manjad). In the Quran

Surah Yusuf (Joseph): سورۃ یُوسُف 12:71

 قَالُواْ وَأَقۡبَلُواْ عَلَيۡهِم مَّاذَا تَفۡقِدُونَ

 They said, as they came towards them, “What are you missing?”

5.2 BARAZA (بَرَزَ): BARZ (بَرْزَ) means an open field, atmosphere. BARAZA (بَرَزَ) means to go out into an open area. Or emerge in open after isolation or anonymity (dictionary Al Manjad). Allah says in the Holy Quran

Surah Ibraheem (Abraham): سورۃ ابراھیم 14:21

وَبَرَزُواْ لِلَّهِ جَمِيعً۬ا

They will emerge before God, altogether. 

Summary:

5.1 AQBALA: To come towards, to face.

5.2 BARAZA: To emerge

To come out

For this the words بَرَزَand اَقبَلَhave been used.

1- اَقبَلَ

قَبَلَ المَكَانmeans to face in the direction and قَابَلَis used to denote when 2 entities confront each other. قَبَّلَmeans to kiss and اَقبَلَmeans to turn one's face in someone's direction to face them or appear in front of them.

قَالُواْ وَأَقۡبَلُواْ عَلَيۡهِم مَّاذَا تَفۡقِدُونَ

“They cried, coming toward them: What is it ye have lost?” 12/71

2- بَرَزَ

بَرزmeans outdoors and open plane and بَرَزَmeans to come into the open plane. At the same time it means to appear or to manifest after a period of hiding.

وَبَرَزُواْ لِلَّهِ جَمِيعً۬ا

“They all come forth unto their Lord” 14/21
