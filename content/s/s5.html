To come out

For this the words بَرَزَand اَقبَلَhave been used.

1- اَقبَلَ

قَبَلَ المَكَانmeans to face in the direction and قَابَلَis used to denote when 2 entities confront each other. قَبَّلَmeans to kiss and اَقبَلَmeans to turn one's face in someone's direction to face them or appear in front of them.

قَالُواْ وَأَقۡبَلُواْ عَلَيۡهِم مَّاذَا تَفۡقِدُونَ

“They cried, coming toward them: What is it ye have lost?” 12/71

2- بَرَزَ

بَرزmeans outdoors and open plane and بَرَزَmeans to come into the open plane. At the same time it means to appear or to manifest after a period of hiding.

وَبَرَزُواْ لِلَّهِ جَمِيعً۬ا

“They all come forth unto their Lord” 14/21
