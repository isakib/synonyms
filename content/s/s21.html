To Listen – To Declare or Denote 

For this the Quran has used the words اَذَنَ, اَذِنَ,إِستَمِع, إِسمَعَ, سَمَّاعَ, سَمِعَ

1- سَمِعَ

To hear something with your ears and اَلسَّمعَis the ability to hear and also the ears.

قَدۡ سَمِعَ ٱللَّهُ قَوۡلَ ٱلَّتِى تُجَـٰدِلُكَ فِى زَوۡجِهَا 

“Allah hath heard the saying of her that disputeth with thee (Muhammad) concerning her husband” 58:1

2- سَمَّاعَ

سَامِع means the one who listens and سَمَّاعَis used for a spy, in other words one who eavesdrops on a conversation.

سَمَّـٰعُونَ لِلۡڪَذِبِ سَمَّـٰعُونَ لِقَوۡمٍ ءَاخَرِينَ لَمۡ يَأۡتُوكَ*ۖ 

“Listeners for the sake of falsehood, listeners on behalf of other folk who come not unto thee” 5:41

3- إِسمَعَ

Means to make someone listen 

إِنَّكَ لَا تُسۡمِعُ ٱلۡمَوۡتَىٰ وَلَا تُسۡمِعُ ٱلصُّمَّ ٱلدُّعَآءَ إِذَا وَلَّوۡاْ مُدۡبِرِينَ 

“Lo! thou canst not make the dead to hear, nor canst thou make the deaf to hear the call when they have turned to flee” 27:80

4- إِستَمِع

It is to try hard to listen to something when its hard to hear. 

قُلۡ أُوحِىَ إِلَىَّ أَنَّهُ ٱسۡتَمَعَ نَفَرٌ۬ مِّنَ ٱلۡجِنِّ فَقَالُوٓاْ إِنَّا سَمِعۡنَا قُرۡءَانًا عَجَبً۬ا 

“Say (O Muhammad): It is revealed unto me that a company of the Jinn gave ear, and they said: Lo! we have heard a marvellous Qur'an” 72:1

5- اَذِنَ

To listen carefully and attentively 

6- اَذَنَ

Means to tell off, to warn 

وَيَوۡمَ يُنَادِيہِمۡ أَيۡنَ شُرَڪَآءِى قَالُوٓاْ ءَاذَنَّـٰكَ مَا مِنَّا مِن شَہِيدٍ۬ 

“And on the day when He calleth unto them: Where are now My partners? they will say: We confess unto Thee, not one of us is a witness (for them)” 41:47

Inference 

1- سَمِعَ: To hear, used commonly

2- سَمَّاعَ: To eavesdrop

3- إِسمَعَ: To declare, to make someone listen

4- إِستَمِع: To try and hear

5- اَذِنَ: To listen attentively

6- اَذَنَ: To warn
