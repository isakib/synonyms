Black – Blackness 

For this, the Quran uses the words اَسوَدَ, غَرَابِيب, اَحوِي, مُد هَامَّتََاند هم (دَهم), قَتَرَةَ,مِدَاد

1- اَسوَدَ

It is the black colour (Ant: اَبيَض) and is a common use word. (Pl:سُود) (Fem:سَودََأ)

وَكُلُواْ وَٱشۡرَبُواْ حَتَّىٰ يَتَبَيَّنَ لَكُمُ ٱلۡخَيۡطُ ٱلۡأَبۡيَضُ مِنَ ٱلۡخَيۡطِ ٱلۡأَسۡوَدِ مِنَ ٱلۡفَجۡرِ*ۖ 

“And eat and drink until the white thread becometh distinct to you from the black thread of the dawn “ 2:187

2- غَرَابِيب

غُرَاب is the crow and غِربِيبis as black as the crow and غُربِيبis a person who dies his hair black. (Pl:غَرَابِيب)

وَمِنَ ٱلۡجِبَالِ جُدَدُۢ بِيضٌ۬ وَحُمۡرٌ۬ مُّخۡتَلِفٌ أَلۡوَٲنُہَا وَغَرَابِيبُ سُودٌ۬ 

“And among the hills are streaks white and red, of divers hues, and (others) raven-black” 35:27

3- اَحوِي

A blackish dark green or a blackish red colour. Quran usesغُثَآءً أَحۡوَىٰ

غُثَا is the rubbish made up of twigs and leaves that forms in the foam of river water and and lands on the bank and when rubbish is trampled upon and the colour becomes blackish then that colour is called اَحوِي

فَجَعَلَهُۥ غُثَآءً أَحۡوَىٰ 

“Then turneth it to russet stubble” 87:5

4- اَدهَم

دَهَمَ is to drown in darkness and اَلدَّهمَةmeans blackness and دَهَّمَتِ النَّارََُالقِدرmeans that the fire has blackened the cooking pot.

مُدۡهَآمَّتَانِ 

“Dark green with foliage “ 55:64

5- قَتَرَةَ

قَتَرَالنََّار means a sooty fire and قَتَرَةَmeans black like smoke, dark and dusty.

وَوُجُوهٌ۬ يَوۡمَٮِٕذٍ عَلَيۡہَا غَبَرَةٌ۬ (﻿٤٠﻿) تَرۡهَقُهَا قَتَرَةٌ

“And other faces, on that day, with dust upon them, (40) Veiled in darkness” 80:40-41

6- مِدَاد

مَدَدتُ الدَّوَاةmeans to pour ink into the ink pot and مِدَادis the ink which is used in writing.

قُل لَّوۡ كَانَ ٱلۡبَحۡرُ مِدَادً۬ا لِّكَلِمَـٰتِ رَبِّى لَنَفِدَ ٱلۡبَحۡرُ قَبۡلَ أَن تَنفَدَ كَلِمَـٰتُ رَبِّى وَلَوۡ جِئۡنَا بِمِثۡلِهِۦ مَدَدً۬ا 

“Say: Though the sea became ink for the Words of my Lord, verily the sea would be used up before the words of my Lord were exhausted, even though We brought the like thereof to help” 18:109

Inference 

1- اَسوَدَ: Black colour, opposite of white

2- غَرَابِيب: Deep black colour

3- اَحوِي: Greenish black

4- اَدهَم: Blackish green

5- قَتَرَةَ: Smoky colour

6- مِدَاد: Ink
