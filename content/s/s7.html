7. SAB, SARAY [Everyone, All]

Synonyms: KULL, KAAFFAH, AJMA’UN

7.1 KULL (کّل): means everyone, all (opposite JUZ جزء). This word is used when KULL (کّل) is a collection of units, and it is also used to represent each portion for instance every person, everyone, everything. In the Quran

Surah Al-Israa (The Night Journey): سورۃ الٕاسرَاء  17:84

قُلۡ ڪُلٌّ۬ يَعۡمَلُ عَلَىٰ شَاكِلَتِهِ

 Say: Everyone acts according to his own disposition:

When using this word as a collection of units, the word still means all, everyone. In the Quran

Surah Al-Baqarah (The cow): سورۃ البَقَرَۃ  2:31

 وَعَلَّمَ ءَادَمَ ٱلۡأَسۡمَآءَ كُلَّهَا

And He taught Adam the names, all of them;

7.2 KAAFFAH (کَافَّۃ): AL-KAFAF (الکفاف) means completely, total girth of anything. KAFF (کََافّ) is an agent noun and KAAFFAH (کَافَّۃ) is the feminine of this noun. It is used in two ways.

1. When KULL (کّل) is a collection of units then it will also represent this complete collection. In this respect it is prolific to KULL (کّل). Allah says in the Quran

Surah Saba (Sheba): سورۃ سَبَإ 34:28

وَمَآ أَرۡسَلۡنَـٰكَ إِلَّا ڪَآفَّةً۬ لِّلنَّاسِ بَشِيرً۬ا وَنَذِيرً۬ا

We sent you only universally to all people, a herald and warner,

2. When KULL (کّل) represents a complete unit then KAAFFAH (کَافَّۃ) represents the enclosure of all its constituents. In this respect it is prolific to KAMIL (کامل). Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Baqarah (The cow): سورۃ البَقَرَۃ  2:208

 يَـٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُواْ ٱدۡخُلُواْ فِى ٱلسِّلۡمِ ڪَآفَّةً۬

O you who believe! Enter into submission, wholeheartedly

In this Ayah KAAFFAH (کَافَّۃ) means both all and completely.

7.3 AJMA’UN (اَجْمَعُوْن): JAMA (جمع) means to gather or collect. AJMA’ (اَجْمَعَ) means to do something collectively. AJAMA’-ALAL-AMAR (اَجْمَعَ عَلَی الْاَمْر) means everyone  agreeing to an opinion. In the Quran

Surah Yusuf (Joseph): سورۃ یُوسُف 12:15

 فَلَمَّا ذَهَبُواْ بِهِۦ وَأَجۡمَعُوٓاْ أَن يَجۡعَلُوهُ فِى غَيَـٰبَتِ ٱلۡجُبِّ‌ۚ

So they went away with him, and agreed to put him at the bottom of the well.

And AJMA’UN (اَجْمَعُوْن) means to perform a task together at one time. Allah says in the Holy Quran

Surah Al-Hijr (The Rock): سورۃ الحِجر 15:30

فَسَجَدَ ٱلۡمَلَـٰٓٮِٕكَةُ ڪُلُّهُمۡ أَجۡمَعُونَ

 So the angels prostrated themselves, all together.

 

Summary:

7.1 KULL: Everyone, all

7.2 KAFFAH: Completely, all, everyone. Prolific to both KULL and KAMIL

7.3 AJMA’UN: Together, collectively.

All, Everyone 

The words, كُلّ، كَافّة، and اجمَعُونhave been used in the Quran.

1- كُلّ

كُلّ means everyone, all (ant:جُزء) and is used when the addressees comprise of numerous denominations. This particular word is also used for every single entity or being, being addressed. For example, every single thing, every single person.

قُلۡ ڪُلٌّ۬ يَعۡمَلُ عَلَىٰ شَاكِلَتِهِ 

“Say: Each one doth according to his rule of conduct” 17:84 

For the assembly as a whole it is used as: 

“ وَعَلَّمَ ءَادَمَ ٱلۡأَسۡمَآءَ كُلَّهَا

“And He taught Adam all the names” 2/31 

2- كَافّة

الكفَاف means to encompass completely. كَافّcan be loosely generalised, in English, as a noun that performs an action but its the performer in question that is being addressed and not the action. So, كَافّis the noun and كَافّةis the feminine gender. It can be used in 2 ways.

a- When كُلّis a collection of several types of units then it is used for the whole collection of units and carries a more concise meaning than كُلّ

وَمَآ أَرۡسَلۡنَـٰكَ إِلَّا ڪَآفَّةً۬ لِّلنَّاسِ بَشِيرً۬ا وَنَذِيرً۬ 

“And We have not sent thee (O Muhammad) save as a bringer of good tidings and a warner unto all” 34/28 

b- When كُلّconsists of only a single unit then it is used to cover all aspects of that unit.

يَـٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُواْ ٱدۡخُلُواْ فِى ٱلسِّلۡمِ ڪَآفَّةً۬ 

“O ye who believe! Come, all of you, into submission” 2/208

In the above ayah, كَافّةis giving both the meanings as in 'all of you' and 'everything of you'.

3- اجمَعُون

جَمع means to gather and اَجمَعَmeans to make a collective effort. اَجمَعَعلَيالاَمرmeans to reach unanimity.

فَلَمَّا ذَهَبُواْ بِهِۦ وَأَجۡمَعُوٓاْ أَن يَجۡعَلُوهُ فِى غَيَـٰبَتِ ٱلۡجُبِّ*ۚ 

“Then, when they led him off, and were of one mind that they should place him in the depth of the pit” 12/15 

اَجمَعُون means to make a collective effort in unison.

فَسَجَدَ ٱلۡمَلَـٰٓٮِٕكَةُ ڪُلُّهُمۡ أَجۡمَعُونَ 

“So the angels fell prostrate, all of them together” 15/30 

Inference 

1- كُلّ: Means each of you and also means everyone of you.

2- كَافّة: Means all of you and everyone of you. It is more concise than كُلّand كَامل

3- اَجمَعُون: Means everyone in unison
