To Rebel 

The Quran uses طَغَي, عَلاَ (علو), عَتَا (عتو), مَرَدَ

1- طَغَي

Means to be extremely or exceptionally disobedient. طَغَااَلمَاءis the rising of water and spreading towards the banks or flooding . طَاغِيةmeans severe punishment or penalty, hurricane. طَاغُوتis everything who is followed rather than Allah, let it be a person or a system. One who oversteps boundaries.

كَلَّآ إِنَّ ٱلۡإِنسَـٰنَ لَيَطۡغَىٰٓ (﻿٦﻿) أَن رَّءَاهُ ٱسۡتَغۡنَىٰٓ

“Nay, but verily man is rebellious (6) That he thinketh himself independent!” 96/6

2- عَتَا

عتويد الاَستكبَارmeans rebelliousness based on arrogance. For instance, man being arrogant deviates from the law of Allah.

بَل لَّجُّواْ فِى عُتُوٍّ۬ وَنُفُورٍ 

“Nay, but they are set in pride and frowardness” 67/21

3- عَلاَ

Means to rise, to overcome, to be arrogant and to rebel because of having a position, tyranny. 

لَتُفۡسِدُنَّ فِى ٱلۡأَرۡضِ مَرَّتَيۡنِ وَلَتَعۡلُنَّ عُلُوًّ۬ا ڪَبِيرً۬ا 

“Ye verily will work corruption in the earth twice, and ye will become great tyrants” 17/4

4- مَرَدَ

Again means disobedience and rebellion. It also means to beat contemporaries. Thus, it also means such a rebellion in which a person exceeds his peers. 

وَإِن يَدۡعُونَ إِلَّا شَيۡطَـٰنً۬ا مَّرِيدً۬ا 

“They pray to none else than Satan, a rebel” 4/117

Inference 

1- طَغَي: Rebellion that also manifests overtaking and severity

2- عَتَا: Rebellion based on arrogance

3- عَلاَ: Arrogance after taking power

4- مَرَدَ: Rebelliousness and then exceeding in it
