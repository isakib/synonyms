To ask for water

Two words have been stated to demonstrate the phrase.

1.إستسقئ: سقئ to make someone drink سقئ to give someone water to drink. إستسقئ to ask for water in order to drink it. Ref (2:60)

2.غيث: is the rain that falls when its direly needed and suffices the need. And grass or verdure that grows in result of that rain fall can also be considered as غيث. غاث الله البلاد means Allah has made the rain fall on that country. إستغاث to plea/supplicate for this kind of rain fall or water. Ref (18:29).
