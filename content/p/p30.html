30.6 WASL (وَصَل) :

[to reach, unite or join]

 WASL can be used with these three meanings separately. For example:

a.       Meaning to reach:

Surah Hud 11:81

قَالُوا يَا لُوطُ إِنَّا رُسُلُ رَبِّكَ لَن يَصِلُوا إِلَيْكَ  

The angels said, "O Lot, indeed we are messengers of your Lord; [therefore], they will never reach you.

b.      Meaning to unite or join:

Surah Al-Baqarah (The Cow) 2:27

وَيَقْطَعُونَ مَا أَمَرَ اللَّهُ بِهِ أَن يُوصَلَ

 “and sever that which Allah has ordered to be joined (or held together)”

 

30.7 AFDAA (اَفْضي): FADA means the vast and empty space between the earth and sky. Afdaa is used figuratively for intimate relations with one’s woman or to tell a secret. For example:

Surah An-Nisa (The Women) 4:21

وَكَيْفَ تَأْخُذُونَهُ وَقَدْ أَفْضَىٰ بَعْضُكُمْ إِلَىٰ بَعْضٍ

 “And how could you take it while you have gone in unto each other”

30.8 MASSA (مَسَّ): Massa literally means to touch physically but can also be used figuratively to refer to an experience. For example, the Quran says:

Surah Al-Araf 7:95

ثُمَّ بَدَّلْنَا مَكَانَ السَّيِّئَةِ الْحَسَنَةَ حَتَّىٰ عَفَوا وَّقَالُوا قَدْ مَسَّ آبَاءَنَا الضَّرَّاءُ وَالسَّرَّاءُ

“Then We exchanged in place of the bad [condition], good, until they increased [and prospered] and said, ‘Our fathers [also] were touched with hardship and ease.’ So We seized them suddenly while they did not perceive.”

30.9 WARADA (وَرَدَ): means finding a well after searching for water. Its antonym is SADARA (صَدَرَ) which means to return after collecting or drinking water from a well.

Surah Al-Qasas 28:23

وَلَمَّا وَرَدَ مَاءَ مَدْيَنَ وَجَدَ عَلَيْهِ أُمَّةً مِّنَ النَّاسِ يَسْقُونَ

 “And when he came to the well of Madyan, he found there a crowd of people watering [their flocks] “

Summary:

30.1BALAGHA: To reach the last stage of an objective.

30.2ASAABA:

30.3NAALA: To reach and obtain something one desires

30.4NAWASHA: To reach out and grab something. Can be used instead of Naala in a more general sense

30.5 TA’ATWAA: To gain something one has no right upon

30.6WASALA: To reach something and join it

30.7AFDAA: Figuratively refers to intimate relations between man and wife

30.8MASSA: Literal meaning is to touch but can be used to mean something gained from an experience.

30.9WARADA: To reach a well
