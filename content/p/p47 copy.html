BEHIND-47

Behind 

Synonyms are:

1)بعدَ

ادْبَار(2)

خلْف(3)

ورَآء(4)

بعدَ(1

It means behind/after or "SUBSEQUENTLY"

it is adverb of time(Munjid - منجد - Dictionary - (Arabic to Urdu)=(منجد)) and the opposite of this word is before.

ثُمَّ بَعَثْنَاكُم مِّن بَعْدِ مَوْتِكُمْ لَعَلَّكُمْ تَشْكُرُونَ (surah 2 baqrah,verse 56)

TRANSLATION:

Then We raised you up after your death, so that you might be grateful.

2)ادْبَار

the back of anything is called دُبر and the opposite of it is front/before.this word is used in terms of time period it is zarf zaman(that is grammatical term for the time period during which action takes place).for example after finishing of a work or soon after completeing an action.e:g دبر الصّلوة which means after prayer.The plural of this word is ادْبَار ALLAH says in quran:

وَمِنَ اللَّيْلِ فَسَبِّحْهُ وَأَدْبَارَ السُّجُودِ (surah 50 QAAF verse 40)

TRANSLATION:

And during part of the night, (also,) celebrate His praises, and (so likewise) after the postures of adoration.

and اِدبارَ it has a kasrah under it and is read as "eidbaar" which means sumthing showing its back and getting disappear. in another place ALLAh says in quran:

وَمِنَ اللَّيْلِ فَسَبِّحْهُ وَإِدْبَارَ النُّجُومِ (surah 52 at tur verse 49)

translation: And in the night-time, also glorify His Praises, and at the setting of the stars.

the eloquence of this verse is that Allah used the word اِدبَار but at the same time the verse portray the meaning of word اَدبَار as well.

3)خلْف

this means behind and its opposite is بِينَ يدين which means in between hands or in hands or in front(Mufraddat - مفرداتby Imam Raghib - (Arabic to Urdu) =(مف))or near it is used as zarf zaman or makaan(both are grammatical terms to explain the time period or the place,to tell when or where the action took place). if it is used as zarf zaman then it is the time period when action took place then the word بِينَ يدين means right now the current time and if it is used as in terms of zarf makani that is the place where action took place then the word بِينَ يدينmeans in front which is the opposite of word خلْف .in Quran it comes as:

فَجَعَلْنَاهَا نَكَالًا لِّمَا بَيْنَ يَدَيْهَا وَمَا خَلْفَهَا وَمَوْعِظَةً لِّلْمُتَّقِينَ (surah 2 al baqrah verse 66)

translation: So We made it an example to their own time and to their posterity, and a lesson to those who fear Allah.

لَهُ مُعَقِّبَاتٌ مِّن بَيْنِ يَدَيْهِ وَمِنْ خَلْفِهِ يَحْفَظُونَهُ مِنْ أَمْرِ اللَّهِ (surah ar-raad 13 verse11)

translation: For each (person), there are angels in succession, before and behind him.

4)ورَآء

This word means before and after and is masculine and feminine Munjid - منجد - Dictionary - (Arabic to Urdu)=(منجد)and is used in both cases to describe time period or to describe place of an action(zarf makan and zarf zamaan) some of the examples are below:

a)in terms of behind and zarf makaan(to tell location):

إِنَّ الَّذِينَ يُنَادُونَكَ مِن وَرَاءِ الْحُجُرَاتِ أَكْثَرُهُمْ لَا يَعْقِلُونَ (surah 49 al hujaraat verse 4)

translation: Verily! Those who call you from behind the dwellings, most of them have no sense.

b)in terms of behind and zarf makaan(to potray time period):

فَبَشَّرْنَاهَا بِإِسْحَاقَ وَمِن وَرَاءِ إِسْحَاقَ يَعْقُوبَ

(surah 11 hud verse 71)

translation: But we gave her glad tidings of Isaac, and after him, of Jacob.

c) in terms of front and zarf makaan (to describe place).

وَكَانَ وَرَاءَهُم مَّلِكٌ يَأْخُذُ كُلَّ سَفِينَةٍ غَصْبًا

(surah 18 al kahaf verse 79)

translation: for there was in front of them a certain king who seized on every boat by force.

ورَآء is also used in terms of other then or apart from.

ALLAh says:

فَمَنِ ابْتَغَىٰ وَرَاءَ ذَٰلِكَ فَأُولَٰئِكَ هُمُ الْعَادُونَ

(surah 23 Al-Mu'minun verse 7)

translation: But whoever desires someone beyond these (lawful women), it is these people who are transgressors (and disobedient).

ورآ ءَ لانسان means grandson as it can be seen above in example (b).

so the word ورَآء is used in terms of beyond, this side or that side, here or there .this word is zarf zaman and makaan, as it is seen in the following verse:

فَإِذَا سَجَدُوا فَلْيَكُونُوا مِن وَرَائِكُمْ وَلْتَأْتِ طَائِفَةٌ أُخْرَىٰ لَمْ يُصَلُّوا فَلْيُصَلُّوا مَعَكَ

(surah 4 an nisa verse 102)

translation: When they finish their prostrations, let them Take their position in the rear. And let the other party come up which hath not yet prayed - and let them pray with you.

Summary:

(1) بعدَ is an adverb of time (opposite of this word is قَبل which means first) according to time and period it means before.

2) ادْبَار it is zarf zaman means mutasil soon after and its opposite is قُبل means before.

3)خَلْف it is adverb of time and place its opposite is بِينَ يدين if it is adverb of time then بِينَ يدين orايدي it means that the people present in that time and if it is used as adverb of place then it means in front.

4) ورَآء it is opposite of itself it means infront or back,this way or that way,here or there, this side or that side.
