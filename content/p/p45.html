45. Peeth – Pusht پیٹھ - پشت (Back – Behind)

Also known as zahr ظَهر , dubur دُبُر  and suulb صُلب .

1.      Zahr ظَهر : The entire external portion of the body from behind, starting from the rectum up till the neck. The plural of zahr ظَهر is zuhoor ظُهور .

In the Holy Quran,

Surah Al Inshiqaq (The Sundering)سُوۡرَةُ الانشقاق

(84:10)

وَأَمَّا مَنۡ أُوتِىَ كِتَـٰبَهُ ۥ وَرَآءَ ظَهۡرِهِۦ

“But as for he who is given his record behind his back,”

 

2.      Dubur دُبُر  : This means the rectum and the back or behind of everything. The plural of dubur دُبُر    is adbaar ادبار  , and the antonym is qubul قُبُل . The meaning of dubur دُبُر    and adbaar ادبار  also define afterward or backward. Dubur-as-salaat (دبرالصلوة ) and adbaar-us-sujood (ادبارالسجود ) (50:40) mean ‘after the salaat(prayer)’.

 

Similarly,

 

Surah Al Hijr (The Rocky Tract) سُوۡرَةُ الحِجر

 

(15:65)

 

وَٱتَّبِعۡ أَدۡبَـٰرَهُمۡ

 

“So set out with your family during a portion of the night and follow behind them and let not anyone among you look back and continue on to where you are commanded."

 

As though, the meaning of dubur دُبُر    is vast and far more descriptive than zahr ظَهر .

 

In the Holy Quran,

 

Surah Al Qamar (The Moon) سُوۡرَةُ القَمَر

 

(54:45)

 

سَيُہۡزَمُ ٱلۡجَمۡعُ وَيُوَلُّونَ ٱلدُّبُرَ

“[Their] assembly will be defeated, and they will turn their backs [in retreat].”

 

3.      Suulb صُلب : salab-al-azm صَلَبَ العَظم  means to extract the fatty tissue from the bone. Salab صلب  means the fats and the marrow of the backbone/vertebra. Now since the backbone lies in the middle of the back, therefore the translation of salab صلب  is taken as back or behind.

Allah Taa’la says:

Surah At Tariq (The Nightcommer) سُوۡرَةُ الطّارق

(86:7)

يَخۡرُجُ مِنۢ بَيۡنِ ٱلصُّلۡبِ وَٱلتَّرَآٮِٕبِ

“Emerging from between the backbone and the ribs.”

 

Conclusion:

(1)   Zahr ظَهر : The external back portion of any living being, starting from the rectum up till the neck.

(2)   Dubur دُبُر  : The rectum and the back or behind of everything.

(3)   Suulb صُلب : The backbone, including the fats and marrow. The behind.
