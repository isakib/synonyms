To up bring/ nurture

رَبَّا،رَبّئ(ربؤ)، انبت، انشَا، ربَّ، كَفّلَ are the terms which applies on the context of upbringing/nurturing/ raising etc.

1.رَبَّا and رَبّئ means upbringing of a baby/infant. رَبّئ exclusively means to up bring or to foster a kid or to civilize. Ref (26:18), (17:24)

2.انبت: نبت (noun)growth of grass or foliation. انبت (verb) to cultivate/grow something. But if the child is being fostered/nurtured in such a careful way that he out stands among others then Allah has used انبت for that kind of brought up. Ref (3:27)

3.انشَا: نشَا to be born, to come to life, to reach youth (مُنجِد). انشَاto give someone existence and then to up bring. نَاشِيyoung/fully grown/adult.نشَا ة= growth. Ref (56:62), (56:76), (13:12) نشَاء ،يُنشِئُ only means to up bring or to raise. Ref (43:18).

4.ربَّ it’s the noun which means to gradually raise someone and make them reach the perfection and completeness and to slack all of one’s necessities. Commonly stated as sustainer. This word is common for subject noun. Ref (1:2). This attribute exclusively belongs to Allah SWT and HE is the ربَّ. But this word can also be taken in the means of master or owner. But in this scenario the noun of this verb would be ربَابيت not ربُوبِية. Ref (12:41). Generally we don’t find plural for ربَّ but as disbelievers had taken many of them so Quran has takenاربَاب as plural. Ref (12:39)

5.كَفّلَ : to up bring someone and to spend on that person. Or to financially support anybody and to look after. Ref (3:44).

Summary:

رَبَّا & تربية to rear and up bring. انبت to up bring meticulously and to take care of one’s diet. انشَا to give someone existence and to up bring.ربَّ to give someone existence and gradually make them reach the wholeness. كَفّلَ to be responsible or to be the guarantor of someone’s rearing.

Synonyms: RABA,RABBI,RABU,ANBAT,ANSHA,RABBA,KAFFALA

1 RABA(رَبي),RABBI(رَبّي):RABA ALWALAD(رَبَا الولد) meaning growing up of child,and RABBI meaning to upbring child,to teach him manners

FIRAUN said to MUSA(AS)

Surat Ash-Shu`ara' [26:18] - The Noble Qur'an

أَلَمْ نُرَبِّكَ فِينَا وَلِيدًا

Did we not raise you among us as a child

Surat Al-'Isra' [17:24] - The Noble Qur'an

وَقُل رَّبِّ ارْحَمْهُمَا كَمَا رَبَّيَانِي صَغِيرًا

say, "My Lord, have mercy upon them as they brought me up [when I was] small."

2 ANBATA(انْبَتَ):NABATA(نَبَتَ) meaning growing up of grass or vegetables etc. and ANBATA meaning grow up.but if a child is brought up in an extraordinary manner such that the growth and development of child is accelerated then for that ANBATA is used in Quran

Surat 'Ali `Imran [3:37] - The Noble Qur'an

فَتَقَبَّلَهَا رَبُّهَا بِقَبُولٍ حَسَنٍ وَأَنبَتَهَا نَبَاتًا حَسَنًا

So her Lord accepted her with good acceptance and caused her to grow in a good manner

3 INSHAA(انْشَاَ),:NASHAA(نَشَاَ) meaning to born,to become alive and to reach youth and INSHAA meaning to give birth to someone and then to up bring . NASHI(

نَاشِي) means young man. and NISHAT(نشاة) meaning elevation

Surat Al-Waqi`ah [56:71-72] - The Noble Qur'an

أَفَرَأَيْتُمُ النَّارَ الَّتِي تُورُونَ أَأَنتُمْ أَنشَأْتُمْ شَجَرَتَهَا أَمْ نَحْنُ الْمُنشِئُونَ 

And have you seen the fire that you ignite?

Is it you who produced its tree, or are We the producer?

Surat Ar-Ra`d [13:12] - The Noble Qur'an

وَيُنشِئُ السَّحَابَ الثِّقَالَ

and generates the heavy clouds

and NASHSHAA(نَشّا) YUNASHSHIU(يُنَشِّيُ) from baabb taf3eel meaning only to upbring and nurture

Surat Az-Zukhruf [43:18] - The Noble Qur'an

أَوَمَن يُنَشَّأُ فِي الْحِلْيَةِ وَهُوَ فِي الْخِصَامِ غَيْرُ مُبِينٍ

So is one brought up in ornaments while being during conflict unevident [attributed to Allah ]?

4 RABB(ربّ):RABBUN(ربّ) is a gerund meaning to upbring someone gradually to perfection and to cater for all the needs.This word is normally used as verbal noun

Surat Al-Fatihah [1:2] - The Noble Qur'an

الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ رَبِّ الْعَالَمِينَ

[All] praise is [due] to Allah , Lord of the worlds

This attribute of RABOBIYAT(ربوبيت) is only for ALLAH and ARRABB(الربّ) is only ALLAH.yet RABB can refer to master and owner in that case the gerund is not RABOBIYAH(ربوبية) but RABABIYAT(ربابيت)

Surat Yusuf [12:41] - The Noble Qur'an

يَا صَاحِبَيِ السِّجْنِ أَمَّا أَحَدُكُمَا فَيَسْقِي رَبَّهُ خَمْرًا ۖ

O two companions of prison, as for one of you, he will give drink to his master of wine;

likewise there is no sense of plural of RABB but as disbeliever had made many RABB therefore Quran has used for that plural ARBABUN(ارباب)

Surat Yusuf [12:39] - The Noble Qur'an

يَا صَاحِبَيِ السِّجْنِ أَأَرْبَابٌ مُّتَفَرِّقُونَ خَيْرٌ أَمِ اللَّهُ الْوَاحِدُ الْقَهَّارُ

O [my] two companions of prison, are separate lords better or Allah , the One, the Prevailing?

5 KAFFALA(كفّل):meaning to upbring and spent on someone.meaning to be responsible for someone boarding and lodging and wellbeing

Surat 'Ali `Imran [3:44] - The Noble Qur'an

وَمَا كُنتَ لَدَيْهِمْ إِذْ يُلْقُونَ أَقْلَامَهُمْ أَيُّهُمْ يَكْفُلُ مَرْيَمَ

And you were not with them when they cast their pens as to which of them should be responsible for Mary

Summary:

1 RABBA: only for upbringing and educatingg

2 ANBATA: careful upbringing and nurturing in excellence

3 NASHAA: meaning to give birth and to upbring for growth

4 RABB: to give birth and then to upbring gradually to perfection and take care of all necessities

5 KAFFALA: to be reponsible for upbringing and education
