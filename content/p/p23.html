Topic: 23        

 

POORA (COMPLETE) - SARA -SAB (The Whole -ALL)

 

Synonyms: Kullun, Kamilun, Ka’affatun and Salamun

 

23.1: Kullun: meaning all, the whole, (its opposite is a part) means which has all parts, and Kullun is also used to encircle (enclose) all parts of it. So it means each one, every individual and everything. Let’s see the example. Allah (SWT) said:

 

Surat-al-Ambiya (The Prophets)

21:35

كُلُّ نَفْسٍ ذَائِقَةُ الْمَوْتِ

Everyone is going to taste death

 

Surat Al-'Anfāl (The Spoils of War)

 39:8

وَقَاتِلُوهُمْ حَتَّىٰ لَا تَكُونَ فِتْنَةٌ وَيَكُونَ الدِّينُ كُلُّهُ لِلَّهِ

And fight them until there is no more Fitnah (disbelief and polytheism: i.e. worshipping others besides Allâh) and the religion (worship) will all be for Allâh Alone [in the whole of the world.

 

23.2 Kamil: The complete (its opposite is inexact) means that thing which has complete parts and complete qualities, means which fulfills the Purpose (need).

 

Surat-Al-Baqarah (The Cow)

2- 233

 

وَالْوَالِدَاتُ يُرْضِعْنَ أَوْلَادَهُنَّ حَوْلَيْنِ كَامِلَيْنِ

The mothers shall give suck to their children for two whole years,

 

 

23.3 Kaffatun: Al-Kifaf: is complete Periphery of any thing, and Kaaf is nominative (subject), and its feminine is Kaffatun.it is said (Ja an-nase Kaffatun) means every one came. This word is ublug from Kamil, because it does not only encircle (encompass) parts but also (jumlae pehloo) Now have a look at the examples:

 

Surat-Al-Baqarah (The Cow)

2- 208

 

يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا ادْخُلُوا فِي السِّلْمِ كَافَّةً وَلَا تَتَّبِعُوا خُطُوَاتِ الشَّيْطَانِ ۚ إِنَّهُ لَكُمْ عَدُوٌّ مُبِينٌ

 

O you who believe! Enter perfectly in Islâm (by obeying all the rules and regulations of the Islâmic religion) and follow not the footsteps of Shaitân (Satan). Verily! He is to you a plain enemy.

Surat-ul-Saba

34-28

وَمَا أَرْسَلْنَاكَ إِلَّا كَافَّةً لِلنَّاسِ بَشِيرًا وَنَذِيرًا وَلَٰكِنَّ أَكْثَرَ النَّاسِ لَا يَعْلَمُونَ

And We have not sent you (O Muhammad SAW) except as a giver of glad tidings and a warner to all mankind, but most of men know not.

 

 

Lets see now SARAY-SAB (The Whole –ALL)

 

Salamun: means be guzand and correct healthy and sound As-salam means to be saved and pure from external and internal calamities. And SALAMUN means which is correct in its nature and no one else has right on it.Allah (SWT) said:

Surat-az-Zumar:

39:29

 

رَجُلًا فِيهِ شُرَكَاءُ مُتَشَاكِسُونَ وَرَجُلًا سَلَمًا لِرَجُلٍ هَلْ يَسْتَوِيَانِ مَثَلًا

a man belonging to many partners at variance with each other, and a man belonging entirely to one master: are those two equal in comparison?

 

 

Summary:

 

Kul: it comes in the opposite of Juz (part) and it is also used to encompasses the all parts (complete, whole, each one)

Kamil: It comes in the opposite of Naqis (inexact) which has no shortcoming and fulfill the purpose.

Kaffatun:every individual from a group or jumlae pehloo of some matter all of all .in theses meanings it is ublugh from  Kul (all)

Salamun:be guzun and correct, means on which no one else has any kinds of right.
