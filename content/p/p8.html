Foot

Foot has been translated in Quran with the help of two words.

1.رجل: from shank/calf to the fingers of foot. And its plural is ارجل. Ref (38:42). And رجل also means man and its plural is رجال and if رجَالاَcomes as adjective then it means on foot. And its anonym is ركبَانَا means rider. Ref (2:239)

2.قدم means the distance between the feet when one walks. And its plural comes as اقدَامin English for both of these words رجل & قدم we use foot. And the measurement of 12 inches is also called as foot so in modern Arabic this foot the scale of measurement can be pronounced as قدم. To describe 40 feet اربعِين اقدَام is used. If قدم is used as a verb then it means to step forward or to walk ahead. And قدم as a noun represents foot. Ref (55:41)
