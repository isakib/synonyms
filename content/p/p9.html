Stone

Three words which have been told in Quran give meaning of stone.

1.حجر: defines hard stone حِجَار حِجَارة are the plurals. Ref (2:54)

2.حَصب: small pebbles/rocks. حَاصب is the wind that takes off these small rocks. حَاصب has been used in Quran for the rain of small rocks and pebbles. Ref (67:17).

3.سِجِّيل: is tiny pebble of mud/clay. Means those nippy sharp pebbles that contains on proportion of clay/mud. Ref (11:82)
