1.        Pure/ Clean:

 

Subhaan سُبحان ,Quddus قُدوس ,zakkiya  زَكيَّه ,tahoora طهورا ,tayyab طيَّب are the words used.

1.        According to Ibn e Faaris,  the word sabaah سَبَحَ , has two basic meanings. a) type of worship/prayers b) type of running.  And according to Imaam Raaghib, sabaah سَبَحَ means something passing /flying in the air or passing the water in a fast speed.  Sabbah  سَبَّاح means a fast swimmer. &  farasunn saboohunn فَرَسٌ سبوحٌ is such a horse that doesn’t deviate here & there because of its fast speed.

 

In the Quran, 21/33: Surat Al-'Anbyā' (The Prophets) - سورة الأنبياء

 

وَهُوَ الَّذِي خَلَقَ اللَّيْلَ وَالنَّهَارَ وَالشَّمْسَ وَالْقَمَرَ ۖ كُلٌّ فِي فَلَكٍ يَسْبَحُونَ

And it is He who created the night and the day and the sun and the moon; all [heavenly bodies] in an orbit are swimming.

Similarly, in another verse: 79/3: Surat An-Nāzi`āt (Those who drag forth) - سورة النازعات

 

وَالسَّابِحَاتِ سَبْحًا

And [by] those who glide [as if] swimming

Then this word started to be used for doing something quickly.

Allah says in the Quran: 73/7: Surat Al-Muzzammil (The Enshrouded One) - سورة المزمل

 

إِنَّ لَكَ فِي النَّهَارِ سَبْحًا طَوِيلًا

Indeed, for you by day is prolonged occupation.

1.        Subhaan سُبحان : its derived from sabbah  سَبَحَ. Like for example ghafraan غُفران is derived from ghafar غَفَر . There are million of galaxies, planets in the space which are moving in their pace. They never bump into each other, they never collide, never shake or move/deviate from their path. This shows that the power controlling them is completely flawless, is the supreme power & is very organised & has full control over its creations & has no defect /flaw whatsoever. This is whats meant by Subhaan سُبحان . This is also important that such power that controls the universe has full ownership of it & does not share this ownership with anyone because the interference of any other power would result in disturbance in the cycle. So subhaan  سُبحان is such a power which is devoid of any flaws.(completely flawless) & has full control over all the moving objects in the universe.

 

Allah says in the Quran: 2/116: Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة

 

وَقَالُوا اتَّخَذَ اللَّهُ وَلَدًا ۗ سُبْحَانَهُ ۖ بَل لَّهُ مَا فِي السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضِ ۖ كُلٌّ لَّهُ قَانِتُونَ

 

They say, " Allah has taken a son." Exalted is He! Rather, to Him belongs whatever is in the heavens and the earth. All are devoutly obedient to Him,

 

Whatever all these things in the universe are doing (following what they are ordered to do) is their way of showing obedience to the Almighty,its their way of worship,prayers. In other words, they are all giving witness by their actions that the one  controlling them is free of all flaws or defects. He is the Al mighty!

 

Allah says in the Quran: 61/1: Surat Aş-Şaf (The Ranks) - سورة الصف

 

سَبَّحَ لِلَّهِ مَا فِي السَّمَاوَاتِ وَمَا فِي الْأَرْضِ ۖ وَهُوَ الْعَزِيزُ الْحَكِيمُ

 

Whatever is in the heavens and whatever is on the earth exalts Allah , and He is the Exalted in Might, the Wise.

 

 There is another aspect to this. When a person ponders over how well organised this system is working in the universe where, there is absolutely no delay in the process, no mistakes, no collision, then, he automatically  praises the creator of the universe. The creator who controls all , that moves in the universe. This is where Subhaan سُبحان  is used. When the skills of the creator of this universe puts a person in awe/surprise. In short, this word is used in praise & when one wonders in awe.So in these situations, we say ‘subhaan teri qudrat’ سُبحان تيرى قدرت  (pure is your power)

 

Allah says in the Quran: 17/1: Surat Al-'Isrā' (The Night Journey) - سورة الإسراء

 

سُبْحَانَ الَّذِي أَسْرَىٰ بِعَبْدِهِ لَيْلًا مِّنَ الْمَسْجِدِ الْحَرَامِ إِلَى الْمَسْجِدِ الْأَقْصَى الَّذِي بَارَكْنَا حَوْلَهُ لِنُرِيَهُ مِنْ آيَاتِنَا ۚ إِنَّهُ هُوَ السَّمِيعُ الْبَصِيرُ

 

Exalted is He who took His Servant by night from al-Masjid al-Haram to al-Masjid al- Aqsa, whose surroundings We have blessed, to show him of Our signs. Indeed, He is the Hearing, the Seeing.

 

So when Subhan سُبحان  is used in this context (above), then it means that this tour/ journey was not spiritual  but physical (He –pbuh did go there physically)

 

2.        Quddus    قُدوس  : quds  قَدُس  means clean & pure. It also means pure & blessed.

Allah says in the Quran:16/102 : Surat An-Naĥl (The Bee) - سورة النحل

 

قُلْ نَزَّلَهُ رُوحُ الْقُدُسِ مِن رَّبِّكَ بِالْحَقِّ لِيُثَبِّتَ الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا وَهُدًى وَبُشْرَىٰ لِلْمُسْلِمِينَ

Say, [O Muhammad], "The Pure Spirit has brought it down from your Lord in truth to make firm those who believe and as guidance and good tidings to the Muslims."

 

So here rooh ul quds   روح القدس (pure spirit) means that Jibraeel (pbuh) presented Allahs verses with purity.( without any amendment or changes). This is the reason why he is called rooh ul amen   روح الامين in other places.

& arze muqaddisa  ارضِ مقدسه means that soil which is pure because the messengers & prophets of Allah are buried or were born there or it is pure because they preached islam on that soil.

Allah says in the Quran: INCORRECT REFERENCE OF AAYAT

 

Quddus is one of the names of Allah. It means clean of all the bad things & a pure power.

 

Allah says in the Quran:  62/1 Surat Al-Jumu`ah (The Congregation, Friday) - سورة الجمعة

 

يُسَبِّحُ لِلَّهِ مَا فِي السَّمَاوَاتِ وَمَا فِي الْأَرْضِ الْمَلِكِ الْقُدُّوسِ الْعَزِيزِ الْحَكِيمِ

 

Whatever is in the heavens and whatever is on the earth is exalting Allah , the Sovereign, the Pure, the Exalted in Might, the Wise.

 

3.        Zakkiya  زَكيَّه : zaka  زَكَى  (   زكو)means grow. To grow,to be better. Zakka  زَكَّى means to make something better than it was originally, to improve it, & to help it move forward/ahead. Zakka nafsah   زَكَّى نفسه means to praise one self. To say good things about yourself.(to boast about oneself)

 

Allah says in the Quran: 53/32: Surat An-Najm (The Star) - سورة النجم

 

الَّذِينَ يَجْتَنِبُونَ كَبَائِرَ الْإِثْمِ وَالْفَوَاحِشَ إِلَّا اللَّمَمَ ۚ إِنَّ رَبَّكَ وَاسِعُ الْمَغْفِرَةِ ۚ هُوَ أَعْلَمُ بِكُمْ إِذْ أَنشَأَكُم مِّنَ الْأَرْضِ وَإِذْ أَنتُمْ أَجِنَّةٌ فِي بُطُونِ أُمَّهَاتِكُمْ ۖ فَلَا تُزَكُّوا أَنفُسَكُمْ ۖ هُوَ أَعْلَمُ بِمَنِ اتَّقَىٰ

 

Those who avoid the major sins and immoralities, only [committing] slight ones. Indeed, your Lord is vast in forgiveness. He was most knowing of you when He produced you from the earth and when you were fetuses in the wombs of your mothers. So do not claim yourselves to be pure; He is most knowing of who fears Him.

 

Tazkeeya  تزكيه means to clean/change, improve. Its generally used with nafs  نفس. Tazkeeya nafs  تزكيه نفس means to clean ones soul/one self from all the spiritual impurities ,diseases of the heart,behavioural issues & purify it & cleanse it.

 

Allah says in the Quran: 91/9: Surat Ash-Shams (The Sun) - سورة الشمس

قَدْ أَفْلَحَ مَن زَكَّاهَا  وَقَدْ خَابَ مَن دَسَّاهَا

He has succeeded who purifies it, And he has failed who instills it [with corruption].

 

Nafs zakkiya   نفس  زكيه  is such a person who is clear of all the corruption & sin & has no accusations attached to him.

 

Quran says: 18/74: Surat Al-Kahf (The Cave) - سورة الكهف

 

 

فَانطَلَقَا حَتَّىٰ إِذَا لَقِيَا غُلَامًا فَقَتَلَهُ قَالَ أَقَتَلْتَ نَفْسًا زَكِيَّةً بِغَيْرِ نَفْسٍ لَّقَدْ جِئْتَ شَيْئًا نُّكْرًا

 

So they set out, until when they met a boy, al-Khidh r killed him. [Moses] said, "Have you killed a pure soul for other than [having killed] a soul? You have certainly done a deplorable thing."
