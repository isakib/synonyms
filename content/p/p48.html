Synonyms Are :

1) خَلَفَ / خِلْفَةً

2) اَردَفَ

3) تليٰ / تلو

1) خَلَفَ khalafa it is opposite of سَلَفَ salafa and it is adverb of time.it means coming after or next ,taking position of sumone after that person is gone or a successor or after comerMunjid - منجد - Dictionary - (Arabic to Urdu)=(منجد). خلفٌ khulafUN comes usually in terms of a an unworthy or non deserving successor Allah says in Quran.

فَخَلَفَ مِن بَعْدِهِمْ خَلْفٌ أَضَاعُوا الصَّلَاةَ وَاتَّبَعُوا الشَّهَوَاتِ ۖ فَسَوْفَ يَلْقَوْنَ غَيًّا.

19:59 (surah 19 maryum,verse 59)

Translation: But after them there followed a posterity who missed prayers and followed after lusts soon, then, will they face Destruction.

And خِلْفَةً khilfataan means two things following one and other e:g first thing coming after second thing and second thing coming after first thing, And this happens consecutively. ALLAH says in Quran.

وَهُوَ الَّذِي جَعَلَ اللَّيْلَ وَالنَّهَارَ خِلْفَةً لِّمَنْ أَرَادَ أَن يَذَّكَّرَ أَوْ أَرَادَ شُكُورًا

25:62 (surah 25 al furqan,verse 62)

Translation: And it is He Who made the Night and the Day to follow each other: for such as have the will to celebrate His praises or to show their gratitude.

2) ARDAFA اَردَفَ means to ride behind someone and ridfoon رِدفٌ means one rider behind another riderMunjid - منجد - Dictionary - (Arabic to Urdu)=(منجد) e;g after one thing same kind of thing following it. And اَردَفَ ardafa means sending one thing after another of same kind, following in succession. رَديف radeef is a famous word ALLAh says in quran .

إِذْ تَسْتَغِيثُونَ رَبَّكُمْ فَاسْتَجَابَ لَكُمْ أَنِّي مُمِدُّكُم بِأَلْفٍ مِّنَ الْمَلَائِكَةِ مُرْدِفِينَ

8:9 (surah 8 al anfaal,verse 9)

Translation: (Remember) when you sought help of your Lord and He answered you (saying): "I will help you with a thousand of the angels each behind the other (following one another) in succession."

3) talaa تليٰ it means something following or something coming behind and keeps on coming again and again Mufraddat - مفرداتby Imam Raghib - (Arabic to Urdu) =(مف) Allah says in Quran.

وَالشَّمْسِ وَضُحَاهَاوَالْقَمَرِ إِذَا تَلَاهَا

91:1-2 (surah 91 as shams,verse 1 and 2)

Translation:And by the sun and its brightness;And by the moon as it follows it (the sun);

SUMMARY:

1)khalafa it is adverb of time for example human beings coming after one another like generations coming after generations and khilfatoon two things following one another continuously.

2)radaf itmeans behind one thing similar things of its type following it continuously.

3)talaa one thing followed by other things consecutively.
