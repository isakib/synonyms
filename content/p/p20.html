Place of Refuge: (Panahgaah #20) in Urdu (page388)

Arabic Words/Synonyms used in Quran:

  1. wazar:  وَزَرَ
  2. mowila: مَوْئِلًا
  3. aknanan: أَكْنَـٰنًا
  4. maljan: مَّلْجَإٍ
  5. mafazatan: مَفَازَ
  6. mahis: مَحِيصًا
  7. multahada:  مُلْتَحَدًا

1. wazar

  1. Mountains/hills or any place of refuge.
  2. According to Imaam Raghib:it can also refer to a place of refuge in a mountain/hill like a cave.

 كَلَّا لَا وَزَرَ ﴿١١

[75:11] www.tanzil.net

No! There is no refuge.

 

 

2. mowila

 

  1. Mowila:[root = wow hamza laam]: a sanctuary or a place to gather for the camels or sheep.
  2. Ibn Faris says," to gather for safety and escape."
  3. So Mowila in other words is a Safe Haven or a Santuary or a Hideout made to avoid getting caught.

 

وَرَبُّكَ ٱلْغَفُورُ ذُو ٱلرَّحْمَةِ ۖ لَوْ يُؤَاخِذُهُم بِمَا كَسَبُوا۟ لَعَجَّلَ لَهُمُ ٱلْعَذَابَ ۚ بَل لَّهُم مَّوْعِدٌ لَّن يَجِدُوا۟ مِن دُونِهِۦ مَوْئِلًا ﴿٥٨﴾

[18:58] www.tanzil.net

But your Lord is Most forgiving, full of Mercy. If He were to call them (at once) to account for what they have earned, then surely He would have hastened their punishment: but they have their appointed time, beyond which they will find no refuge.

3. aknanan

Singular is Akinnatun:

  1. Shed
  2. Canopy
  3. shade
  4. shelter from the heat and rain

 

 

 وَٱللَّـهُ جَعَلَ لَكُم مِّمَّا خَلَقَ ظِلَـٰلًا وَجَعَلَ لَكُم مِّنَ ٱلْجِبَالِ أَكْنَـٰنًا وَجَعَلَ لَكُمْ سَرَٰبِيلَ تَقِيكُمُ ٱلْحَرَّ وَسَرَٰبِيلَ تَقِيكُم بَأْسَكُمْ ۚ كَذَٰلِكَ يُتِمُّ نِعْمَتَهُۥ عَلَيْكُمْ لَعَلَّكُمْ تُسْلِمُونَ ﴿٨١﴾

[16:81] www.tanzil.net

And Allah hath given you, of that which He hath created, shelter from the sun; and hath given you places of refuge in the mountains, and hath given you coats to ward off the heat from you, and coats (of armour) to save you from your own foolhardiness. Thus doth He perfect His favour unto you, in order that ye may surrender (unto Him).

4. maljan: مَّلْجَإٍ

  1. fortress
  2. sanctuary
  3. any place of exceptional security from the enemies
  4. citadel

ٱسْتَجِيبُوا۟ لِرَبِّكُم مِّن قَبْلِ أَن يَأْتِىَ يَوْمٌ لَّا مَرَدَّ لَهُۥ مِنَ ٱللَّـهِ ۚ مَا لَكُم مِّن مَّلْجَإٍ يَوْمَئِذٍ وَمَا لَكُم مِّن نَّكِيرٍ ﴿٤٧﴾

 [42:47]  Hearken ye to your Lord, before there come a Day which there will be no putting back, because of (the Ordainment of) Allah! that Day there will be for you no place of refuge nor will there be for you any room for denial (of your sins)!

 

5. mafazatan

 

  1. a place of peace and security
  2. a safe place where one is brought after being rescued
  3. where one makes it to safety far from the threats and trauma

وَيُنَجِّى ٱللَّـهُ ٱلَّذِينَ ٱتَّقَوْا۟ بِمَفَازَتِهِمْ لَا يَمَسُّهُمُ ٱلسُّوٓءُ وَلَا هُمْ يَحْزَنُونَ ﴿٦١﴾ [39:61] But Allah will deliver the righteous to their place of salvation: no evil shall touch them, nor shall they grieve.

6. mahis

 

 

  1. haies is tight situation or hardship
  2. Mahis is the place where one goes after being in a tight situation or hardship
  3. a place of refuge where one can find some relief from the severity of their difficulty

 أُو۟لَـٰٓئِكَ مَأْوَىٰهُمْ جَهَنَّمُ وَلَا يَجِدُونَ عَنْهَا مَحِيصًا ﴿١٢١﴾

[4:121]  These are they whose abode is hell, and they shall not find any refuge from it.

 

7. multahada

 

  1. lahad means to incline , to lean sideways
  2. multahada means a place where one can momentarily relax, pause
  3. recess, time out, break time, respite
  4. step sideways to catch one's breath

قُلْ إِنِّى لَن يُجِيرَنِى مِنَ ٱللَّـهِ أَحَدٌ وَلَنْ أَجِدَ مِن دُونِهِۦ مُلْتَحَدًا ﴿٢٢﴾

[72:22] Say: Surely no one can protect me against Allah, nor can I find besides Him any place of refuge:
