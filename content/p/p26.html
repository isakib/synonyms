  2. Rawasi:رَوَٰسِىَ
  3. Tawd: ٱلطَّوْدِ

1. Jabal: جَبَلٍ

  1. Plural is 'Jibaal'
  2. All kinds of mountains, big and small
  3. Hill

[2:260]When Abraham said, "Show me, my Lord, how You revive the dead!" God said, "Do you not believe?" Abraham answered, "Yes, indeed I do believe, but just to reassure my heart." Then God said, "Take four birds, and train them to come back to you. Then place them separately on each hilltop, and call them: they will come flying to you. Know that God is almighty and wise."

وَإِذْ قَالَ إِبْرَٰهِـۧمُ رَبِّ أَرِنِى كَيْفَ تُحْىِ ٱلْمَوْتَىٰ ۖ قَالَ أَوَلَمْ تُؤْمِن ۖ قَالَ بَلَىٰ وَلَـٰكِن لِّيَطْمَئِنَّ قَلْبِى ۖ قَالَ فَخُذْ أَرْبَعَةً مِّنَ ٱلطَّيْرِ فَصُرْهُنَّ إِلَيْكَ ثُمَّ ٱجْعَلْ عَلَىٰ كُلِّجَبَلٍ مِّنْهُنَّ جُزْءًا ثُمَّ ٱدْعُهُنَّ يَأْتِينَكَ سَعْيًا ۚ وَٱعْلَمْ أَنَّ ٱللَّـهَ عَزِيزٌ حَكِيمٌ ﴿٢٦٠

 

 

2.  Rawasi: رَوَٰسِىَ

 

  1. Singular:Raasi
  2. Rasoo means 'to be anchored'
  3. 'Rasaa Safinah' means 'the anchored ship'
  4. 'Marsaa' means Port , harbor
  5. 'Rasi' also means a big pot dug in place
  6. Rasiya is a strong , solid mountain
  7. Rawasi is a chain of mountains

[21:31]We set firm mountains upon the earth lest it should sway under them, and We placed therein passages for paths so that they might find their way.

 وَجَعَلْنَا فِى ٱلْأَرْضِ رَوَٰسِىَ أَن تَمِيدَ بِهِمْ وَجَعَلْنَا فِيهَا فِجَاجًا سُبُلًا لَّعَلَّهُمْ يَهْتَدُونَ ﴿٣١

 

3.  Tawd:ٱلطَّوْدِ

  1. A very big mountain
  2. Wide and huge mountain

[26:63] Then We bade Moses strike the sea with his staff. And it parted, and each part was like a huge mountain.

فَأَوْحَيْنَآ إِلَىٰ مُوسَىٰٓ أَنِ ٱضْرِب بِّعَصَاكَ ٱلْبَحْرَ ۖ فَٱنفَلَقَ فَكَانَ كُلُّ فِرْقٍ كَٱلطَّوْدِ ٱلْعَظِيمِ ﴿٦٣ 

 

4.  Sawkhratun: ٱلصَّخْرَ

 

  1. Rocky Hill
  2. Boulder Mountain
  3. a Big Boulder
  4. According to Ibn FAris its a 'Hijr azeem'

 [89:9] and with the Thamud, who cut out [huge] rocks in the valley,

 وَثَمُودَ ٱلَّذِينَ جَابُوا۟ ٱلصَّخْرَ بِٱلْوَادِ ﴿٩

 

5.  Aa'laam: ٱلْأَعْلَـٰمِ

  1. Singular: Alama
  2. 'Alama' means a SignPost, Flag, LightHouse or a Mountain
  3. Aa'laam are those things or people that stand out amongnst the rest.

[55:24] And His are the Ships sailing smoothly through the seas, lofty as mountains:

وَلَهُ ٱلْجَوَارِ ٱلْمُنشَـَٔاتُ فِى ٱلْبَحْرِ كَٱلْأَعْلَـٰمِ ﴿٢٤
