31: POHANCHANA [TO DELIVER]

Synonyms: ABLAGHA or BALLAGHA, AWRADA, JABAA, ADLAA

31.1 ABLAGHA or BALLAGHA ( بَلَّغَ ,اَبْلَغ): Both can be used to describe the delivery of a thing or a message to its final destination. For example

Surah At-Taubah 9:6

وَإِنْ أَحَدٌ مِّنَ الْمُشْرِكِينَ اسْتَجَارَكَ فَأَجِرْهُ حَتَّىٰ يَسْمَعَ كَلَامَ اللَّهِ ثُمَّ أَبْلِغْهُ مَأْمَنَهُ ذَٰلِكَ بِأَنَّهُمْ قَوْمٌ لَّا يَعْلَمُونَ

“And if any one of the polytheists seeks your protection, then grant him protection so that he may hear the words of Allah. Then deliver him to his place of safety. That is because they are a people who do not know.”

In Surah Al-Ma’idah 5:67

يَا أَيُّهَا الرَّسُولُ بَلِّغْ مَا أُنزِلَ إِلَيْكَ مِن رَّبِّكَ

 “O Messenger, announce that which has been revealed to you from your Lord”

 

31.2 AWRADA (اَوْرَدَ): To lead someone to a destination. For example

Surah Al-Huud 11:98

يَقْدُمُ قَوْمَهُ يَوْمَ الْقِيَامَةِ فَأَوْرَدَهُمُ النَّارَ ۖ وَبِئْسَ الْوِرْدُ الْمَوْرُودُ

 “He (Pharoah) will precede his people on the Day of Resurrection and lead them into the Fire; and wretched is the place to which they are led.”

 

31.3 JABAA (جَبَا): The basic meaning refers to two steps a. to gather something and then b. to deliver it to some other place. For example

Surah Al-Qasas 28:57

                             أَوَلَمْ نُمَكِّن لَّهُمْ حَرَمًا آمِنًا يُجْبَىٰ إِلَيْهِ ثَمَرَاتُ كُلِّ شَيْءٍ أَوَلَمْ نُمَكِّن لَّهُمْ حَرَمًا آمِنًا يُجْبَىٰ إِلَيْهِ ثَمَرَاتُ كُلِّ شَيْءٍ  

“Have we not established for them a safe sanctuary to which are brought the fruits of all things as provision from Us?”

 

31.3 ADLAA (اَدْلى): Dalw (دَلْو) refers to an empty water bucket. Adlaa then means to gradually lower an empty bucket into a well. Adlaa ilaa falaan (فلان ادلى ادلى) means to take one’s dispute to another person for resolution and then trying to use family connections or bribery for unfair advantage.  For example:

Surah Al-Baqarah 2:188

وَلَا تَأْكُلُوا أَمْوَالَكُم بَيْنَكُم بِالْبَاطِلِ وَتُدْلُوا بِهَا إِلَى الْحُكَّامِ لِتَأْكُلُوا فَرِيقًا مِّنْ أَمْوَالِ النَّاسِ بِالْإِثْمِ وَأَنتُمْ تَعْلَمُونَ

“And do not consume one another's wealth unjustly or send it [in bribery] to the rulers in order that [they might aid] you [to] consume a portion of the wealth of the people in sin, while you know [it is unlawful].”

 

Summary:

31.1 ABLAGHA or BALLAGHA: To deliver a thing or a message to its final destination. Ballagha is used specifically for a message while Ablagha is more generic.

31.2 AWRADA: To lead someone to a place

31.3 JABAA: To gather something and then deliver it to another place

31.4 ADLAA ILAA FALAN: To take one’s dispute to another person for resolution and use nepotism and bribery to gain unfair advantage
