Paak karna ( page 265) to Cleanse/ purify/clean

  1. mahassaw
  2. zakaa
  3. tawhhara
  4. sawfa
  5. barra

1. Mahassaw: [ محَّص ]  :

  1. to purify something of its impurities
  2. mahassawl dahab [محَّصَ الذَّهَب ] means to melt gold metal to make it pure
  3. [3:141] Sahih International
  4. And that Allah may purify the believers [through trials] and destroy the disbelievers.
  5. وَلِيُمَحِّصَ اللَّـهُ الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا وَيَمْحَقَ الْكَافِرِينَ ﴿١٤١
  6. Here Allah swt wants to purify the believers of their weakness in actions  and shortcomings.

2. Zakkaa: [  زَكَّا ]

  1. purification of the soul, spiritual cleansing

91:9[91:9] Sahih International

He has succeeded who purifies it,

قَدْ أَفْلَحَ مَن زَكَّاهَا ﴿٩﴾ 

3. Tawhhara [طَهَّرَ  ]:  

  1. to clean the outwardly impurities [74:4] وَثِيَابَكَ فَطَهِّرْ
  2. also to have a clean/pure heart, pure from shirk or temptations [33:33]

4. Sawffa [صَفًّى  ]:

  1. to be free from its impurities
  2. 47:15  ' عَسَلٍ مُّصَفًّ ' 'purified honey..'

5. Barraa  [ بَرَّاَ   ]:

  1. Bariaa [بَرِءَ   ] means to be cured from the sickness
  2. Abraaa [ اَبْرَاَ     ] means to get healthy
  3. Barraaa [   بَرَّاءَ   ]  means to be free from the blame or sin, to absolve
  4. [33:69] Sahih International
  5. O you who have believed, be not like those who abused Moses; then Allah cleared him of what they said. And he, in the sight of Allah, was distinguished.

يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا لَا تَكُونُوا كَالَّذِينَ آذَوْا مُوسَىٰ فَبَرَّأَهُ اللَّـهُ مِمَّا قَالُوا ۚ وَكَانَ عِندَ اللَّـهِ وَجِيهًا ﴿٦٩﴾

Summary (Paak karna ):

  1. Mahassaw  محَّص  : to frequently purify something from internal and external impurities
  2. Zakkaa  زَكَّا    : Constant act of Spiritual cleansing or Purification of the soul
  3. Tawhhara  طَهَّرَ  : to keep cleansing and purging (1) from external impurities/pollutants and (2)also from Shirk and Temptations
  4. Sawffa صَفًّى  : to keep in its pure and  unadulterated form
  5. Barraa:  بَرَّاَ   : to be free from all blame and defects
