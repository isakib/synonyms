To ask for or to give Refuge(Panah dena or lena # 21) page 289

Synonyms:

  1. Ajaar/istejaar: اجارَ
  2. a'azaa:  أَعُوذُ

 

1. Ajaara/istejaar

 

  1. jaar is a neighbor or a  personal assistant
  2. istejaar is to ask for asylum and protection from the enemy
  3. ajaara is to give someone refuge or asylum from an enemy

وَإِنْ أَحَدٌ مِّنَ ٱلْمُشْرِكِينَ ٱسْتَجَارَكَ فَأَجِرْهُ حَتَّىٰ يَسْمَعَ كَلَـٰمَ ٱللَّـهِ ثُمَّ أَبْلِغْهُ مَأْمَنَهُۥ ۚ ذَٰلِكَ بِأَنَّهُمْ قَوْمٌ لَّا يَعْلَمُونَ ﴿٦﴾

[9:6]  And if one of the idolaters seek protection from you, grant him protection till he hears the word of Allah, then make him attain his place of safety; this is because they are a people who do not know.

2. a'azoo/isteaaz

  1. auz is one who gives refuge
  2. ma'aaz is a place of refuge
  3. taawuz is the 'chantings' or 'mantras' written on a piece of paper/fabric  and hung around the neck or the house to ward off evil
  4. isteaaz is to seek refuge from invisible lurkings and the evils of the devil, jinn or magic and magicians
  5. a'azaa/a'auzoo (a) is to come under someone's refuge and remain close (b) hold on tight and not let go of the One who is providing the protection from the danger (c) enter a safe-haven already and be given respite immediately (d) cling to the anchor

وَإِذْ قَالَ مُوسَىٰ لِقَوْمِهِۦٓ إِنَّ ٱللَّـهَ يَأْمُرُكُمْ أَن تَذْبَحُوا۟ بَقَرَةً ۖ قَالُوٓا۟ أَتَتَّخِذُنَا هُزُوًا ۖ قَالَ أَعُوذُ بِٱللَّـهِ أَنْ أَكُونَ مِنَ ٱلْجَـٰهِلِينَ ﴿٦٧﴾

[2:67]  And when Musa said to his people: Surely Allah commands you that you should sacrifice a cow; they said: Do you ridicule us? He said: I seek the protection of Allah from being one of the ignorant.

 

 وَإِمَّا يَنزَغَنَّكَ مِنَ ٱلشَّيْطَـٰنِ نَزْغٌ فَٱسْتَعِذْ بِٱللَّـهِ ۚ إِنَّهُۥ سَمِيعٌ عَلِيمٌ ﴿٢٠٠﴾

 [7:200] And if a false imputation from the Shaitan afflict you, seek refuge in Allah; surely He is Hearing, Knowing.

 

Summary:

  1. Ajaar/istejaar:[ اجارَ ] refuge from an enemy
  2. a'azaa:[  أَعُوذُ ]  refuge from all evil spirits
