Fath,”Fath hona,dena”, P.659

{

Synonyms: FATAHA,AZFARA

1 FATAHA(فَتَحَ):meaning to resolve complication or remove hurdle.this word is used for opening,to take decision,to give victory all of these meanings.when this word is used for victory against enemy in the fight then it means such an clear and open decision that after which the enemy has no doubt in his defeat

Quran example: Surat An-Naşr (The Divine Support)[110:1]

إِذَا جَاءَ نَصْرُ اللَّهِ وَالْفَتْحُ

When the victory of Allah has come and the conquest,

2 AZFARA(اَظْفَرَ):ZAFARA(ظفر)means human or animal nails and ZAFARA FULLANUN means someone has dug one's nail in someone else or to get victory.AZFARA meaning to give such a victory that nail got dug in enemy's chest. meaning victory and then domination on the enemy

Quran example:Surat Al-Fatĥ (The Victory)[48:24]

وَهُوَ الَّذِي كَفَّ أَيْدِيَهُمْ عَنكُمْ وَأَيْدِيَكُمْ عَنْهُم بِبَطْنِ مَكَّةَ مِن بَعْدِ أَنْ أَظْفَرَكُمْ عَلَيْهِمْ ۚ

And it is He who withheld their hands from you and your hands from them within [the area of] Makkah after He caused you to overcomethem.

Summary

1 FATAHA:it's only used for VICTORY

2 AZFARA:it includes FATAHA as well as domination/control
