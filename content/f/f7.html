7- FIRQA------GAROOH [Sect - Group]

Quran has referred them by the words FIRQAH (فرقة), FAREEQ (فريق) TA’IFAH (طائفة) ZAMURA (زمر) SHEE’AH (شيعه) and UNAAS (اناس).

 

 

7.1   FIRQAH (فرقة) : FARAQA (فرق ) means to separate, tearing and FIRQ (فرق ) means separated part and FIRQAH (فرقة) means separated (smaller group of) people apart from a (bigger) community. The group of like-minded people in big numbers separated from some nation or community. Allah says:

 

Page-664

 

Surat At-Tawbah (The Repentance) - سورة التوبة 9:122

 فَلَوْلَا نَفَرَ مِنْ كُلِّ فِرْقَــةٍ مِّنْھُمْ طَاۗىِٕفَةٌ لِّيَتَفَقَّهُوْا فِي الدِّيْنِ

For there should separate from every division of them a group [remaining] to obtain understanding in the religion.

 

7.2  FAREEQ (فريق): FIRQAH (فرقة) and FAREEQ (فريق) have approximately similar meanings. FAREEQ (فريق) is used when some other party is also present or goes against it. Like Allah Said:

Surat Ash-Shūraá (The Consultation) - سورة الشورى 42:7

 فَرِيْقٌ فِي الْجَنَّةِ وَفَرِيْقٌ فِي السَّعِيْرِ

  A party will be in Paradise and a party in the Blaze.

 

At another occasion, it is said:

Surat Maryam (Mary) - سورة مريم 19:73

اَيُّ الْفَرِيْقَيْنِ خَيْرٌ مَّقَامًا وَّاَحْسَنُ نَدِيًّا        

 "Which of [our] two parties is best in position and best in association?"

 

 

7.3   TA’IFAH (طائفة): The people of one point-of-view and religion. This word can be applied to the smallest (group) and even to a single person as well. just like some commentators says about this verse: Allah says:

Surat Al-Ĥujurāt (The Rooms) - سورة الحجرات 49:9

   وَاِنْ طَاۗىِٕفَتٰنِ مِنَ الْمُؤْمِنِيْنَ اقْتَتَلُوْا فَاَصْلِحُوْا بَيْنَهُمَا

And if two factions among the believers should fight, then make settlement between the two. That the word TAIFA (طائفة) is applied to a single person in this verse.

 

 

 

7.4   ZAMURA (زمر): The plural of ZUMARAH (زمرہ ) means Group. When a big group moves after being divided into small units or groups then these groups are called as ZUMAR (زمر) (See the explanation in JAMAAT (جماعت )) Allah says:

Surat Az-Zumar (The Troops) - سورة الزمر 39:73

   وَسِيْقَ الَّذِيْنَ اتَّقَوْا رَبَّهُمْ اِلَى الْجَنَّةِ زُمَرًا

But those who feared their Lord will be driven to Paradise in groups.

 

 

7.5   SHEE’AH (شيعه): means party, faction, political group. The people from whom man gets power and they are scattered around him. Someone's helper or follower. The base of such party is normally due to difference of beliefs. Allah says:

Surat Al-Qaşaş (The Stories) - سورة القصص 28:15

   فَوَجَدَ فِيْهَا رَجُلَيْنِ يَـقْتَتِلٰنِ ڭ هٰذَا مِنْ شِيْعَتِهٖ وَهٰذَا مِنْ عَدُوِّه

And found therein two men fighting: one from his faction and one from among his enemy.

 

And the plural of SHEE’AH (شيعه) is SHAI’YA (شعیا) as well and also ASHYAA’ (اشیاع). ASHIYAA’ (اشیاع) meaning the people who possess similar habits and manners whether they had passed before or present. Same gender. Allah Said:

 

Surat Saba' (Sheba) - سورة سبإ 34:54

   وَحِيْلَ بَيْنَهُمْ وَبَيْنَ مَا يَشْتَهُوْنَ كَمَا فُعِلَ بِاَشْيَاعِهِمْ مِّنْ قَبْلُ

And prevention will be placed between them and what they desire, as was done with their kind before.

7.6   UNAAS (اناس): Groups that are present or made according to the division, or separate groups related to different tribes. (According to some, there is no difference between UNAAS (اناس) and NAAS (ناس ). It is in the Qur’an:

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة 2:60

قَدْ عَلِمَ كُلُّ اُنَاسٍ مَّشْرَبَھُمْ

And every people knew its watering place.

 

Summary:

7.1  FIRQAH: The separated part of many people from a big group.

7.2  FAREEQ: That group to whom there is an opposite group present or listed.

7.3  TA’IFAH: The small community of people with same views and religion.

7.4  ZUMAR: Small groups and units.

7.5  SHEE’AH: The party or faction with opposite beliefs.

7.6  UNAAS: Sects made because of tribes or distribution.
