10- FASAAD KARNA - DALNA [Causing corruption - Making Violence]

The Qur’an has referred to it by the words AFSADA (أفسد) ‘ATHA or ‘AATH (عثایاعاث) and NAZA’ (نزع).

 

 

10.1    AFSADA (أفسد): FASADA (فسد ) means the deterioration of something, decaying and AFSADA (أفسد) means to deteriorate something and decay it. FASAAD (فساد ) is the antonym of ISLAH (اصلاح ). The word FASAAD (فساد ) is used in both physical and spiritual sense. Allah Said:

            Surat Al-'A`rāf (The Heights) - سورة الأعراف 7:56

           وَلَا تُفْسِدُوْا فِي الْاَرْضِ بَعْدَ اِصْلَاحِهَا

  And cause not corruption upon the earth after its reformation.

10.2  ASA or AAS (عثایاعاث): It is in Quran:

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة 2:60        

   وَلَا تَعْثَوْا فِى الْاَرْضِ مُفْسِدِيْنَ  

And do not commit abuse on the earth, spreading corruption.

 

TA’THAWA (تعثوا ) substance is written as (ع ث ی ) by some glossaries, and means ATHA, YA’THA, ‘ATHIYA (عثی یعثی عثیا ) and it denotes mental and intellectual deterioration. For example, the promotion of wrong beliefs and ideologies by using literature or speeches. And some have written its substance as ع ی ث. ‘AATHA, YA’EETHA, ‘ATHYA (عاث یعیث عثیا) that advocates deterioration of sensory or material things, such as abusing or doing injustice to someone. The note section in both cases is always LA TA’THAWA (لاتعثوا ).

10.3     NAZA’ (نزع): means to cast violence between two things, to create a dispute, to persuade in favour of violence, to create disorder, to make a quarrel, to create rivalry and means to deteriorate work and interfere in, or create violence. It is mentioned in the Qur’an:

Surat Yūsuf (Joseph) - سورة يوسف  12:100

   مِّنَ الْبَدْوِ مِنْۢ بَعْدِ اَنْ نَّزَغَ الشَّيْطٰنُ بَيْنِيْ وَبَيْنَ اِخْوَتِيْ

After Satan had induced [estrangement] between me and my brothers.

Summary:

10.1ANSADA can be applied for creating estrangement, and once violence takes the form of habit then ‘AATHA or ATHAA will be used. And the reality of NAZAGHA is to interfere whether it is in one or more thing. Then cast estrangement among them.
