Surat An-Nūr (The Light) - سورة النور 24:22

 

 اَنْ يُّؤْتُوْٓا اُولِي الْقُرْبٰى وَالْمَسٰكِيْنَ

(And) wealth swear not to give [help] to their relatives and the poor.  

Summary:

 

3.1 MAYASRA: To have an easy living.

3.2 TOWL: Have sufficient (amount) for living.

3.3 BASTAT:  Wideness and openness in anything.

3.4 ALWAASAAT: The relieved people. People who can do in the manner.
