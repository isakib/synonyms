13- FAZEELAT DENA - (BUZURGI DENA) [To Give Preference ()]

The Qur’an has referred to it by the words FADALA (فضل) and KARAMA (کرم).

 

13.1 FADALA (فضل): FADAL (فضل ) means according/financing some good works beyond moderate means. And FADALA (فضل) means for the sake of giving favour, endowing someone with goodness more than he is privileged to. Allah Said:

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة 2:122

  يٰبَنِىْٓ اِسْرَاۗءِيْلَ اذْكُرُوْا نِعْمَتِىَ الَّتِىْٓ اَنْعَمْتُ عَلَيْكُمْ وَاَنِّىْ فَضَّلْتُكُمْ عَلَي الْعٰلَمِيْنَ

O Children of Israel, remember My favor which I have bestowed upon you and that I preferred you over the worlds.

13.2 KARAMA (کرم): KARAM (کرم) is a type of honour that is present in oneself due to his (good) conduct. And KARAMA (کرم) means to install the attributes of honour in someone. Allah Said:

(Surat-BaniIsrael) (Children of Israel) سورة بنی اسراءیل 17:70

   وَلَقَدْ كَرَّمْنَا بَنِيْٓ اٰدَمَ وَحَمَلْنٰهُمْ فِي الْبَرِّ وَالْبَحْرِ

And We have certainly honoured the children of Adam and carried them on the land and sea.
