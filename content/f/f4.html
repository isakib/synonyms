4- FARAAKH KARNA- HONA [To Open, be Opened]

Quran has referred to them with the words like RAHABA (رحب) , WASI’ (وسع), TAFSUHU (تفسح).

 

4.1 RAHIBA (رحب) and RAHABA  (رحب):  mean wideness of space. It is only used for wideness of the space. The wideness of space or (of a) house, and its antonym is DHAAQA (ضاق). Allah Said:

 

Surat At-Tawbah (The Repentance) - سورة التوبة 9:25

وَّضَاقَتْ عَلَيْكُمُ الْاَرْضُ بِمَا رَحُبَتْ

And the earth was confining for you with its vastness.

And MARHAB (مرحب) word is used for pray. MARHABA (مرحبا) means welcome. And LA MARHABA (لامرحبا) is used as a curse (i.e. there is no capacity or space for you).

 

It is in Qur’an:

 

Surat Şād (The Letter "Saad") - سورة ص 38:59

لَا مَرْحَبًۢا بِهِمْ ۭ اِنَّهُمْ صَالُوا النَّارِ

No welcome for them. Indeed, they will burn in the Fire.

 

4.2 WASI’ (وسع) is used for both space and condition. It means wideness, holding, capacity Allah Said:

 

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة 2:255

وَسِعَ كُرْسِـيُّهُ السَّمٰوٰتِ وَالْاَرْضَ

His Kursi extends over the heavens and the earth.

 

And Allah Said:

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة 2:286

لَا يُكَلِّفُ اللّٰهُ نَفْسًا اِلَّا وُسْعَهَا

 

Allah does not burden a soul except [what is within] its capacity.

 

And AWSA’ (اوسع) means to have much wealth. And AWSA’ AL-MAWDI’ (اوسع الموضع) means to make a space wide.

 

Allah Said:

 

Surat Adh-Dhāriyāt (The Winnowing Winds) - سورة الذاريات 51:47

وَالسَّمَاۗءَ بَنَيْنٰهَا بِاَيْىدٍ وَّاِنَّا لَمُوْسِعُوْنَ

 

And the heaven We constructed with strength, and indeed, We are [its] expander.

 

4.3 TAFSUHU (تفسح) in relation to FASAHA  (فسح):  means to take a wide step and FASAHA  (فسح) and TAFASAHA FAL-MAJLEES ( تفسح فلمجليس ) means to give space in gatherings, meaning to sit openly in a manner so that space is created for others. Allah Said:

Surat Al-Mujādila (The Pleading Woman) - سورة المجادلة 58:11

اِذَا قِيْلَ لَكُمْ تَفَسَّحُوْا فِي الْمَجٰلِسِ فَافْسَحُوْا يَفْسَحِ اللّٰهُ لَكُمْ  

When you are told, "Space yourselves" in gatherings, then make space; Allah will make space for you.

 

 

Summary:

4.1  RAHIBA: only for the openness of space.

4.2  WASI’: holding or capacity.

AWSA’: To make widening space.

4.3 TAFSUHU: Open to make space for others.
