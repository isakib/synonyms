1 FAIDA, FAIDA DENA

[Benefit, to benefit someone]

Synonyms: NAFA’(نفع), MATTA’(مَتَـٰعٌ), RABIHAH(ربح), MA’ARIB(مآرب)

1.1 nafa’(نفع): meaning a good thing or to get its part, to benefit. Its antonym is zarar(ضرر) which means difficulty, disadvantage. Its use is common. As Allah states that:

________________

Surat Al-Rad (The thunder) - سُوۡرَةُ الرّعد

13:16

قُلۡ أَفَٱتَّخَذۡتُم مِّن دُونِهِۦۤ أَوۡلِيَآءَ لَا يَمۡلِكُونَ لِأَنفُسِهِمۡ نَفۡعً۬ا وَلَا ضَرًّ۬ا‌ۚ

Say (O Muhammad): Who is Lord of the heavens and the earth? Say: Allah. Say: Take ye then (others) beside Him for protectors, which, even for themselves, have neither benefit nor hurt? Say: Is the blind man equal to the seer, or is darkness equal to light? Or assign they unto Allah partners who created the like of His creation so that the creation (which they made and His creation) seemed alike to them? Say: Allah is the Creator of all things, and He is the One, the Almighty.

________________

1.2 matta’ (مَتَـٰعٌ): mataa’(متاع) meaning all those things from the items of use that a person or any living being benefits from to keep itself alive. And matta’(متع) means to benefit from any such items. Allah states that:

________________

79:31-33

أَخۡرَجَ مِنۡہَا مَآءَهَا وَمَرۡعَٮٰهَا وَٱلۡجِبَالَ أَرۡسَٮٰهَا مَتَـٰعً۬ا لَّكُمۡ وَلِأَنۡعَـٰمِكُمۡ 

And produced therefrom the water thereof and the pasture thereof, (31) And He made fast the hills, (32) A provision for you and for your cattle.

________________

And matta’(متع) means to give such things to someone so that he may benefit from them, to benefit someone. Allah states that:

وَمَتِّعُوهُنَّ عَلَى ٱلۡمُوسِعِ قَدَرُهُ ۥ وَعَلَى ٱلۡمُقۡتِرِ قَدَرُهُ ۥ

It is no sin for you if ye divorce women while yet ye have not touched them, nor appointed unto them a portion. Provide for them, the rich according to his means, and the straitened according to his means, a fair provision. (This is) a bounden duty for those who do good.

And tamatta’(تمتع) means to benefit one’s own self, to enjoy. As is stated:

تَمَتَّعُواْ فِى دَارِڪُمۡ ثَلَـٰثَةَ أَيَّامٍ۬‌ۖ

But they hamstrung her, and then he said: enjoy life in your dwelling-place three days! This is a threat that will not be belied.

1.3 rabihah (ربح): that gain which is achieved from buying and selling, to profit in trade merchandise. And its antonym is khasir(خسر).  Allah states that:

________________

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سُوۡرَةُ البَقَرَة

2:16

فَمَا رَبِحَت تِّجَـٰرَتُهُمۡ وَمَا كَانُواْ مُهۡتَدِينَ

These are they who purchase error at the price of guidance, so their commerce doth not prosper, neither are they guided.

________________

1.4 ma’arib(مآرب): arab(ارب) meaning intense requirement without which one cannot manage and you strive to achieve it; meaning requirement, need, extreme, and ma’arib(مآرب), maariba(مآربة), maaraba(مآربه), maaruba(مآربة) meaning requirement and its fulfillment. And its plural is maarib(مارب). And that thing also through which this requirement is fulfilled. Allah states that:    

________________

Surat Ta-Ha  - سُوۡرَةُ طٰه

20:18

قَالَ هِىَ عَصَاىَ أَتَوَڪَّؤُاْ عَلَيۡہَا وَأَهُشُّ بِہَا عَلَىٰ غَنَمِى وَلِىَ فِيہَا مَـَٔارِبُ أُخۡرَىٰ

He said: This is my staff whereon I lean, and wherewith I beat down branches for my sheep, and wherein I find other uses.

________________

Summary:

1.NAFA’(نفع): common word, to receive a part from something beneficial, its antonym is zarar.

2. MATTA’A(مَتَـٰعٌ):  to benefit from items of use.

3. RABIHAH(ربح): to benefit from trade merchandise.

4. MAARIB(مآرب): needs and ways of its fulfillment.
