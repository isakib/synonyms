To reveal  ظاہر کرنا – ۲

Synonyms:

اظھر from ظھر, ابدا from بدا, اعثر from عثر, جھر and اعلن

The first four synonyms have already been defined above (in previous entry). Examples are as follows:

1.اظھر :

فَلَا يُظْهِرُ عَلَىٰ غَيْبِهِ أَحَدًا

He does not disclose His [knowledge of the] unseen to anyone. [Al-Jinn 72:26]

2. ابدا :

وَأَعْلَمُ مَا تُبْدُونَ وَمَا كُنتُمْ تَكْتُمُونَ

And I know what you reveal and what you have concealed. [Al-Baqarah 2:33]

3. اعثر :

وَكَذَٰلِكَ أَعْثَرْنَا عَلَيْهِمْ لِيَعْلَمُوا أَنَّ وَعْدَ اللَّهِ حَقٌّ

We caused them to be found that they [who found them] would know that the promise of Allah is truth.. [Al-Kahf 18:21]

4. جھر :

وَأَسِرُّوا قَوْلَكُمْ أَوِ اجْهَرُوا بِهِ ۖ إِنَّهُ عَلِيمٌ بِذَاتِ الصُّدُورِ

And conceal your speech or publicize it; indeed, He is Knowing of that within the breasts. [Al-Mulk 67:13]

5. اعلن : (opposite of اسر) To express something. Rarely used for body language. Verbal expression is not compulsory either.

Example: وَاللَّهُ يَعْلَمُ مَا تُسِرُّونَ وَمَا تُعْلِنُونَ

And Allah knows what you conceal and what you declare. [An-Nahl 16:19]

علانیۃ ,ظاہر، شُرَّعًا are used for “zaahir” (to reveal). All have been explained above.

ظلم کرنا - to do injustice (see بے انصافی کرنا)
