Summary of the Appendices in the book. These span from page 905 - 1008

 

The subappendices mentioned are not a complete list but a sample of what  is covered. A few of these appendices I believe will be of special interest to people studying the balaghah "eloquence" of the arabic language. Translating these can be a secondary task - or a separate project altogether.

 

Appendices

 

1- Appendix 1

 

All proper nouns appearing in the Quran

 Sub Appendices

 

a- Prophetsd and Messageners

b- Angels

c- Places

d- Mountains

 

2- Appendix 2

 

All common nouns appearing in the Quran without synonyms

 

Sub Appendices

 

a- Animals

b- Body Parts

etc.

 

3- Appendix 3

 

Antonyms

 

Subappendices

 

a- Words which carry a meaning and its antonyms meaning according to the context

b- Words in which the change of a single letter causes the meaning to be changed to its antonym

c- Common words and their antonyms (the one we translated)

 

4- Appendix 4

 

Verbs that have a different meaning by changing the "Ain Kalima" (i.e. central letter of the root) or a letter in the "masdar"

 

5- Appendix 5

 

Miscellaneous

 

Subappendices

a- Some Proverbs

b- Words which have a different meaning by changing the "harkat/tashkeel/fatha damma, kasra" of the "fa Kalima" of a word
