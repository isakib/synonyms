3 YAQEEN KARNA [To Believe-To be sure of]

Synonyms: AYQANA اَيْقَنَ ISTAYQANA اِسْتَيْقَنَ ZANNA ظَنَّ

3.1 AYQANA:YA-QA-NA يقن meaning to leave no doubt or uncertainity. and as per Imam Raghib YA-QA-NA means to comprehend some affair/matter completely as well as reaching conclusive evidence/proof.and it's leve is above 'acquaintance' and 'tact'.and as per Abu Hilal 3skari,YAQIYN's يقين level is more than Knowledge.he writes ALYAQIYN SAKUWN ANNAFS WA NAHJ ASSADR BIMA 3ILM(اليقين سكون النفس و نهج الصدر بما علم) meaning 'YAQIYN is the rest/peace of heart and coolness of chest on known thing.ALLAH said

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة

2:4

وَالَّذِينَ يُؤْمِنُونَ بِمَا أُنزِلَ إِلَيْكَ وَمَا أُنزِلَ مِن قَبْلِكَ وَبِالْآخِرَةِ هُمْ يُوقِنُونَ

And who believe in what has been revealed to you, [O Muhammad], and what was revealed before you, and of the Hereafter they are certain [in faith].

and ISTAYQANA means 'to be certain of something gradually slowly after experiencing it'.ALLAH said

Surat An-Naml (The Ant) - سورة النمل

27:14

وَجَحَدُوا بِهَا وَاسْتَيْقَنَتْهَا أَنفُسُهُمْ ظُلْمًا وَعُلُوًّا ۚ فَانظُرْ كَيْفَ كَانَ عَاقِبَةُ الْمُفْسِدِينَ

And they rejected them, while their [inner] selves were convinced thereof, out of injustice and haughtiness. So see how was the end of the corrupters

3.2 ZANNA:it comes in the meaning of approximating with evidence and observation and to assume.now this assumption would be as weak as the signs and observations are.and similarly as strong as the signs and observations are. in this case of strong ZANN gives off the meaning of YAQIYN and its sign is that after ZANNA comes of normally word of ANNA اَنَّ or AN اَنْ.(for details please see GUMAN KARNA [To Assume]).ALLAH said

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة

2:46

الَّذِينَ يَظُنُّونَ أَنَّهُم مُّلَاقُو رَبِّهِمْ وَأَنَّهُمْ إِلَيْهِ رَاجِعُونَ

Who are certain that they will meet their Lord and that they will return to Him.

Summary:

3.1 AYQANA:to fully comprehend some affair and to reach its conclusive proof.

3.2 ZANNA ANNA ظَنَّ اَنَّ:to achieve certainity by assuming from signs and evidence
