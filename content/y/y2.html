2- YAAD KARNA-AANA-RAKHNA [To Remember,Remind,To Remember(retain) ]

Synonyms: ZAKARA ذَكَرَ IDDAKARA اِدَّكَرَ HAFIZA حَفِظَ WA3A وَعَي

2.1 ZAKARA:meaning some matter manifesting on tongue or heart whether any thing remain remembered or get recalled after being forgotten.it's usage is common.ALLAH said

Surat Maryam (Mary) - سورة مريم

19:67

أَوَلَا يَذْكُرُ الْإِنسَانُ أَنَّا خَلَقْنَاهُ مِن قَبْلُ وَلَمْ يَكُ شَيْئًا

Does man not remember that We created him before, while he was nothing?

and IDDAKARA meaning getting rememberance of some forgotten thing.In the QURAN

Surat Yūsuf (Joseph) - سورة يوسف

12:45

وَقَالَ الَّذِي نَجَا مِنْهُمَا وَادَّكَرَ بَعْدَ أُمَّةٍ أَنَا أُنَبِّئُكُم بِتَأْوِيلِهِ فَأَرْسِلُونِ

But the one who was freed and remembered after a time said, "I will inform you of its interpretation, so send me forth."

2.2 HAFIZA:to remember something and then to be mindful of it.to prevent something from being wasted ad destroyed.to protect from being flawed. ALLAH said

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة

2:238

حَافِظُوا عَلَى الصَّلَوَاتِ وَالصَّلَاةِ الْوُسْطَىٰ وَقُومُوا لِلَّهِ قَانِتِينَ

Maintain with care the [obligatory] prayers and [in particular] the middle prayer and stand before Allah , devoutly obedient.

2.3 WA3A:meaning to tie up the mouth of some container after putting something inside it. to protect it and to not let anything comes out of it.and WI3A- meaning tied up goods.ALLAH said

Surat Al-Ĥāqqah (The Reality) - سورة الحاقة

69:12

لِنَجْعَلَهَا لَكُمْ تَذْكِرَةً وَتَعِيَهَا أُذُنٌ وَاعِيَةٌ

That We might make it for you a reminder and [that] a conscious ear would be conscious of it.

SUMMARY:

2.1 ZAKARA:to remember-to retain-remind-to remember by tongue(speech) or heart.use in all places.and is of common usage

2.2 HAFIZA:to remember something by tongue-and preserve it in the heart and then to be mindful of it.and not to let it be forgotten

2.3 WA3A:meaning to remember and not to let it vary.
