4 YAKSUW HUWNA [To Concentrate,To be Wholly Devoted]

Synonyms: HANAFA حَنَفَ, TABATTALA تَبَتَّلَ

4.1 HANIYF حَنِيْف:HANAFA (antonym JANAFA جَنَفَ) to incline toward perseverance by leaving all false paths. and HANIYF means the one who comes towards only straight path, leaving all wrong paths.ALLAH said

Surat Yūnus (Jonah) - سورة يونس

10:105

وَأَنْ أَقِمْ وَجْهَكَ لِلدِّينِ حَنِيفًا وَلَا تَكُونَنَّ مِنَ الْمُشْرِكِينَ

And [commanded], 'Direct your face toward the religion, inclining to truth, and never be of those who associate others with Allah ;

4.2 TABTIYL تَبْتِيْل:BATALA(بَتَلَ) meaning to cut of something to detach it from some other. and BATTALA بَتَّلَ and TABATTALA meaning to free to turn to GOD by turning/diverting away one's attention from every type of business and fuss.to be involved in GOD's worship with sincere intention.ALLAH said

Surat Al-Muzzammil (The Enshrouded One) - سورة المزمل

73:8

وَاذْكُرِ اسْمَ رَبِّكَ وَتَبَتَّلْ إِلَيْهِ تَبْتِيلًا

And remember the name of your Lord and devote yourself to Him with [complete] devotion.

Summary:

HANAFA to come towards straight path by leaving false paths.and TABATTALA meaning to concentrate by letting go of worldy matters and fuss.
