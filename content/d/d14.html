دل میں بات ڈالنا – Inspiration in the heart

These wordsوحى  الهام  القاء وسواس  همزات          are in Quran

1.         وحى – the lexical meaning is to conceal, and quick indication, and وحى ا means to indicate swiftly about some concealed hidden matter (MF, M L) , then there are  some different types of وحى , one is connected to matters which are directly controlled by Allah:

 

In Surah Fussilat: (41:12):

 

وَأَوْحَىٰ فِي كُلِّ سَمَاءٍ أَمْرَهَا

 

and inspired in each heaven its command.

 

Secondly the natural instinct is also called وحى, such as the child hastens towards the mother as soon as it is born,

 

In Surah An-Nahl: (16:68):

 

وَأَوْحَىٰ رَبُّكَ إِلَى النَّحْلِ

 

And your Lord inspired to the bee,

 

Therefore, the inspiration from Allah is to all inanimate objects, animate beings and mankind and this kind of inspiration can be to any, as well as a prophet.

 

In Surah  Qasas: (28:7):

 

وَأَوْحَيْنَا إِلَىٰ أُمِّ مُوسَىٰ أَنْ أَرْضِعِيهِ

 

And We inspired to the mother of Moses, "Suckle him;

 

In all the above examples the word  وحى has been used in the lexical meaning.  Therefore it can be said that one man can inspire another as:

 

In Surah Maryam: (19:11):

 

فَأَوْحَىٰ إِلَيْهِمْ أَن سَبِّحُوا بُكْرَةً وَعَشِيًّا

 

and signaled to them to exalt [ Allah ] in the morning and afternoon.

 

The same could be applied to devils inspiring one another:

 

In Surah An-Aam:   (6:121):

 

وَإِنَّ الشَّيَاطِينَ لَيُوحُونَ إِلَىٰ أَوْلِيَائِهِمْ لِيُجَادِلُوكُمْ

 

And indeed do the devils inspire their allies [among men] to dispute with you.

 

Therefore inspiration can be divine or satanic. Divine inspiration is always based on good, and the inspiration which is against the divine inspiration is satanic. In religious technical terms وحى is the teachings, rulings and unseen news which Allah sends to his special servants (prophets) for the guidance of common people and its three types have been mentioned in the Quran:

 

a)         Puts it in the heart directly

b)        Sends his angel to his messenger

c)         Talks behind a screen ( like with prophet Musa)

 

Although the common way is through Angel Jibraeel Allah sends messages to his prophets which then flows from the Prophet’s mouth.

 

2.           الهام – issue/matter which is put in to someone’s heart, by the Almighty or through the great angels (MF) and means to give understanding, comprehension, to give divine help (MJD) same as  وحى, الهام can also be satanic, especially when it cannot be confirmed by any ayah or religious statement, that’s why.  With the exception of Sufi scholars; according to all the scholars الهام  is not sufficient evidence or  proof (M.M). The differences between وحى and  الهام, a) الهام is only used for when referring to intelligent beings, ie mankind, whereas   وحىis can be used for a wider application.  B)  the connection of   الهام is usually referred to either completing an action or refraining from it, whereas وحى has a much wider application.

 

In Surah As-Shams:  (91:8):

 

فَأَلْهَمَهَا فُجُورَهَا وَتَقْوَاهَا

 

And inspired it [with discernment of] its wickedness and its righteousness,

 

3.          القاء- lexical meaning to put/pour, and القى على  means to give education (M. M.).

 

In Surah Qamar: (54:25):

 

أَأُلْقِيَ الذِّكْرُ عَلَيْهِ مِن بَيْنِنَا

 

Has the message been sent down upon him from among us?

 

And تلقى الشئ منه means to learn, to gain knowledge (MJD)

 

In Surah Baqarah: (2:37):

 

فَتَلَقَّىٰ آدَمُ مِن رَّبِّهِ كَلِمَاتٍ

 

Then Adam received from his Lord [some] words

 

Therefore القاء  means only the thing which is put in the heart which is connected with education, learning and teaching. Both الهام and وحى it can be satanic.

 

In Surah Haj: (22:52):

 

فَيَنسَخُ اللَّهُ مَا يُلْقِي الشَّيْطَانُ

 

But Allah abolishes that which Satan throws in

 

4.      وسواس – according to the view point of medical science it is an illness which occurs due to a chemical imbalance which afflicts the brain, resulting in the man talking incoherently which does not reflect what is in his heart and nor is there any good in it (M. M.) and وسواس means illness of doubt , the evil that comes from the mouth, or useless speech, devil (MJD), and also means the inclining of the devil to an evil thing, and to put bad thoughts (MF), and the beginning state of insanity (F L, 135) to sum it up  وسوسه is all evil thoughts that come in the heart or is inspired by the devil and it is only connected with the devil (shaitaan).

 

 

In Surah Naas:   (114: 4-5):

 

مِن شَرِّ الْوَسْوَاسِ الْخَنَّاسِ

 

From the evil of the retreating whisperer

 

الَّذِي يُوَسْوِسُ فِي صُدُورِ النَّاسِ

 

Who whispers [evil] into the breasts of mankind

 

5.          همزات         همز يدل على ضغظ و عصر  (M. L.) to pinch, to prick, to press, means to use a pointed stick to poke the side or hip joint of an animal so he walks faster, shaitaan’s whispering evil in man and then to instigate it and this word can only be connected to the devil (shaitaan).

 

In Surah:  (23:97):

 

وَقُل رَّبِّ أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنْ هَمَزَاتِ الشَّيَاطِينِ

 

And say, "My Lord, I seek refuge in You from the incitements of the devils,

Summary:

1.         وحى, الهام and القاء can be from Allah and can also be from devil, where وسوسه and همز is only from Shaitaan devil.

2.         The connection of  وحى  is with education, beliefs, rulings and unseen news, and the connection of الهام is with the doing or not doing a thing, and the connection of القاء is only with education.

3.         وسوسه is only evil thoughts that come inside the heart, where as همز with the evil thoughts the devils instigation is included.

4.         All وحى, الهام and القاء which is according to categorical Islamic injunction then it is from Allah and if it not then it is devilish.
