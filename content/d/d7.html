7- DARAKTH AUR PODAY [Trees and plants]

Synonyms: SHAJARAT (شَجَرَة), SHAJAR(شجر ), NAJAM(نَجْم), ATHAL(اَثْل ), YAQTEEN(يَقْطِيْن ), DAREE (ضَرِيْع), ZAQ QOM.

7.1 SHAJARAT(شَجَرَة), SHAJAR (شجر ): SHAJARAT (شَجَرَة)means a tree (Arabic lexicon jeem, Tree) any plant having a woody trunk (in referance to MAF) can be considered as SHAJAR either it grows high or spread on ground. So, word SHAJARAT(شَجَرَة) is common.

Allah SWT states in Qur'an:-

Surat Al-Wāqi`ah (The Inevitable) - سورة الواقعة

[56:71-72]

 أَفَرَأَيْتُمُ النَّارَ الَّتِي تُورُونَ         

أَأَنتُمْ أَنشَأْتُمْ شَجَرَتَهَا أَمْ نَحْنُ الْمُنشِئُونَ

 

And have you seen the fire that you ignite?

 Is it you who produced its tree, or are We the producer?

7.2 NAJAM(نَجْم ): It is also used for 'star' and those plants which do not have woody trunk like herbs etc, (in referance to MAF).

Allah SWT states in Qur'an:-

Surat Ar-Raĥmān (The Beneficent) - سورة الرحمن

[55:6]

وَالنَّجْمُ وَالشَّجَرُ يَسْجُدَانِ

And the stars and trees prostrate.

7.3 ATHAL(اَثْل): ATHALA (اَثَلَ) is to have a grip in ground. ATHAL(اَثْل) trees which have stong roots. Generally it applies to the tree of tamarisk.

Allah SWT states in Qur'an:

Surat Saba' (Sheba) - سورة سبإ

[34:16]

وَبَدَّلْنَاهُم بِجَنَّتَيْهِمْ جَنَّتَيْنِ ذَوَاتَيْ أُكُلٍ خَمْطٍ وَأَثْلٍ وَشَيْءٍ مِّن سِدْرٍ قَلِيلٍ

We replaced their two [fields of] gardens with gardens of bitter fruit, tamarisks and something of sparse lote trees.

7.4 YAQTEEN (يَقْطِيْن ) : Such trees which do not have proper (stem or stalk). It includes climbers/ vines for example pumpkin/gourd vines.(meem-qaaf)

Allah SWT states in Qur'an:-

Surat Aş-Şāffāt (Those who set the Ranks) - سورة الصافات

[37:146]

وَأَنبَتْنَا عَلَيْهِ شَجَرَةً مِّن يَقْطِينٍ

And We caused to grow over him a gourd vine.

7.5 DAREE(ضَرِيْع): means thorny bushes or spiny shrub. (in referance to MAF).

Allah SWT states in Qur'an:-

Surat Al-Ghāshiyah (The Overwhelming) - سورة الغاشية

[88:6]

لَّيْسَ لَهُمْ طَعَامٌ إِلَّا مِن ضَرِيعٍ

For them there will be no food except from a poisonous, thorny plant.

7.6 ZAQ QOM (زَقُوْم): means Cereus tree whose leaves are broad, thick and spiny. It tatses really bitter.

Allah SWT states in Qur'an:-

Surat Ad-Dukhān (The Smoke) - سورة الدخان

[44:43-44]

إِنَّ شَجَرَتَ الزَّقُّومِ

طَعَامُ الْأَثِيمِ

Indeed, the tree of zaqqum.

Is food for the sinful.

Summary:

(i) SHAJRAT(شَجَرَة): It is a common word. It is applicable for all plant types having solid woody trunk.

(ii) NAJAM (نَجْم ): Non-woody plants, usually herbs.

(iii) ATHAL(اَثْل): Frimly rooted plants and tree of tamarisk.

(iv) YAQTEEN(يَقْطِيْن ): Such plants which do not have solid trunk/wood. Fruiting plants. Specially vines of pumpkin/gourd.

(v) DAREE (ضَرِيْع) : Thorny bushes.

(vi) ZAQ QOM (زَقُوْم) : Plants that spread over the earth having thick spiny leaves. e.g. Canopy of Cereus.
