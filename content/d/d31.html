DEKHNA (Condition/state of watching/see/look) pg 507

{

synonyms:SHAKHASA(شَخَصَ),LAMAHA(لَمَحَ),HATA3A (هَطَعَ),

EASHAA(عَشَا),AGHMADA(اغْمَضَ),ZAGHA(� �َاغَ),

BARAQA(بَرَقَ)

1 SHAKHASA(شَخَصَ):meaning eyes become motionless,to lose ability to see,to stop blinking,

Quran example:

Surat Al-'Anbyā' (The Prophets)[21:97]

وَاقْتَرَبَ الْوَعْدُ الْحَقُّ فَإِذَا هِيَ شَاخِصَةٌ أَبْصَارُ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا

nd [when] the true promise has approached; then suddenly the eyes of those who disbelieved will be staring [in horror]

2 LAMAHA(لَمَحَ):swiftness of sight,to quickly glance at anything,blink of an eye

Surat An-Naĥl (The Bee)[16:77]

ۚ وَمَا أَمْرُ السَّاعَةِ إِلَّا كَلَمْحِ الْبَصَرِ أَوْ هُوَ أَقْرَبُ

And the command for the Hour is not but as a glance of the eye or even nearer

3 HATA3A(هَطَعَ):staring ahead,and keep on running

Surat 'Ibrāhīm (Abrahim)[14:43]

مُهْطِعِينَ مُقْنِعِي رُءُوسِهِمْ لَا يَرْتَدُّ إِلَيْهِمْ طَرْفُهُمْ ۖ

Racing ahead, their heads raised up, their glance does not come back to them,

4 EASHAA(عَشَا):ASHU(عشو) basic meaning is unable to see things clearly owing to darkness,and sometimes it means night time. AL-ISHA(العشاء)meaning time after evening.weak night vision,dinner. and

3ASHI(عشي يعشي عشيَّا عشاوة)meaning invisble at night

3SHWA(عِشوة)meaning blaze that is visible afar at night

Surat Az-Zukhruf (The Ornaments of Gold)[43:36]

وَمَن يَعْشُ عَن ذِكْرِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ نُقَيِّضْ لَهُ شَيْطَانًا فَهُوَ لَهُ قَرِينٌ

And whoever is blinded from remembrance of the Most Merciful - We appoint for him a devil, and he is to him a companion.

5 AGHMADA(اغْمَضَ):AGHMADA 3AINA(اغمض عينه)meaning to close one's eyes,AGHMADA 3AN ASSHAYEE(اغمض عن الشّي) to cover up (by closing eyes),to exceed,meaning to pretend with other about no knowledge of evil even though fully aware of it

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow)[2:267]

وَلَا تَيَمَّمُوا الْخَبِيثَ مِنْهُ تُنفِقُونَ وَلَسْتُم بِآخِذِيهِ إِلَّا أَن تُغْمِضُوا فِيهِ ۚ

And do not aim toward the defective therefrom, spending [from that] while you would not take it [yourself] except with closed eyes

6 ZAGHA(زَاغَ):meaning to deviate from right path,ZAGH AL BASRU(زَاغَ البصر) exhaustion of sight.imam raghib refers it as "sight mistake and then swerve"(side note:sight cross it's limit and then back off)

Surat An-Najm (The Star)[53:17]

مَا زَاغَ الْبَصَرُ وَمَا طَغَىٰ

The sight [of the Prophet] did not swerve, nor did it transgress [its limit]

7 BARAQA(بَرَقَ):lightning and BARIQ ALBASARU( بَرِقَ البَصَرُ) meaning,bedazzled by bolt of lightening or brilliance of light

Surat Al-Qiyāmah (The Resurrection)[75:7]

فَإِذَا بَرِقَ الْبَصَرُ

So when vision is dazzled

summary

1 SHAKHASA:motionless eyes

2 LAMAHA:blink of an eye

3 HATA3A:stare ahead

4 EASHAA:night blind

5 AGHMADA:to cover up

6 ZAGHA:sight swerving after exhaustion

7 BARAQA:bedazzled by bright light
