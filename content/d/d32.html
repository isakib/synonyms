DIKHLANA(to make other see)

Synonyms:AARA,REYA,BASSARA,TABBARAJA

1 AARA(اَرَي):from baab(افعال) meaning to make others see.this word is used for physical as well conceptual connotation

Surat Al-'Aĥqāf (The Wind-Curved Sandhills)[46:4]

أَرُونِي مَاذَا خَلَقُوا مِنَ الْأَرْضِ

Show me what they have created of the earth

Surat An-Nisā' (The Women)[4:105]

لِتَحْكُمَ بَيْنَ النَّاسِ بِمَا أَرَاكَ اللَّهُ ۚ

so you may judge between the people by that which Allah has shown you

(here ( أَرَاكَ) means GOD gifted wisdom as well as hidden Revelation).and (اَرَي يُرَاء) from baab (تفاعل) meaning to "make each other see something"

Surat An-Nisā' (The Women)[4:142]

وَإِذَا قَامُوا إِلَى الصَّلَاةِ قَامُوا كُسَالَىٰ يُرَاءُونَ النَّاسَ وَلَا يَذْكُرُونَ اللَّهَ إِلَّا قَلِيلًا

And when they stand for prayer, they stand lazily, showing [themselves to] the people and not remembering Allah except a little,

and RIYA-(رِيَاء) meaning to show off,to exhibit,to do something which is meant to show off

Surat An-Nisā' (The Women)[4:38]

وَالَّذِينَ يُنفِقُونَ أَمْوَالَهُمْ رِئَاءَ النَّاسِ

And [also] those who spend of their wealth to be seen by the people

2 BASSARA(بَصَّرَ):meaning to show to others such that what is shown is become known and understood.to get

acquainted with the conditions and signs of

Surat Al-Ma`ārij (The Ascending Stairways)[70:10-11]

لَا يَسْأَلُ حَمِيمٌ حَمِيمًا يُبَصَّرُونَهُمْ ۚ

no friend will ask [anything of] a friend, They will be shown each other.

3 TABBARAJA(تَبَرَّجَ): BARAJA(بَرَجَ) meaning to be high,to rise,to be visible.BURJ(بُرج) is a famous word and TABARJAT AL MIRAH(تَبرجت المراة) meaning women showing off their beauty and ornament

Surat Al-'Aĥzāb (The Combined Forces)[33:22]

وَقَرْنَ فِي بُيُوتِكُنَّ وَلَا تَبَرَّجْنَ تَبَرُّجَ الْجَاهِلِيَّةِ الْأُولَىٰ

And abide in your houses and do not display yourselves as [was] the display of the former times of ignorance

Summary

1 AARA:just to make others see

2 BASSARA: to make other seen such that it will be

known

3 TABBARAJA:to show off beauty and ornament
