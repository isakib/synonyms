دل – Heart

These words   ) صدر نفس قلب فؤاد (فاد are in Quran.

1.         قلب – a well-known organ of the body, the source of soul and life (قلوب) Allah addresses the heart for thinking, thought, intelligence, understanding.   All the things which modern science and medicine have related to the brain, the Quran has related to the heart.

 

In Surah Al-AAraaf:   (7:179):

 

لَهُمْ قُلُوبٌ لَّا يَفْقَهُونَ بِهَا

 

They have hearts with which they do not understand,

 

2.         فؤاد – some scholars have written that the same difference between ain  عينand basarبصر   or uzun  اذنand samaسمع  , is the same difference in قلب and فؤاد , the proof is in this Ayah below.

 

In Surah Al-Israa:   (17:36):

 

إِنَّ السَّمْعَ وَالْبَصَرَ وَالْفُؤَادَ كُلُّ أُولَٰئِكَ كَانَ عَنْهُ مَسْئُولًا

 

Indeed, the hearing, the sight and the heart - about all those [one] will be questioned.

 

But this is not the fact, because Allah has addressed the heart directly for intelligence, to understand, to think, to comprehend. The real fact is that this part of the body, the heart has many components, just as the brain has different components with different strengths.  In the same way the heart has special components related to special emotions, feelings and actions. According to the science of medicine فؤاد is the upper part of the heart which is above the opening of the stomach, and وجع الفؤاد  means pain of that particular part of the body, فؤاد plural افئدة is derived from فأد.  فأد اللحم means to roast meat on fire, and لحم فئيد  means roasted meat on fire. According to Ibn Faaris الفأد يدل على حمى وشدة الحرارة (ML) – this word indicates heat and severe heat, therefore whenever human’s experience strong emotions and its effects are mentioned then this word would be used.

 

In Surah Al-Qasas: (28:10):

 

وَأَصْبَحَ فُؤَادُ أُمِّ مُوسَىٰ فَارِغًا ۖ

 

And the heart of Moses' mother became empty [of all else].

In Surah Ibrahim ( 14;43)

 

مُهْطِعِينَ مُقْنِعِي رُءُوسِهِمْ لَا يَرْتَدُّ إِلَيْهِمْ طَرْفُهُمْ ۖ وَأَفْئِدَتُهُمْ هَوَاءٌ

 

Racing ahead, their heads raised up, their glance does not come back to them, and their hearts are void.

 

And the mentioning of when Allah will question the فؤاد , it is related to those deeds which have occurred due to strong emotions.

 

3.         صدر – means chest, (plural صدور) and the heart is in the chest.

 

In Surah Haj: (22:46):

 

وَلَٰكِن تَعْمَى الْقُلُوبُ الَّتِي فِي الصُّدُورِ

 

but blinded are the hearts which are within the breasts.

 

Therefore sometimes when the word صدور is used it means hearts, such as:

 

In Surah Yunus: (10:57):

 

وَشِفَاءٌ لِّمَا فِي الصُّدُورِ

 

and healing for what is in the breasts,

Now because the connection of صدر is to adverb of place, therefore whenever there is a need of mentioning the tightness or spaciousness of the heart then the word صدر is used.

 

In Surah Al-Hijr:  (15:97):

 

وَلَقَدْ نَعْلَمُ أَنَّكَ يَضِيقُ صَدْرُكَ بِمَا يَقُولُونَ

 

And We already know that your breast is constrained by what they say

In Surah As-Sarh: (94:1):

 

أَلَمْ نَشْرَحْ لَكَ صَدْرَكَ

 

Did We not expand for you, [O Muhammad], your breast?

 

And شرح صدر  or انشراح صدر is an idiom, when your has  heart has a right feeling about a complicated, difficult matter you call it شرح صدر  .  In order to hide something you need an adverb, therefore to hide a secret, a superstition, a thought, the word  صدر will be used.

 

In Surah Gafir: (40:19):

 

يَعْلَمُ خَائِنَةَ الْأَعْيُنِ وَمَا تُخْفِي الصُّدُورُ

 

He knows that which deceives the eyes and what the breasts conceal.

In Surah Maida: (5:7):

 

إِنَّ اللَّهَ عَلِيمٌ بِذَاتِ الصُّدُورِ

 

Indeed, Allah is Knowing of that within the breasts.

 

In Surah Naas:  (114:5):

 

الَّذِي يُوَسْوِسُ فِي صُدُورِ النَّاسِ

 

Who whispers [evil] into the breasts of mankind.

 

4.         نفس – soul, life, heart, mind , plural نفوس – the source/ origin and refuge of desires. Meaning a heart that desires things and is happy.

 

a.         the connection of concealing thoughts and secrets, this attribute is shared in both  نفوس and صدور.

 

In Surah Ahzaab: (33:37):

 

وَتُخْفِي فِي نَفْسِكَ مَا اللَّهُ مُبْدِيهِ

 

While you concealed within yourself that which Allah is to disclose.

 

b.           the connection of desires is with  نفس either good or bad.

 

In Surah Najm:  (53:23):

 

إِن يَتَّبِعُونَ إِلَّا الظَّنَّ وَمَا تَهْوَى الْأَنفُسُ

 

They follow not except assumption and what [their] souls desire,

c.       the connection of happiness is with    نفس too.

 

In Surah Nisaa: (4:4):

 

فَإِن طِبْنَ لَكُمْ عَن شَيْءٍ مِّنْهُ نَفْسًا فَكُلُوهُ هَنِيئًا مَّرِيئًا

 

But if they give up willingly to you anything of it, then take it in satisfaction and ease.

 

 

Summary:

 

1.     قلب – the source origin of intelligence sense, comprehension.

2.     فؤاد – the centre of strong emotions

3.     صدر – adverb for tightness , spaciousness and to conceal matters

4.     نفس – the centre of desires, it is used for ‘to be happy’ and ‘to hide matters’
