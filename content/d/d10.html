10. between, in the middle of, center point (DARMIYAN)

Synonyms: BAYN, KHILAAL, WAST, SAWAAA, QASD

 

10.1 BAYN (بَيْن): Between two points. It defines the connectivity or separation of two different objects. Bana (بَانَ) means to be distant from, so bayn is used to express separation between two objects. According to Ibn Alfaris the word bayn is used to express distinction between two different things

 

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - البقرة سورة

2:164

بَيْنَ السَّمَاءِ وَالْأَرْضِ

between the heaven and the earth

 

Surat Şād (The Letter "Saad") - ص سورة

38:22

فَاحْكُم بَيْنَنَا بِالْحَقِّ

so judge between us with truth

 

10.2 KHILAAL (خِلٰل): Point or space between two integrated things. A toothpick is called Alkhilala (اَلْخِلَالَ). Khalal is the act of moving the fingers (of hand) across the beard during ablution. Allah says in the Quran:

 

Surat Al-Kahf (The Cave) - الكهف سورة

18:33

وَفَجَّرْنَا خِلَالَهُمَا نَهَرًا

And We caused to gush forth within them a river.

 

 

This word  is also used to express the space; opening  between the different components of one object as in Quran:

 

Surat An-Nūr (The Light) - النور سورة

24:43

فَتَرَى الْوَدْقَ يَخْرُجُ مِنْ خِلَالِهِ

then makes them into a heap of layers, and you see the rain comes forth from between them

 

10.3 WAST (وَسْط): midway or halfway. To be at the middle of - be in the center of. Allah says in Holy Quran:

 

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - البقرة سورة

2:238

حَافِظُوا عَلَى الصَّلَوَاتِ وَالصَّلَاةِ الْوُسْطَىٰ

Maintain with care the [obligatory] prayers and [in particular] the middle prayer

 

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - البقرة سورة

2:143

وَكَذَٰلِكَ جَعَلْنَاكُمْ أُمَّةً وَسَطًا

And thus We have made you a medium (just) nation

 

10.4 SAWAAA (سَوَاۤء): In the midst of; center point. According to Ibn Alfaris it means “الشَّيْئَينِ بَيْنَ الْاِعْتِدَال وَ  اَلْاِسْتِقَامَة”. In Holy Quran:

 

Surat Ad-Dukhān (The Smoke) - الدخان سورة

44:47

خُذُوهُ فَاعْتِلُوهُ إِلَىٰ سَوَاءِ الْجَحِيمِ

[It will be commanded], "Seize him and drag him into the midst of the Hellfire

 

10.5 QASD (قَصْد): moderation or reasonableness. Just like generosity between extravagance and parsimony and gallantry between derring-do and tameness. Qasada (قَصَدَ) and النفقة فى اقتصد means economization; to spend moderately. Qasad as sabeel (السَّبِيْل قَصَدَ) means to walk on a path straightly. Allah says in the quran:

 

Surat Luqmān (Luqman) - لقمان سورة

31:19

وَاقْصِدْ فِي مَشْيِكَ وَاغْضُضْ مِن صَوْتِكَ

And be moderate in your pace and lower your voice

 

Summary:

10.1 BAYN: In between two different things

10.2 KHILALA: Point or space between two integrated things

 10.3 WAST: To be at the middle of (with respect to amount or distance)

10.4 SAWAAA: In the midst of; center point

10.5 QASD: Moderation
