3)Alyooum اليوم: means today .and the quantity of time in a day will be calculated as per the day timing in the day not the full day. It means  the  timing between sunrise and sunset Munjid - منجد.

 ظلّ اليوم Zil alyooum means “today full day it was sahodow“ like at the time of pharos  submergence Munjid - منجد.

 Allah says in Quran:

فَالْيَوْمَ نُنَجِّيكَ بِبَدَنِكَ لِتَكُونَ لِمَنْ خَلْفَكَ آيَةً

10:92 (surah 10 younas verse number 92)

Translation: So today We will save you in body that you may be to those who succeed you a sign.

And Alyooum اليوم word is used at several places in Quran for the Day of Judgment as well. Wherever day of judgment is mentioned.

for example.

قَالَ كَذَٰلِكَ أَتَتْكَ آيَاتُنَا فَنَسِيتَهَا ۖ وَكَذَٰلِكَ الْيَوْمَ تُنسَىٰ

20:126 (surah 20 ta-ha verse 126)

translation: [ Allah ] will say, "Thus did Our signs come to you, and you forgot them; and thus will you this Day be forgotten."

 

4)youmaizzin يَوْمَىئِذٍ: after youm an addition of izz has generated this word which is used to point towards a specific time period, means that day or the other day Mufraddat – مفردات.

As ALLAH says in Quran:

وُجُوهٌ يَوْمَئِذٍ مُّسْفِرَةٌ  ضَاحِكَةٌ مُّسْتَبْشِرَةٌ

80:38 (surah number 80 abasa  verse number 38)

Translation: Some faces that Day will be beaming, Laughing, rejoicing.

Summary:

1)nahar:the time between sunset and sunrise.

2)Youam:the total time between day and night.

3) alyoum:means today.Time between sunrise and sunset.

4)youmaizzin: means that day or other day.
