8. DARUST/ THEEK. [Correct/Sound]

Synonyms:- SAUAAB (صَوَاب ), HAQ (حَقّ).

8.1 SAUAAB (صَوَاب ) :- means right, correct. applicable (in referance to MANJID). (Antonym: error/mistake) and SUABA WA ASUABA AS SAHM (صَابَ وَ اَصَابَ السَّهْم)  means correct hit of an arrow on target ISTASUAB (اِسْتِصوَاب)  means to find an act or option to be right. To consider it correct. SAUAIB (صَوَيْب)  and MUSAIB (مُصِيْب ) Man of correct opinion. Righteous (In referance to MANJID). and if an arrow strikes at the right place and stays there, it is called MUSIBAH مصيبه  and this word is used for any soleful event (in referance to MAF). Therefore, SAUAAB (صواب) is basically related to opinion and qoute, it is often related to work.

Allah SWT states in Qur'an:

Surat An-Naba' (The Tidings) - سورة النبإ

[78:38]

لَّا يَتَكَلَّمُونَ إِلَّا مَنْ أَذِنَ لَهُ الرَّحْمَٰنُ وَقَالَ صَوَابًا

they will not speak except for one whom the Most Merciful permits, and he will say what is correct.

8.2 HAQ (حَقّ): Truth, truthfulness and reality. Affirmative (Antonym: superficial). Any thing or talk proved right after experimentation and observation.(in referance to MAF).

Allah SWT states in Qur'an:-

Surat Fuşşilat (Explained in Detail) - سورة فصلت

[41:53]

سَنُرِيهِمْ آيَاتِنَا فِي الْآفَاقِ وَفِي أَنفُسِهِمْ حَتَّىٰ يَتَبَيَّنَ لَهُمْ أَنَّهُ الْحَقُّ

We will show them Our signs in the horizons and within themselves until it becomes clear to them that it is the truth.

Summary:-  

In SAUAB (صواب ) truthness relates with one's opinion or qoute, whereas HAQ (حَقّ) involves truthfulness of any thing or dialouge which is proved to be right after observation and experimentation.
