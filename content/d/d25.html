25.  DHUTKARA HUA [drive out, revile, reprove]

Synonyms: RAJEEM, DUHOOR, MADHOOR, KHASI

 

25.1 RAJEEM (رَجِيْم): Rajam ( رَجَم) means to throw stones and Rajeem means cursed, damned. Allah called shaitan in Holy Quran:

 

Surat An-Naĥl (The Bee) -  النحل سورة

16:98

فَإِذَا قَرَأْتَ الْقُرْآنَ فَاسْتَعِذْ بِاللَّهِ مِنَ الشَّيْطَانِ الرَّجِيمِ

So when you want to recite the Quran, seek refuge with Allah from Shaitan (Satan), the outcast (the cursed one).

 

25.2 DUHOOR (دُحُوْر) : The word Dahara (دَحَرَ) means to reprove; to drive out (force someone to leave). In Quran:

 

Surat Aş-Şāffāt (Those who set the Ranks) - الصافات سورة

37:9

دُحُورًا ۖ وَلَهُمْ عَذَابٌ وَاصِبٌ

Outcast, and theirs is a constant (or painful) torment.

 

Surat Al-'A`rāf (The Heights) - الأعراف سورة

7:18

قَالَ اخْرُجْ مِنْهَا مَذْءُومًا مَّدْحُورًا

(Allah) said (to Iblis) "Get out from this (Paradise) disgraced and expelled.

 

25.3 KHASAA (خَسَاَ) means to revile a dog or a pig. In other words, to reprove someone because he is observed to be contemptible and despicable. Allah says in the Quran:

 

Surat Al-Mu'minūn (The Believers) - المؤمنون سورة

23:108

قَالَ اخْسَئُوا فِيهَا وَلَا تُكَلِّمُونِ

He will say, "Remain despised therein and do not speak to Me.

 

Summary:

25.1 RAJEEM: cursed

25.2 DUHOOR: to reprove or to drive out

25.3 KHASAA: to reprove someone because he is observed to be contemptible and despicable
