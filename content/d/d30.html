Ra`ayta, “dekhna”, P.505

Synonyms: RAAA(RAY),NAZARA,BASARA,BASURA,AANASA,ZARA

all these are function of heart meaning they relate not only to eye but also to heart and mind

another catergory of words that refer to the conditon of the act of seeing that are used in the quran are

SHAKHASA(شَخَصَ),LAMAHA(لَمَحَ),HATA3A (هَطَعَ),

BARAQA(بَرَقَ)

1 RAAA(رَاَي)RO-YA(رءي):to be aware of something,to see ,whether by means of eyes or contemplating or pondering or intelligence or wisdom or doubt or imagination .it is of general usage.

whether awake or dream and meaning last glance,

Quran example( with eyes):Surat Yūsuf (Joseph)[12:4]

إِنِّي رَأَيْتُ أَحَدَ عَشَرَ كَوْكَبًا

indeed I have seen [in a dream] eleven stars

Quran example(by pondering,):Surat Al-Mā`ūn (The Small Kindesses)[107:1]

أَرَأَيْتَ الَّذِي يُكَذِّبُ بِالدِّين

Have you seen the one who denies the Recompense?

2 NAZARA(نَظَرَ):to glance,,to seek whether that thing is found or not .it is also used in both sense i.e by eyes or by pondering 

Quran example:Surat At-Tawbah (The Repentance)[9:127] 

وَإِذَا مَا أُنزِلَتْ سُورَةٌ نَّظَرَ بَعْضُهُمْ إِلَىٰ بَعْضٍ

And whenever a surah is revealed, they look at each other

Quran example: 

Surat An-Naml (The Ant)[27:33] 

فَانظُرِي مَاذَا تَأْمُرِينَ

so see (result of) what you will command

generally it is used to see with eyes 

3 BASARA(بَصَرَ) ,BASURA(بَصُرَ):BASARA is used for sight and insight. it is rarely used for seeing with eyes

Quran example: 

Surat Al-'A`rāf (The Heights)[7:198] 

ۖ وَتَرَاهُمْ يَنظُرُونَ إِلَيْكَ وَهُمْ لَا يُبْصِرُونَ

and you see them looking at you while they do not see.

In this ayah RA-YA,NAZARA and BASARA all three synonyms are used.This ayah also clarified the difference between them RA-YA=imagine,NAZARA=see with eyes,BASARA=insight

BASURA is more specific/peculiar than BASARA

Quran example: 

Surat Ţāhā (Ta-Ha)[20:96] 

قَالَ بَصُرْتُ بِمَا لَمْ يَبْصُرُوا بِهِ

He said, "I saw what they did not see,

here 'saw' something which he figures out but not others. 

4 AANASA(اَنَسَ):it means neither see by eyes or insight,but it means to be acquainted or to know something from its signs.perceived

Quran example: 

Surat Ţāhā (Ta-Ha)[20:10]

فَقَالَ لِأَهْلِهِ امْكُثُوا إِنِّي آنَسْتُ نَارًا

said to his family, "Stay here; indeed, I have perceived a fire

Quran example: 

Surat An-Nisā' (The Women)[4:6] 

فَإِنْ آنَسْتُم مِّنْهُمْ رُشْدًا

Then if you perceive in them sound judgement

5 ZAR(زَارَ):ZARAH(زَارَه) meaning to visit someone,and visit to see or meet something,pilgrimage.ZAWR( زَوْر)meaning upper part of chest ZAWRT FULANA means i put my chest in front of him or i decide for his chest

Quran example:

Surat At-Takāthur (The Rivalry in world increase)[102:1-2]

أَلْهَاكُمُ التَّكَاثُرُ 

Competition in [worldly] increase diverts you 

حَتَّىٰ زُرْتُمُ الْمَقَابِرَ

Until you visit the graveyards

Summary:

1 RAAA(RAY):to ponder on a matter

2 NAZARA:generally used for looking

3 BASARA,BASURA:insight

4 AANASA:see, meaning find out.found

5 ZAR: to go for pilgrimage,to visit to see something
