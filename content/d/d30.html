Ra`ayta, “dekhna”, P.505

Synonyms: RAAA(RAY),NAZARA,BASARA,BASURA,AANASA,ZARA

all these are function of heart meaning they relate not only to eye but also to heart and mind

another catergory of words that refer to the conditon of the act of seeing that are used in the quran are

SHAKHASA(شَخَصَ),LAMAHA(لَمَحَ),HATA3A (هَطَعَ),

EASHAA(عَشَا),AGHMADA(اغْمَضَ),ZAGHA(� �َاغَ)

BARAQA(بَرَقَ)

1 RAAA(رَاَي)RO-YA(رءي):to be aware of something,to see ,whether by means of eyes or contemplating or pondering or intelligence or wisdom or doubt or imagination .it is of general usage.

whether awake or dream and meaning last glance,

Quran example( with eyes):Surat Yūsuf (Joseph)[12:4]

إِنِّي رَأَيْتُ أَحَدَ عَشَرَ كَوْكَبًا

indeed I have seen [in a dream] eleven stars

Quran example(by pondering,):Surat Al-Mā`ūn (The Small Kindesses)[107:1]

أَرَأَيْتَ الَّذِي يُكَذِّبُ بِالدِّين

Have you seen the one who denies the Recompense?

2 NAZARA(نَظَرَ):to glance,,to seek whether that thing is found or not .it is also used in both sense i.e by eyes or by pondering 

Quran example:Surat At-Tawbah (The Repentance)[9:127] 

وَإِذَا مَا أُنزِلَتْ سُورَةٌ نَّظَرَ بَعْضُهُمْ إِلَىٰ بَعْضٍ

And whenever a surah is revealed, they look at each other

Quran example: 

Surat An-Naml (The Ant)[27:33] 

فَانظُرِي مَاذَا تَأْمُرِينَ

so see (result of) what you will command

generally it is used to see with eyes 

3 BASARA(بَصَرَ) ,BASURA(بَصُرَ):BASARA is used for sight and insight. it is rarely used for seeing with eyes

Quran example: 

Surat Al-'A`rāf (The Heights)[7:198] 

ۖ وَتَرَاهُمْ يَنظُرُونَ إِلَيْكَ وَهُمْ لَا يُبْصِرُونَ

and you see them looking at you while they do not see.

In this ayah RA-YA,NAZARA and BASARA all three synonyms are used.This ayah also clarified the difference between them RA-YA=imagine,NAZARA=see with eyes,BASARA=insight

BASURA is more specific/peculiar than BASARA

Quran example: 

Surat Ţāhā (Ta-Ha)[20:96] 

قَالَ بَصُرْتُ بِمَا لَمْ يَبْصُرُوا بِهِ

He said, "I saw what they did not see,

here 'saw' something which he figures out but not others. 

4 AANASA(اَنَسَ):it means neither see by eyes or insight,but it means to be acquainted or to know something from its signs.perceived

Quran example: 

Surat Ţāhā (Ta-Ha)[20:10]

فَقَالَ لِأَهْلِهِ امْكُثُوا إِنِّي آنَسْتُ نَارًا

said to his family, "Stay here; indeed, I have perceived a fire

Quran example: 

Surat An-Nisā' (The Women)[4:6] 

فَإِنْ آنَسْتُم مِّنْهُمْ رُشْدًا

Then if you perceive in them sound judgement

5 ZAR(زَارَ):ZARAH(زَارَه) meaning to visit someone,and visit to see or meet something,pilgrimage.ZAWR( زَوْر)meaning upper part of chest ZAWRT FULANA means i put my chest in front of him or i decide for his chest

Quran example:

Surat At-Takāthur (The Rivalry in world increase)[102:1-2]

أَلْهَاكُمُ التَّكَاثُرُ 

Competition in [worldly] increase diverts you 

حَتَّىٰ زُرْتُمُ الْمَقَابِرَ

Until you visit the graveyards

Summary:

1 RAAA(RAY):to ponder on a matter

2 NAZARA:generally used for looking

3 BASARA,BASURA:insight

4 AANASA:see, meaning find out.found

5 ZAR: to go for pilgrimage,to visit to see something

}

DEKHNA (Condition/state of watching/see/look) pg 507

{

synonyms:SHAKHASA(شَخَصَ),LAMAHA(لَمَحَ),HATA3A (هَطَعَ),

EASHAA(عَشَا),AGHMADA(اغْمَضَ),ZAGHA(� �َاغَ),

BARAQA(بَرَقَ)

1 SHAKHASA(شَخَصَ):meaning eyes become motionless,to lose ability to see,to stop blinking, 

Quran example: 

Surat Al-'Anbyā' (The Prophets)[21:97] 

وَاقْتَرَبَ الْوَعْدُ الْحَقُّ فَإِذَا هِيَ شَاخِصَةٌ أَبْصَارُ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا

nd [when] the true promise has approached; then suddenly the eyes of those who disbelieved will be staring [in horror]

2 LAMAHA(لَمَحَ):swiftness of sight,to quickly glance at anything,blink of an eye 

Surat An-Naĥl (The Bee)[16:77] 

ۚ وَمَا أَمْرُ السَّاعَةِ إِلَّا كَلَمْحِ الْبَصَرِ أَوْ هُوَ أَقْرَبُ

And the command for the Hour is not but as a glance of the eye or even nearer

3 HATA3A(هَطَعَ):staring ahead,and keep on running

Surat 'Ibrāhīm (Abrahim)[14:43] 

مُهْطِعِينَ مُقْنِعِي رُءُوسِهِمْ لَا يَرْتَدُّ إِلَيْهِمْ طَرْفُهُمْ ۖ

Racing ahead, their heads raised up, their glance does not come back to them,

4 EASHAA(عَشَا):ASHU(عشو) basic meaning is unable to see things clearly owing to darkness,and sometimes it means night time. AL-ISHA(العشاء)meaning time after evening.weak night vision,dinner. and

3ASHI(عشي يعشي عشيَّا عشاوة)meaning invisble at night

3SHWA(عِشوة)meaning blaze that is visible afar at night

Surat Az-Zukhruf (The Ornaments of Gold)[43:36] 

وَمَن يَعْشُ عَن ذِكْرِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ نُقَيِّضْ لَهُ شَيْطَانًا فَهُوَ لَهُ قَرِينٌ

And whoever is blinded from remembrance of the Most Merciful - We appoint for him a devil, and he is to him a companion.

5 AGHMADA(اغْمَضَ):AGHMADA 3AINA(اغمض عينه)meaning to close one's eyes,AGHMADA 3AN ASSHAYEE(اغمض عن الشّي) to cover up (by closing eyes),to exceed,meaning to pretend with other about no knowledge of evil even though fully aware of it 

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow)[2:267] 

وَلَا تَيَمَّمُوا الْخَبِيثَ مِنْهُ تُنفِقُونَ وَلَسْتُم بِآخِذِيهِ إِلَّا أَن تُغْمِضُوا فِيهِ ۚ

And do not aim toward the defective therefrom, spending [from that] while you would not take it [yourself] except with closed eyes

6 ZAGHA(زَاغَ):meaning to deviate from right path,ZAGH AL BASRU(زَاغَ البصر) exhaustion of sight.imam raghib refers it as "sight mistake and then swerve"(side note:sight cross it's limit and then back off) 

Surat An-Najm (The Star)[53:17] 

مَا زَاغَ الْبَصَرُ وَمَا طَغَىٰ

The sight [of the Prophet] did not swerve, nor did it transgress [its limit]

7 BARAQA(بَرَقَ):lightning and BARIQ ALBASARU( بَرِقَ البَصَرُ) meaning,bedazzled by bolt of lightening or brilliance of light 

Surat Al-Qiyāmah (The Resurrection)[75:7] 

فَإِذَا بَرِقَ الْبَصَرُ

So when vision is dazzled

summary 

1 SHAKHASA:motionless eyes 

2 LAMAHA:blink of an eye 

3 HATA3A:stare ahead 

4 EASHAA:night blind 

5 AGHMADA:to cover up 

6 ZAGHA:sight swerving after exhaustion

7 BARAQA:bedazzled by bright light

}

DIKHLANA(to make other see)

{

Synonyms:AARA,REYA,BASSARA,TABBARAJA

1 AARA(اَرَي):from baab(افعال) meaning to make others see.this word is used for physical as well conceptual connotation

 

Surat Al-'Aĥqāf (The Wind-Curved Sandhills)[46:4] 

أَرُونِي مَاذَا خَلَقُوا مِنَ الْأَرْضِ

Show me what they have created of the earth

Surat An-Nisā' (The Women)[4:105] 

لِتَحْكُمَ بَيْنَ النَّاسِ بِمَا أَرَاكَ اللَّهُ ۚ

so you may judge between the people by that which Allah has shown you

(here ( أَرَاكَ) means GOD gifted wisdom as well as hidden Revelation).and (اَرَي يُرَاء) from baab (تفاعل) meaning to "make each other see something"

Surat An-Nisā' (The Women)[4:142] 

وَإِذَا قَامُوا إِلَى الصَّلَاةِ قَامُوا كُسَالَىٰ يُرَاءُونَ النَّاسَ وَلَا يَذْكُرُونَ اللَّهَ إِلَّا قَلِيلًا 

And when they stand for prayer, they stand lazily, showing [themselves to] the people and not remembering Allah except a little,

and RIYA-(رِيَاء) meaning to show off,to exhibit,to do something which is meant to show off 

Surat An-Nisā' (The Women)[4:38] 

وَالَّذِينَ يُنفِقُونَ أَمْوَالَهُمْ رِئَاءَ النَّاسِ

And [also] those who spend of their wealth to be seen by the people

2 BASSARA(بَصَّرَ):meaning to show to others such that what is shown is become known and understood.to get

acquainted with the conditions and signs of

Surat Al-Ma`ārij (The Ascending Stairways)[70:10-11] 

لَا يَسْأَلُ حَمِيمٌ حَمِيمًا يُبَصَّرُونَهُمْ ۚ

no friend will ask [anything of] a friend, They will be shown each other.

3 TABBARAJA(تَبَرَّجَ): BARAJA(بَرَجَ) meaning to be high,to rise,to be visible.BURJ(بُرج) is a famous word and TABARJAT AL MIRAH(تَبرجت المراة) meaning women showing off their beauty and ornament

Surat Al-'Aĥzāb (The Combined Forces)[33:22] 

وَقَرْنَ فِي بُيُوتِكُنَّ وَلَا تَبَرَّجْنَ تَبَرُّجَ الْجَاهِلِيَّةِ الْأُولَىٰ

And abide in your houses and do not display yourselves as [was] the display of the former times of ignorance

Summary 

1 AARA:just to make others see 

2 BASSARA: to make other seen such that it will be

known

3 TABBARAJA:to show off beauty and ornament
