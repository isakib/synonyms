5  DARAZ HONA---- KERNA [EXTENSION----TO EXTEND]

Synonyms: TAALA and TAWAL, MADD and MADAAD, BA ADA.

5.1 TAALA (طَالَ) (TAULAN   طَوْلاً) means to increase in length. TAUL (  طُوْل) meaning increase in length/height (Antonym: Broad/wide), ATAAL (اَطَال) to increase the length.

It is stated in Qur'an:-

Surat Al-'Isrā' (The Night Journey) - سورة الإسراء

[17:37]

وَلَا تَمْشِ فِي الْأَرْضِ مَرَحًا ۖ إِنَّكَ لَن تَخْرِقَ الْأَرْضَ وَلَن تَبْلُغَ الْجِبَالَ طُولًا

And do not walk upon the earth exultantly. Indeed, you will never tear the earth [apart], and you will never reach the mountains in height.

TA-TAWALA ALAYHIL UMR (تَطَاوَلَ عَلِيْهِ العُمْر ) means growing of someone's age. Iong age(MANJID).

In Qur'an it is stated:-

Surat Al-Qaşaş (The Stories) - سورة القصص

[28:45]

وَلَٰكِنَّا أَنشَأْنَا قُرُونًا فَتَطَاوَلَ عَلَيْهِمُ الْعُمُرُ

But We produced [many] generations [after Moses], and prolonged was their duration.

5.2 MUDD (مَدّ ), means to stretch and lengthify. To increase the lenght and widht of a thing in such a manner that it does not break apart.(In referance to MAF)

and MUDDAT (مدّت) means  Age's(era) length.

In Qur'an it is stated:-

Surat Al-Furqān (The Criterian) - سورة الفرقان

[25:45]

أَلَمْ تَرَ إِلَىٰ رَبِّكَ كَيْفَ مَدَّ الظِّلَّ

Have you not considered your Lord - how He extends the shadow

and MADDAT (مدّت) meaning duration of period (like 'long time').It is stated in Qur'an:-

Surat Al-Humazah (The Traducer) - سورة الهمزة

[104:9]

فِي عَمَدٍ مُّمَدَّدَةٍ

In extended columns.

5.3 BA ADA (بَاعَدَ) : BAO DA(بَعُدَ) menans far away. BA ADA(بَاعَدَ) means to increase the distance between two things.

In Qur'an it is stated:-

Surat Saba' (Sheba) - سورة سبإ

34:19

فَقَالُوا رَبَّنَا بَاعِدْ بَيْنَ أَسْفَارِنَا

But [insolently] they said, "Our Lord, lengthen the distance between our journeys,"

 

Summary:- (1)TAALA (طَالَ) :- increase in height. (2) MADDA ( مَدَّ) :- to stretch something (3) BAA ADA (بَاعَدَ) : to increase distance between two things, to extend.
