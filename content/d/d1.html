Yadkhuloona, “daakhil hona”, P.473 [any] man

Synonyms:DAKHALA,IDKHIL,WALAJA,AULIJ,SALA,SALLA

SALAKA

1 DAKHALA(دَخَلَ):famous word,come inside(opp KHARAJA(خَرَجَ) meaning to go outside and its usage is common

Surat Yūsuf (Joseph)[12:36]

وَدَخَلَ مَعَهُ السِّجْنَ فَتَيَانِ

And there entered the prison with him two young men

and IDKHIL(إدخل) is used for "to enter"

Surat Al-'Insān (The Man)[76:31] 

يُدْخِلُ مَن يَشَاءُ فِي رَحْمَتِهِ ۚ

He admits whom He wills into His mercy

2 WALAJA(وَلَجَ):ALWALUJ(الوَلوج) meaning to enter into a narrow place ,to enter with difficulty,

Surat Al-'A`rāf (The Heights)[7:40] 

وَلَا يَدْخُلُونَ الْجَنَّةَ حَتَّىٰ يَلِجَ الْجَمَلُ فِي سَمِّ الْخِيَاطِ ۚ

nor will they enter Paradise until a camel enters into the eye of a needle

and to enter forcefully is AULAJ(اُولِج)

Surat Al-Ĥaj (The Pilgrimage)[22:61] 

يُولِجُ اللَّيْلَ فِي النَّهَارِ وَيُولِجُ النَّهَارَ فِي اللَّيْلِ

causes the night to enter the day and causes the day to enter the night

3 SALA(صَلي): is particularly used for 'entering into fire'.meaning to enter into fire and burn

Surat 'Ibrāhīm (Abrahim)[14:29] 

جَهَنَّمَ يَصْلَوْنَهَا ۖ وَبِئْسَ الْقَرَارُ

[It is] Hell, which they will [enter to] burn, and wretched is the settlement.

and to make some one enter into fire or burn SALLA(صلّي) will be used

Surat Al-Ĥāqqah (The Reality)[69:31] 

ثُمَّ الْجَحِيمَ صَلُّوهُ

Then into Hellfire drive him.

4 SALAKA(سَلَكَ):has many meanings and can be transitive or intransitive and all of these meaning refers to 'to enter'

A) meaning to thread like beads or pearls on thread

Surat Ash-Shu`arā' (The Poets)[26:200] 

كَذَٰلِكَ سَلَكْنَاهُ فِي قُلُوبِ الْمُجْرِمِينَ

Thus have We inserted disbelief into the hearts of the criminals

B) to put into like putting hand in to shirt

Surat Al-Qaşaş (The Stories)[28:32] 

اسْلُكْ يَدَكَ فِي جَيْبِكَ تَخْرُجْ بَيْضَاءَ مِنْ غَيْرِ سُوء

Insert your hand into the opening of your garment; it will come out white, without disease

same piece of ayah is used at another place with IDKHIL

Surat An-Naml (The Ant)[27:12] 

وَأَدْخِلْ يَدَكَ فِي جَيْبِكَ تَخْرُجْ بَيْضَاءَ مِنْ غَيْرِ سُوءٍ

And put your hand into the opening of your garment [at the breast]; it will come out white without disease

C)to follow a path,ALLAH said to the honey bee

Surat An-Naĥl (The Bee)[16:69] 

فَاسْلُكِي سُبُلَ رَبِّكِ ذُلُلًا

and follow the ways of your Lord laid down [for you]."

in the above ayah USLLUKI( اسلَُكَي) has been translated as UDKHULI(اُدخلي)

Surat Al-Muddaththir (The Cloaked One)[74:42] 

مَا سَلَكَكُمْ فِي سَقَرَ

[And asking them], "What put you into Saqar?"

by putting all of these specifications in view sahib muntaha alarib has describe it's summarized meaning as "to pull one thing to another thing or from another thing" as per sahib munjad "to put onething in another thing" whereas sahib miqyas allugha defines two basic meanings 1)to follow along the path 2)to enter one thing in to other

Summary:

1 DAKHALA:general usage

2 WALAJA: enter into narrow place

3 SALA:enter into fire

4 SALAK:to put or pull one thing into another
