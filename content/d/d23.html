Angels of hell-23

Synonyms are:

1)    خَزْنَة

2)    زبانية (زبن)

3)    مالك

 

 

1)khzanah خَزْنَة:it is the plural of khazin خازن.and khzan خزن  portrays  the meaning of two words, to accumulate  and to care. And khazin  خازن means the treasurer who protects the accumulated money/goods. Keeper or guard for the people of hell.

Allah says:

سَأَلَهُمْ خَزَنَتُهَا أَلَمْ يَأْتِكُمْ نَذِيرٌ

67:8 (surah 67 al mulk verse 8)

Translation: its keepers ask them, "Did there not come to you a warner?"

2)zabaniah زبانية: it is the plural of zabniayh زبنية. Zabani alaakraab زبانى العَقْرَب  means  scorpion sting  Munjid - منجد  and zabaniah  زبانية  are bad-temperd /strict and peevish  angels of hell who will push the people of hell towards hell.

Allah says in Quran:

سَنَدْعُ الزَّبَانِيَةَ

96:18 (surah 96 alaq verse 18)

Translation:

We will call the angels of Hell.

3)malik مالك:the leader of the angels of hell (or the keeper of hell)or it’s the name of the leader.

In Quran:

وَنَادَوْا يَا مَالِكُ لِيَقْضِ عَلَيْنَا رَبُّكَ

43:77 (surah 43 az zukhraf,verse 77)

Translation: And they will call, "O Malik, let your Lord put an end to us!"
