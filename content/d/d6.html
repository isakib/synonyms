6 DARPAY HONA [Pursuit/Quest]

Synonyms: TASADDA(تَصَدّي ) , HAFA(حَفَا ).

6.1 TASADDA (تَصَدّي ): SADDA (صَدي ) meaning  uproar, the sound which stirkes an object e.g. dome etc then bounces back (echo). TASADDA(تَصَدّي ) meaning to be incessant after something like echoing voice. To be attentive (in referance to MAF)

Allah SWT stated in Qur'an:-

Surat `Abasa (He Frowned) - سورة عبس

[104: 5-6]

أَمَّا مَنِ اسْتَغْنَىٰ[5]

فَأَنتَ لَهُ تَصَدَّىٰ[6]

As for he who thinks himself without need,

To him you give attention.

6.2 HAFA (حَفَا ) (HAFU حفو ) means insisting to attain something. Tailing and advocation for someone's estimation (in referance to MAF). Therefore, word HAFU (حفو) is used for constant pursuance and chasing  of  something .

It is stated in Qur'an:-

Surat Al-'A`rāf (The Heights) - سورة الأعراف

[7:187]

لَا تَأْتِيكُمْ إِلَّا بَغْتَةً ۗ يَسْأَلُونَكَ كَأَنَّكَ حَفِيٌّ عَنْهَا

It will not come upon you except unexpectedly." They ask you as if you are familiar with it

In another versus:-

Surat Maryam (Mary) - سورة مريم

[19:47]

سَأَسْتَغْفِرُ لَكَ رَبِّي ۖ إِنَّهُ كَانَ بِي حَفِيًّا

I will ask forgiveness for you of my Lord. Indeed, He is ever gracious to me.

In this ayah, real meaning of HAFFIYAN حَفِيًّا is  to be in pursuit every moment. Shortly it is translated as for kind/compassonate.

Summary:-

(i) TASADA(تَصَدّي ): temprory pursuits/quests.

(ii) HAFA(حَفَا ): permenant pursuits/quests.
