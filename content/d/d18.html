Far-18

Synonyms are:

1)    بَعيد

2)    سَحِيق

3)    عَمِيق

4)    قَصِيّاً

1)baeeid بَعيد: بُعد opposite is qurb which means near and baiied بَعيد means distant. far in terms of distance and also in terms of time periods. its use is usual in Quran its mentioned:

وَإِنْ أَدْرِي أَقَرِيبٌ أَم بَعِيدٌ مَّا تُوعَدُونَ

21:109 (surah 21 al anbiya verse 109)

Translation: but I know not whether that which ye are promised is near or far.

Other than noticeable things this word is also Uused in literal terms.

وَيُرِيدُ الشَّيْطَانُ أَن يُضِلَّهُمْ ضَلَالًا بَعِيدًا

 

4:60 (surah 4 an nisa verse 60)

Translation: But Satan´s wish is to lead them astray far away (from the right).

 

 

2)sahiq سَحِيق :saahqa سَحَقَ means beating and grinding. And sahiiqaa سَحِقَ means to be far.and suhqun سُحْقاً  means to be away from Allah’s mercy (curse) Munjid – منجد. “ سَحَقَ الرّيحُ الَّارضَ” peeling off the earth due to harsh wind. and sahiiq سَحِيْق means a far away destination and it is only used for locations Fiqhul lughat 29. Allah says in quran:

فَكَأَنَّمَا خَرَّ مِنَ السَّمَاءِ فَتَخْطَفُهُ الطَّيْرُ أَوْ تَهْوِي بِهِ الرِّيحُ فِي مَكَانٍ سَحِيقٍ

22:31 (surah 22 hajj, verse 31 )

Translation: as if he had fallen from heaven and been snatched up by birds, or the wind had swooped (like a bird on its prey) and thrown him into a far-distant place.

 

3)ameeq عَمِيق:amaq means depth and ameeq means deep  and fajin ameeq فجٍّ عميق means a way having ups and downs or a dangerous and  distant way it is in Quran :

 

يَأْتِينَ مِن كُلِّ فَجٍّ عَمِيقٍ

22:27 (surah 22 hajj verse 27)

Transaltion: lean on account of journeys through deep and distant mountain highways.

 

4)qasian قَصِيّاً :is from qasaa قصَىٰ  and it means to be far. Aqsaa اَقْصا and quswaa قصْوٰى means extremely far things Munjid - منج and aqsa means edge or end on the other or opposite side. Allah says in Quran:

وَجَاءَ رَجُلٌ مِّنْ أَقْصَى الْمَدِينَةِ يَسْعَىٰ

28:20 (surah  28 qasas verse 20)

Transaltion: And there came a man, running, from the furthest end of the City.

In the manner of speaking Aqsaa اَقْصا and qasian قَصِيّاً not only gives the meaning of distance the direction is also covered within.

Allah says in Quran:

فَحَمَلَتْهُ فَانتَبَذَتْ بِهِ مَكَانًا قَصِيًّا

19:22 (surah 19 maryum verse 22)

Transaltion: So she conceived him, and she retired with him to a remote place.

Means at first maryum went on the eastern destination and later went far away to some other location.

Summary:

1)baeeid its use is general and is used in both ways as adverb of time and adverb of place.

2)sahiiq:it is adverb of time and  a collection of two words, far and irritation.

3)ammeq means a way with ups and downs .distance and difficulties.

4)qasiiaan:  distance in which  a little of direction is known (distance and direction).
