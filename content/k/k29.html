29. KEH, GOYA KEH [to, as if]

Synonyms: AN, KANA, KANNA, KA’ANAMA, ALLA

Page 715

29.1 AN (اَنْ): ANNA MUKAFAFAH (اَنَّ مخففة) when this word comes in sentence it gives a meaning of ‘to’ and ‘as if’. In the Quran

Surah Al-Baqarah (The cow): سورۃ البَقَرَۃ  2:26

 إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ لَا يَسۡتَحۡىِۦۤ أَن يَضۡرِبَ مَثَلاً۬

Indeed, Allah is not timid to present an example

29.2 KANA (کَاَنَ), KANNA (کَاَنَّ), KA’ANAMA (کَاَنَّمَا): These words are composed of simile ‘ك’ and AN (اَنْ), ANNA (اَنَّ). And give the meaning of ‘as if’. Allah says in the Holy Quran

Surah Luqman (Luqman): سُوۡرَةُ لقمَان 31:7

 كَأَن لَّمۡ يَسۡمَعۡهَا كَأَنَّ فِىٓ أُذُنَيۡهِ وَقۡرً۬ا‌ۖ

as if he heard them not, as if there was deafness in both his ears:

AN (اَنْ) and KA’N (کَاَنْ), are verbs and ANNA (اَنَّ) and KA’ANNA (کَاَنَّ) are nouns as shown in the ayah above.

Surah Al-Anfal (The Spoils of War): سُوۡرَةُ الاٴنفَال 8:6

 كَأَنَّمَا يُسَاقُونَ إِلَى ٱلۡمَوۡتِ

 as if they were being driven to death

29.3 ALLA (اَلَّا): ALLA (اَلَّا) is actually AN (اَنْ) and LA (لَا) and means ‘as if not’ when it comes in a sentence. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Fussilat (Explained in Detail): سورۃ فُصّلَت 41:30

 تَتَنَزَّلُ عَلَيۡهِمُ ٱلۡمَلَـٰٓٮِٕڪَةُ أَلَّا تَخَافُواْ وَلَا تَحۡزَنُواْ

the angels will descend upon them: “Do not fear, and do not grieve,
