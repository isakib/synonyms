32 KHAAL [skin, hide]

Synonyms: JILD, SHAWAA

32.1 JILD (جِلْد): Skin or hide of an animal, upper layer of body that is skin or hide. A very common word. Allah says in the Quran

Surah An-Nisa (The women): سورۃ النِّسا 4:56

 كُلَّمَا نَضِجَتۡ جُلُودُهُم بَدَّلۡنَـٰهُمۡ جُلُودًا غَيۡرَهَا

Every time their skins are cooked, We will replace them with other skins,

Page 717

32.2 SHAWAA(شَوٰی): Corners or sides of body, hands, feet and those parts of body which when injured are not life threatening. The idiom “رَمَاہُ فَاَشْوَاہُ” means the arrow struck him on a side or didn’t strike on an organ that would fatally injure the person. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Maarij (The Ascending Stairs): سُوۡرَةُ المعَارج 70:15

 كَلَّآ‌ۖ إِنَّہَا لَظَىٰ نَزَّاعَةً۬ لِّلشَّوَىٰ

By no means! It is a Raging Fire. It strips away the scalps.
