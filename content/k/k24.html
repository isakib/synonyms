24: KUNWAN [well]

Synonyms: BI’R, JUBB, RASS

24.1 BI’R (بِئر): Means a well, it’s a common word regardless that it is deserted or inhibited. Also a dried well can be called BI’R (بِئر) too. In the Holy Quran

Surah Al-Hajj  (The Pilgrimage): سُوۡرَةُ الحَجّ 22:45

 وَبِئۡرٍ۬ مُّعَطَّلَةٍ۬ وَقَصۡرٍ۬ مَّشِيدٍ

They lie in ruins; with stilled wells, and lofty mansions.

24.2 JUBB (جُبّ): A well that is only dug up and its walls are not stoned or bricked. A mud well. In the Quran

Surah Yusuf (Joseph): سورۃ یُوسُف 12:15

فَلَمَّا ذَهَبُواْ بِهِۦ وَأَجۡمَعُوٓاْ أَن يَجۡعَلُوهُ فِى غَيَـٰبَتِ ٱلۡجُبِّ‌ۚ

So they went away with him, and agreed to put him at the bottom of the well

24.3 RASS (رَسّ): A very large well. Which has ample of water in it. In the Quran

Surah Qaf (Qaf): سُوۡرَةُ قٓ 50:12

كَذَّبَتۡ قَبۡلَهُمۡ قَوۡمُ نُوحٍ۬ وَأَصۡحَـٰبُ ٱلرَّسِّ وَثَمُودُ

The people of Noah denied before them, and the companions of the well and Thamud.
