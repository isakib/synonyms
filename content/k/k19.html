19. KAM KARNA- KAMI KARNA- GHATANA ( To decrease, Reduce, To diminish)

Synonyms: ZALAM, ALA, QASARA, ALATA, HAZHAMA, FATARA, QALLA, TAFFAFA, KHASIRA, NAQASA, BAKHASA, FARRATA, WATARA.

 

19.1 ZALAMA(ظَلَمَ): Means to place something other than it’s actual location. And it’s opposite is A’DL(عَدْل). Therefore, anything  against the justice will be cruelty. In a way, the word ZALAMA(ظَلَمَ) is widely used. In anything, whether there’s any deficiency or excess and no matter how much less or greater this variation may be, ZULM(ظلم) applies to everyone. In addition to this, this word is used as both, materialistically and meaningfully. In the Quran:

Surat Al-Kahf (The Cave) - سورة الكهف 18:33

كِلْتَا الْجَنَّتَيْنِ آتَتْ أُكُلَهَا وَلَمْ تَظْلِم مِّنْهُ شَيْئًا ۚ

Each of the two gardens produced its fruit and did not fall short thereof in anything.

 

19.2 ALA(اَلَا): Means to show negligence. Not to do any work the way it should be done. To spare. In the Quran:

Surat 'Āli `Imrān (Family of Imran) - سورة آل عمران 3:118

يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا لَا تَتَّخِذُوا بِطَانَةً مِّن دُونِكُمْ لَا يَأْلُونَكُمْ خَبَالًا

O you who have believed, do not take as intimates those other than yourselves, for they will not spare you [any] ruin.

 

19.3 QASARA(قَصَرَ): Not to do the amount of work as required. And according to Imam Raghib, not to reach the height or peak of something. Not fulfilling the fixed quantity or number. To lessen or shorten. In the Quran:

Surat An-Nisā' (The Women) - سورة النساء 4:101

وَإِذَا ضَرَبْتُمْ فِي الْأَرْضِ فَلَيْسَ عَلَيْكُمْ جُنَاحٌ أَن تَقْصُرُوا مِنَ الصَّلَاةِ

And when you travel throughout the land, there is no blame upon you for shortening the prayer.

 

19.4 ALATA(اَلَتَ): Means to diminish from one’s right. Diminish from one’s labour. Not to reward fully or delay for one’s work. ALQASR WA IBTA’(اَلقَصر وَ اِبطَاء). In the Quran:

Surat Aţ-Ţūr (The Mount) - سورة الطور 52:21

مَا أَلَتْنَاهُم مِّنْ عَمَلِهِم مِّن شَيْءٍ

We will not deprive them of anything of their deeds.

 

19.5 HAZHAMA (هَضَمَ): Means to crush, squeeze or squash something that is soft, and reduce it. To deprive a weak person from his/her right. To extort. HAZEEM (هَضِيم) means both, soft and fragile, also quick digestible. In the Quran:

Surat Ţāhā (Ta-Ha) - سورة طه 20:112

وَمَن يَعْمَلْ مِنَ الصَّالِحَاتِ وَهُوَ مُؤْمِنٌ فَلَا يَخَافُ ظُلْمًا وَلَا هَضْمًا

But he who does of righteous deeds while he is a believer - he will neither fear injustice nor deprivation.

 

19.6 FATARA(فَتَرَ): The occurrence of  weakness in anything. The decrease or subsidy in pace due to constant working or exhaustion. In the Quran:

Surat Az-Zukhruf (The Ornaments of Gold) - سورة الزخرف 43:75

إِنَّ الْمُجْرِمِينَ فِي عَذَابِ جَهَنَّمَ خَالِدُونَ ْ لَا يُفَتَّرُ عَنْهُمْ وَهُمْ فِيهِ مُبْلِسُونَ ْ

Indeed, the criminals will be in the punishment of Hell, abiding eternally. It will not be allowed to subside for them, and they, therein, are in despair.

 

19.7 QALLA(قَلَّ): Basically it has two meanings.

1. To be elevated.

2. To be less.

Here the second one is discussed. Means to be less in quantity or numbers. (Opposite KASURA كَثُرَ). In the Quran:

Surat Al-Kahf (The Cave) - سورة الكهف 18:39

إِن تَرَنِ أَنَا أَقَلَّ مِنكَ مَالًا وَوَلَدً ْ

Although you see me less than you in wealth and children.

 

19.8 TAFFAFA (طَفَّفَ): TAFEEF(طَفِيف) means an ordinary and worthless thing. And TAFFAFA(طَفَّفَ) means to fill a measurement scale a little less from the mark or even taking a small scale or parameter. In the Quran:

Surat Al-Muţaffifīn (The Defrauding) - سورة المطففين 83:1

وَيْلٌ لِّلْمُطَفِّفِينَْ

Woe to those who give less [than due].

 

19.9 KHASIRA(خَسِرَ): Means the decrease in capital or stock. Generally, this word is specific for trade(Opposite of RABIHA رَبِحَ). To suffer a loss. And KHASR-UL-MEEZAAN(خسرالميزان) means to bring a decrease in weight after seizing someone’s right . Like it’s said in the following Aayah,

Surat Al-Muţaffifīn (The Defrauding) - سورة المطففين 83:3

وَإِذَا كَالُوهُمْ أَو وَّزَنُوهُمْ يُخْسِرُونَ ْ

But if they give by measure or by weight to them, they cause loss.

 

19.10 NAQASA(نَقَصَ): To decrease or reduce.( opposite of ZADA زَادَ and ZIYADAH زِيَادَة is the opposite of NUQSAAN نُقصَان). This word is most common to KHASIRA (خَسِرَ). Every damage is a loss but every loss is not a damage. In the Quran:

Surat Al-Muzzammil (The Enshrouded One) - سورة المزمل 73:3

نِّصْفَهُ أَوِ انقُصْ مِنْهُ قَلِيلًا ْ أَوْ زِدْ عَلَيْهِ وَرَتِّلِ الْقُرْآنَ تَرْتِيلًا ْ

Half of it - or subtract from it a little. Or add to it, and recite the Qur'an with measured recitation.

19.11 BAKHASA(بَخَسَ): BAKHS (بَخس) means defective, cheap or inferior. And BAKHASA(بَخَسَ) means to give something defective or inferior in return or to give in a reduced quantity. In the Quran:

Surat Yūsuf (Joseph) - سورة يوسف 12:20

وَشَرَوْهُ بِثَمَنٍ بَخْسٍ دَرَاهِمَ مَعْدُودَةٍ

And they sold him (Hazrat Yusuf A.S) for a reduced price - a few dirhams.
