22 KAMZOR [weak]

Synonyms:  ZAEEF, WAAHIYAH, AWHAN, AZILLAH

PAGE 708

 

22.1 ZAEEF (ضَعِیْف): (opposite QAWI (قوی)) Means inferior in strength and power. Weak. A famous word. And ZAE’F (ضَعْف) and ZU’UF(ضِعْف) mean weakness. (plural ZUA’FAA’ (ضُعَفَاء) and ZI’AAF (ضِعَاف)). In the Quran

Surah Al-Baqarah (The cow): سورۃ البَقَرَۃ  2:282

 كَانَ ٱلَّذِى عَلَيۡهِ ٱلۡحَقُّ سَفِيهًا أَوۡ ضَعِيفًا

 But if the debtor is mentally deficient, or weak,

22.2 WAAHIYAH (وَاھِیَة):  WAHIA (وَاھِی) means weakness due to old age for instance a worn out cloth. WAHIA (وَاھِیَ) mean loosening of something for instance these connections have gone loose. This word is used mostly for clothes and ropes. ALWAHI (اَلْوَاھْی) means a puncture in leather. Thus it means frailness, wearing out, weakness or dilapidation of something due to old age. Allah says in the Holy Quran

Surah Al-Haqqah (The reality): سورۃ الحَاقَّۃ 69:16

 وَٱنشَقَّتِ ٱلسَّمَآءُ فَهِىَ يَوۡمَٮِٕذٍ۬ وَاهِيَةٌ۬

And the heaven will crack; so on that Day it will be frail.

 

22.3 AWHAN (اَوْھَن): WAHANA (وَھَنَ) means weak, if used material things then it refers to weakening of a bone or strong object. And if used semantically then it refers to weakness in action or moral weakness. WAHANA (وَھَنَ) means to falter, to become lethargic and weak due to lack of strength, fragile. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Ankaboot  (The Spider): سُوۡرَةُ العَنکبوت 29:41

ا‌ۖ وَإِنَّ أَوۡهَنَ ٱلۡبُيُوتِ لَبَيۡتُ ٱلۡعَنڪَبُوتِ‌ۖ

But the most fragile of houses is the spider’s house

In another place in Quran

Surah An-Nisa (The women): سورۃ النِّسا 4:104

 

 وَلَا تَهِنُواْ فِى ٱبۡتِغَآءِ ٱلۡقَوۡمِ‌ۖ

And do not falter in the pursuit of the enemy

22.4 AZILLAH (اَذِلَّة): (singular ZALEEL (َذلیل), opposite AZEEZ(عزیز)) Means inferior. Inferior in honor or respect. A low grade citizen. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Ale- Imran (The family of Imran): سورۃ آل عِمْرن 3:123

وَلَقَدۡ نَصَرَكُمُ ٱللَّهُ بِبَدۡرٍ۬ وَأَنتُمۡ أَذِلَّةٌ۬‌ۖ

God had given you victory at Badr, when you were weak

Summary:

22.1 ZAEEF: Lesser in strength or power

22.2 WAAHIYAH: Weak due to wearing out

22.3 WAHAN: Weak or lethargic due to lack of strength

22.3 AZILLAH: Inferior people

 

 

KAMZOR KARNA [to weaken]:

ISTADAFA (اِسْتَضْعَفَ) for making and keeping weak and AWHANA (اَوْھَن) for making weak and slow. In the Quran

Surah Al-Anfal (The Spoils of War): سُوۡرَةُ الاٴنفَال 8:18

 ذَٲلِكُمۡ وَأَنَّ ٱللَّهَ مُوهِنُ كَيۡدِ ٱلۡكَـٰفِرِينَ

Such is the case. God will weaken the strategy of the disbelievers.
