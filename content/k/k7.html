7 KAMYAAB HONA, MURAD PANA [to succeed, to find what is willed]

Synonyms: AFLAHA, FAZA

7.1 AFLAHA (افَلَحَ): The meaning of FALAHA (فَلَحَ) consists of three things. 1. To tear apart, 2. Success, 3. Survival. FALLAH (افَلَّاحَ) means when a farmer ploughs a field to lay the seeds, And he succeeds when the crop is ready and gets the benefit from the crop’s outcome. And AFALAH (افَلَحَ) means success. To get what is willed by hard work. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Mumenoon (The believers): سُوۡرَةُ المؤمنون 23:1-2

قَدۡ أَفۡلَحَ ٱلۡمُؤۡمِنُونَ ٱلَّذِينَ هُمۡ فِى صَلَاتِہِمۡ خَـٰشِعُونَ

Successful are the believers. Those who are humble in their prayers.

7.2 FAZA (فَازَ): Means to escape a trouble and reach something that is wished for. To get safety accompanied by goodness. Allah says in the Holy Quran

Surah Ale- Imran (The family of Imran): سورۃ آل عِمْرن 3:185

 فَمَن زُحۡزِحَ عَنِ ٱلنَّارِ وَأُدۡخِلَ ٱلۡجَنَّةَ فَقَدۡ فَازَ‌ۗ

 Whoever is swayed from the Fire, and admitted to Paradise, has won

FAZ (فَاز):  FAZ (فَاز) means to be successful or to get salvation or to get despair, or to die. FAWAZ-AR-RUJL (فََوَّزَالرَّجُل) means a man died. MAFAZA  مَفَازَةٍ۬ 3:188 means a reason or a place for success and also a reason or place for despair. The word in this ayah means the place of despair or a reason for despair. But usually this word means to get rid of a problem and get something one desires for, or get successful. In the Quran

Surah An-Nisa (The women): سورۃ النِّسا 4:73

 يَـٰلَيۡتَنِى كُنتُ مَعَهُمۡ فَأَفُوزَ فَوۡزًا عَظِيمً۬ا

Oh, would that I had been with them, then should I have achieved a great success!

Summary:

7.1 AFLAHA : Success of getting fruits of hard work

7.2 FAZA: A situation in which on a fault one gets penalized and on success gets a huge reward.

 

For KAAN AZAN and SAMA’ are used and they are different from one other exactly like AAIEN [eyes] and BASAR [sight], RIJL [lower leg] and QADAM [feet]
