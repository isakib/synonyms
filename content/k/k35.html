It'aam – Feeding

It means feeding and the words Razaqa and At'ama have been used.

1- At'ama

It means to feed a person.

“And it is He, who feeds me and gives me drink” 26/79

2- Rizq

It carries a wide meaning and carries the meaning or feeding both body and soul.

“But provide for them with it and clothe them (orphans)” 4/5
