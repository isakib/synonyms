21 KAMRAH [room, chamber]

Synonyms: HUJRAH, GHURFAH

21.1 HUJRAH(حُجْرَۃ): Means a room or grave (dictionary al Manjad). (Plural HUJRAAT (حُجْرات)).  Means a place to live. Thus HUJRAH (حُجْرَۃ) refers to that simple room which is used as a house. Allah says in the Holy Quran

Surah Al-Hujraat  (The Inner Apartments): سُوۡرَةُ الحُجرَات 49:4

نَّ ٱلَّذِينَ يُنَادُونَكَ مِن وَرَآءِ ٱلۡحُجُرَٲتِ

Those who call you from behind the chambers

 

21.1 GHURFAH (غُرْفَة): Means a room or chamber on the upper floor (dictionary al Manjad). GHURAF (غُرَف) and GHURAFAT (غُرَفَات) are stages and positions in paradise. Thus GHURFAH (غُرْفَة) means a well built and lofty room. Allah says in the Holy Quran.

Surah Al-Furqaan (The criterion): سورۃ الفُرْقان 25:75

 أُوْلَـٰٓٮِٕكَ يُجۡزَوۡنَ ٱلۡغُرۡفَةَ بِمَا صَبَرُواْ

Those will be awarded the Chamber for their patience
