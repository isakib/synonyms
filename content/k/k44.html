44. KESE (How?)

Synonyms: KAIFA, ANNA.

44.1. KAIFA (کیف): Is used to ask about the stance and condition of something. In the Quran:

 

Page 725--

 

Surat Al-Ghāshiyah (The Overwhelming) - سورة الغاشية 18:17

أَفَلَا يَنظُرُونَ إِلَى الْإِبِلِ كَيْفَ خُلِقَتْ

Then do they not look at the camels - how they are created?

 

44.2. ANNA(اَنَّى):  Is used to ask the reason regarding a task. “How”. In the Quran:

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة 2:247

أَنَّىٰ يَكُونُ لَهُ الْمُلْكُ عَلَيْنَا

How can he have kingship over us?
