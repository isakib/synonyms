11. KATRA JANA – BACH KAY NIKAL JANA [to clip, to escape safely]

Synonyms: ZAAWARA, QARADA, HAADA

 

11.1ZAAWARA (زَاوَرَ): QAWL-AZ-ZAAWAR (قول الزاور) means a wrong or twisted statement, AZWAR (اَزْوَر) means a person who has an obstructive nature (dictionary Al Manjad). BI’RUN-ZAWRAA’ (بِئْرٌزَوْرَاءَ) means a well that is dug up distortedly, ZAWARA (زَاوَرَ) means to bend or escape by bending.

11.3 QARADA (قَرَضَ): Means to clip and escape by clipping, QARAD-AL-MAAKAN (قَرَضَ المَکَان) means he evaded from the place by moving away. QARADA-FIS-SAEER (قَرَضَ فیِ السَّیْر ) means to bend around while walking. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Kahf (The Cave): سورۃ الکھف 18:17

 وَتَرَى ٱلشَّمۡسَ إِذَا طَلَعَت تَّزَٲوَرُ عَن كَهۡفِهِمۡ ذَاتَ ٱلۡيَمِينِ وَإِذَا غَرَبَت تَّقۡرِضُہُمۡ ذَاتَ ٱلشِّمَالِ

You would have seen the sun, when it rose, veering away from their cave towards the right, and when it sets, moving away from them to the left,

11.4 HAADA (حَادَ): Means to turn away from proper path and evade. To change direction. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Qaf (Qaf): سُوۡرَةُ قٓ 50:19

وَجَآءَتۡ سَكۡرَةُ ٱلۡمَوۡتِ بِٱلۡحَقِّ‌ۖ ذَٲلِكَ مَا كُنتَ مِنۡهُ تَحِيدُ

The daze of death has come in truth: “This is what you tried to evade.”

Synonyms:

11.1 ZAAWARA: To twist and escape

11.2 QARADA: To lop and leave a place and then follow a proper path.

11.3 HAADA: To lop and runaway and not return to a proper path
