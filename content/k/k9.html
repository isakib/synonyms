9 KAB? [When?]

Synonyms: AYYAANA, MATAA

9.1 AYYAANA (اَیَّان): Used when inquiring for time for an event or task. Allah says in the Quran

Surah An-Nazi'at (Those who drag forth): سورۃ النَّازعَات 79:42

 يَسۡـَٔلُونَكَ عَنِ ٱلسَّاعَةِ أَيَّانَ مُرۡسَٮٰهَا

They ask you about the Hour, “When will it take place?”

9.2 MATAA (متٰی): Besides inquiry is also used for challenging.  In the Quran

Surah As-Sajda (The prostration ): سُوۡرَةُ السَّجدَة 32:28

 مَتَىٰ هَـٰذَا ٱلۡفَتۡحُ إِن ڪُنتُمۡ صَـٰدِقِينَ

 “When is this victory, if you are truthful?”

Surah Yaseen (Yaseen): سورۃ ٰیٰس 36:48

 مَتَىٰ هَـٰذَا ٱلۡوَعۡدُ إِن كُنتُمۡ صَـٰدِقِينَ

“When will this promise be, if you are truthful?”
