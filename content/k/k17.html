17. Different Names Of Kaabah

Synonyms: KAABAH, AL-BAEET, BAEET-UL-ANEEQ, HARAM, BAEET-UL-HARAAM, BAEET-UL-MUHARRAM, MASJID-UL-HARAAM.

 

17.1 KAABAH(كَعبَة): It refers to all cubical shaped things. With same correspondence, the KAABAH (کعبہ) is called KAABAH. Whose altitudes and dimensions are equal or almost equal. A square or constructed in resemblance with a square. In the Quran:

Surah Al-Mā'idah (The Table Spread) - سورة المائدة 5:95

يَحْكُمُ بِهِ ذَوَا عَدْلٍ مِّنكُمْ هَدْيًا بَالِغَ الْكَعْبَةِ

As judged by just two men among you as an offering [to Allah ] delivered to the Ka'bah.

 

17.2 AL_BAEET (اَلبَيْت): A specific house. Bait-Ullah Shareef (House of ALLAH). In the Quran:

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة 2:125

وَإِذْ جَعَلْنَا الْبَيْتَ مَثَابَةً لِّلنَّاسِ وَأَمْنًا

And [mention] when We made the House, a place of return for the people and [a place of] security

17.3 BAEET-UL-ATEEQ(بَيتُ الْعَتِيق): Means ancient house. ATEEQ (عَتِيق) refers to such a thing which, together with being old, features nobility  and doesn’t shows any decrease at all. In the Quran:

Surah Al-Ĥaj (The Pilgrimage) - سورة الحج 22:29

وَلْيَطَّوَّفُوا بِالْبَيْتِ الْعَتِيقِ

And perform Tawaf around the ancient House.

17.4 HARAM (حَرَم): Means Strong Prohibition (اَلمنع الشديد)

1. HARAAM(حَرَام) are those things whose use is strongly prohibited by the Sharia and its inverse is HALAAL(حَلآل) which refers to those things, on the use of which there is no restriction.

2. HARAM (حَرَم) and HARAAM (حَرَام) means respectful. One who deserves respect and courtesy. In the Quran:

Surat Al-Qaşaş (The Stories) - سورة القصص 28:57

أَوَلَمْ نُمَكِّن لَّهُمْ حَرَمًا آمِنًا

Have we not established for them a safe sanctuary.

17.5 BAEET-UL-HARAAM(بَيتُ الحَرَام)

17.6 BAEET-UL-MUHARRAM(بَيتُ المُحَرَّم): Both have the same meaning i.e.a respectful and sacred house. In the Quran:

Surat Al-Mā'idah (The Table Spread) - سورة المائدة 5:2

وَلَا آمِّينَ الْبَيْتَ الْحَرَامَ

And nor the people resorting to the sacred house.

Surat 'Ibrāhīm (Abrahim) - سورة ابراهيم 14:37

17.7 MASJID-UL-HARAAM(مَسجِدُ الحَرَام): Honourable and respectful Masjid (Mosque).Bait-Ullah Shareef (House of ALLAH). In the Quran:

Surat Al-'Isrā' (The Night Journey) - سورة الإسراء 17:1

سُبْحَانَ الَّذِي أَسْرَىٰ بِعَبْدِهِ لَيْلًا مِّنَ الْمَسْجِدِ الْحَرَامِ إِلَى الْمَسْجِدِ الْأَقْصَى

Exalted is He who took His Servant by night from al-Masjid al-Haram to al-Masjid al- Aqsa
