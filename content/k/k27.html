27 KON [who, which]

Synonyms: MAN, AYYU

27.1 MAN (مَنْ): For anyone capable of intelligence, this word is used for singular or plural, feminine or masculine everyone. Means ‘who’ For instance “مَنْ رَّبُّكَ” who is your God? (dictionary al Manjad). Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Baqarah (The cow): سورۃ البَقَرَۃ  2:255

 مَن ذَا ٱلَّذِى يَشۡفَعُ عِندَهُ ۥۤ إِلَّا بِإِذۡنِهِۦ‌ۚ

Who is he that can intercede with Him except with His permission?

27.2 AYYU (اَیُّ): When used in questions, this word becomes a pronoun and gives the meaning of ‘who’, it usually is prefixed by something else (which gives a meaning of challenge) like who amongst the following? Or which of you. This word is also used for everyone. Allah says in the Quran
