45. KYUN-KYUN NA (Why – Why not)

Synonyms: LIMA, LAULA, LAUMA.

 

45.1 LIMA(لِمَ): Means why, for what. In the Quran:

Surat Ţāhā (Ta-Ha) - سورة طه 20:125

رَبِّ لِمَ حَشَرْتَنِي أَعْمَىٰ

My Lord, why have you raised me blind

 

45.2 LAULA(لولا): Means why not. HALLA(هَلاَّ) means rebuke and particularization. And a “Verb” should follow it.

In terms of particularization, it is exclusive with the Verb aorist. In the Quran:

Surat An-Naml (The Ant) - سورة النمل 27:46

لَوْلَا تَسْتَغْفِرُونَ اللَّهَ

Why do you not seek forgiveness of Allah

And in terms of rebuke, past tense makes it particular. In the Quran:

Surat An-Nūr (The Light) - سورة النور 24:13

لَّوْلَا جَاءُوا عَلَيْهِ بِأَرْبَعَةِ شُهَدَاءَ ۚ

Why did they [who slandered] not produce for it four witnesses?

 

45.2 LAUMA(لوما): it is a word for particularization. And after using it with aorist, it means the same as “لولا”. In the Quran:

Surat Al-Ĥijr (The Rocky Tract) - سورة الحجر 15:7

لَّوْ مَا تَأْتِينَا بِالْمَلَائِكَةِ

Why do you not bring us the angels?
