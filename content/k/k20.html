20 KAMANA – KAMAI KERNA [to earn, to work]

Synonyms: AMILA, KASABA, IKTASABA, IKTARAFA

20.1 A’MILA (عَمِلَ): Anything that a man does intentionally is his act. And because earning/working is also an act thus it also refers to earning. Allah says in the Quran.

Surah Al-Kahf (The Cave): سورۃ الکھف 18:79

 

أَمَّا ٱلسَّفِينَةُ فَكَانَتۡ لِمَسَـٰكِينَ يَعۡمَلُونَ فِى ٱلۡبَحۡرِ

 As for the boat, it belonged to paupers working at sea.

 

20.2 KASABA (کَسَبَ): For earning, to earn or reap this word is related to AMILA (عَمِلَ). To work in order to get benefit or success. Regardless of whom it is done for and regardless of the nature of profession i.e. it may be good or bad, forbidden or legal. KAASIB (کَاسِبَ) is a laborer or employee. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Maeda (The table spread): سورۃ المَائدہ 5:38

 وَٱلسَّارِقُ وَٱلسَّارِقَةُ فَٱقۡطَعُوٓاْ أَيۡدِيَهُمَا جَزَآءَۢ بِمَا كَسَبَا

As for the thief, whether male or female, cut their hands as a penalty for what they have reaped

20.3 IKTASAB (اِکْتَسَب): AKATASAB (اَکَتَسَب) is anything done keeping in account ones’ personal benefit or gain. Allah says in the Holy Quran

Surah An-Nisa (The women): سورۃ النِّسا 4:32

 لِّلرِّجَالِ نَصِيبٌ۬ مِّمَّا ٱڪۡتَسَبُواْ‌ۖ وَلِلنِّسَآءِ نَصِيبٌ۬ مِّمَّا ٱكۡتَسَبۡنَ‌ۚ

For men is a share of what they have earned, and for women is a share of what they have earned.

20.4 IKTARAFA (اِقْتَرَفَ): KARAFA (قَرَفَ) means to peel of the crust of the wound.  IKTARFA (اِقْتَرَفَ) means to maledict work. To work or earn using illegal ways. The idiom “اَلْاِعِتَرَافُ یَزِیْلُ الْاِقْتِرَاف” means admitting an offense, vanishes an offense. This IKTARAAF (اِقْتَرَفَ) refers to sin and malign activities. Allah says in the Holy Quran.

Surah Al-Ana’am (The Cattle): سورۃ الانعام 6:120

 إِنَّ ٱلَّذِينَ يَكۡسِبُونَ ٱلۡإِثۡمَ سَيُجۡزَوۡنَ بِمَا كَانُواْ يَقۡتَرِفُونَ

those who earn sin will get due recompense for their "earnings."

Summary:

20.1 A’MILA: Common word for an act or work. Also used for earning.

20.2 KASABA: Do some work for benefit regardless of whom it is done for.

20.3 IKTASABA: To do something for personal gain.

20.4 IKTARFA: To do an illegal work. Earn through illegal works.
