8. KANMPNA [to shake, to tremble]

Synonyms: ZALZAL, MARA, RAJJA, RAJAFA

8.1 ZALZAL (زَلْزَلَ): ZALL (زَلَّ) mean slipping of feet, ZALZAL (زَلْزَل) has recurrence in it, which also relates to the meaning, so ZALZAL (زَلْزَل) means to slip around repeatedly. ZALZALA (زَلْزَلَة) means an earthquake or earth tremor. This word ZALZALA (زَلْزَلة) is specific for an earthquake. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Zalzala (The earthquake): سُوۡرَةُ الزّلزَلة 99:1

إِذَا زُلۡزِلَتِ ٱلۡأَرۡضُ زِلۡزَالَهَا

When the earth is shaken with its quake.

 

8.2 RAJJ (رَجّ): To shake something, to jerk. This is the initial state of an earthquake. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Waqiah (The Inevitable): سُوۡرَةُ الواقِعَة 56:4

إِذَا رُجَّتِ ٱلۡأَرۡضُ رَجًّ۬ا

When the earth is shaken with a shock.

 

8.3 RAJAFA (رَجَفَ): Basically means extreme agitation. BAHRUN-RAJJAFUN (بَحْرٌرَجَّافٌ) means a violent sea. WAL-MURJFUUN-FIL-MADINAH  وَٱلۡمُرۡجِفُونَ فِى ٱلۡمَدِينَةِ  33:60 means something causing wave of excitement or unrest. When the word is used in relation with earth, then it means strong tremors or a strong earthquake. In the Quran

Surah Al-Muzammil (The enshrouded one): سورۃ المُزمّل 73:14

يَوۡمَ تَرۡجُفُ ٱلۡأَرۡضُ وَٱلۡجِبَالُ وَكَانَتِ ٱلۡجِبَالُ كَثِيبً۬ا مَّهِيلاً

 On the Day when the earth and the mountains tremble, and the mountains become heaps of sand.

8.4 MAAR (مَارَ): basically refers to movement or speed. AN-NAQATU-TAMOORU-FI-SEERIHA (الناقة تَمُوْرُفْیِ سَیْرِھَا) means when a camel runs speedily raising dust. MAUR means to turn to dust and blown with winds. MAR-ASH-SHAI (مَارالشئ) means trembling and shaking of something due to speed. To lose balance (dictionary Al Manjad). Allah says in the Quran

Surah At-Tur (The Mount): سورۃ الطُّور 52:9

 يَوۡمَ تَمُورُ ٱلسَّمَآءُ مَوۡرً۬ا

On the Day when the heaven sways in agitation.

Summary:

ZALZALA, RAJJ and RAJAF are related to earthquake

8.1 ZALZALA: Common word for earthquake

8.2 RAJJ: Initial state of earthquake

8.3 RAJAF: A violent earthquake

8.4 MARA: Shaking and trembling due to high speed
