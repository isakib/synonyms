36 KHAJOOR [date (fruit)]

Synonyms:  NAKHILAH, NAKHAL, NAKHEEL, LEENAH, RUTAB

36.1 NAKHILAH (نَخْلَة): Means a date palm. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Maryam (Mary): سُوۡرَةُ مَریَم 19:25

 وَهُزِّىٓ إِلَيۡكِ بِجِذۡعِ ٱلنَّخۡلَةِ

And shake the trunk of the palm-tree towards you

NAKHAL (نَخْل) is the plural of NAKILAH (نَخْلَة), date palms and its fruit. Dates or date plum. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Kahf (The Cave): سورۃ الکھف 18:32

جَعَلۡنَا لِأَحَدِهِمَا جَنَّتَيۡنِ مِنۡ أَعۡنَـٰبٍ۬ وَحَفَفۡنَـٰهُمَا بِنَخۡلٍ۬

To one of them We gave two gardens of vine, and We surrounded them with palms-trees,

 NAKHEEL (نَخِیْل) is that orchard or plantation which contains a large number of date palms. Allah says on the Quran

Surah Al-Baqarah (The cow): سورۃ البَقَرَۃ  2:266

 أَيَوَدُّ أَحَدُڪُمۡ أَن تَكُونَ لَهُ ۥ جَنَّةٌ۬ مِّن نَّخِيلٍ۬ وَأَعۡنَابٍ۬

Would anyone of you like to have a garden of palms and vines,

36.2 LEENAH (لِیْنَة): All types of date palms and its fruits except AJWAH (عجوہ) (A very exquisite type of date). Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al Hashr (The exile): سورۃ الحَشر 59:5

مَا قَطَعۡتُم مِّن لِّينَةٍ أَوۡ تَرَڪۡتُمُوهَا قَآٮِٕمَةً عَلَىٰٓ أُصُولِهَا فَبِإِذۡنِ ٱللَّهِ

Whether you cut down a tree, or leave it standing on its trunk, it is by God’s will.

36.3 RUTAB (رُطَب): A fresh date. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Maryam (Mary): سُوۡرَةُ مَریَم 19:25

تُسَـٰقِطۡ عَلَيۡكِ رُطَبً۬ا جَنِيًّ۬ا

and it will drop ripe dates by you.”
