20 MAZAK URANA

[To make fun of]

Synonyms: ISTAHZA’A(استهزأ), SAKHARA(سخر), FANNAD(فند)

20.1 istahza’a(استهزأ): alhaz(الهز) meaning to make fun of someone internally and istahza(استهزأ) meaning to make fun of someone on such an act which he did not even commit. It is stated in the Quran:

________________

Surat Al-Baqarah(The Cow) - سُوۡرَةُ البَقَرَة

2:14

وَإِذَا خَلَوۡاْ إِلَىٰ شَيَـٰطِينِهِمۡ قَالُوٓاْ إِنَّا مَعَكُمۡ إِنَّمَا نَحۡنُ مُسۡتَہۡزِءُونَ

And when they fall in with those who believe, they say: We believe; but when they go apart to their devils they declare: Lo! we are with you; verily we did but mock.

________________

20.2 sakhara (سخر): maskhar(مسخر) includes the aspect of ridicule and pity. Sakhara(سخر) meaning to be against someone and sakkhara(سخر) meaning to ridicule someone, to suppress someone. It also means to describe one’s faults and make fun of him in such a way so as to mock and insult. Allah states that:

________________

Surat Al-Hujraat (The Chambers) - سُوۡرَةُ الحُجرَات

49:11

يَـٰٓأَيُّہَا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُواْ لَا يَسۡخَرۡ قَوۡمٌ۬ مِّن قَوۡمٍ عَسَىٰٓ أَن يَكُونُواْ خَيۡرً۬ا مِّنۡہُمۡ

O ye who believe! Let not a folk deride a folk who may be better than they (are), not let women (deride) women who may be better than they are; neither defame one another, nor insult one another by nicknames. Bad is the name of lewdness after faith. And whoso turneth not in repentance, such are evil-doers.

________________

20.3 fannada (فند): fanad(فند) meaning weakness in opinion and fannada(فند) means to call someone as having weak opinion and of low intelligence. Fanida(فند) means the decline of intelligence because of old age and to talk aimlessly. Fannada(فند) means to rebuke an old man on his talk, to hold him responsible, to reprimand. It is stated in the Quran:

________________

Surat Yusuf - سُوۡرَةُ یُوسُف

12:94

قَالَ أَبُوهُمۡ إِنِّى لَأَجِدُ رِيحَ يُوسُفَ‌ۖ لَوۡلَآ أَن تُفَنِّدُونِ

When the caravan departed their father had said: Truly I am conscious of the breath of Joseph, though ye call me dotard.

________________

Summary:

1.ISTAHZA(استهزأ): to make fun of something by considering it to be against common sense.

2. SAKHARA(سخر): to make fun of someone and ridicule someone by describing his faults.

3. FANNADA(فند): to make fun of an old man’s talks.

(for ‘muraad pana’, see ‘kamyaab hona’)
