1. MAARNA [to hit, to strike]

Synonyms: DARABA, WAKAZA, SAKK, DAA’A’, DAMAGHA, RAJAMA, WAQAZA, NATAHA, JALADA

1.1 DARABA (ضَرَبَ): This word is usually if followed by ‘ب’. DARABA (ضَرَبَ) means to hit or strike someone with hand or something and when it is followed by ‘ب’ then it refers the thing that is used for hitting; the tool or instrument used for hitting. This word is very commonly used to refer to striking. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Baqarah (The cow): سورۃ البَقَرَۃ  2:60

 فَقُلۡنَا ٱضۡرِب بِّعَصَاكَ ٱلۡحَجَرَ‌ۖ

 “Strike the rock with your staff.”

 

1.2 WAKAZA (وَکَزَ): To punch someone; to strike someone’s chest with palm or fist. In the Quran

Surah Al Qasas (The stories): سورۃ القَصَص 28:15

فَوَكَزَهُ مُوسَىٰ فَقَضَىٰ عَلَيْهِ ۖ

So Musa (Moses) struck him with his fist and killed him.

 

1.3 SAKKA (صَكَّ): Means collision of two things with force and power causing sound. SAKK-AL-BAABA (صَكَّ الْبَابَ) means colliding of doors; also to hit on face, to slap or beat on face. In the Quran

Surah Adh-Dhariyaat (The Winnowing Winds): سورة الذاريات 51:29

 فَصَكَّتْ وَجْهَهَا وَقَالَتْ عَجُوزٌ عَقِيمٌ

Then she beat her face, and said, "An old sterile woman!"

 

1.4 DAA’A’ (دَعَّ): To push or repulse, to get someone out harshly. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Maun (The Small Kindnesses): سورة الماعون 107:2

فَذَٰلِكَ الَّذِي يَدُعُّ الْيَتِيمَ

That is he who repulses the orphan (harshly),

 

1.5 DAMAGHA (دَمَغَ): Means to hit or injure someone on head or brains. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Anbiya (the prophets): سورۃ الانَبیاء 21:18

بَلْ نَقْذِفُ بِالْحَقِّ عَلَى الْبَاطِلِ فَيَدْمَغُهُ

Nay! We cast the truth against the falsehood, so that it breaks its head,

 

1.6 RAJAMA (رَجَمَ): 1. To pelt stones at someone, or hit with stones from a distance. 2. To kill someone by hitting someone from a distance; to stone to death. Used in both the meanings. Both semantically and materially this word is used.   رَجۡمَۢا بِٱلۡغَيۡبِ‌ۖ (guessing at the unknown) (18:23) mean to throw stones without aiming and to talk on base of a guess or supposition. In the Quran

Surah Hud (Hud): سورۃ ھود 11:91

وَلَوۡلَا رَهۡطُكَ لَرَجَمۡنَـٰكَ‌ۖ

 And if not for your family, we would have stoned you [to death];

1.7 WAQAZA (وَقَذَ): Means to strike someone harshly with stone or stick that kills.  WAQZA (وَقْذَ) means a severe blow. WAQAZA (وَقَذَ) means to hit someone fatally (dictionary al Manjad).

1.8 NATAHA (نَطَحَ): NATAH-AS-SAUR (نَطَحَ الثور) means a bull hitting with its horns. TANATA-HAL-KABSHAAN (تَنَاطَحَ الْکَبْشَان) means two sheeps banging against one another. NATEEH (نَطِیْح) means an animal killed by getting a blow from horns; or hit by horns (dictionary al Manjad). Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Maeda (The table spread): سورۃ المَائدہ 5:3

الْمُنْخَنِقَةُ وَالْمَوْقُوذَةُ وَالْمُتَرَدِّيَةُ وَالنَّطِيحَةُ

 and [those animals] killed by strangling or by a violent blow or by a head-long fall or by the goring of horns,

1.9 JALADA (جَلَدَ): JILD (جِلْد) (Plural JULOOD (جُلُوْد)) means skin on body, hide and JALADA (جَلَدَ) means to hit on skin. JALDATAN (جَلْدَۃً) means a whip whose effect remains till skin and causes no wound. To hit with a lash.

Surah Al-Noor (The light): سورۃ النور 24:2

الزَّانِيَةُ وَالزَّانِي فَاجْلِدُوا كُلَّ وَاحِدٍ مِّنْهُمَا مِائَةَ جَلْدَةٍ ۖ

The [unmarried] woman or [unmarried] man found guilty of sexual intercourse - lash each one of them with a hundred lashes,

Summary:

1.1 DARABA: Commonly used. To hit.

1.2 WAKAZA: To punch.

1.3 SAKK: To hit or beat face with hand.

1.4 DAA’A’: To push or shove.

1.5 DAMAGHA: To hit and injure head.

1.6 RAJAMA: To stone from distance, stone to death.

1.7 WAQAZA: To hit harshly with a stick or stone.

1.8 NATAHA: Hitting with horns by a four legged animal.

1.9 JALADA: To lash.
