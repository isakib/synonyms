11 MACHILI [Fish]

Synonyms: HOOT, NOON

11.1 HOOT (حُوْت): Gender noun. Refers to all kind of fish (Plural HEETAN (حِیْتَان))  حِيتَانُهُمْ (7:163). A big fish is called SAMAK (سمك) but this word is not used in Quran. The largest of fish i.e. whale is called NOON (نون). In case of Jonah (Hazrat Yunus (A.S)) both the words HOOT (حوت) and NOON (نون). For instance in Quran

Surah Al-Qalam (The Pen):سورۃ القلم  68:48

وَلَا تَكُن كَصَاحِبِ الْحُوتِ إِذْ نَادَىٰ وَهُوَ مَكْظُومٌ

 do not be like the Fellow of the Fish who cried out in despair.

In another place in Holy Quran

Surah Al-Anbiya (the prophets): سورۃ الانَبیاء 21:87

وَذَا النُّونِ إِذ ذَّهَبَ مُغَاضِبًا

And [mention] the man of the fish, when he went off in anger

Both the ayah refer to same meaning that he (Jonah (Hazrat Yunus (A.S))) went into the belly of a fish.
