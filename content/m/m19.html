And unto each We have appointed heirs of that which parents and near kindred leave; and as for those with whom your right hands have made a covenant, give them their due. Lo! Allah is ever Witness over all things.

________________

The custom of inheritance was common in Arab i.e. people would make any of their friends heirs apart from their relatives. Then this heir would be entitled to his inheritance and superior to the relatives. At first, Islam kept this tradition. This was common among the ansaar and mahajreen. After the conquest of Makkah, when Allah specified shares of relatives in the inheritance, then this custom was abolished. Thus the words wali(ولي) and mawali(موالى) include much more wholeness in various aspects as compared to nasir(ناصر) and naseer(نصير). It also means the one who favors, helps and is a friend. Wali(ولي) means someone who helps through love and affection and not just to show other people.

Summary:

1.NASIR AND NASEER(ناصر, نصير): helper in times of oppression and misery.

2.HAWARI(حواري): group of companions of prophets.

3.WALI AND MAULA(ولي,  موالى): companion and close friend and helper.

4.SHAHEED(شهيد): to arrive at the right time and help someone by testifying in their favor.

5.ZAHEER(ظهير): such a helper on which one can rely.

6.WAZEER(وزير): to lend a hand in loads of work.

7. A’DUD(عضد): helper who proves himself useful.
