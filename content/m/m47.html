47 MAHEENAY [months]

Synonyms: SHAHOOR, ASHHAR

47.1 SHAHOOR (شُھُوْر): Means total months. Maximum amount of months. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al Tawbah (The repentance): سورۃ التوبھ 9:36

إِنَّ عِدَّةَ الشُّهُورِ عِندَ اللَّهِ اثْنَا عَشَرَ شَهْرًا فِي كِتَابِ اللَّهِ يَوْمَ خَلَقَ السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضَ

The number of months, according to God, is twelve months—in the decree of God—since the Day He created the heavens and the earth, 

47.2 ASHURU (اَشْھُرُ): Means months in a given time period. But its total sum would always be less then twelve. When a simple count is stated then the word ASHAR (اشھر) is used. ASHUR (اَشْھُر) is the small sum which can be from 3 to 10. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Baqarah (The cow): سورۃ البَقَرَۃ  2:226

لِّلَّذِينَ يُؤْلُونَ مِن نِّسَائِهِمْ تَرَبُّصُ أَرْبَعَةِ أَشْهُرٍ

Those who vow abstinence from their wives must wait for four months.
