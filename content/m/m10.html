10 MAJLIS [Gathering, council]

Synonyms: MAJLIS, NAADI

10.1 MAJLIS (مجلس): (Plural MAJALIS (مجالس)) Any place where people gather for some purpose. A gathering for some event at home. A famous word. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Mujadila (The Pleading Women) سُوۡرَةُ المجَادلة 58:11

إِذَا قِيلَ لَكُمْ تَفَسَّحُوا فِي الْمَجَالِسِ فَافْسَحُوا

When you are told to make room in your gatherings, make room;

10.2 NAADI (نَادِی): A gathering of people in which there is chatting and storytelling. Club, feast, places for recreation, or obscenity or lewdness. ANNADWAH (الندوۃ) means welfare. Gathering on National or religious matters. In the Quran

Surah Al-Ankaboot  (The Spider): سُوۡرَةُ العَنکبوت 29:29

 

وَتَأْتُونَ فِي نَادِيكُمُ الْمُنكَرَ

commit lewdness in your gatherings

Synonyms:

10.1 MAJLIS: Gathering of family and friends

10.2 NAADI: Specific places where obscene things happen during gatherings i.e. clubs feasts, recreational places.
