37 MAKKHI [fly or bee]

Synonyms: DUBAB, NAHL

37.1 DUBAB (ذُبَاب): Means a small fly, DABB ANHU (ذَبَّ عَنْهُ) means to remove flies from someone. To disperse flies or exempt from blame.  MADIBBAH (مَذِبَّة) means a fly whisk or a fan made from feather. (Plural DUBBUN (ذُبٌّ), ADIBBAH (اَذِبَّة)). Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Hajj  (The Pilgrimage): سُوۡرَةُ الحَجّ 22:73

وَإِن يَسْلُبْهُمُ الذُّبَابُ شَيْئًا لَّا يَسْتَنقِذُوهُ مِنْهُ

 And if the fly steals anything from them, they cannot recover it from it.

37.2 NAHL (نَحْل): Means a honey bee. For both male and female honey bee. (Singular NAHLAH (نَحْلَةٌ)) Allah says in the Quran

Surah An-Nahl (The Bee): سورۃ النّحل16:68

وَأَوْحَىٰ رَبُّكَ إِلَى النَّحْلِ أَنِ اتَّخِذِي مِنَ الْجِبَالِ بُيُوتًا وَمِنَ الشَّجَرِ وَمِمَّا يَعْرِشُونَ

And your Lord inspired to the bee, "Take for yourself among the mountains, houses,
