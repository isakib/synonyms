17 MUDDAT

[Time period]

Synonyms: MUDDAT(مدت), AMAD(امد), IDDAT(عدت), UMMAH(امه), MALIYYAH(مليا), MAHL(مهل), UMUR(عمر)

(For ajjal(اجل), heen(حين) and meeqaat(ميقات), see waqt(وقت))

Summary:

1.MUDDAT(مدت): the length of time, its use is common.

2. AMADA(امد): long time period and its limit or distance.

3. IDDAT(عدت): that time which must be accounted for by a divorced woman, it is according to Shariah.

4. UMMAH(امة): the time which has passed.

5. MALLI(مليا): long time period.

6. MUHLAT(مهل): to increase time gradually and give leniency.

7. UMUR(عمر): the time from birth of a living being to the present.
