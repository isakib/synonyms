Allah says in the Quran.

Surah Al-Baqarah (The cow): سورۃ البَقَرَۃ  2:236

عَلَى الْمُوسِعِ قَدَرُهُ وَعَلَى الْمُقْتِرِ قَدَرُهُ

But bestow on them (a suitable gift), the rich according to his means, and the poor according to his means,

Summary:

35.1: WUJD: A famous word for current items.

35.2: SAA’AH: Comfort and to signify its level.

35.3 QADAR: Necessitousness and to signify its level.
