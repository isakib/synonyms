18 MADAD DENA, KERNA AUR CHAHNA

[To help, be helped and ask for help]

Synonyms: AA’ANA(اعان), ISTA’AN(استعان), NASARA(نصر), ISTANSAR(استنصر), AYYAD(أيد), UZZAR(عزر), AZZAZ(عزز), ZAAHARA(ظاهر), RAFADA(رفد), RADA(ردأ), AMADDA(أمد)

18.1 aa’ana(اعان): awn(عون) meaning helper and aa’an(اعان) meaning to help someone, to assist and ta’awun(تعاون) meaning to help each other and assist each other. This word is common. It is stated in the Quran:

18:95

He said: That wherein my Lord hath established me is better (than your tribute). Do but help me with strength (of men), I will set between you and them a bank.

Summary:

1.AA’AN(اعان):to help someone, any kind of help, it is common.

2. NASAR(نصر): to help someone out of his misery.

3. AYYAD(أيد): to help someone and make him gain strength.

4. AZZAR(عزر): to help someone out of act of reverence.

5. AZZAZ(عزز): to help someone to such an extent such that his weakness is overcome.

6. ZAAHARA(ظاهر): to be on someone’s back.

7. RAFADA(رفد): to help some poor through charity and aid and continue to help them.

8. RADA(ردأ): to provide help all the time.

9. AMADDA(أمد): to reinforce.
