9 MAJAMA’T KERNA [coition]

Synonyms: BAASHAR, QADAA, WATRAN, TAGHASHAA, RAFATHA, MASSA, LAMASA, TAMATHA, QURB

All the words that are used in Quran for coition are metaphors. Not a single word is used that literary means coition due to modesty.

9.1 BAASHAR (بَاشَر): BASHARA (بَشَر) means to peel of skin. BASHARAH (بَشَرَۃ) means upper side of skin, BAASHARA (بَاشَر) means touching of a body’s skin to another body. BASHAR-AL-MARAH (باشر المرۃ) means coition (dictionary al Manjad). Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Baqarah (The cow): سورۃ البَقَرَۃ  2:187

 

تُبَاشِرُوهُنَّ وَأَنتُمْ عَاكِفُونَ فِي الْمَسَاجِدِ

And have no contact with them while you keep to the mosques;

9.2 QADAA (قَضٰی) WATARAN (وَطَرًا): WATR (وطر) means a necessity, or a need and QADAA WATRAN (قَضٰی وَطَرًا) means to fulfill a need. In the Quran

Surah Al-Ahzaab (The clans): سورۃ الأحزَاب 33:37

 

فَلَمَّا قَضَىٰ زَيْدٌ مِّنْهَا وَطَرًا

 So when Zaid had accomplished his desire from her (i.e. divorced her)

9.3 TAGHASHAA (تَغَشّیٰ): One thing covering another. In the Quran

Surah Al-Araf (The heights): سورۃ الأعرَاف 7:189

 

فَلَمَّا تَغَشَّاهَا حَمَلَتْ حَمْلًا خَفِيفًا

 Then, when he has covered her, she conceives a light load,

9.4 RAFATHA (رَفَثَ): Any talk that causes absement. To unveil. To talk about coition and things related to it. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Baqarah (The cow): سورۃ البَقَرَۃ  2:187

أُحِلَّ لَكُمْ لَيْلَةَ الصِّيَامِ الرَّفَثُ إِلَىٰ نِسَائِكُمْ

It has been made permissible for you the night preceding fasting to go to your wives [for sexual relations]

9.5 MASSA (مَسَّ): To touch something. Something coming in contact with body. In the Quran

Surah Ale- Imran (The family of Imran): سورۃ آل عِمْرن 3:47

قَالَتْ رَبِّ أَنَّىٰ يَكُونُ لِي وَلَدٌ وَلَمْ يَمْسَسْنِي بَشَرٌ

She said, “My Lord, how can I have a child, when no man has touched me?”

9.6 LAAMASA (لَامَسَ): LAMASA (لَمَسَ) To examine something using fingers. To find something using finger or any other part of body. In the Quran

Surah An-Nisa (The women): سورۃ النِّسا 4:43

 

أَوْ لَامَسْتُمُ النِّسَاءَ

 or you have had contact with women,

9.7 TAMATHA (طَمَثَ): TAMATHA (طَمَثَ) means menstrual blood. TAMTHA YATMITHU (طَمَثَ یَطْمِثُ) means a woman during her menstruation period (dictionary al Manjad) and also a man deflowering a woman. Thus this word also refers to having sex for the first time. In the Quran

Surah Ar-Rahman (The Beneficent): سُوۡرَةُ الرَّحمٰن 55:74

لَمْ يَطْمِثْهُنَّ إِنسٌ قَبْلَهُمْ وَلَا جَانٌّ

Whom neither humankind nor the jinn (race) have deflowered before them.

9.8 QARUB (قَرُبَ): Means to be intimate, to approach someone. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Baqarah (The cow): سورۃ البَقَرَۃ  2:222

وَلَا تَقْرَبُوهُنَّ حَتَّىٰ يَطْهُرْنَ

 And do not approach them until they have become pure.
