40 MUNH PHAIRNA [to turn away]

Synonyms: AE’RADA (A’N), TAWALLA (A’N), SADAFA

40.1 AE’RADA (A’N) ((عن) اَعْرَضَ): A’RADA (عَرَضَ) means to present someone with something. A’RD (عَرْض) means a supplication; A request (dictionary al Manjad). AE’RADA-AN ((عن) اَعْرَضَ) means to get away from something, to turn away from. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Ta-Ha (Ta-ha): سورۃ طٰھٰ  20:124

وَمَنْ أَعْرَضَ عَن ذِكْرِي فَإِنَّ لَهُ مَعِيشَةً ضَنكًا

But whoever turns away from My Reminder, for him is a confined life.

40.2 TAWALLA (A’N) ((عن) تَوَلّٰی): TAWALLA (تَوَلّٰی) means to befriend, TAWALLA-AN ((عن) تَوَلّٰی) means to renounce from friendship, turn away, avert or reject (dictionary al Manjad). Allah says in the Quran

Surah As-Saaffat (The Aligners): سُوۡرَةُ الصَّافات37:178

وَتَوَلَّ عَنْهُمْ حَتَّىٰ حِينٍ

So turn (O Muhammad SAW) away from them for a while,

Page 827

 

40.3 SADAFA (صَدَفَ): Means AE’RADA-ANHU-WASADD ( اَعْرَضَ عَنْهُ وصَدّ) to refuse vehemently, to turn down vehemently. SADAF (صَدَف) means a shell, edge of a mountain,  the bend in the camels’ leg. All of these three have toughness and curvature in them. Sometimes it is followed by A’N (عن) but it also gives its meaning without it. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Ana’am (The Cattle): سورۃ الانعام 6:157

سَنَجْزِي الَّذِينَ يَصْدِفُونَ عَنْ آيَاتِنَا سُوءَ الْعَذَابِ بِمَا كَانُوا يَصْدِفُونَ

We will repay those who turn away from Our messages with the worst kind of punishment, because of their turning away.

In another place in the Quran

Surah Al-Ana’am (The Cattle): سورۃ الانعام 6:46

انظُرْ كَيْفَ نُصَرِّفُ الْآيَاتِ ثُمَّ هُمْ يَصْدِفُونَ

 Note how We explain the revelations in various ways, yet they still turn away.

Summary:

40.1 AE’RADA: Turn away or get away from front.

40.2 TAWALLA: To renounce from friendship.

40.3 SADAFA: To turn away vehemently.
