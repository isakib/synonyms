14 MEHNAT MUSHAQQAT KERNA, UTHAANA

[To work hard]

Synonyms: AMILA(عمل), JAHADA(جهد), NASABA(نصب), KALLAFA(كلف), KADAHA(كدح), KURHUN(كره), SHIQQA(شق), SHUQQA(شقه)

14.1 amila(عمل): amal(عمل) is all those acts which any living being does with its own will and consent although the act may be right or wrong. And the word amila(عمل) has been used to depict some act done by hard work and labor or in other words, it is also used to depict earning of living. Allah states that:

Summary:

1.AMILA(عمل): to work hard and do labor, common word with common use.

2. JAHADA(جهد): to utilize all resources in getting something done.

3. NASABA(نصب): that hard work which tires you or is the reason for tiring.

4.KALLAFA(كلف): hard work according to one’s capacity and strength.

5. KADAHA(كدح): to keep on working even while enduring pain.

6. KURAH(كره): forced labor.

7.SHIQQ(شق): the agony of hard work.

8. SHUQQA(شقه): that destination which is achieved through hard work.
