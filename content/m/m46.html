46 MEHMAN [guests]

Synonyms: DAIEF, WAFD

46.1 DAIEF (ضَیْف): Means a guest. A famous word. (Plural DAEYAAF (ضیاف), DAEYUF (ضیوف)), DAIEF (ضَیْف) word is used both as a singular and plural. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Hud (Hud): سورۃ ھود 11:78

فَاتَّقُوا اللَّهَ وَلَا تُخْزُونِ فِي ضَيْفِي ۖ

 So fear God, and do not embarrass me before my guests.

46.2 WAFD (وَفد): WAFADA (وَفَدَ) means to go to the king or ruler as a representative of people. WAFD (وَفد) means a royal guest. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Maryam (Mary): سُوۡرَةُ مَریَم 19:85

يَوْمَ نَحْشُرُ الْمُتَّقِينَ إِلَى الرَّحْمَٰنِ وَفْدًا

On the Day when We will gather the righteous to the Most Merciful, as guests.
