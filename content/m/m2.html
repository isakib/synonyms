2 MAAR DALNA MARNA [to kill, to die]

Synonyms: MAATA, AMAATA, QATAL, QATTALA, SALABA, SALLABA, HALAKA, AHLAKA, ZABBAHA, RAJAMA, INKHANAQ, TAWAFA, SHAHADAT

2.1 MAATA (مَاتَ): MAUT (مَوْت) (Opposite HAYYAT (حَیَات)) means a living being losing its soul and power, departing of soul from body. To die, regardless the condition which caused death. In the Quran

Surah Al-Baqarah (The cow): سورۃ البَقَرَۃ  2:161

إِنَّ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا وَمَاتُوا وَهُمْ كُفَّارٌ

Those who reject Faith, and die rejecting,-

 

 AMAATA (اَمَاتَ) means to kill someone, to put someone to death. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Baqarah (The cow): سورۃ البَقَرَۃ  2:28

ثُمَّ يُمِيتُكُمْ ثُمَّ يُحْيِيكُمْ ثُمَّ إِلَيْهِ تُرْجَعُونَ

And He gave you life; then will He cause you to die, and will again bring you to life; and again to Him will ye return.

2.2 QATAL (قَتَلَ): A living being overpowering other living being and putting it to death. According to Ibn Al Faris to kill someone disgracefully. In the Quran

Surah Al-Kahf (The Cave): سورۃ الکھف 18:74

قَالَ أَقَتَلْتَ نَفْسًا زَكِيَّةً بِغَيْرِ نَفْسٍ

He said, “Did you kill a pure soul, who killed no one?

 QATTALA (قَتَّلَ) means to kill someone by torturing or by putting in agony. In the Quran

Surah Al-Araf (The heights): سورۃ الأعرَاف 7:127

قَالَ سَنُقَتِّلُ أَبْنَاءَهُمْ وَنَسْتَحْيِي نِسَاءَهُمْ

“We will kill their sons, and spare their women.

 

2.3 HALAKA (ھَلَكَ): Means to perish (dictionary al Manjad). Used for both living and non living things. For living things it means to die helplessly. According to Ibn-Al Faris ‘یَدُلُّ عَلٰی گرٍوَّ سَقُوْطٍ ’ means to die a horrific death. To die in an accident. In the Quran

Surah An-Nisa (The women): سورۃ النِّسا 4:176

إِنِ امْرُؤٌ هَلَكَ لَيْسَ لَهُ وَلَدٌ

If a man dies, and leaves no children,

 

Another place in the Quran

Surah Al Qasas (The stories): سورۃ القَصَص 28:88

كُلُّ شَيْءٍ هَالِكٌ إِلَّا وَجْهَهُ

All things perish, except His presence.

 

 AHLAKA (اَھْلَكَ) means to kill someone horrifically, to slay or murder someone. In the Quran

Surah Al-Mulk (The Sovereignty ): سُوۡرَةُ المُلک67:28

قُلْ أَرَأَيْتُمْ إِنْ أَهْلَكَنِيَ اللَّهُ وَمَن مَّعِيَ

Say, [O Muhammad], "Have you considered: whether Allah should cause my death and those with me

 

2.4 SALABA (صَلَبَ): To hang someone, to execute someone on gallows or to crucify. SALEEB (صلیب) is that piece with which a criminal is executed on gallows. Allah says in the Quran

Surah An-Nisa (The women): سورۃ النِّسا 4:157

وَمَا قَتَلُوهُ وَمَا صَلَبُوهُ وَلَٰكِن شُبِّهَ لَهُمْ

In fact, they did not kill him, nor did they crucify him, but it appeared to them as if they did

 

 And SALLABA (صَلَّبَ) means torturing a criminal while hanging him upside down or to torture him more while crucifying him. In the Quran

Surah Ta-Ha (Ta-ha): سورۃ طٰھٰ  20:71

فَلَأُقَطِّعَنَّ أَيْدِيَكُمْ وَأَرْجُلَكُم مِّنْ خِلَافٍ وَلَأُصَلِّبَنَّكُمْ فِي جُذُوعِ النَّخْلِ

 I will cut off your hands and your feet on alternate sides, and I will crucify you on the trunks of the palm-trees.

 

2.5 ZABBAHA (ذَبَّحَ): ZABAHA (ذَبَحَ) to slaughter an animal according to shariah law by bleeding, ZABBAHA (ذَبَّحَ) means to painfully slaughter according to shariah law or overdo the slaughter (dictionary al Manjad). In this manner ZABBAHA (ذَبَّحَ) and QATTALA (قَتَّلَ) become synonyms. In the Quran

Surah Al Qasas (The stories): سورۃ القَصَص 28:4

يُذَبِّحُ أَبْنَاءَهُمْ وَيَسْتَحْيِي نِسَاءَهُمْ

He persecuted a group of them, slaughtering their sons, while sparing their daughters.

 

2.6 RAJAMA (رَجَمَ): To throw a stone from a distance or kill by stoning, RAJAM (رجم) is one limit of Hudood Allah in which a married adulteress or adulterer is stoned to death. To stone to death. In the Quran

Surah Maryam (Mary): سُوۡرَةُ مَریَم 19:46

لَئِن لَّمْ تَنتَهِ لَأَرْجُمَنَّكَ

 If you do not desist, I will stone you.

 

2.7 INKHANAQ (اِنْخَنَقَ): KHANAQA (خَنَقَ) means to strangle, KHINAAQ (خِنَاق) is that rope which is used to strangle, KHUNNAQ (خُنَّاق) is that disease that causes strangulation i.e. Diphtheria and it gets difficult to breathe (dictionary al Manjad). KHANAQA (خَنَقَ) means to strangulate to death. MUNKHNIQ (مُنْخَنِقُ) is that animal which dies due to strangulation. In the Quran

Surah Al-Maeda (The table spread): سورۃ المَائدہ 5:3

وَالْمُنْخَنِقَةُ وَالْمَوْقُوذَةُ

That which has been killed by strangling, or by a violent blow

2.8 TAWAFFA (تَوَفّٰی): WAFAA (وَفٰی) refers to the conclusion or end of something. And TAWAFFA (تَوَفّٰی) means soul departing the body after completion of the time of life, to take away; this task is of Allah and is done by Angels so in this word the death cannot be related to any other animal or human. In the Quran

Surah Yusuf (Joseph): سورۃ یُوسُف 12:101

تَوَفَّنِي مُسْلِمًا وَأَلْحِقْنِي بِالصَّالِحِينَ

 Take me up to You as a Muslim (One who submits to you) and join me with the righteous."

2.9 SHAHADAT (شَھَادَت): SHAHIDA (شَھِدَ) means to give a testimony. This testimony can be of eye or of heart. SHAHID (شَاھِدْ) and SHAHEED (شَھَیْد) both refer to a witness or testimony. The word SHAHEED (شَھَیْد) is also used for a person who lays his life in path of Allah to uphold religion and virtue; martyr. And so SHAHADAT (شھادت) means to lay one’s life in path of Allah. Lay one’s life in path of Allah to provide testimony for being a devout believer of Day of Judgment and reckoning. Allah says in the Quran

Surah An-Nisa (The women): سورۃ النِّسا 4:69

فَأُولَٰئِكَ مَعَ الَّذِينَ أَنْعَمَ اللَّهُ عَلَيْهِم مِّنَ النَّبِيِّينَ وَالصِّدِّيقِينَ وَالشُّهَدَاءِ وَالصَّالِحِينَ

Those will be with the ones upon whom Allah has bestowed favor of the prophets, the steadfast affirmers of truth, the martyrs and the righteous. And excellent are those as companions.

Summary:

2.1 MAATA: Departing of soul from body. Common word.

2.2 QATAL: A living being overcoming other and killing it, QATTALA to kill horrifically,

2.3 HALAKA: To perish, to die helplessly. Also used for non living.

2.4 SALABA: To execute on gallows, SALLABA: to execute on gallows after torturing,

2.5 ZABBAHA: To kill by torturing in intervals

2.6 RAJAMA: Stone to death,

2.7 INKHANAQ: Strangulating to death,

2.8 TAWAFA: To complete the duration of life, departing of soul from body, this can only be done by Allah and Angels

2.9 SHAHADAT: To sacrifice one’s life in path of Allah
