Nasr, To help ”madad karna”,P.797

{

Synonyms: AA3ANA,NASARA,ISTANSARA,AYYAD,

3ZZARA,3ZZAZA,ZAHARA,RAFADA,

RADAAA,AAMADDA,

1 AA3ANA(اَعَان): from (عون) meaning helper and AA3ANA meaning

to assist .

TA3AWANA(تَعَاوَنَ)meaning to help each other and to assist

each other.This is a general word

 Quran example:Surat Al-Kahf (The Cave)[18:95]

فَأَعِينُونِي بِقُوَّةٍ أَجْعَلْ بَيْنَكُمْ وَبَيْنَهُمْ رَدْمًا

but assist me with strength; I will make between you and them a dam.

and ISTA3AN(اِسْتَعَان) meaning to ask help and support from someone

Quran example:Surat Al-Fātiĥah (The Opener)[1:5]إِيَّاكَ نَعْبُدُ وَإِيَّاكَ نَسْتَعِينُ

It is You we worship and You we ask for help.

2 NASARA( نَصَرَ) ISTANSARA(اِسْتَنْصَرَ): NASARA meaning to remove pain or cruelty or injustice from someone by helping them and this is more peculiar/specific than 3AAANAT( اعَانْت)

Quran example:Surat 'Āli `Imrān (Family of Imran)[3:123]

وَلَقَدْ نَصَرَكُمُ اللَّهُ بِبَدْرٍ وَأَنتُمْ أَذِلَّةٌ ۖ

And already had Allah given you victory at [the battle of] Badr while you were few in number

and ISTANSARA meaning to ask someone to remove pain or cruelty or injustice from oneself

Quran example:Surat Al-'Anfāl (The Spoils of War)[8:72]

وَإِنِ اسْتَنصَرُوكُمْ فِي الدِّينِ فَعَلَيْكُمُ النَّصْرُ

And if they seek help of you for the religion, then you must help,

3 AYYAD(اَيَّدَ): ALAYAD(الأيد) means strong force and AYYAD means to help someone by reinforcement promptly. to support

Quran example:Surat At-Tawbah (The Repentance)[9:40]

فَأَنزَلَ اللَّهُ سَكِينَتَهُ عَلَيْهِ وَأَيَّدَهُ بِجُنُودٍ لَّمْ تَرَوْهَا

And Allah sent down his tranquillity upon him and supported him with angels you did not see

4 3ZZARA(عَزَّرَ): meaning to help someone out of their respect

Quran example:Surat Al-Fatĥ (The Victory)[48:9]

لِّتُؤْمِنُوا بِاللَّهِ وَرَسُولِهِ وَتُعَزِّرُوهُ وَتُوَقِّرُوهُ

That you [people] may believe in Allah and His Messenger and honor him and respect the Prophet

5 3ZZAZA(عَزَّزّ):meaning to help some one to such an extent to remove weakness

Quran example:Surat Yā-Sīn (Ya Sin)[36:14]

إِذْ أَرْسَلْنَا إِلَيْهِمُ اثْنَيْنِ فَكَذَّبُوهُمَا فَعَزَّزْنَا بِثَالِثٍ

When We sent to them two but they denied them, so We strengthened them with a third,

6 ZAHARA(ظَََا هَرَ):ZAHAR(ظهر) implies

1) Obvious,Prominent,Visible 2) back

so ZAHARA means to give such strength

upon which something can be done.backing

Quran example:Surat Al-'Aĥzāb (The Combined Forces)[33:26]

وَأَنزَلَ الَّذِينَ ظَاهَرُوهُم مِّنْ أَهْلِ الْكِتَابِ مِن صَيَاصِيهِمْ

And He brought down those who supported them among the People of the Scripture from their fortresses

7 RAFADA(رَفَدَ):meaning to help poor or needy by alms and

charity

Quran example:Surat Hūd (Hud)[11:99]

وَأُتْبِعُوا فِي هَٰذِهِ لَعْنَةً وَيَوْمَ الْقِيَامَةِ ۚ بِئْسَ الرِّفْدُ الْمَرْفُودُ

And they were followed in this [world] with a curse and on the Day of Resurrection. And wretched is the gift which is given.

8 RADAAA( رَدَأ): this ALHAIT( الحََائط)means to support a wall,RADA ALRAJAL( رَدَأَ الرَجَل) meaning to become someone supporter,helperand RIDUUN(رِدْءْْ) meaning helper,supporter ,a supporter who always accompany

Quran example:Surat Al-Qaşaş (The Stories)[28:34]وَأَخِي هَارُونُ هُوَ أَفْصَحُ مِنِّي لِسَانًا فَأَرْسِلْهُ مَعِيَ رِدْءًا يُصَدِّقُنِي

And my brother Aaron is more fluent than me in tongue, so send him with me as support, verifying me

9 AAMADDA( أَمَدَّ): MADDA(مَدَّ) means to elongate or spread something such that it remains integrated and AMMADA means to increase something in quantity or number for help. to send reinforcement promptly 

Quran example:Surat Nūĥ (Noah)[71:12]وَيُمْدِدْكُم بِأَمْوَالٍ وَبَنِي

And give you increase in wealth and children

Quran example:Surat 'Āli `Imrān (Family of Imran)[3:125]

يُمْدِدْكُمْ رَبُّكُم بِخَمْسَةِ آلَافٍ مِّنَ الْمَلَائِكَةِ مُسَوِّمِينَ

your Lord will reinforce you with five thousand angels having marks [of distinction]

Summary

1 AA3ANA:to help someone ,every time of help,it is

generally used

2 NASARA: to help someone for removal of infliction

3 AYYAD:to help someone by strengthening them

4 3ZZARA:to help someone with respect

5 3ZZAZA:to help someone to such extent to remove

weakness

6 ZAHARA:backing someone

7 RAFADA:to help needy/poor by alms and charity and

keep on doing that

8 RADAAA:to support,and always be there for it

9 AAMADDA:to reinforce promptly
