8 MATTI [Earth, Soil and its different conditions]

Synonyms: TURAB, TEEN, LAZIB, HAMA, SALSAAL, FAKH-KHAAR, SAEED, SULA-LAH, THARA,

8.1 TURAB (تُرَاب): Means dust or dirt, dry soil that contains no moisture. Which is easily blown away with wind. A common word for soil. In the Quran

Surah An-Naba (The Tidings) : سورۃ النّبَإ 78:40

وَيَقُولُ الْكَافِرُ يَا لَيْتَنِي كُنتُ تُرَابًا

And the faithless will say, “O, I wish I were dust.”

8.2 TEEN (طِیْن): A water soaked soil that contains no husk or straw; clay. Even if the moisture in the mud ends, even then this dry mud will be called TEEN (طِیْن). Allah says in the Quran

Surah As-Sajda (The prostration): سُوۡرَةُ السَّجدَة 32:8

وَبَدَأَ خَلْقَ الْإِنسَانِ مِن طِينٍ

And originated the creation of man from clay.

8.3 LAZIB (لَازِب): Soil the sticks to hand (dictionary al Manjad). Sticky and viscous clay. Allah says in the Quran

Surah As-Saaffat (The Aligners): سُوۡرَةُ الصَّافات37:11

إِنَّا خَلَقْنَاهُم مِّن طِينٍ لَّازِبٍ

We created them from sticky clay.

In the above ayah LAZIB (لَازِب) is not used as a property of TEEN (طِیْن) but LAZIB (لَازِب) itself is sticky and viscous mud.

8.4 HAMA (حَمَا): Means a smelly mud, when a mud turns black and starts to stink. HAM-AL-BIRR (حَمَا الْبِئْرَ) ‘he took out stinking black mud from the well’. HAM-AL-MAA’ (حَمَا الْمَاء) means water mixed with black soil.

8.5 SALSAAL (صَلْصَال): When the black stinking mud turns dry, and creates noise on a light blow this is called SALSAAL (صَلْصَال).

Surah Al-Hijr (The Rock): سورۃ الحِجر 15:26

وَلَقَدْ خَلَقْنَا الْإِنسَانَ مِن صَلْصَالٍ مِّنْ حَمَإٍ مَّسْنُونٍ

And We did certainly create man out of clay from an altered black mud.

In another place in the Quran

Surah Ar-Rahman (The Beneficent): سُوۡرَةُ الرَّحمٰن 55:14

خَلَقَ الْإِنسَانَ مِن صَلْصَالٍ كَالْفَخَّارِ

He created man (Adam) from sounding clay like the clay of pottery.

8.6 FAKH-KHAAR (فَخَّار): Means an earthen shard, or crock, a fire hardened or cooked earth (dictionary al Manjad). That cooked earth or earthen crock that produces sound when it is clanked or tapped. And FAKHARI (فخاری) is a potter who makes clay pots and sells them after cooking them in fire (dictionary al Manjad). Man was also made from this kind of clay or mud as said in the ayah above.

8.7 SAEED (صَعِیْد): SAA’DA (صَعَدَ) means to rise or get covered. SAEED (صَعِیْد) is the top layer of earth which gets covered with dirt and grime. All flat land is SAEED (صَعِیْد) and refers to the top layer of soil and grime and dirt. Allah says in the Holy Quran

Surah Al-Maeda (The table spread): سورۃ المَائدہ 5:6

 

فَلَمْ تَجِدُوا مَاءً فَتَيَمَّمُوا صَعِيدًا طَيِّبًا

Then seek clean earth and wipe over your faces and hands with it

8.8 SULA-LAH (سُلَالَۃ): Means the summary or overview, important area, an extracted thing. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Al-Mumenoon (The believers): سُوۡرَةُ المؤمنون 23:12

 

وَلَقَدْ خَلَقْنَا الْإِنسَانَ مِن سُلَالَةٍ مِّن طِينٍ

We created man from an extract of clay.

8.9 THARA (ثَرٰی): The water soaked soil from the deeper parts of earth. Moist earth. Allah says in the Quran

Surah Ta-Ha (Ta-ha): سورۃ طٰھٰ  20:6

لَهُ مَا فِي السَّمَاوَاتِ وَمَا فِي الْأَرْضِ وَمَا بَيْنَهُمَا وَمَا تَحْتَ الثَّرَىٰ

To Him belongs everything in the heavens and the earth, and everything between them, and everything beneath the soil.

Synonyms:

8.1 TURAB: Dry earth or soil, common word for all kind of earth.

8.2 TEEN: Water soaked soil without husk.

8.3 LAZIB: Viscous, sticky clay.

8.4 HAMA: Black, stinking mud.

8.5 SALSAAL: Dried black, stinking mud which clanks.

8.6 FAKH-KHAAR: Clinking and sounding cooked earth.

8.7 SAEED: The grime and dirt on the topmost layer of earth.

8.8 SULA-LAH: Extract

8.9 THARA: The moist soil from the deeper layer of earth
