{

synonyms: ELAHA,ALLAH

1 ELAHA(اِلَه):everything that a person worship by understanding it to be owner of their benefit or loss.it may be idol,place ,animal,trees,stone,nature's manifestations.plural is AALEHA(اَلِهة) and femine is ELAHA(الاَهة)(meaning goddess)therefore sun worshipper called sun(which is feminine in arabic) as

ELAHATOON( اَلِهةُُ).Prophet has always have this mission to purify humans of this false belief

Quran example:Surat Hūd (Hud)[11:101]

ۖ فَمَا أَغْنَتْ عَنْهُمْ آلِهَتُهُمُ الَّتِي يَدْعُونَ مِن دُونِ اللَّهِ مِن شَيْءٍ لَّمَّا جَاءَ أَمْرُ رَبِّكَ ۖ

And they were not availed at all by their gods which they invoked other than Allah when there came the command of your Lord

2ALLAH(الّله):is actually ALELAHA( اَلاِلَهَ)meaning actual god.by removing alif of ELAHA and adding AL makes this word ALLAH.This is the best explanation which means that in reality only ALLAH holds the benefit

and loss of human and he is the only one worthy of

worship and praise

Quran example:

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow)[2:163]

وَإِلَٰهُكُمْ إِلَٰهٌ وَاحِدٌ ۖ لَّا إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا هُوَ

And your god is one God. There is no deity [worthy of worship] except Him,

Quran example:

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow)[2:255]

اللَّهُ لَا إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا هُوَ

Allah - there is no deity except Him

Calling word YA(يَا)is not used with ALLAH instead

ALLAHUMA(اللَّهُمَّ) meaning O ALLAH is used

Quran example:

Surat 'Āli `Imrān (Family of Imran)[3:26]

قُلِ اللَّهُمَّ مَالِكَ الْمُلْكِ تُؤْتِي الْمُلْكَ مَن تَشَاءُ

Say, "O Allah , Owner of Sovereignty, You give sovereignty to whom You will

Summary

1 ALLAH:Creator,Lord, Sustainer, Cherisher, Master of everything in the universe,and having unlimited power and authority that is why he is worthy of worship 

2 ELAHA:anything that as per one's belief has power of benefit and loss for one and that's why one consider to worship that thing
