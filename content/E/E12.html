12.  Aurat [woman]

The word aurat is used in opposite to man which means it is used for female. But the same is not true in Arabic. Aurat means private parts of body and auraat means places of the private parts of the body. As  in Quran :

Surat Al-Nur (The light)

24:31

 أَوِ الطِّفْلِ الَّذِينَ لَمْ يَظْهَرُوا عَلَىٰ عَوْرَاتِ النِّسَاءِ

or children who are not yet aware of the private aspects of women.(can come into houses)

Synonyms: unsa, imraat,nisa,niswah

The last three are also used for wives.

12.1. UNSA: meaning female. It applies to the females of all the living things. Thus when describing

              human beings, it means woman. As in Quran

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة

2:178

يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا كُتِبَ عَلَيْكُمُ الْقِصَاصُ فِي الْقَتْلَى ۖ الْحُرُّ بِالْحُرِّ وَالْعَبْدُ بِالْعَبْدِ وَالْأُنْثَىٰ بِالْأُنْثَىٰ

O you who believe! Al-Qisas (the Law of Equality in punishment) is prescribed for you in case of murder: the free for the free, the slave for the slave, and the female for the female.

12.2. IMRAATUM: comes against imrun or murun. Means any person or any male. Its applies when the male or female reaches the age of marriage. The tasnia(plural) of imraat is imraataan and jama(plural) is nisa or niswah.

In Quran:

Surat Al-Nisa (The women)

4:128

وَإِنِ امْرَأَةٌ خَافَتْ مِنْ بَعْلِهَا نُشُوزًا أَوْ إِعْرَاضًا

And if a woman fears cruelty or desertion on her husband's part

12.3. NISA: its opposite is rijaal which is the plural of imruun. The plural of imraatun is nisa and niswah. A man is called rajul when his physical strength is to be pointed out.

For details refer to the topic “the human”

As in quran

Surat Al-hujuraat (The dwellings.) –

49:11

يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا لَا يَسْخَرْ قَوْمٌ مِنْ قَوْمٍ عَسَىٰ أَنْ يَكُونُوا خَيْرًا مِنْهُمْ وَلَا نِسَاءٌ مِنْ نِسَاءٍ

Let not a group scoff at another group, it may be that the latter are better than the former; nor let (some) women scoff at other women, .

 

12.4. NISWAH: womens dealing with a particular matter. As in Quran

Surat yusuf (prophet yusuf.) –

12:50

 ارْجِعْ إِلَىٰ رَبِّكَ فَاسْأَلْهُ مَا بَالُ النِّسْوَةِ اللَّاتِي قَطَّعْنَ أَيْدِيَهُنَّ ۚ

Return to your lord and ask him, 'What happened to the women who cut their hands?

Summary:

 

12.1: UNSA:  female of every living thing. when referring to human beings then it is used for woman.

12.2. IMRAATUM: plural of mirun or imrun. When he/she reaches the age of marriage. Its plural is also nisa.

12.3. NISWAH: women related to a particular matter.
