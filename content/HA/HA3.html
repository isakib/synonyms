3      HAALAT

         [condition or situation]

 

SYNONYMS: BAAL, KHATAB, DA’AB, and TOOR

 

3.1 BAAL (بَال) : means the present condition or situation. It is a common word.

Refer to 20:52

__________________________________

3.2 KHATAB (خَطْب) : to react to a problem, whether it is big or small. Usually used for a disliked problem.

Refer to 51:31

__________________________________

3.3 DA’AB (دَأب) : means to keep walking. A habit to continue. **************************** (AADAAT WAATWAAR). Way of walking.

Refer to 3:11

_________________________________

3.4 TOOR (طَوْر) : means estimate, limit, status, condition, (************ BAARI). And  اَطْوَارٌ اَلنَّاسُ means that men are of several types. And تَطَوُّر means to change from one condition to another condition. طَوُر means a condition or status which according to estimates wants a change after sometime.

Refer to 71:14

________________________________

SUMMARY

BAAL: used for the present condition while questioning

KHATAB: to ask about a disliked problem

DA’AB: habits and way of walking

TOOR: a status after another status
