13 HAMLA KARNA [To Attack]

SYNONYMS: SATAA سَطَاَ AAGHARA(GH-WA-RA) (اَغَارَ (غور)

13.1 SATAA:(SATWAH)BIHI WA 3LAYHI سَطْوَة به و عليه meaing to attack on some one to dominate. and SATA ALFARS سطا الفرس meaning horse running by pacing wide.and SATAA meaning to intense hold or to attack on someone.ALLAH said

Surat Al-Ĥaj (The Pilgrimage) - سورة الحج

22:72

وَإِذَا تُتْلَىٰ عَلَيْهِمْ آيَاتُنَا بَيِّنَاتٍ تَعْرِفُ فِي وُجُوهِ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا الْمُنكَرَ ۖ يَكَادُونَ يَسْطُونَ بِالَّذِينَ يَتْلُونَ عَلَيْهِمْ آيَاتِنَا ۗ قُلْ أَفَأُنَبِّئُكُم بِشَرٍّ مِّن ذَٰلِكُمُ ۗ النَّارُ وَعَدَهَا اللَّهُ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا ۖ وَبِئْسَ الْمَصِيرُ

And when Our verses are recited to them as clear evidences, you recognize in the faces of those who disbelieve disapproval. They are almost on the verge of assaulting those who recite to them Our verses. Say, "Then shall I inform you of [what is] worse than that? [It is] the Fire which Allah has promised those who disbelieve, and wretched is the destination."

13.2 AAGHARA:(GHWR)GHARATAN WA IGHARATAN(غَارَةًوََ اِغَارَةً ) meaning to break down. AGHARU ALFARS(اَغَارُ الْفَرْس) meaning horse fast pacing.ALLAH said

Surat Al-`Ādiyāt (The Courser) - سورة العاديات

100:3

فَالْمُغِيرَاتِ صُبْحًا

And the chargers at dawn,

SUMMARY:

13.1 SATAA:to be stern in attack and endeavour to dominate

13.2 AAGHARA:to attack and to decimate.
