9 HIFAZAT KARNA [To Guard]

SYNONYMS:HAFIZA حَفَِظَ RA3A رَعي AHSANA اَحْصَنَ KALA-A كَلأ

9.1 HAFIZA:(Antonym ADA3 اضاع) meaning to prevent from wastage and destruction.to look after.to try to protect some thing from external dangers/threats.In the QURAN

Surat Yūsuf (Joseph) - سورة يوسف

12:65

وَلَمَّا فَتَحُوا مَتَاعَهُمْ وَجَدُوا بِضَاعَتَهُمْ رُدَّتْ إِلَيْهِمْ ۖ قَالُوا يَا أَبَانَا مَا نَبْغِي ۖ هَٰذِهِ بِضَاعَتُنَا رُدَّتْ إِلَيْنَا ۖ وَنَمِيرُ أَهْلَنَا وَنَحْفَظُ أَخَانَا وَنَزْدَادُ كَيْلَ بَعِيرٍ ۖ ذَٰلِكَ كَيْلٌ يَسِيرٌ

And when they opened their baggage, they found their merchandise returned to them. They said, "O our father, what [more] could we desire? This is our merchandise returned to us. And we will obtain supplies for our family and protect our brother and obtain an increase of a camel's load; that is an easy measurement."

9.2 RA3A:to protect something and to remove the causes which can lead to external threats/dangers. and RAA3IY(رَاعِي) meaning shepherd-Guards.(Antonym IHMAAL اِهمال). meaning cattle grazing without th shepherd.In the QURAN.

Surat Al-Mu'minūn (The Believers) - سورة المؤمنون

23:8

وَالَّذِينَ هُمْ لِأَمَانَاتِهِمْ وَعَهْدِهِمْ رَاعُونَ

And they who are to their trusts and their promises attentive

9.3 AHSANA:HSN(حصن) meaning fort or secure place and it's plural is HUSUWN(حُصُوْن)

there are two basic things in HASAN, ALHIFZH WA ALHYATAH(الحفظ والحياطة) meaning protection and surrounded/covered. and as per author of MANJAD it means to protect in a protected place.also a woman guarding her chastity.In the QURAN

Surat At-Taĥrīm (The Prohibtiion) - سورة التحريم

66:12

وَمَرْيَمَ ابْنَتَ عِمْرَانَ الَّتِي أَحْصَنَتْ فَرْجَهَا فَنَفَخْنَا فِيهِ مِن رُّوحِنَا وَصَدَّقَتْ بِكَلِمَاتِ رَبِّهَا وَكُتُبِهِ وَكَانَتْ مِنَ الْقَانِتِينَ

And [the example of] Mary, the daughter of 'Imran, who guarded her chastity, so We blew into [her garment] through Our angel, and she believed in the words of her Lord and His scriptures and was of the devoutly obedient.

and also to commit marriage contract.because marriage serves as a fort againt shamelessness. as said

Surat An-Nisā' (The Women) - سورة النساء

4:24

وَالْمُحْصَنَاتُ مِنَ النِّسَاءِ إِلَّا مَا مَلَكَتْ أَيْمَانُكُمْ ۖ كِتَابَ اللَّهِ عَلَيْكُمْ ۚ وَأُحِلَّ لَكُم مَّا وَرَاءَ ذَٰلِكُمْ أَن تَبْتَغُوا بِأَمْوَالِكُم مُّحْصِنِينَ غَيْرَ مُسَافِحِينَ ۚ فَمَا اسْتَمْتَعْتُم بِهِ مِنْهُنَّ فَآتُوهُنَّ أُجُورَهُنَّ فَرِيضَةً ۚ وَلَا جُنَاحَ عَلَيْكُمْ فِيمَا تَرَاضَيْتُم بِهِ مِن بَعْدِ الْفَرِيضَةِ ۚ إِنَّ اللَّهَ كَانَ عَلِيمًا حَكِيمًا

And [also prohibited to you are all] married women except those your right hands possess. [This is] the decree of Allah upon you. And lawful to you are [all others] beyond these, [provided] that you seek them [in marriage] with [gifts from] your property, desiring chastity, not unlawful sexual intercourse. So for whatever you enjoy [of marriage] from them, give them their due compensation as an obligation. And there is no blame upon you for what you mutually agree to beyond the obligation. Indeed, Allah is ever Knowing and Wise.

9.4 KALA-A:to keep something under visual surveillance and to keep it sustained.so it also includes two things a)contemplation b)stability and meaning to prevent something from getting inclined from peace to disaster. as said

Surat Al-'Anbyā' (The Prophets) - سورة الأنبياء

21:42

قُلْ مَن يَكْلَؤُكُم بِاللَّيْلِ وَالنَّهَارِ مِنَ الرَّحْمَٰنِ ۗ بَلْ هُمْ عَن ذِكْرِ رَبِّهِم مُّعْرِضُونَ

Say, "Who can protect you at night or by day from the Most Merciful?" But they are, from the remembrance of their Lord, turning away.

SUMMARY:

9.1 HAFIZA:to prevent something from being wasted

9.2 RA3A:safety and removing causes of external dangers

9.3 AHSANA:safety by means of enclosure.

9.4 KALA-A:to keep valid and intact.
