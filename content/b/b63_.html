63 – To forget, to make another forget

 

نَسِيَ, سَهَا, ضَلَّ and ذَهَل have been used

 

63.1 نَسِيَ: This is a common word used for forgetting, whether due to heedlessness or any other reason.  Allah (the exalted) states:

 

Surat Yā-Sīn (Ya Sin) -  يس سورة

36:78

(﻿٧٨﻿) خَلۡقَهُ وَنَسِىَ مَثَلاً۬ لَنَا وَضَرَبَ

And he presents for Us an example and forgets his [own] creation (78).

 

أَنسَئَ refers to make another forget. In the holy Quran Allah (the exalted)  states:

 

Surat Yūsuf (Joseph) - سورة يوسف

12:42

(﻿٤٢﻿)رَبِّهِ ذِڪۡرَ ٱلشَّيۡطَـٰنُ فَأَنسَٮٰهُ

But Satan made him forget the mention [to] his master (42).

_________________________________

 

63.2 سَهَا (سَهواً): to not pay attention to something due to heedlessness or because of preoccupation with something else.  To have your attention diverted towards a secondary matter.  سَاهِي refers to one who is heedless and forgetful.  Sajdah Sahw (the prostration of forgetfulness) is a famous word. Meaning that you were suppose to do one thing but due to forgetfulness you ended up doing something else. Allah (the exalted) states:

 

Surat Adh-Dhāriyāt (The Winnowing Winds) - الذاريات سورة

51:11

(﻿١١﻿) سَاهُونَ غَمۡرَةٍ۬فِى هُمۡٱلَّذِينَ

Who are within a flood [of confusion] and heedless (11).

___________________________________

 

63.3 ضَلَّ: to forget something entirely or some part of it due to the lack of ability to retain it. Allah (the exalted) states:

 

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - البقرة سورة

2:282

 (﻿٢٨٢﻿) ٱلۡأُخۡرَىٰ‌ۚ إِحۡدَٮٰهُمَا فَتُذَڪِّرَ إِحۡدَٮٰهُمَا تَضِلَّ أَن

So that if one of the women errs, then the other can remind her (282).

___________________________________

 

63.4 ذَهَل (ذَهُولاً): when the cause of forgetfulness is fear or a preoccupation that causes fear and grief it is called ذَهُول.  ذَهَل (ذَهُولاً) refers to being neglectful, to forget and ذَهِلَ refers to be astounded or bewildered. Allah (the exalted) states:

 

Surat Al-Ĥaj (The Pilgrimage) - الحج سورة

22:2

 يَوۡمَ تَرَوۡنَهَا تَذۡهَلُ ڪُلُّ مُرۡضِعَةٍ عَمَّآ أَرۡضَعَتۡ وَتَضَعُ ڪُلُّ ذَاتِ حَمۡلٍ حَمۡلَهَا وَتَرَى ٱلنَّاسَ سُكَـٰرَىٰ وَمَا هُم بِسُكَـٰرَىٰ (﻿٢﻿)

On the Day you see it every nursing mother will be distracted from that [child] she was nursing, and every pregnant woman will abort her pregnancy, and you will see the people [appearing] intoxicated while they are not intoxicated (2).

________________________________________

 

Summary:

1.      نَسِيَ: commonly used word for forgetting, due to any reason

2.      سَهَا: forgetting something due to negligence

3.      ضَلَّ: forgetting due to an inability to remember

4.      ذَهَل: forgetting due to fear or grief
