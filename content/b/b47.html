47: TO MAKE/Create:

Jaa’al,Bana’a,Sana’a, Istanaa and ittakhaz has been used in the Quran when it was meant to make or create or build something.

1.Jaa’al: This word has been used when ‘making’ was intended. May it be inventing,creating or just organising something. As Allah says in the Quran:(2:22)’[He] who made for you the earth a bed [spread out] and the sky a ceiling’.

2.Bana’a: It means to construct/build something like a building and bunyaan means erecting a wall or contructing a building. According to Ibne Faaris ,It means to make something in such a way that one thing merges in another.

Allah says in the Quran: (51:47)’And the heaven We constructed with strength, and indeed, We are [its] expander.

3.Sana’a: To create something beautifully with expertise. Sannaa’a is used for an expert creator or artist. And Istanaa’a means to create something with full expertise & concentration.

Allah says in the Quran: (11:27):’And construct the ship under Our observation and Our inspiration’

Also, in (20:41),the Quran says:’ (Moosa) And I produced you for Myself.’

4.Ittakhaz: Akhaz means to hold,take or get something. To take something in possession. (couldnt translate the second part of the sentence –tough urdu) .This does not include organising or creating something.

As Allah says in the Quran: (12:21):And the one from Egypt who bought him said to his wife, "Make his residence comfortable. Perhaps he will benefit us, or we will adopt him as a son."
