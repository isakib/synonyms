48. To Close –To Be:

When the above was meant, Ghallaq,rasad,qasar & qabaz are used.

1.Ghallaq: Means close./enclose. Opposite of it is Faqq, which means to release. Ghallaqa means To put in key & lock. Ghallaqul baab means to shut the door tight, to lock it properly .

The Quran says: (12:23):She closed the doors and said, "Come, you."

2.Rasad. It means to shut/close something so tight that nothing can escape out of it. According to Ibne Faaris, it means to cover the cooking pot with the lid properly so that the steam doesn’t escape.. It also means that the door be shut tightly especially when the windows are not open. According to the Quran:(90:20): ‘Over them will be fire closed in.’

3.Qasar: Is basically used in two ways. A) Inability of something to reach its pinnacle. B) suffocate/lock. The second meaning is considered when it is said that someone locked himself in his home.

According to the Quran (55:72) ‘Fair ones reserved in pavilions ‘.

5.Qabaz:Means to hold something tightly in fist.Qabz means fist,so whenever it is used, it means, shrink,reduce,tighten & close. As Allah says in the Quran,(9:67):’They enjoin what is wrong and forbid what is right and close their hands.’

Summary:

a)Ghallaq:To shut the door .

b)Wasad:to close something so tight that nothing can escape out of it.

c)Qasar: to lock oneself somewhere.

d)Qabz:To hold something tightly in the fist so that it doesn’t escape.
