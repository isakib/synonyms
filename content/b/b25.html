25. To curse (BAD-DUA DENA) page 195

Synonyms: LA3ANA, BA3IDA, SUHKAN, IBTAHALA

25.1 LA3ANA (لَعَنَ): means to put someone far away from you, or to put down someone, on the basis of being displeased with them. And when it is attributed to Allah (SWT) then it means to be away from Allah (SWT)’s mercy and provisions. And it means giving a curse, keeping away from righteousness, and putting down. And LA3NAT is a commonly used word for giving a curse.

Surah Al Ma’ida (The table spread with food)

5:78

لُعِنَ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُواْ مِن بَنِي إِسْرَائِيلَ عَلَى لِسَانِ دَاوُودَ وَعِيسَى ابْنِ مَرْيَمَ

Those among the Children of Israel who disbelieved were cursed by the tongue of Dawud (David) and Isa (Jesus), son of Maryam (Mary).

25.2 BA3IDA (بَعِدَ): means to be far, to die. And BU3DAN (بُعْدًا) is a word of curse meaning may Allah (SWT) make that person die or make him far from His Mercy.

_______________________________

Surah Hud (Prophet Hud)

11:95

كَأَن لَّمْ يَغْنَوْاْ فِيهَا أَلاَ بُعْدًا لِّمَدْيَنَ كَمَا بَعِدَتْ ثَمُودُ

As if they had never lived there! So away with Madyab! As away with Thamud! (All these nations were destroyed)

25.3 SUHKAN (سُحْقًا): سَحَقَ meaning *************************. And الثَوُب سْحَقَ اَ means a cloth getting old, and اللّهِ اَسْحَقَهُ means may Allah (SWT) make him die. سُحْقًا سَحِقَ means to become far, and اِسْتَحَقَهُ also means to make something far. Therefore even this is a word of curse, and this means being far from goodness. But it is found to be more extreme than بُعْدًا .

Surah Al Mulk (The Dominion)

67:11

فَاعْتَرَفُوا بِذَنبِهِمْ فَسُحْقًا لِّأَصْحَابِ السَّعِيرِ

Then they will confess their sin. So, away with the dwellers of the blazing fire.

_________________________________

25.4 IBTAHALA (اِبْتَهَلَ) : فُلَانًا اَبْهَلْتُ is used idiomatically and it means to give someone freedom in his opinions and thoughts. And الدُعَاءِ فيِ الاِبَتِهَالُ وَ الْبَهْلُ means full freedom in prayer and prayer with humility. Then, due to Allah (SWT)’s curse is mentioned in the ayah, the word MABAHILA was made famous for meaning to curse each other, therefore it is interpreted in a negative way.

_________________________________

Surah Ale-Imran (The Family of Imran)

3:61

ثُمَّ نَبْتَهِلْ فَنَجْعَل لَّعْنَةُ اللّهِ عَلَى الْكَاذِبِينَ

Then we pray and invoke (sincerely) the Curse of Allah upon those who lie.

_________________________________

SUMMARY

LA3ANA : A curse of keeping away from Allah (SWT)’s Mercy and Provision.

BA3IDA : being devoid of Mercy and prayer for death.

SUHKAN : commonly used word for cursing someone. It is more extreme than بُعْدًا .

IBTIHAL : two parties praying for a curse on each other with full freedom.
