25 BAD DUA DENA

[to curse]

Synonyms : LA3ANA, BA3IDA,SUHKHAN,IBTAHAL

 

25.1  LA3ANA(لَعَنَ) : means to distance oneself from someone or to condemn someone because of disliking them. And when it is attributed to ALLAH(SWT), its meaning is to be deprived of Allah’s Mercy and Blessings. And particularly,it means to curse, to stay away from righteousness and to condemn . ALLAH(SWT) says:

________________

Surah Al Ma’ida (The table spread with food) 

5:78 

لُعِنَ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا مِن بَنِي إِسْرَائِيلَ عَلَىٰ لِسَانِ دَاوُودَ وَعِيسَى ابْنِ مَرْيَمَ  

Cursed were those who disbelieved among the Children of Israel by the tongue of David and of Jesus, the son of Mary.

________________

 

25.2   BA3IDA(بَعِدَ) and BA3UDA(بَعُدَ)[BU’DAN] means to be far from or to die. And BUDAN(بُعْداً) is a swear-word i.e. May Allah(SWT) make him die or deprive him of HIS Mercy. ALLAH(SWT) says:

________________

Surah Hud (Prophet Hud) 

11:95 

كَأَن لَّمْ يَغْنَوْاْ فِيهَا أَلاَ بُعْدًا لِّمَدْيَنَ كَمَا بَعِدَتْ ثَمُودُ

As if they had never prospered therein. Then, away with Madyan as Thamud was taken away.

________________

 

 

25.3   SHUHQAN(سُحْقاً):sahaqa(سَحَقَ) means to grind medicines and the like of them. And meaning of ASHAQAS-SHOWB(اَسْحَقَ الثَّوْب) is for a cloth to become old and ASHAQUHU ALLAH(اَسْحَقَهُ الّلَه) means – may Allah(SWT) cause him to die. Particularly, sahiqa, SUHQAN(سَحِقَ سُحْقاً) means to be far away from and ISTAHAQA(اِسْتَحَقَ) also means to move someone far away from.  In fact, this is also a swear word and its meaning to move someone far away from God. But it is more harsh than BUDAN(بُعْداً). ALLAH(SWT) says:

________________

Surat Al-Mulk (The Sovereignty) - سورة الملك

67:11

فَاعْتَرَفُوا بِذَنبِهِمْ فَسُحْقًا لِّأَصْحَابِ السَّعِيرِ

And they will admit their sin, so [it is] alienation for the companions of the Blaze.

________________

25.4  IBTAHAL(اِبْتَهَل): (اَبْهَلْتُ فُلاَناً) is used as an expression and it means to give freedom to someone in their ways and intentions and meaning of WAL BATIHALU FIDDAAE(وَالاِبَتِهاَلُ فِي الدُّءاَءِ) is to supplicate God with full freedom and humbleness. Then, since the Quranic verse of Mubahila mentions the curse of Allah(SWT),  the word mubahala is notably used to invoke curses  on one another. In fact, its use also, is in a negative connotation. ALLAH(SWT) says:

________________

Surat 'Āli `Imrān (Family of Imran) - سورة آل عمران

3:61

ثُمَّ نَبْتَهِلْ فَنَجْعَل لَّعْنَتَ اللَّهِ عَلَى الْكَاذِبِينَ

then supplicate earnestly [together] and invoke the curse of Allah upon the liars [among us].

________________

Summary:

25.1 LA3ANA:a curse to move someone away from God’s Mercy and Blessings.

25.2 BA3IDA:being deprived of Mercy and a curse to invoke death upon someone.

25.3 SHUHQAN: commonly used plural of curse. Among its connotations, budan is the harshest.

25.4 IBTAHAL: two parties, invoking and supplicating for curses on one another, with full freedom.

25. To curse (BAD-DUA DENA) page 195

Synonyms: LA3ANA, BA3IDA, SUHKAN, IBTAHALA

25.1 LA3ANA (لَعَنَ): means to put someone far away from you, or to put down someone, on the basis of being displeased with them. And when it is attributed to Allah (SWT) then it means to be away from Allah (SWT)’s mercy and provisions. And it means giving a curse, keeping away from righteousness, and putting down. And LA3NAT is a commonly used word for giving a curse.

25.4 IBTAHALA (اِبْتَهَلَ) : فُلَانًا اَبْهَلْتُ is used idiomatically and it means to give someone freedom in his opinions and thoughts. And الدُعَاءِ فيِ الاِبَتِهَالُ وَ الْبَهْلُ means full freedom in prayer and prayer with humility. Then, due to Allah (SWT)’s curse is mentioned in the ayah, the word MABAHILA was made famous for meaning to curse each other, therefore it is interpreted in a negative way.

Then we pray and invoke (sincerely) the Curse of Allah upon those who lie.

SUMMARY

LA3ANA : A curse of keeping away from Allah (SWT)’s Mercy and Provision.

BA3IDA : being devoid of Mercy and prayer for death.

SUHKAN : commonly used word for cursing someone. It is more extreme than بُعْدًا .

IBTIHAL : two parties praying for a curse on each other with full freedom.
