27 BAD KARI

[fornication]

Synonyms : ZINA, BIGAAA, SAAFAHA, FAAHISHAH.

 

27.1 ZINA (زِناَ) : is a well-known word (i.e.) the act of performing an immoral act by any man with a woman to whom he is not married, or a woman with other than her husband is called zina. ALLAH(SWT) says:

________________

Surat Al-'Isrā' (The Night Journey) - سورة الإسراء

17:32

وَلَا تَقْرَبُوا الزِّنَا ۖ إِنَّهُ كَانَ فَاحِشَةً وَسَاءَ سَبِيلًا

And do not approach unlawful sexual intercourse. Indeed, it is ever an immorality and is evil as a way.

________________

 

27.2 BIGAAA (بِغَاء) : bagi means to exceed proper limits, to disobey, to force. BAAKHATIL AMATU BIGAAAN(بِغَآءً الاَمَةُ بَاغَتِ ) means fornication by a slave girl and BAGIYYA(بَغِیّ) means the fornicator being a lewd woman(i.e.) the word BIGAAA(بِغَاء) is particularly associated with fornication by slave girl or for a promiscuous woman i.e a prostitute – who has become popular among others for fornication. Quran indicates both these meaning. ALLAH(SWT) says :

________________

Surat An-Nūr (The Light) - سورة النور

24:33

وَلَا تُكْرِهُوا فَتَيَاتِكُمْ عَلَى الْبِغَاءِ

And do not compel your slave girls to prostitution

________________

In another place, Jews says this when addressing Maryam.

________________

Surat Maryam (Mary) - سورة مريم

19:28

سَوْءٍ وَمَا كَانَتْ أُمُّكِ بَغِيًّا

 nor was your mother unchaste.

________________

 

27.3 SAAFAHA(سَافَحَ): SAFAHA(سفح) means to make blood or water to flow and SAAFAHA(سَافَحَ) means to fornicate. In the words of Ibn Farsi “SABBAL MAA BILA AKHDI NIKAH”( عَقْدِ بِلَا الماَء صَبَّ) i.e.to fornicate without a nikah contract. In fact, the difference between zina and SAAFAHA(سَافَحَ) is that, it contains many connotations. And the Quran promotes this by using the word MUSAFIHEEN(مُسَافِحِيْن) in comparision with MUHSINEEN(مُحْصِنِيْن). The meaning of SAAFAHA(سَافَحَ)  in our language is “to have a mistress” and MUSAAFIH(مُسَافِح) indicates the man and woman between whom there is an immoral relationship. The word paramour is also used nowadays, in this meaning. ALLAH(SWT) says:

________________

Surat An-Nisā' (The Women) - سورة النساء

4:25

فَانكِحُوهُنَّ بِإِذْنِ أَهْلِهِنَّ وَآتُوهُنَّ أُجُورَهُنَّ بِالْمَعْرُوفِ مُحْصَنَاتٍ غَيْرَ مُسَافِحَاتٍ وَلَا مُتَّخِذَاتِ أَخْدَانٍ ۚ فَإِذَا أُحْصِنَّ فَإِنْ أَتَيْنَ بِفَاحِشَةٍ فَعَلَيْهِنَّ نِصْفُ مَا عَلَى الْمُحْصَنَاتِ مِنَ الْعَذَابِ ۚ ذَٰلِكَ لِمَنْ خَشِيَ الْعَنَتَ مِنكُمْ ۚ وَأَن تَصْبِرُوا خَيْرٌ لَّكُمْ ۗ وَاللَّهُ غَفُورٌ رَّحِيمٌ

So marry them with the permission of their people and give them their due compensation according to what is acceptable. [They should be] chaste, neither [of] those who commit unlawful intercourse randomly nor those who take [secret] lovers. But once they are sheltered in marriage, if they should commit adultery, then for them is half the punishment for free [unmarried] women. This [allowance] is for him among you who fears sin, but to be patient is better for you. And Allah is Forgiving and Merciful.

________________

27.4 FAAHISHAH (فَاحِشَة): FAHASHA(فحش) is any word or deed that transgresses in lewdness and badness.  And FAAHASHAH(فَاحَشَة) is a desire or any word or deed that takes one closer to fornication. i.e. this word is often used in the Holy Quran for shameless acts and conversations and for similar meanings and the word FAAHASHA MUBAYYINAH(مُبَيِّنَة فاحشتہ) has been used for fornication. ALLAH(SWT) says:

________________

Surat An-Nisā' (The Women) - سورة النساء

4:19

وَلَا تَعْضُلُوهُنَّ لِتَذْهَبُوا بِبَعْضِ مَا آتَيْتُمُوهُنَّ إِلَّا أَن يَأْتِينَ بِفَاحِشَةٍ مُّبَيِّنَةٍ

And do not make difficulties for them in order to take [back] part of what you gave them unless they commit a clear immorality.

________________

And the word FAHASHAH(فَاحَشَة) indicates that act of immorality which requires more evidence.

________________

Surat An-Nisā' (The Women) - سورة النساء

4:15

وَاللَّاتِي يَأْتِينَ الْفَاحِشَةَ مِن نِّسَائِكُمْ فَاسْتَشْهِدُوا عَلَيْهِنَّ أَرْبَعَةً مِّنكُمْ

Those who commit unlawful sexual intercourse of your women - bring against them four [witnesses] from among you.

________________

Summary:

27.1  ZINA: well-known word. An immoral act by a man and woman not married to each other.

27.2 BIGAAA: an immoral act by a slave girl or fornication by a promiscuous woman.

27.3 SAAFAHA: maintaining immoral relationship between a man and woman not married to each other, to have a mistress.

27.4  FAAHISHAH: acts or words that take one towards fornication or is used for blaming someone for fornication-which is worthy of evidence.

27. BADKAARI

[prostitution]

Synonyms: ZINA, BAGHAAE, SAAFAHA, FAHISHA

27.1 ZINA (زِنَا): is a popular word meaning the committing of sexual act of a man with a woman who is a stranger, or of a woman with a man other than her husband.

__________________________________

Surah Al-Isra' (The Journey by Night)

17:32

وَلاَ تَقْرَبُواْ الزِّنَى إِنَّهُ كَانَ فَاحِشَةً وَسَاء سَبِيلاً

And come not near to the unlawful sexual intercourse. Verily, it is a Fâhishah.

__________________________________

27.2 BAGHAAe (بَغَاء) : it means to go beyond limits, to disobey, to force. And بِغَآءً الاَمَةُ بَاغَتِ means a slave girl committing zina and بَغِيّ means a shameless woman who has committed zina. The word بَغَاء either specifies a slave girl who has committed zina or for a woman who is of a bad profession i.e. a prostitute, the woman who has become famous for committing zina. The Quran shows both these meanings:

_________________________________

Surah An-Nur (The Light)

24:33

وَلَا تُكْرِهُوا فَتَيَاتِكُمْ عَلَى الْبِغَاء

And force not your maids to prostitution

__________________________________

At another place the Jews say related to Maryam (AS)

Surah Maryam (Mary)

19:28

يَا أُخْتَ هَارُونَ مَا كَانَ أَبُوكِ امْرَأَ سَوْءٍ وَمَا كَانَتْ أُمُّكِ بَغِيًّا

"O sister (i.e. the like) of Hârûn (Aaron) ! Your father was not a man who used to commit adultery, nor your mother was an unchaste woman."

__________________________________

27.3 SAAFAHA (سَافَحَ) : سفح means to make blood or water flow and سَافَحَ means to commit zina. نِكَاح عَقْدِ بِلَا الماَء صَبَّ means to commit sexual intercourse without being married to each other. Therefore its difference with zina is that argument is found in this. And in the Holy Quran, it is promoted by using مُحْصِنِيْن with مُسَافِحِيْن

So سَافَحَ means to keep an unlawful relationship and مُسَافِح is the man and woman involved in this. The word آشنَا is also used nowadays to mean the same.

__________________________________

Surah An-Nisa' (The Women)

4:25

فَانكِحُوهُنَّ بِإِذْنِ أَهْلِهِنَّ وَآتُوهُنَّ أُجُورَهُنَّ بِالْمَعْرُوفِ مُحْصَنَاتٍ غَيْرَ مُسَافِحَاتٍ وَلاَ مُتَّخِذَاتِ أَخْدَانٍ

Wed them with the permission of their own folk (guardians, Auliyâ' or masters) and give them their Mahr according to what is reasonable; they (the above said captive and slave-girls) should be chaste, not adulterous, nor taking boy-friends.

__________________________________

27.4 FAHISHA (فَاحِشَة) : فحش means a noun or verb for someone who has crossed his limits in vice. And فَاحِشَة refers to nouns or verbs which bring a person close to zina, acts and talk of shamelessness. مُبَيّنَة فَحشَة is used for zina.

__________________________________

Surah An-Nisa' (The Women)

4:19

وَلاَ تَعْضُلُوهُنَّ لِتَذْهَبُواْ بِبَعْضِ مَا آتَيْتُمُوهُنَّ إِلاَّ أَن يَأْتِينَ بِفَاحِشَةٍمُّبَيِّنَةٍ

and you should not treat them with harshness, that you may take away part of the Mahr you have given them, unless they commit open illegal sexual intercourse

______________________________________

And the word FAAHISHA is used for such an act of adultery which is yet to be proven.

Surah An-Nisa' (The Women)

4:15

وَاللاَّتِي يَأْتِينَ الْفَاحِشَةَ مِن نِّسَآئِكُمْ فَاسْتَشْهِدُواْ عَلَيْهِنَّ أَرْبَعةً مِّنكُمْ

And those of your women who commit illegal sexual intercourse, take the evidence of four witnesses from amongst you against them

________________________________________

SUMMARY

ZINA : it is a popular word. A strange man and a strange woman having illegal relations.

BAGHAAE : A slave girl involved in zina or a prostitute.

SAFAHA : a strange man and woman involved in an illegal relationship.

FAHISHA : refers to the acts or the talk that bring a person close to zina, or the accusation of zina without any proof.
