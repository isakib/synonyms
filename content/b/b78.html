78: Care free:

 

 Ghani   غَنى& samad   صَمَد are the words used.

1.                    Ghani غَنى : opposite of ghani  غَنى is beggar /faqeer         فقير . Meaning dependant for needs on others. & ghani غَنى  is such a person who does not need anyones help for sustenance (rich person)-its plural is aghniya اغنياء  .This word is generally used for being care free as far as wealth is concened (as they have it in abundance). This means that the person is self sufficient.

Allah says in the Quran: 2:273: Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة

 

لِلْفُقَرَاءِ الَّذِينَ أُحْصِرُوا فِي سَبِيلِ اللَّهِ لَا يَسْتَطِيعُونَ ضَرْبًا فِي الْأَرْضِ يَحْسَبُهُمُ الْجَاهِلُ أَغْنِيَاءَ مِنَ التَّعَفُّفِ تَعْرِفُهُم بِسِيمَاهُمْ لَا يَسْأَلُونَ النَّاسَ إِلْحَافًا ۗ وَمَا تُنفِقُوا مِنْ خَيْرٍ فَإِنَّ اللَّهَ بِهِ عَلِيمٌ

[Charity is] for the poor who have been restricted for the cause of Allah , unable to move about in the land. An ignorant [person] would think them self-sufficient because of their restraint, but you will know them by their [characteristic] sign. They do not ask people persistently [or at all]. And whatever you spend of good - indeed, Allah is Knowing of it.

 

2.        Samad   صَمَد: it basically has two aspects to it. A) something that is solid & tough/hard wearing. B) people come to that source from all around. Meaning a power that does not dependant on anyone but all others are dependent on Him.(refers to Allah)

 

The second difference between ghani غَنى & samad  صَمَد is that ghani غَنى relates mostly to financial matters ,whereas samad   صَمَد refers to being independent & carefree in all aspects & others are dependent on it.

 

Allah says in the Quran: 112:1-2: Surat Al-'Ikhlāş (The Sincerity) - سورة الإخلاص

 

قُلْ هُوَ اللَّهُ أَحَدٌ اللَّهُ الصَّمَدُ

Say, "He is Allah , [who is] One, Allah , the Eternal Refuge.
