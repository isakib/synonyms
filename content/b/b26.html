26. BADSOORAT BANANA - HONA

[to make ugly – to be ugly]

Synonyms:MASAKHA,KALAHA, QABUHA

26.1 MASAKHA (مَسَخَ) :means to spoil the face/appearance.

_________________________________

Surah Ya-Sin

36:67

وَلَوْ نَشَاء لَمَسَخْنَاهُمْ عَلَى مَكَانَتِهِمْ فَمَا اسْتَطَاعُوا مُضِيًّا وَلَا يَرْجِعُونَ

And if it had been Our Will, We could have transformed them in their places. Then they would have been unable to go forward (move about) nor they could have turned back.

__________________________________

26.2 KALAHA (كَلَحَ) : كَلَحَة means face and its surrounding structures and كالح is that person who’s lips do meet each other, instead they keep open, and كَلَحَ means to frown. اِكلَوَّحَ means to show the teeth while frowning. ****************************************** in which a person’s appearance is spoilt and he looks very ugly because of teeth being shown.

__________________________________

Surah Al-Mu'minun (The Believers)

23:104

تَلْفَحُ وُجُوهَهُمُ النَّارُ وَهُمْ فِيهَا كَالِحُونَ

The Fire will burn their faces, and therein they will grin, with displaced lips (disfigured).

__________________________________

26.3 QABUHA (قَبُحَ) : means to be ugly, disfigured. قَبِيح means bad, disfigured, ugly. And the word قَبَحَ can be used in an implied form to mean a noun or verb or the face being bad.

_________________________________

Surah Al-Qasas (The Naration)

28:42

وَيَوْمَ الْقِيَامَةِ هُم مِّنَ الْمَقْبُوحِينَ

and on the Day of Resurrection, they will be among the ones in a bad state.

In this ayah maqbuheen is implying both meanings.

_________________________________

SUMMARY

MASAKHA : used for spoiling or disfiguring a good face.

KALAHA : **********************************

QABUHA : used for having a bad face from birth. Also used for SIFAAT.
