26 BAD SOORAT BANA-HONA

[to become or be ugly]

Synonyms : MASAKHA, KALAHA, QABAHA

 

26.1  MASAKHA(مَسَخَ) : means to modify your face or appearance and spoil it. ALLAH(SWT) says:

________________

Surat Yā-Sīn (Ya Sin) - سورة يس

36:67

وَلَوْ نَشَاءُ لَمَسَخْنَاهُمْ عَلَىٰ مَكَانَتِهِمْ فَمَا اسْتَطَاعُوا مُضِيًّا وَلَا يَرْجِعُونَ

 And if We willed, We could have deformed them, [paralyzing them] in their places so they would not be able to proceed, nor could they return.

________________

 

26.2  KALAHA (كَلَحَ) : (كَلَحَة) means the mouth and its nearby areas and KALAHA(کالح) is that person whose lips are parted and KALLAHA(كَلَّحَ), KALAHA(كَلَحَ) means to frown. And IKLAWWA( اِكْلَوَّحَ ) means to show teeth when frowning, and according to author of Fiqh Al-Lugah, this is the most extreme category of frowning,(see TAWRY CHIDNA[to frown]) in which a person’s features are distorted and becomes very ugly due to teeth being exposed. ALLAH(SWT) says :

________________

Surat Al-Mu'minūn (The Believers) - سورة المؤمنون

23:104

تَلْفَحُ وُجُوهَهُمُ النَّارُ وَهُمْ فِيهَا كَالِحُونَ

The Fire will sear their faces, and they therein will have taut smiles.

________________

 

 

26.3  QABUHA(قَبُحَ): means to be ugly, disfigured and QABATAHA( قبتح ) means bad, disfigured, ugly and the word QABAHA( قبح ) is also used in implied sense meaning a saying, an action or the face being ugly. ALLAH(SWT) says:

________________

Surat Al-Qaşaş (The Stories) - سورة القصص

28:42

وَيَوْمَ الْقِيَامَةِ هُم مِّنَ الْمَقْبُوحِينَ

And on the Day of Resurrection they will be of the despised.

________________

In this ayat MAQBOOHEEN(مقبوحين) gives two or three meanings.           

Summary:

26.1MASAKA: to distort or make ugly a good face or appearance.

26.2 KALAHA: momentary distortion of face due to some temporary difficulty or emotion and

26.3 QABUHA: indicates having an ugly face or appearace by birth. It is also used for features/attributes.

 

For Badfali and Badshagooni, refer “BADBAKHTI” and “NAMBARAK SAMAJNA”

26. BADSOORAT BANANA - HONA

[to make ugly – to be ugly]

Synonyms:MASAKHA,KALAHA, QABUHA

26.1 MASAKHA (مَسَخَ) :means to spoil the face/appearance.

_________________________________

Surah Ya-Sin

36:67

وَلَوْ نَشَاء لَمَسَخْنَاهُمْ عَلَى مَكَانَتِهِمْ فَمَا اسْتَطَاعُوا مُضِيًّا وَلَا يَرْجِعُونَ

And if it had been Our Will, We could have transformed them in their places. Then they would have been unable to go forward (move about) nor they could have turned back.

__________________________________

26.2 KALAHA (كَلَحَ) : كَلَحَة means face and its surrounding structures and كالح is that person who’s lips do meet each other, instead they keep open, and كَلَحَ means to frown. اِكلَوَّحَ means to show the teeth while frowning. ****************************************** in which a person’s appearance is spoilt and he looks very ugly because of teeth being shown.

__________________________________

Surah Al-Mu'minun (The Believers)

23:104

تَلْفَحُ وُجُوهَهُمُ النَّارُ وَهُمْ فِيهَا كَالِحُونَ

The Fire will burn their faces, and therein they will grin, with displaced lips (disfigured).

__________________________________

26.3 QABUHA (قَبُحَ) : means to be ugly, disfigured. قَبِيح means bad, disfigured, ugly. And the word قَبَحَ can be used in an implied form to mean a noun or verb or the face being bad.

_________________________________

Surah Al-Qasas (The Naration)

28:42

وَيَوْمَ الْقِيَامَةِ هُم مِّنَ الْمَقْبُوحِينَ

and on the Day of Resurrection, they will be among the ones in a bad state.

In this ayah maqbuheen is implying both meanings.

_________________________________

SUMMARY

MASAKHA : used for spoiling or disfiguring a good face.

QABUHA : used for having a bad face from birth. Also used for SIFAAT.
