49.To knit/Weave:

When knitting/weaving was meant, the words, habaka,& wazana are used.

1.Habaka:Habbak means weaver& Habak as Saub means he weaved a fabric. Habbaka Haabikaas Saub means the weaver weaved the fabric with expertise. To weave it beautifully. Habak also means path.

As Allah says in the Quran: (51:7):’ By the heaven containing pathways.’

2.Wazana: Means to knit,weave something which is strong . Mozoon means delicately weaved or something weaved/made with jewels. As Allah says in the Quran: (56:15-16):’ On thrones woven [with ornament],,reclining on them,facing each other.’

Summary: to weave something with ordinary stuff ,habaq is used whereas when jewels are used in weaving, then wazan is used.
