32 BADMAST HUWNA [Loss of Intellect/Senses]

Synonyms:NAZAFA(نَزَفَ),GHAL(GHWL)[(غَال (غول)]and SAKARA(سَكَرَ)

32.1 NAZAFA(نَزَفَ):meaning something getting finished gradually.NAZAFA ALMA- ALBI-RA(نَزَفَ الْمَاءَ الْبِئرَ)meaning to gradually take out all the water from the well. and NUZIFA ALMAA-(نُزِفَ الْمَآء) meaning water finished gradually.like wise NUZIFA DAM3AHU(نُزِفَ دَمْعَهُ) meaning Tears of his eyes dries by keeping on crying.and NUZIFA ARRAJULU(نُزِفَ الرَّجُلُ) means gradual loss of intellect of some person, to be without intellect or to be intoxicated.no matter whatever the reason maybe . ALLAH said

Surat Al-Wāqi`ah (The Inevitable) - سورة الواقعة

56:19

لَّا يُصَدَّعُونَ عَنْهَا وَلَا يُنزِفُونَ

No headache will they have therefrom, nor will they be intoxicated -

32.2 GHAL(GHWL)[(غَال (غول)]:ALGHWL(الغول)meaning JINN(genie) or Magician that keeps on changing shapes.Shape changing genie. and

ALGHWL meaning out of sense, headache and GHALA(غَال) meaning to kill someone that it is not even know.so GHWL refers to such a loss of intellect or state of intoxication whose reason is not known. ALLAH said

Surat Aş-Şāffāt (Those who set the Ranks) - سورة الصافات

37:47

لَا فِيهَا غَوْلٌ وَلَا هُمْ عَنْهَا يُنزَفُونَ

No bad effect is there in it, nor from it will they be intoxicated.

32.3 SAKARA(سَكَرَ):SUKR(سُكر) is normally used for loss of intellect or state of intoxication owing to alcohol/wine.and SAKARA is every intoxicant.meaning when due to intoxication of anything whether overtaking of love,or hardship of death there is loss of intellect then SAKKAR(سكّر) will be used .SAKARAT ALMWT(سكرات الموت) is a famous word. ALLAH said:

Surat An-Nisā' (The Women) - سورة النساء

4:43

يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا لَا تَقْرَبُوا الصَّلَاةَ وَأَنتُمْ سُكَارَىٰ حَتَّىٰ تَعْلَمُوا مَا تَقُولُونَ وَلَا جُنُبًا إِلَّا عَابِرِي سَبِيلٍ حَتَّىٰ تَغْتَسِلُوا ۚ وَإِن كُنتُم مَّرْضَىٰ أَوْ عَلَىٰ سَفَرٍ أَوْ جَاءَ أَحَدٌ مِّنكُم مِّنَ الْغَائِطِ أَوْ لَامَسْتُمُ النِّسَاءَ فَلَمْ تَجِدُوا مَاءً فَتَيَمَّمُوا صَعِيدًا طَيِّبًا فَامْسَحُوا بِوُجُوهِكُمْ وَأَيْدِيكُمْ ۗ إِنَّ اللَّهَ كَانَ عَفُوًّا غَفُورًا

O you who have believed, do not approach prayer while you are intoxicated until you know what you are saying or in a state of janabah, except those passing through [a place of prayer], until you have washed [your whole body]. And if you are ill or on a journey or one of you comes from the place of relieving himself or you have contacted women and find no water, then seek clean earth and wipe over your faces and your hands [with it]. Indeed, Allah is ever Pardoning and Forgiving.

Summary

32.1 NAZAF:gradual loss of intellect ,whatever the reason may be but is known reason

32.2 GHAWL:such a state of intoxication whose reason is unknown

32.3 SUKR:such an instant state of intoxication owing to alchol/wine/intoxicants. yet this word is used for the occasion of  overtaking of love or terror
