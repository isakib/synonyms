Conclusion:

(1)   Khilm خِلم   : To demonstrate a degree of temperance at the time of grief and anger. To delay a deserved punishment in order to grant some allow time.

(2)   Sabr صَبر  : To avoid acts of desperation and endure in times of all sorts of trials and punishments.

(3)   Kzm كظم  : To tolerate and show no signs, no matter the magnitude of sorrow and exasperation.
