10 FIRQAY YA MAZAHIB [Sects or Religions]

Sects or Religions which are mentioned in the Quran are as follows

1 MUSLIMIYN(مُسْلِمِيْن):Every such Person that accepts the call of any prophet or messenger is a Muslim. and ASLAM(اَسْلَمَ) meaning obedient and follow willingly. ALLAH said

Surat Al-Ĥaj (The Pilgrimage) - سورة الحج

22:78

وَجَاهِدُوا فِي اللَّهِ حَقَّ جِهَادِهِ ۚ هُوَ اجْتَبَاكُمْ وَمَا جَعَلَ عَلَيْكُمْ فِي الدِّينِ مِنْ حَرَجٍ ۚ مِّلَّةَ أَبِيكُمْ إِبْرَاهِيمَ ۚ هُوَ سَمَّاكُمُ الْمُسْلِمِينَ مِن قَبْلُ وَفِي هَٰذَا لِيَكُونَ الرَّسُولُ شَهِيدًا عَلَيْكُمْ وَتَكُونُوا شُهَدَاءَ عَلَى النَّاسِ ۚ فَأَقِيمُوا الصَّلَاةَ وَآتُوا الزَّكَاةَ وَاعْتَصِمُوا بِاللَّهِ هُوَ مَوْلَاكُمْ ۖ فَنِعْمَ الْمَوْلَىٰ وَنِعْمَ النَّصِيرُ

And strive for Allah with the striving due to Him. He has chosen you and has not placed upon you in the religion any difficulty. [It is] the religion of your father, Abraham. Allah named you "Muslims" before [in former scriptures] and in this [revelation] that the Messenger may be a witness over you and you may be witnesses over the people. So establish prayer and give zakah and hold fast to Allah . He is your protector; and excellent is the protector, and excellent is the helper.

2 MU-MINIYN(مُؤمِنِيْن):when Islam becomes fixed/hold strong roots in the heart then it is called IYMAN(ايمان)faith and one having faith is called MWMN(مومن). This difference is mentioned by the Quran as:

Surat Al-Ĥujurāt (The Rooms) - سورة الحجرات

49:14

قَالَتِ الْأَعْرَابُ آمَنَّا ۖ قُل لَّمْ تُؤْمِنُوا وَلَٰكِن قُولُوا أَسْلَمْنَا وَلَمَّا يَدْخُلِ الْإِيمَانُ فِي قُلُوبِكُمْ ۖ وَإِن تُطِيعُوا اللَّهَ وَرَسُولَهُ لَا يَلِتْكُم مِّنْ أَعْمَالِكُمْ شَيْئًا ۚ إِنَّ اللَّهَ غَفُورٌ رَّحِيمٌ

The bedouins say, "We have believed." Say, "You have not [yet] believed; but say [instead], 'We have submitted,' for faith has not yet entered your hearts. And if you obey Allah and His Messenger, He will not deprive you from your deeds of anything. Indeed, Allah is Forgiving and Merciful."

so ,actually IYMAN is the next grade/level of ISLAM and Muslims then becomes MU-MIN

3 KUFFAR(كُفّار):Every such person which discard the call of any prophet or messenger and denies it is a KAFIR(كافر) and KFR(كفر) meaning to deny whether one belongs to any religion. In the Quran:

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة

2:6

إِنَّ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا سَوَاءٌ عَلَيْهِمْ أَأَنذَرْتَهُمْ أَمْ لَمْ تُنذِرْهُمْ لَا يُؤْمِنُونَ

Indeed, those who disbelieve - it is all the same for them whether you warn them or do not warn them - they will not believe.
