15 FAWZ(فَوز):FAAZ(فَازَ)meaning to get freed,to be released,to get slavation,to be successful.to have wish fulfilled, are known . and it's second meaning is to die, or to be killed. and FAWWAZA(فَوَّزَ) baab(باب)is peculiar for this meaning. FAWWAZA ALRAJUL(فَوَّزَ الرَّجُل) meaning 'man died' and MUFAZAH(مفازة)meaning place of slavation or reason of slavation, place of success or it's reason, also place of destruction or its reason. ALLAH said

Surat Az-Zumar (The Troops) - سورة الزمر

39:61

وَيُنَجِّي اللَّهُ الَّذِينَ اتَّقَوْا بِمَفَازَتِهِمْ لَا يَمَسُّهُمُ السُّوءُ وَلَا هُمْ يَحْزَنُونَ

And Allah will save those who feared Him by their attainment; no evil will touch them, nor will they grieve.

In this ayah MUFAZAH comes in the meaning of success. and in the ayah below the same word is used in the meaning of slavation .(This word is not used in the meaning of destruction in the Holy Quran)

Surat 'Āli `Imrān (Family of Imran) - سورة آل عمران

3:188

لَا تَحْسَبَنَّ الَّذِينَ يَفْرَحُونَ بِمَا أَتَوا وَّيُحِبُّونَ أَن يُحْمَدُوا بِمَا لَمْ يَفْعَلُوا فَلَا تَحْسَبَنَّهُم بِمَفَازَةٍ مِّنَ الْعَذَابِ ۖ وَلَهُمْ عَذَابٌ أَلِيمٌ

And never think that those who rejoice in what they have perpetrated and like to be praised for what they did not do - never think them [to be] in safety from the punishment, and for them is a painful punishment.

16 QIST(قسط): means to give someone their right utterly whether this right is given in one go or distribute in several parts.In that case each part is also said QIST(قِسْط)(plural IQSAT(اَقْسَاط)). and its means also to do justice and also to do oppression.(For details see "INSAAF KARNA")

17 QA3ADA(قَعَدَ): means to sit and also to stand(For details see "BAYTHNA")

18 QAWY(قوي):QAWIYA(قَوِيَ) meaning to be powerful and also to be severely hungry. and AQWAY(اقوي) meaning to be destitute and also to be wealthy. and AQWAY ALQAWM(اَقوَي الْقَوْم) meaning nation's provision of journey finished. and QUWWAH(قُوَّة) meaning force,power and QUWT(قُوْت) meaning such a low quantity of food which keeps a human alive.and QAWIYUN(قَوِيٌ) meaning powerful and dominant. and MUQWIY(مُقْوِي) meaning the one searching desparately for food,traveller,destitute. ALLAH said

Surat Al-Wāqi`ah (The Inevitable) - سورة الواقعة

56:73

نَحْنُ جَعَلْنَاهَا تَذْكِرَةً وَمَتَاعًا لِّلْمُقْوِينَ

We have made it a reminder and provision for the travelers,

19 NASABA(نَصَبَ):YANSIBU(يَنْصِبُ),YANSUBU(يَنْصُب� �) meaning to stand something upright on the ground, to fix. and NASEEB(نصيب) meaning fixed stones in the way as signs and statue and idol which are fixed at some place for worship.(plural NUSUB نُصُب and ANSAB انصاب)and in that meaning this word comes a lot in the Quran. but when it comes from باب BAAB 3ALIMA YA3LAM(عَلِمَ يَعْلَم) then it produce inverted meaning .meaning a human unable to keep his body leveled owing to hard work and fatigue. also see appendix 4 entry 89. 18:62

فَلَمَّا جَاوَزَا قَالَ لِفَتَاهُ آتِنَا غَدَاءَنَا لَقَدْ لَقِينَا مِن سَفَرِنَا هَٰذَا نَصَبًا

So when they had passed beyond it, [Moses] said to his boy, "Bring us our morning meal. We have certainly suffered in this, our journey, [much] fatigue."

20 NAFQ(نفق):It is all those underground path which had both openings (entrance and exit) open, like mouse or snake hole(in which it live) or tunnel. and NAFQ ALYRBW3(نفق اليربوع) meaning mouse entering its hole(from one side) and also to exit from other way.In Quran this word is not used in the form of Antonyms but NAFQ is used in the meaning of tunnel. and NAFIQ(نافق)means Discourse of Faith to get entered from one way and get exited out from other(it won't remain inside). and ANFAQ(انفق) meaning to keep on spending wealth so it comes from one way and spent on other way (as per ALLAH's instructions.)

22 WA3AD(وَعَد): from its is WA3DATAN(وَعْدَةً) meaning to give someone hope of something good. and WA3YD(وَعيد) meaning to threaten or to terrify. and its examples are alot in the Holy Quran. and AW3ADA(اوْعَدَ) word is peculiar in this meaning. ALLAH said

Surat Al-'A`rāf (The Heights) - سورة الأعراف

7:86

وَلَا تَقْعُدُوا بِكُلِّ صِرَاطٍ تُوعِدُونَ وَتَصُدُّونَ عَن سَبِيلِ اللَّهِ مَنْ آمَنَ بِهِ وَتَبْغُونَهَا عِوَجًا ۚ وَاذْكُرُوا إِذْ كُنتُمْ قَلِيلًا فَكَثَّرَكُمْ ۖ وَانظُرُوا كَيْفَ كَانَ عَاقِبَةُ الْمُفْسِدِينَ

And do not sit on every path, threatening and averting from the way of Allah those who believe in Him, seeking to make it [seem] deviant. And remember when you were few and He increased you. And see how was the end of the corrupters.

23 WARAA-( وَرَاء) :meaning in front or behind. used for both adverb of time and place.(for details see PIYCHAY)

24 HJD(هجد):HJD(HJWD هجود)meaning to sleep or awake at night. and HAJJADA(هَجَّدَ) meaning wake up from sleep,sleep at night,wake up at night and pray. and TAHAJJADA(تَهَجَّد) meaning to sleep or stay awake at night. ALLAH said

Surat Al-'Isrā' (The Night Journey) - سورة الإسراء

17:79

وَمِنَ اللَّيْلِ فَتَهَجَّدْ بِهِ نَافِلَةً لَّكَ عَسَىٰ أَن يَبْعَثَكَ رَبُّكَ مَقَامًا مَّحْمُودًا

And from [part of] the night, pray with it as additional [worship] for you; it is expected that your Lord will resurrect you to a praised station.
