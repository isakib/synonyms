13 ISM ZAMEER [Pronouns]

Pronouns are also a type of proper noun.It represents assumed name and normally after it.Pronouns are of two types

MUSTATAR(مستتر)[hidden] and BAREZ(بارز)[visible] .

a) Mustatar is that which are understood from verb and its different SIYGHA,صيغه

Side Note:[Corresponding Form of Verb for Particular Person(1st/ 2nd/3rd),Singularity/Plurality,Gender masculine/feminine,Tense Maadi/Mudaari] .

for e.g QALA(قَالَ) the pronoun is Singular,maculine,3rd person

and QULNA(قُلْنَ) the pronoun is plural,feminine,3rd person are MUSTATAR pronoun.

b)BAREZ is that which is apparent from words and its further sub categorized as MUTTASIL(متصل)[attached] and MUNFASIL(منفصل)[detached].MUTTASIL is that which is attached to the word like KITABAKA LAHUMA BIHIM (كِتَابَكَ،لَهُمَا،بِهِمْ) etc . and MUNFASIL is that which exist as a separate word like HUWA HUMA HUM -- ANA NAHNU (هُوَ هُمَا هُم اَنَا نَحْنُ).Detached pronouns are used in the nominative state and attached pronouns are used in accusative and genitive state. In accusative state IYYA(اِيَّا) is prefixed to these pronouns to produce exclusivity in the speech. like IYYAHU(اِيَّاهُ) meaning 'only to that one man'. as in the Quran

Surat Al-Fātiĥah (The Opener) - سورة الفاتحة

1:5

إِيَّاكَ نَعْبُدُ وَإِيَّاكَ نَسْتَعِينُ

It is You we worship and You we ask for help.

for further details please refer to a grammar book.
