Appendix 5 Miscellanious

1 Some Comprehensive Names

1.1 AYAMA(اَيَامَي):is plural of AYYAM اَيّم meaning widow or widower used for both gender.meaning women without husband,or husband without wife,AMA YAIYMU AYMAN AYUMAN AIMATAN(اَمَ يَئِيْمُ اَيْماً اَيُوْماً اَئِمَّةً)meaning man or woman become widow,and TAAYYUM(تَاَيُّم) meaning to remain unmarried for a long time,to be single. ALLAH said

Surat An-Nūr (The Light) - سورة النور

24:32

وَأَنكِحُوا الْأَيَامَىٰ مِنكُمْ وَالصَّالِحِينَ مِنْ عِبَادِكُمْ وَإِمَائِكُمْ ۚ إِن يَكُونُوا فُقَرَاءَ يُغْنِهِمُ اللَّهُ مِن فَضْلِهِ ۗ وَاللَّهُ وَاسِعٌ عَلِيمٌ

And marry the unmarried among you and the righteous among your male slaves and female slaves. If they should be poor, Allah will enrich them from His bounty, and Allah is all-Encompassing and Knowing.

2 B3YR( بعير):Actually IBL(ابل)is used as ISM JINS(اسم جنس) for camel and B3YR is such a camel whis is 4 year old young,able for transportation and powerful.with this condition B3YR can be used as ISM JINS and comes for both gender. even though in the lexicon for she-camel there is word for NAQAH(ناقة). so B3YR means young,transport,powerful whether camel or she-camel. In the Quran

Surat Yūsuf (Joseph) - سورة يوسف

12:72

قَالُوا نَفْقِدُ صُوَاعَ الْمَلِكِ وَلِمَن جَاءَ بِهِ حِمْلُ بَعِيرٍ وَأَنَا بِهِ زَعِيمٌ

They said, "We are missing the measure of the king. And for he who produces it is [the reward of] a camel's load, and I am responsible for it."

3 BAQR(بقر): is ISM JINS.which applies to Cow,Ox,whether of any age on all of them and BAQR ALWHSH( بقر الوحش) meaning swamp deer,ibex,wild ox,blue bull and BAQRAH(بقرة)used as singular. and from lexical point of view used for both gender. but for ox as there is separate entry in lexicon of CWR(ثور). so BAQRAH generally means cow. and plural of BAQRAH is BAQRATبقرات,BUQURبُقُر,ABQURاَبْقُ ر,ABQARابقار,ABAQIRاَبَاقِر and ABAQIYR(اَبَاقِيْر). cow's owner and shepherd is called BAQQAR(بقّار). and herd of cows BAQR(باقر),BAQUR(باقور),BAYQWR(بيقور ). In the Quran

Surat Yūsuf (Joseph) - سورة يوسف

12:43

وَقَالَ الْمَلِكُ إِنِّي أَرَىٰ سَبْعَ بَقَرَاتٍ سِمَانٍ يَأْكُلُهُنَّ سَبْعٌ عِجَافٌ وَسَبْعَ سُنبُلَاتٍ خُضْرٍ وَأُخَرَ يَابِسَاتٍ ۖ يَا أَيُّهَا الْمَلَأُ أَفْتُونِي فِي رُؤْيَايَ إِن كُنتُمْ لِلرُّؤْيَا تَعْبُرُونَ

And [subsequently] the king said, "Indeed, I have seen [in a dream] seven fat cows being eaten by seven [that were] lean, and seven green spikes [of grain] and others [that were] dry. O eminent ones, explain to me my vision, if you should interpret visions."

4 BIKR(بِكْر): meaning virgin male or female,used for both. even for animals and its both genders. In the Quran

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة

2:68

قَالُوا ادْعُ لَنَا رَبَّكَ يُبَيِّن لَّنَا مَا هِيَ ۚ قَالَ إِنَّهُ يَقُولُ إِنَّهَا بَقَرَةٌ لَّا فَارِضٌ وَلَا بِكْرٌ عَوَانٌ بَيْنَ ذَٰلِكَ ۖ فَافْعَلُوا مَا تُؤْمَرُونَ

They said, "Call upon your Lord to make clear to us what it is." [Moses] said, "[ Allah ] says, 'It is a cow which is neither old nor virgin, but median between that,' so do what you are commanded."

5 CAYYIB(ثَيِّب): (antonym BIKR)that which is not virgin whether male or female.CAYYIB is used in wide meanings and come for both male and female.married man and woman are also CAYYIB,divorced woman is also CAYYIB,and widow and widower is also CAYYIB. so any male or female who has copulated is now CAYYIB. RAJL CAYYIB( رجل ثَيِّب) meaning married man,husband.meaning woman separated from husband whether due to husband's death or from divorce. ALLAH said

Surat At-Taĥrīm (The Prohibtiion) - سورة التحريم

66:5

عَسَىٰ رَبُّهُ إِن طَلَّقَكُنَّ أَن يُبْدِلَهُ أَزْوَاجًا خَيْرًا مِّنكُنَّ مُسْلِمَاتٍ مُّؤْمِنَاتٍ قَانِتَاتٍ تَائِبَاتٍ عَابِدَاتٍ سَائِحَاتٍ ثَيِّبَاتٍ وَأَبْكَارًا

Perhaps his Lord, if he divorced you [all], would substitute for him wives better than you - submitting [to Allah ], believing, devoutly obedient, repentant, worshipping, and traveling - [ones] previously married and virgins.

In this Ayah CAYYIB's meaning is limited to only 'without husband' and from SAHIH MUSLIM ,BOOK OF LIMITS as per 3BADA BIN SAMAT's narration it is obvious that any man or women who is not BIKR is CAYYIB

6 KHDN(خدن):(Plural AKHDAN اخدان) meaning secret friends,chums,those man or women who commit adultery in secret. and in QURAN this word is used for both adulterous man and woman. ALLAH said

Surat Al-Mā'idah (The Table Spread) - سورة المائدة

5:5

الْيَوْمَ أُحِلَّ لَكُمُ الطَّيِّبَاتُ ۖ وَطَعَامُ الَّذِينَ أُوتُوا الْكِتَابَ حِلٌّ لَّكُمْ وَطَعَامُكُمْ حِلٌّ لَّهُمْ ۖ وَالْمُحْصَنَاتُ مِنَ الْمُؤْمِنَاتِ وَالْمُحْصَنَاتُ مِنَ الَّذِينَ أُوتُوا الْكِتَابَ مِن قَبْلِكُمْ إِذَا آتَيْتُمُوهُنَّ أُجُورَهُنَّ مُحْصِنِينَ غَيْرَ مُسَافِحِينَ وَلَا مُتَّخِذِي أَخْدَانٍ ۗ وَمَن يَكْفُرْ بِالْإِيمَانِ فَقَدْ حَبِطَ عَمَلُهُ وَهُوَ فِي الْآخِرَةِ مِنَ الْخَاسِرِينَ

This day [all] good foods have been made lawful, and the food of those who were given the Scripture is lawful for you and your food is lawful for them. And [lawful in marriage are] chaste women from among the believers and chaste women from among those who were given the Scripture before you, when you have given them their due compensation, desiring chastity, not unlawful sexual intercourse or taking [secret] lovers. And whoever denies the faith - his work has become worthless, and he, in the Hereafter, will be among the losers.

and on a second place.

Surat An-Nisā' (The Women) - سورة النساء

4:25

وَمَن لَّمْ يَسْتَطِعْ مِنكُمْ طَوْلًا أَن يَنكِحَ الْمُحْصَنَاتِ الْمُؤْمِنَاتِ فَمِن مَّا مَلَكَتْ أَيْمَانُكُم مِّن فَتَيَاتِكُمُ الْمُؤْمِنَاتِ ۚ وَاللَّهُ أَعْلَمُ بِإِيمَانِكُم ۚ بَعْضُكُم مِّن بَعْضٍ ۚ فَانكِحُوهُنَّ بِإِذْنِ أَهْلِهِنَّ وَآتُوهُنَّ أُجُورَهُنَّ بِالْمَعْرُوفِ مُحْصَنَاتٍ غَيْرَ مُسَافِحَاتٍ وَلَا مُتَّخِذَاتِ أَخْدَانٍ ۚ فَإِذَا أُحْصِنَّ فَإِنْ أَتَيْنَ بِفَاحِشَةٍ فَعَلَيْهِنَّ نِصْفُ مَا عَلَى الْمُحْصَنَاتِ مِنَ الْعَذَابِ ۚ ذَٰلِكَ لِمَنْ خَشِيَ الْعَنَتَ مِنكُمْ ۚ وَأَن تَصْبِرُوا خَيْرٌ لَّكُمْ ۗ وَاللَّهُ غَفُورٌ رَّحِيمٌ

And whoever among you cannot [find] the means to marry free, believing women, then [he may marry] from those whom your right hands possess of believing slave girls. And Allah is most knowing about your faith. You [believers] are of one another. So marry them with the permission of their people and give them their due compensation according to what is acceptable. [They should be] chaste, neither [of] those who commit unlawful intercourse randomly nor those who take [secret] lovers. But once they are sheltered in marriage, if they should commit adultery, then for them is half the punishment for free [unmarried] women. This [allowance] is for him among you who fears sin, but to be patient is better for you. And Allah is Forgiving and Merciful.

7 DAAAABAH(دَآبَّة):is used in very wide meanings. DABBA(دَبَّ) literally eans HARKAH 3LY ALARD AKHFF MIN ALMASHY(حركة علي الارض اخفّ من المشي) meaning to walk slower than the normal pace on the earth and meaning crawling.to wallk like snake or toddler that learns to walk on hands and feets all are included in DABBA. from this point of view any living thing that walks on the surface of the earth is encompassed by the word DABBAH. [These shares are] an obligation [imposed] by Allah . Indeed, Allah is ever Knowing and Wise.
