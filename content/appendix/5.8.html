8. HUROOF KA F3L KAY ZAMANAY PAR ASAR

[Effect of Particles on the Tense of Verb]

1 QAD(قد):Its a particle of research(Proven) . When it enters on past tense .it converts it in to near past. and itself gves the meaning of Indeed, Certainly. In the Quran

Surat Al-Mujādila (The Pleading Woman) - سورة المجادلة

58:1

قَدْ سَمِعَ اللَّهُ قَوْلَ الَّتِي تُجَادِلُكَ فِي زَوْجِهَا وَتَشْتَكِي إِلَى اللَّهِ وَاللَّهُ يَسْمَعُ تَحَاوُرَكُمَا ۚ إِنَّ اللَّهَ سَمِيعٌ بَصِيرٌ

Certainly has Allah heard the speech of the one who argues with you, [O Muhammad], concerning her husband and directs her complaint to Allah . And Allah hears your dialogue; indeed, Allah is Hearing and Seeing.

and when it enters on MUDARI مضارع (Present-Future)then in it gives the meaning in verb of TAQLEEL(تقليل)[reduction].meaning sometimes that verb occurs and sometimes not. as said

Surat An-Nūr (The Light) - سورة النور

24:63

لَّا تَجْعَلُوا دُعَاءَ الرَّسُولِ بَيْنَكُمْ كَدُعَاءِ بَعْضِكُم بَعْضًا ۚ قَدْ يَعْلَمُ اللَّهُ الَّذِينَ يَتَسَلَّلُونَ مِنكُمْ لِوَاذًا ۚ فَلْيَحْذَرِ الَّذِينَ يُخَالِفُونَ عَنْ أَمْرِهِ أَن تُصِيبَهُمْ فِتْنَةٌ أَوْ يُصِيبَهُمْ عَذَابٌ أَلِيمٌ

Do not make [your] calling of the Messenger among yourselves as the call of one of you to another. Already Allah knows those of you who slip away, concealed by others. So let those beware who dissent from the Prophet's order, lest fitnah strike them or a painful punishment.

meaning if someone slips away then ALLAH certainly has its knowledge.

2 KANA(كَانَ):It comes as perfect verb and as imperfect verb as well. as imperfect if it enters on other past verb then it makes it far past. like KANA ZAHABA(كاَنَ ذَهَبَ) He had gone. and if it enter MUDARI then it converts it to MADI ISTIMRAR (Past Conitnuous)[something that was habitualy done in the past]. as in the Quran

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة

2:59

فَبَدَّلَ الَّذِينَ ظَلَمُوا قَوْلًا غَيْرَ الَّذِي قِيلَ لَهُمْ فَأَنزَلْنَا عَلَى الَّذِينَ ظَلَمُوا رِجْزًا مِّنَ السَّمَاءِ بِمَا كَانُوا يَفْسُقُونَ

But those who wronged changed [those words] to a statement other than that which had been said to them, so We sent down upon those who wronged a punishment from the sky because they were defiantly disobeying.

3 LAM(لَمْ):enters on MUDARI to convert it into negative past. In the Quran

Surat Al-Ĥujurāt (The Rooms) - سورة الحجرات

49:14

قَالَتِ الْأَعْرَابُ آمَنَّا ۖ قُل لَّمْ تُؤْمِنُوا وَلَٰكِن قُولُوا أَسْلَمْنَا وَلَمَّا يَدْخُلِ الْإِيمَانُ فِي قُلُوبِكُمْ ۖ وَإِن تُطِيعُوا اللَّهَ وَرَسُولَهُ لَا يَلِتْكُم مِّنْ أَعْمَالِكُمْ شَيْئًا ۚ إِنَّ اللَّهَ غَفُورٌ رَّحِيمٌ

The bedouins say, "We have believed." Say, "You have not [yet] believed; but say [instead], 'We have submitted,' for faith has not yet entered your hearts. And if you obey Allah and His Messenger, He will not deprive you from your deeds of anything. Indeed, Allah is Forgiving and Merciful."

adding MA(ما) after LAM gives the meaning of "not yet" as in the ayah above

like LAM , LAMMA(لمّا) also make MUDARI ,negative past and it contains the meaning of ISTAGHRAQ( استغراق)[Immersion/Encompass] which is not found in LAM. To make negative past ,interrogative HUMZA(أ) will come before LAM. ALLAH said

Did He not find you an orphan and give [you] refuge?

4 LAN(لَنْ):enters on MUDARI to make it negative future with emphasis. meaning it gives three changes. a)limits MUDARI to future b)negate it c)this negation is emphatic. like LAYYADRIBA (لَنْ يَّضْرِبَ) meaning He will never going to strike.

In the Quran

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة

2:55

وَإِذْ قُلْتُمْ يَا مُوسَىٰ لَن نُّؤْمِنَ لَكَ حَتَّىٰ نَرَى اللَّهَ جَهْرَةً فَأَخَذَتْكُمُ الصَّاعِقَةُ وَأَنتُمْ تَنظُرُونَ

And [recall] when you said, "O Moses, we will never believe you until we see Allah outright"; so the thunderbolt took you while you were looking on.

5 LAW(لَوْ):(meaning if)enter on MADI makes it past conditional, and on it's answer ('then' part of the condition) starts with LA( لَ) LAM MAFTUHA(Letter Lam with Fatha ) meaning 'then'.In the Quran

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة

2:103

وَلَوْ أَنَّهُمْ آمَنُوا وَاتَّقَوْا لَمَثُوبَةٌ مِّنْ عِندِ اللَّهِ خَيْرٌ ۖ لَّوْ كَانُوا يَعْلَمُونَ

And if they had believed and feared Allah , then the reward from Allah would have been [far] better, if they only knew.

6 IN(اِنْ):(meaning if)enters on MUDARI to make it conditional and limit it to future.and on its 'then' part also LA (LAM MAFTUHA) enters as in the Quran

Surat Yā-Sīn (Ya Sin) - سورة يس

They said, "Indeed, we consider you a bad omen. If you do not desist, we will surely stone you, and there will surely touch you, from us, a painful punishment."

7 LAYTA(لَيْتَ):(meaning "I wish")enters on past to make it wishful past. and it applies to such a matter which is impossible or atleast difficult. In the Quran

Surat An-Naba' (The Tidings) - سورة النبإ

78:40

إِنَّا أَنذَرْنَاكُمْ عَذَابًا قَرِيبًا يَوْمَ يَنظُرُ الْمَرْءُ مَا قَدَّمَتْ يَدَاهُ وَيَقُولُ الْكَافِرُ يَا لَيْتَنِي كُنتُ تُرَابًا

Indeed, We have warned you of a near punishment on the Day when a man will observe what his hands have put forth and the disbeliever will say, "Oh, I wish that I were dust!"

like (optative sentence )wishful past , LAYTA also converts MUDARI to wishful past. the difference between LAYTA and LAW is that LAYTA comes normally for impossible matters whereas LAW for something that is possible. as said
