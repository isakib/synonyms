2.1.10 TIYN(تِيْن): meaning figg's tree and its fruit as well and its singular is TIYNAHتِيْنة and MATANAH مَتَانَة meaning figg's orchard or such a place where figg's trees are in abundance. and TAYYAN تَيَّان meaning figg's seller or figg's merchant

2.1.11 ZAYTUN(زَيْتُون): A tree from which Olive oil is extracted and its fruit is called ZAYTUNAH(زَيْتُوْنة) and its oil is ZAYT(زَيْت). then ZAYT was getting used to all types of oils from whatever the source it may be obtained from . and ZAYYAT(زَيّاَت) meaning the oil extractor or oil seller. In the Quran

Surat At-Tīn (The Fig) - سورة التين

95:1

وَالتِّينِ وَالزَّيْتُونِ

By the fig and the olive

2.1.12 SIDR(سِدْر) and SIDRAH(سِدْرَة ): both are synonyms meaning berry tree whose fruit is of no importance but its shadow is dense and owing to spread of tree it spreads out wide and far. that's why its counted as one of the blessing of paradise. plural of SIDR is SUDURسُدُوْر and of SIDRAH is SIDRATسِدْرات

2.1.13 TALH(طَلْح): singular TALHAH طَلْحَة meaning banana ,blooming date tree and TALHAH meaning SHAJR ALMAWZ شَجَر المَوْز meaning banana tree.In the tree

14 RUMMAN(رُمَّان):singular RUMMANAH رُمَّانة ,meaning pomegranate tree and its fruit. and MARMANAH مَرْمَنَة meaning place where pomegranate grows. In the Quran

Surat Al-'An`ām (The Cattle) - سورة الأنعام

6:141

وَهُوَ الَّذِي أَنشَأَ جَنَّاتٍ مَّعْرُوشَاتٍ وَغَيْرَ مَعْرُوشَاتٍ وَالنَّخْلَ وَالزَّرْعَ مُخْتَلِفًا أُكُلُهُ وَالزَّيْتُونَ وَالرُّمَّانَ مُتَشَابِهًا وَغَيْرَ مُتَشَابِهٍ ۚ كُلُوا مِن ثَمَرِهِ إِذَا أَثْمَرَ وَآتُوا حَقَّهُ يَوْمَ حَصَادِهِ ۖ وَلَا تُسْرِفُوا ۚ إِنَّهُ لَا يُحِبُّ الْمُسْرِفِينَ

And He it is who causes gardens to grow, [both] trellised and untrellised, and palm trees and crops of different [kinds of] food and olives and pomegranates, similar and dissimilar. Eat of [each of] its fruit when it yields and give its due [zakah] on the day of its harvest. And be not excessive. Indeed, He does not like those who commit excess.

15 3INAB(عِنَب):meaning grape vine and its fruit ,plural A3NAB(اعناب) and a single grape of a bunch is 3INBAH(عِنْبَة). and 3ANNAB(عَنَّاب) meaning grape seller in the Quran

Surat Al-'An`ām (The Cattle) - سورة الأنعام

6:99

وَهُوَ الَّذِي أَنزَلَ مِنَ السَّمَاءِ مَاءً فَأَخْرَجْنَا بِهِ نَبَاتَ كُلِّ شَيْءٍ فَأَخْرَجْنَا مِنْهُ خَضِرًا نُّخْرِجُ مِنْهُ حَبًّا مُّتَرَاكِبًا وَمِنَ النَّخْلِ مِن طَلْعِهَا قِنْوَانٌ دَانِيَةٌ وَجَنَّاتٍ مِّنْ أَعْنَابٍ وَالزَّيْتُونَ وَالرُّمَّانَ مُشْتَبِهًا وَغَيْرَ مُتَشَابِهٍ ۗ انظُرُوا إِلَىٰ ثَمَرِهِ إِذَا أَثْمَرَ وَيَنْعِهِ ۚ إِنَّ فِي ذَٰلِكُمْ لَآيَاتٍ لِّقَوْمٍ يُؤْمِنُونَ

And it is He who sends down rain from the sky, and We produce thereby the growth of all things. We produce from it greenery from which We produce grains arranged in layers. And from the palm trees - of its emerging fruit are clusters hanging low. And [We produce] gardens of grapevines and olives and pomegranates, similar yet varied. Look at [each of] its fruit when it yields and [at] its ripening. Indeed in that are signs for a people who believe.
