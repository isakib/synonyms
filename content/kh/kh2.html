SUMMARY:

following are the differences between KHABR and NABA

a) KHABR is common,NABA is particular.-NABA is that KHABR which is pertinent to the listener and it can effect him.

as per Imam Raghib words important news-it is said that 'this matter can be NABA for someone'.it is not said that 'it can be KHABR'.

b)NABA can relate to future as well as paranormal whereas KHABR only relates to past or present.

c)ANBAA and NABAA implies 'to inform'. and AKHBARAاَخْبَرَ implies 'to announce'. it is said HAZA ALAMRU YUNBI-U KAZA هَذَا الاَمْرُ يُنْبِئُ كَذَا (this matter gives NABA of such a matter) and it is not said TUKHBIRU KAZAتُخْبِرُ كَذَا it gives KHABR of this matter. and KHUBR is that KHABR which proves true after research/verification.
