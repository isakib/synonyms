18 KHWSH HALIY [Prosperity]

SYNONYMS:SARRA- سَرَّاء NA3MA- نَعْمَاءTUBA طُوبَي

18.1 SARRA-: antonym is DARRA- ضَرَّاء which means poverty and period of suffering.and SARRAAA-I means that time which passes with peace and comfort.and also absence of financial constraint. meaning such an era of convenience and peace and prosperity in which delight/joy is obtained.ALLAH said

Surat 'Āli `Imrān (Family of Imran) - سورة آل عمران

3:134

الَّذِينَ يُنفِقُونَ فِي السَّرَّاءِ وَالضَّرَّاءِ وَالْكَاظِمِينَ الْغَيْظَ وَالْعَافِينَ عَنِ النَّاسِ ۗ وَاللَّهُ يُحِبُّ الْمُحْسِنِينَ

Who spend [in the cause of Allah ] during ease and hardship and who restrain anger and who pardon the people - and Allah loves the doers of good;

18.2 NA3MAAA-نَعْمآء-:NI3MATنِعْمَت meaning good state,good and NA3MATنََعْمَت meaning comfort and luxury and NI3MAT meaning benefit ,grace-AN3AM and NA3MAT meaning betterment-convenience-wealth-cattle and NU3MAH نُعْمَةmeaning happiness,and NU3MAAA- is such a period of prosperity in which there is excess of divine blssings.and those blessings would be seen by others as well.its antonym is DARRAA-. ALLAH said

Surat Hūd (Hud) - سورة هود

11:10

وَلَئِنْ أَذَقْنَاهُ نَعْمَاءَ بَعْدَ ضَرَّاءَ مَسَّتْهُ لَيَقُولَنَّ ذَهَبَ السَّيِّئَاتُ عَنِّي ۚ إِنَّهُ لَفَرِحٌ فَخُورٌ

But if We give him a taste of favor after hardship has touched him, he will surely say, "Bad times have left me." Indeed, he is exultant and boastful -

18.3 TUBA:TABA طَابَ meaning to obtain such a joy which is enjoyed by heart as well as the other senses of the human as well. and TAYYIB طَيِّب meaning purt and TIYB طِيْب meaning fragrance. and TUBA طُوبَي meaning such a prosperity in which heart is satisfied and joyful as well.ALLAH said

Surat Ar-Ra`d (The Thunder) - سورة الرعد

13:29

الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا وَعَمِلُوا الصَّالِحَاتِ طُوبَىٰ لَهُمْ وَحُسْنُ مَآبٍ

Those who have believed and done righteous deeds - a good state is theirs and a good return.

SUMMARY:

18.1 SARRA-:peace and bliss and normal living

18.2 NA3MA-:excess of blessing and comfort and luxury

18.3 TUBA:when along with prosperity, heart is also satisfied
