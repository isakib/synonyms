3 KHATAM HUWNA [To be finished]

SYNONYMS:NAFAD نفد KHATAM ختم

3.1 NAFADA:some thing being finished,goes out of existence.-to annihilate- and its antonym is BAQIYA بَقِيَ in the QURAN.

Surat An-Naĥl (The Bee) - سورة النحل

16:96

مَا عِندَكُمْ يَنفَدُ ۖ وَمَا عِندَ اللَّهِ بَاقٍ ۗ وَلَنَجْزِيَنَّ الَّذِينَ صَبَرُوا أَجْرَهُم بِأَحْسَنِ مَا كَانُوا يَعْمَلُونَ

Whatever you have will end, but what Allah has is lasting. And We will surely give those who were patient their reward according to the best of what they used to do

3.2 KHATAMA:To close and then sealed something after it's completion so that it cannot be varied excess or lessen.AAKHIR ALAMR آخر الامر and KHATAM AL3AML ختم العمل meaning to finish some deed and KHATAM ALKITAB ختم الكتاب meaning read entire book.ALLAH said

Surat Al-'Aĥzāb (The Combined Forces) - سورة الأحزاب

33:40

مَّا كَانَ مُحَمَّدٌ أَبَا أَحَدٍ مِّن رِّجَالِكُمْ وَلَٰكِن رَّسُولَ اللَّهِ وَخَاتَمَ النَّبِيِّينَ ۗ وَكَانَ اللَّهُ بِكُلِّ شَيْءٍ عَلِيمًا

Muhammad is not the father of [any] one of your men, but [he is] the Messenger of Allah and last of the prophets. And ever is Allah , of all things, Knowing.
