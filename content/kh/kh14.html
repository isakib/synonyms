14 KHWAHISH [Desire]

SYNONYMS:AMLامْل UMNIYAHاُمْنِيَة HAWAهَوَي SHAHWAHشَهْوَة WATARوَطَرَ

for 14.1 AML-14.2 UMNIYAH:please see AARZOO AWR UMIYD LAGANA [To wish and have hope]

14.3 HAWA:(plural AHWA- اهواء) meaning desire of self.and inclination towards them.HAWA YAHWA هوي يهوي meaning to fall from top.and ALMAHWAHالمهواة is pit. and AHWA- is those desires by following which human lost his value and status.this word is normally used in bad context.as per Ibn-e-Faris HAWA ANNAFSU KHALIN MIN KULLI KHAYRIN(هَوَي النَّفْسُ خَالٍ مِّنْ كُلِّ خَيْرٍ) meaning desires of self,thos desires in which there is no good.ALLAH said

Surat An-Nāzi`āt (Those who drag forth) - سورة النازعات

79:40

وَأَمَّا مَنْ خَافَ مَقَامَ رَبِّهِ وَنَهَى النَّفْسَ عَنِ الْهَوَىٰ

But as for he who feared the position of his Lord and prevented the soul from [unlawful] inclination,

Surat An-Nāzi`āt (Those who drag forth) - سورة النازعات

79:41

فَإِنَّ الْجَنَّةَ هِيَ الْمَأْوَىٰ

Then indeed, Paradise will be [his] refuge.

14.4 SHAHWAH:desires of human natural needs.plural SHAHWATشَهْوَات such desires towards which human self keeps on attracted.like hunger,thurst,greed of wealth,sexual desires.whether their usage is good or bad.and that has nothing to do with human intention.In the QURAN

Surat 'Āli `Imrān (Family of Imran) - سورة آل عمران

3:14

زُيِّنَ لِلنَّاسِ حُبُّ الشَّهَوَاتِ مِنَ النِّسَاءِ وَالْبَنِينَ وَالْقَنَاطِيرِ الْمُقَنطَرَةِ مِنَ الذَّهَبِ وَالْفِضَّةِ وَالْخَيْلِ الْمُسَوَّمَةِ وَالْأَنْعَامِ وَالْحَرْثِ ۗ ذَٰلِكَ مَتَاعُ الْحَيَاةِ الدُّنْيَا ۖ وَاللَّهُ عِندَهُ حُسْنُ الْمَآبِ

Beautified for people is the love of that which they desire - of women and sons, heaped-up sums of gold and silver, fine branded horses, and cattle and tilled land. That is the enjoyment of worldly life, but Allah has with Him the best return.

SHAW YASHAWشهو يشهو meaning meal being tasty.and ISHTAHAاشتها word is peculiarly used for the demand of eatable and drinkable things or hunger.In the QURAN

Surat Fuşşilat (Explained in Detail) - سورة فصلت

41:31

نَحْنُ أَوْلِيَاؤُكُمْ فِي الْحَيَاةِ الدُّنْيَا وَفِي الْآخِرَةِ ۖ وَلَكُمْ فِيهَا مَا تَشْتَهِي أَنفُسُكُمْ وَلَكُمْ فِيهَا مَا تَدَّعُونَ

We [angels] were your allies in worldly life and [are so] in the Hereafter. And you will have therein whatever your souls desire, and you will have therein whatever you request [or wish]

14.5 WATAR:intense desire of something and important/indispensable need.intercourse,lust.In QURAN this word is used for sexual desire. It is said

Surat Al-'Aĥzāb (The Combined Forces) - سورة الأحزاب

33:37

وَإِذْ تَقُولُ لِلَّذِي أَنْعَمَ اللَّهُ عَلَيْهِ وَأَنْعَمْتَ عَلَيْهِ أَمْسِكْ عَلَيْكَ زَوْجَكَ وَاتَّقِ اللَّهَ وَتُخْفِي فِي نَفْسِكَ مَا اللَّهُ مُبْدِيهِ وَتَخْشَى النَّاسَ وَاللَّهُ أَحَقُّ أَن تَخْشَاهُ ۖ فَلَمَّا قَضَىٰ زَيْدٌ مِّنْهَا وَطَرًا زَوَّجْنَاكَهَا لِكَيْ لَا يَكُونَ عَلَى الْمُؤْمِنِينَ حَرَجٌ فِي أَزْوَاجِ أَدْعِيَائِهِمْ إِذَا قَضَوْا مِنْهُنَّ وَطَرًا ۚ وَكَانَ أَمْرُ اللَّهِ مَفْعُولًا

And [remember, O Muhammad], when you said to the one on whom Allah bestowed favor and you bestowed favor, "Keep your wife and fear Allah ," while you concealed within yourself that which Allah is to disclose. And you feared the people, while Allah has more right that you fear Him. So when Zayd had no longer any need for her, We married her to you in order that there not be upon the believers any discomfort concerning the wives of their adopted sons when they no longer have need of them. And ever is the command of Allah accomplished.

SUMMARY:

14.1 AML:such a desire which would require long period

14.2 UMNIYAH:baseless and false desire

14.3 HAWA:bad/evil desires or bad use of natural desires

14.4 SHAHWAH:intense desire and indispensable need. particularly intercourse

14.5 WATAR:natural human desires
