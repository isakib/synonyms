15 KHAWBSUWRAT [BEAUTY]

SYNONYMS: BAHIYJبَهِيْج NADIRAHنَاضِرَة HISANحِسَان

15.1 BAHIYJ:meaning to be beautiful. and ABHAJ ALMAKANابهج المكان meaning something being lush green and BAHJAH بَهْجَة meaning beauty-greenery-joy-happiness or expression of happiness.so BAHJAH word commonly used for beauty of plantation.such a beauty which cheers heart.and BAHIYJ meaning beauty,booming,blooming.ALLAH said

Surat Qāf (The Letter "Qaf") - سورة ق

50:7

وَالْأَرْضَ مَدَدْنَاهَا وَأَلْقَيْنَا فِيهَا رَوَاسِيَ وَأَنبَتْنَا فِيهَا مِن كُلِّ زَوْجٍ بَهِيجٍ

And the earth - We spread it out and cast therein firmly set mountains and made grow therein [something] of every beautiful kind,

15.2 NADIRAH:NADARA نَضَرَ meaning to be refreshed.and NADARA ALWAJH نَضَرَ الْوَجْه meaning face being soft,fresh and beautiful.and NADRAH نَضْرَةmeaning beauty-boom-bliss and NADIR نَاضِر meaning beautiful-soft and delicate.this word is also used for the beauty of refreshing plantation yet it also applies to the beauty of human face which is owing to refereshness and softness.ALLAH said

Surat Al-Qiyāmah (The Resurrection) - سورة القيامة

75:22

وُجُوهٌ يَوْمَئِذٍ نَّاضِرَةٌ

[Some] faces, that Day, will be radiant,

Surat Al-Qiyāmah (The Resurrection) - سورة القيامة

75:23

إِلَىٰ رَبِّهَا نَاظِرَةٌ

Looking at their Lord.

15.3 HISAN:HASUNA حَسُنَ meaning to be beautiful.and HASIYNحَسِيْن meaning beautiful-prettiness.feminine HASANAHحَسَنَة and HASANA-حَسَنَاء(plural HISAN) and HUSNحُسْن meaning beauty-grace. and MAHASINمَحَاسِن meaning beautiful places of body.ALLAH said

Surat Ar-Raĥmān (The Beneficent) - سورة الرحمن

55:70

فِيهِنَّ خَيْرَاتٌ حِسَانٌ

In them are good and beautiful women -

SUMMARY:

15.1 BAHIYJ:commonly applies to beauty of plantation

15.2 NADIRAH:for beauty of face owing to softness and refreshness

15.3 HISAN:used for being favourite and good looking
