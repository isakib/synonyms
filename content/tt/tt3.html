3. THANDA HONA – KARNA

[ To be Cold, To Cool ]

Synonyms: BARADA, QARRA.

3.1 BARADA (بَرَدَ): means to be cold, and bard. BUROODAT means “coldness” or “beng chilly”. And BARAD means “hailstones”. BURIDATIL ARD means “hailstones falling on the earth” and Baarid means “anything cold”.

__________________________

Surah Saad

38:42

ارْكُضْ بِرِجْلِكَ هَذَا مُغْتَسَلٌ بَارِدٌ وَشَرَابٌ

(Allâh said to him): "Strike the ground with your foot: This is (a spring of) water to wash in, cool and a (refreshing) drink.”

____________________________

3.2 QARRA (قَرِّ): qarral yawm means the “cooling of day”. YAWMUN QARRA means “a cold day”. LAYLATUN QARRATUN means “cold night”. Then it can also be used in an idiomatic way such as in QURRATUN 3YNAHU which means “the coolness of the eyes”. To gain the wish and happiness of the heart. In a manner of speaking, QARRA is used for a coolness which is also pleasant.

___________________________

Surah Maryam

19:26

فَكُلِي وَاشْرَبِي وَقَرِّي عَيْنًا

So (O Maryam) eat and drink and cool your eyes.

___________________________

Summary:

3.1 BARAD means coldness

3.2 QARRA is a coolness which is pleasant and a source of peace.
