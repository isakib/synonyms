3 THANDA HUWNA-KARNA [To be Cool,To Cool]

SYNONYMS:BARADA(بَرَدَ),QARRA(قَرَّ)

3.1 BARADA(بَرَدَ):meaning to be cooled,and BARD(بَرْد)-BURUDAT(بُرُوْدَت)meaning cold or winter. and BARAD meaning hail.BURIDATI ALARD(بُرِدَتِ الارض) meaning hail shower on earth.and BARID(بارد) meaning any cool thing.ALLAH said

Surat Şād (The Letter "Saad") - سورة ص

38:42

ارْكُضْ بِرِجْلِكَ ۖ هَٰذَا مُغْتَسَلٌ بَارِدٌ وَشَرَابٌ

[So he was told], "Strike [the ground] with your foot; this is a [spring for] a cool bath and drink."

3.2 QARRA(قَرَّ ):QARRA ALYAUWM(قَرَّ الْيَوْم)meaning cooling of day.YAUWMUN QARRUN(يَوْمٌ قَرٌّ)meaning cold day. LAYLATUN QIRRATUN(لَيْلَةٌ قرَّةٌ)meaning cold night. then it is also used as literal/abstract.for e.g QARRAT(قَرَّتْ )[QURATUN QURRATUN (قُرَةٌ و قُرَّةٌ )]i,3AYNUHU(عَيْنُهُ) meaning someone eyes getting cooled.happiness of heart and getting one's desire.so such a coolness which is also pleasant then it's QARRA. ALLAH said

Surat Maryam (Mary) - سورة مريم

19:26

فَكُلِي وَاشْرَبِي وَقَرِّي عَيْنًا ۖ فَإِمَّا تَرَيِنَّ مِنَ الْبَشَرِ أَحَدًا فَقُولِي إِنِّي نَذَرْتُ لِلرَّحْمَٰنِ صَوْمًا فَلَنْ أُكَلِّمَ الْيَوْمَ إِنسِيًّا

So eat and drink and be contented. And if you see from among humanity anyone, say, 'Indeed, I have vowed to the Most Merciful abstention, so I will not speak today to [any] man.' "

SUMMARY:

3.1 BARADA is common for coolness

3.2 QARRA is such coolness which is pleasant and a means of peace and tranquility
