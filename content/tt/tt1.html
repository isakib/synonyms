1- TUKDRA [Piece/ section]

Synonyms:- JUZ-, QITA3, BUKAH, KISFA, ZUBAR, ANKAC, 3IDIYIN(3A-DA-WA), JUZAZ(JUZZ), FIRQ, BAAD.

1.1  JUZ (جُزء): means piece or a seperated portion of a thing. Its antonym is كُلّ'whole'. It means those parts of a thing whose summing up can compile an entire thing. JAZA-ASHAYE (جَزَءَ الشئ) means to divide a thing in different units. To take one portion .

Allah states in Qur'an:-

Surat Al-Baqarah (The Cow) - سورة البقرة

[2:260]

ثُمَّ اجْعَلْ عَلَىٰ كُلِّ جَبَلٍ مِّنْهُنَّ جُزْءًا

Then [after slaughtering them] put on each hill a portion of them.

1.2 QITA3UN(قِطَعٌ): is a plural of QIT3ATUN(قِطَعَةٌ), it means a seperated portion or piece of a thing.QIT3AH(قِطعَه) refers to an anthology of poems in which a poem has four or less then four verses.It is also used for divided land e.g. plot etc.

Allah SWT states in Qur'an:-

Surat Ar-Ra`d (The Thunder) - سورة الرعد

[13:4]

وَفِي الْأَرْضِ قِطَعٌ مُّتَجَاوِرَاتٌ

And within the land are neighboring plots.

If the word QIT3UN(قِطعٌ) is related to night, then it means a portion of night, but most translators descibe it as a last portion of night.

Allah SWT states in Qur'an:-

Surat Hūd (Hud) - سورة هود

[11:81]

فَأَسْرِ بِأَهْلِكَ بِقِطْعٍ مِّنَ اللَّيْلِ

So set out with your family during a portion of the night.

1.3 BUQ3AH (بُقعَة) According to Author of Miqyas-ul-Lugha (arabic lexicon) (in referenece to KHALIL[most probably a lexiconologist]) BUQAHT(بقعة) is a section of land which is prominent as compared to other sections.

It is stated in Qur'an:-

Surat Al-Qaşaş (The Stories) - سورة القصص

[28:30]

فَلَمَّا أَتَاهَا نُودِيَ مِن شَاطِئِ الْوَادِ الْأَيْمَنِ فِي الْبُقْعَةِ الْمُبَارَكَةِ مِنَ الشَّجَرَةِ

But when he came to it, he was called from the right side of the valley in a blessed spot - from the tree.

1.4 KISAF(كِسَف):is plural of KISFAH كِسْفَة. meaning porous bodies like clouds or piece of cotton. which can be separated easily and can be joined easily.In the QURAN.

Surat Ash-Shu`arā' (The Poets) - سورة الشعراء

26:187

فَأَسْقِطْ عَلَيْنَا كِسَفًا مِّنَ السَّمَاءِ إِن كُنتَ مِنَ الصَّادِقِينَ

So cause to fall upon us fragments of the sky, if you should be of the truthful."

1.5 ZUBR(زُبر):is plural of ZUBRAH  زُبْرة.meaning big piece of iron.sheets of iron, slabs,then it applies to every spread out thing whose thickness is less but spread is more for e.g slabs of paper-leafs/pages of book,scrolls etc. In the QURAN.

Surat Al-Kahf (The Cave) - سورة الكهف

18:96

آتُونِي زُبَرَ الْحَدِيدِ ۖ حَتَّىٰ إِذَا سَاوَىٰ بَيْنَ الصَّدَفَيْنِ قَالَ انفُخُوا ۖ حَتَّىٰ إِذَا جَعَلَهُ نَارًا قَالَ آتُونِي أُفْرِغْ عَلَيْهِ قِطْرًا

Bring me sheets of iron" - until, when he had leveled [them] between the two mountain walls, he said, "Blow [with bellows]," until when he had made it [like] fire, he said, "Bring me, that I may pour over it molten copper."

Surat Ash-Shu`arā' (The Poets) - سورة الشعراء

26:196

وَإِنَّهُ لَفِي زُبُرِ الْأَوَّلِينَ

And indeed, it is [mentioned] in the scriptures of former peoples.

there is also literal/abstract usage of ZUBR as well.ALLAH said

1.6 ANKAC(انكاث):is plural of NUKC(نُكْث). meaning to tore blanket or tent for respinning.and NIKKAC(نكّاث) is toring of something which is made up or wrapped up.then because in the process of tearing up such things can also become bad or broken in to pieces.so ANKAC comes in the meaning of such pieces.ALLAH said

Surat An-Naĥl (The Bee) - سورة النحل

16:92

And do not be like she who untwisted her spun thread after it was strong [by] taking your oaths as [means of] deceit between you because one community is more plentiful [in number or wealth] than another community. Allah only tries you thereby. And He will surely make clear to you on the Day of Resurrection that over which you used to differ.

1.7 3ADIYN(عَضِيْن): AL3UDW اَلْعُضْو and AL3IDWاَلْعِضْو meaning part of body,and it's plural is A3DA-.اَعْضاء and 3ADA YA3DUW(عَضَا يَعْضُموا) meaning to make pieces.and AL3IDATU(العِضَةُ) means piece-faction.it's plural is 3IDIYN (عِضِين)and 3IDUWN(عضون). and as per author of MUQYAS ALLUGHA , 3ADANA means to make pieces of something.and from it's derived 3UDW عُضْو and 3IDW عِضْو. pieces of meat-bits/morsels.ALLAH said

1.8 JUZAZ(جُذاذ):JAZZA(جَذّ) meaning cutting,breaking. and JIZZ(جِذّ) is small pieces of thing being cut.its plural is JUZAZ and JAZAZATجذاذات.meaning fine bits or pieces of something being cut.JAZAZAT MIN ALFIDDAH جذاذات من الفضّة meaning particles of silver.and JAZAZ is also said for lump/piece of Gold. so JAZAZ is used for fine pieces or particles of precious metals.ALLAH said

1.9 FIRQ(فِرْق):meaning broken off or cutt of part of something.it includes the connotation of two pieces in general.one cut of detached piece.second that which remains after cutting.and FARQ  فَرْق is also said to the middle path between hair of head or hairdo/coiffure. and FARAQ  فَرَق is also the distane between two frontal teeth.ALLAH said

Summary:

1.1 JUZ-:those parts of something which completes it.

1.2 QIT3AH:piece of part of land,poetry or night

1.3 BUQ3AH:that portion of land which is prominent from others

1.4 KISAF:porous bodies for e.g cloud,cotton or piece of sky

1.5 ZUBUR:sheet or slabs of iron or paper etc.

1.6 ANKAC:spinned cotton or pieces of wool

1.7 3IDIYN:pieces or morsel/chop of meat

1.8 JUZAZ:pieces or particles of precious metals

1.9 FIRQ: generally any one part of the two

1.10 BA3D: for 'part' in comparison with 'whole' and for 'member'or 'some member'in comparison with collection.
