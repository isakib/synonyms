4. THEHERNA

[ to stop ]

Synonyms : SAKANA, RAKADA, JAMADA, RAHWAA, QARRA, WAQAFA.

4.1 SAKANA (سَكَنَ) : to be at rest after a state of motion.

_____________________________

4.2 RAKADA (رَكَدَ) : a thing being constant on its own support after its being in need of something else. RAKADAL MEEZAAN means a balance which comes to rest after being equal. RAKADA ASHAMSH means the sun reaching above our heads and resting there.

______________________________

Surah Ash Shurah

(The Consultation)

42:33

إِن يَشَأْ يُسْكِنِ الرِّيحَ فَيَظْلَلْنَ رَوَاكِدَ عَلَى ظَهْرِهِ

If He wills, He causes the wind to cease, then they would become motionless on the back (of the sea).

______________________________

4.3 JAMADA (جَمَدَ) : non living things becoming completely motionless, indifferent. JAMD AL MA means freezing up of water. JAMD AL DAM means the drying up of blood. And JAMD refers to frozen water.

______________________________

Surah Al Naml

(The Ants)

27:88

وَتَرَى الْجِبَالَ تَحْسَبُهَا جَامِدَةً وَهِيَ تَمُرُّ مَرَّ السَّحَابِ

And you will see the mountains and think them solid, but they shall pass away, as the passing away of the clouds.

______________________________

4.4 RAHAWAA (رَهْوًا) : the stopping of waves or ripples in the sea or on the surface of water.

The other meaning is this : an open space or path in the middle of two heights.

This word is used only once in the whole Quran. And it shows both meanings.

_______________________________

Surah Ad Dukhan

(The Smoke)

44:24

وَاتْرُكْ الْبَحْرَ رَهْوًا إِنَّهُمْ جُندٌ مُّغْرَقُونَ

And leave the sea calm as it is.
