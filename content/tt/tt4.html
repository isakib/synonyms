4 THERNA (BA MA3NY RUKNA) [To Wait (Meaning To Stop)]

SYNONYMS:SAKANA(سَكَنَ),RAKADA(رَكَدَ),JAMADA(جَمَدَ),RAHWAN(رَهْواً),QARRA(قَرَّ),WAQAFA(وَقَفَ)

4.1 SAKANA(سَكَنَ):used for idle after motion

4.2 RAKADA(رَكَدَ):after lack of something, some other thing being established and firm(side note:the agent of change is removed).RAKAD ALMIYZAN(ركد الميزان) meaning weighing balance(N.B.old ones not the new digital one) being idle after being balanced.and RAKADA ASSHAMS(رَكَدَ الشّمس) meaning to know sun as idle above one's head. ALLAH said

Surat Ash-Shūraá (The Consultation) - سورة الشورى

42:33

إِن يَشَأْ يُسْكِنِ الرِّيحَ فَيَظْلَلْنَ رَوَاكِدَ عَلَىٰ ظَهْرِهِ ۚ إِنَّ فِي ذَٰلِكَ لَآيَاتٍ لِّكُلِّ صَبَّارٍ شَكُورٍ

If He willed, He could still the wind, and they would remain motionless on its surface. Indeed in that are signs for everyone patient and grateful.

4.3 JAMADA(جَمَدَ):nonliving things being absolutely motionless.JAMAD ALMA-(جمد الماء) meanign water being frozen. and JAMAD ALDAM(جمد الدم) meaning blood being dry. and JAMD(جَمْد) is said for frozen water.ALLAH said

Surat An-Naml (The Ant) - سورة النمل

27:88

وَتَرَى الْجِبَالَ تَحْسَبُهَا جَامِدَةً وَهِيَ تَمُرُّ مَرَّ السَّحَابِ ۚ صُنْعَ اللَّهِ الَّذِي أَتْقَنَ كُلَّ شَيْءٍ ۚ إِنَّهُ خَبِيرٌ بِمَا تَفْعَلُونَ

And you see the mountains, thinking them rigid, while they will pass as the passing of clouds. [It is] the work of Allah , who perfected all things. Indeed, He is Acquainted with that which you do.

4.4 RAHWAN(رَهْواً):idling of waves or currents or tides on sea or water surface.second lexical meaning is that of open place or path between two heights

In the HOLY QURAN this word is used only once and give both the meanings. ALLAH said

Surat Ad-Dukhān (The Smoke) - سورة الدخان

44:24

وَاتْرُكِ الْبَحْرَ رَهْوًا ۖ إِنَّهُمْ جُندٌ مُّغْرَقُونَ

And leave the sea in stillness. Indeed, they are an army to be drowned."

4.5 QARRA(قَرَّ):To root in at some place, to stay firmly.ALLAH said

Surat Al-Ĥaj (The Pilgrimage) - سورة الحج

22:5

يَا أَيُّهَا النَّاسُ إِن كُنتُمْ فِي رَيْبٍ مِّنَ الْبَعْثِ فَإِنَّا خَلَقْنَاكُم مِّن تُرَابٍ ثُمَّ مِن نُّطْفَةٍ ثُمَّ مِنْ عَلَقَةٍ ثُمَّ مِن مُّضْغَةٍ مُّخَلَّقَةٍ وَغَيْرِ مُخَلَّقَةٍ لِّنُبَيِّنَ لَكُمْ ۚ وَنُقِرُّ فِي الْأَرْحَامِ مَا نَشَاءُ إِلَىٰ أَجَلٍ مُّسَمًّى ثُمَّ نُخْرِجُكُمْ طِفْلًا ثُمَّ لِتَبْلُغُوا أَشُدَّكُمْ ۖ وَمِنكُم مَّن يُتَوَفَّىٰ وَمِنكُم مَّن يُرَدُّ إِلَىٰ أَرْذَلِ الْعُمُرِ لِكَيْلَا يَعْلَمَ مِن بَعْدِ عِلْمٍ شَيْئًا ۚ وَتَرَى الْأَرْضَ هَامِدَةً فَإِذَا أَنزَلْنَا عَلَيْهَا الْمَاءَ اهْتَزَّتْ وَرَبَتْ وَأَنبَتَتْ مِن كُلِّ زَوْجٍ بَهِيجٍ

O People, if you should be in doubt about the Resurrection, then [consider that] indeed, We created you from dust, then from a sperm-drop, then from a clinging clot, and then from a lump of flesh, formed and unformed - that We may show you. And We settle in the wombs whom We will for a specified term, then We bring you out as a child, and then [We develop you] that you may reach your [time of] maturity. And among you is he who is taken in [early] death, and among you is he who is returned to the most decrepit [old] age so that he knows, after [once having] knowledge, nothing. And you see the earth barren, but when We send down upon it rain, it quivers and swells and grows [something] of every beautiful kind.

4.6 WAQAFA(وَقَفَ):To stand motionless,senseless.to stop for a short time while continuously doing work.[SAYYARAT(سيّارات) is bus stand].or such an idleness after which is expected motion or work as before. and usage of this word is both intransitive and transitive.ALLAH said

a) as intransitive

Surat Al-'An`ām (The Cattle) - سورة الأنعام

6:30

وَلَوْ تَرَىٰ إِذْ وُقِفُوا عَلَىٰ رَبِّهِمْ ۚ قَالَ أَلَيْسَ هَٰذَا بِالْحَقِّ ۚ قَالُوا بَلَىٰ وَرَبِّنَا ۚ قَالَ فَذُوقُوا الْعَذَابَ بِمَا كُنتُمْ تَكْفُرُونَ

If you could but see when they will be made to stand before their Lord. He will say, "Is this not the truth?" They will say, "Yes, by our Lord." He will [then] say, "So taste the punishment because you used to disbelieve."

b) as transitive

Surat Aş-Şāffāt (Those who set the Ranks) - سورة الصافات

37:24

وَقِفُوهُمْ ۖ إِنَّهُم مَّسْئُولُونَ

And stop them; indeed, they are to be questioned."

SUMMARY:

4.1 SAKANA:idle after movement

4.2 RAKADA:idling of something in motion after being established and balanced on its own

4.3 JAMADA:to freeze,permanent settlement of nonliving thing

4.4 RAHWAN:calmness of water surface, or open space between two established things.

4.5 QARRA:something getting established at some place. to stay for some time

4.6 WAQAFA:pause,work break
